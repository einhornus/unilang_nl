verzekeringspremie (66790)



Etymology

From verzekering (“insurance”) + -s- + premie (“price”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧ze‧ke‧rings‧pre‧mie

Noun

verzekeringspremie f (plural verzekeringspremies, diminutive verzekeringspremietje n)

  1. The amount to be paid for an insurance policy, insurance premium
