strooien (17106)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /stroːi̯ə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: strooi‧en
  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From stro +‎ -en.

Adjective[edit]

strooien (not comparable)

  1. strawen, made of straw
Related terms[edit]
  • stro

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch strôyen, from Old Dutch *strōien, from Proto-Germanic *strawjaną.

Verb[edit]

strooien

  1. to strew, scatter
  2. to sprinkle

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

strooien
