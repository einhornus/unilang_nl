Etymology 1[edit]

From gas (geven) +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

gassen

  1. (intransitive, colloquial) to hit the gas, to accelerate a motor vehicle
  2. (intransitive, colloquial) to drive wildly and at high speed
Synonyms[edit]
