doorloopt (38376)


Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /doːrˈloːpt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -oːpt

Verb

doorloopt

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of doorlopen (“to go through”)
  2. (archaic) plural imperative of doorlopen (“to go through”)
