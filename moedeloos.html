moedeloos (41917)



Etymology

From moed (“courage”) +‎ -e- +‎ -loos (“-less”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmudəloːs/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

moedeloos (comparative moedelozer, superlative meest moedeloos or moedeloost)

  1. despondent, discouraged (in low spirits from loss of hope or courage)
