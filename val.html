  • Rhymes: -ɑl
  • audio

Etymology 1

From the verb vallen (“to fall”).

Noun

val m (plural vallen, diminutive valletje n)

  1. fall (act of falling)
  2. downfall, demise
    Synonym: ondergang
  3. trap, snare
  4. (in compounds) case
  5. (in compounds) nightfall
