eruitzien (13027)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From er +‎ uitzien.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɛˈrœy̯tsin/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

eruitzien

  1. (copulative) to look, to look like, to have a certain appearance

Usage notes[edit]

In a main clause, the separable part eruit can be further separated when a predicate is added:

  • Ik zie er goed uit.
    I look good.
