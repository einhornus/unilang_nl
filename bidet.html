bidet (41035)


Etymology

From French bidet.

Pronunciation

  • (Uk) IPA(key): [ˈbiːdeɪ]
  • Audio (Us)
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -iːdeɪ
  • (Us) IPA(key): [bɨˈdeɪ]

Noun

bidet (plural bidets)

  1. a low-mounted plumbing fixture or type of sink intended for washing the external genitalia and the anus.
  2. (obsolete) A small horse formerly allowed to each trooper or dragoon for carrying his baggage.
