esdoorn (42352)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch asdoren. Equivalent to es (“ash”) +‎ doorn (“thorn”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛs.doːrn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: es‧doorn

Noun

esdoorn m (plural esdoorns or esdoornen, diminutive esdoorntje n)

  1. maple, tree of the genus Acer.
    Synonym: ahorn
