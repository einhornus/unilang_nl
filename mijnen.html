Etymology 1[edit]

Verb[edit]

mijnen

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to mine (to excavate mineral resources)
  2. (transitive, intransitive) to sap, to mine (to undermine the enemy with tunnelling and explosives)
    Synonym: mineren
