rijgen (40241)

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch rien, rigen, from Old Dutch *rīan, from Proto-Germanic *rīhaną.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɛi̯.ɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rij‧gen
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

rijgen

  1. (transitive) to pierce, to impale, to spit
  2. (transitive) to baste (to sew a thread through something)
