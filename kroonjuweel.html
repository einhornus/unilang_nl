kroonjuweel (43528)


Etymology

Compound of kroon (“crown”) +‎ juweel (“jewel”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkroːn.jyˌ(ʋ)eːl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kroon‧ju‧weel

Noun

kroonjuweel n (plural kroonjuwelen, diminutive kroonjuweeltje n)

  1. crown jewel, one of a set of regalia
  2. (chiefly politics) core ideal, the part(s) of a political platform that are considered essential
  3. (chiefly in the plural) See kroonjuwelen.
