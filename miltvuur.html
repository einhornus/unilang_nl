miltvuur (23424)


Etymology

Compound of milt (“spleen”) +‎ vuur (“fire, inflammation, infection”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɪltfyr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: milt‧vuur

Noun

miltvuur n (uncountable)

  1. The highly contagious disease anthrax which kills humans and animals

Synonyms

  • antrax (Latin)
  • miltbrand m
  • milttering
  • miltziekte (wider?)

Related terms

  • mildzucht
