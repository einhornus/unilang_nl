Noun[edit]

ree f or n (plural reeën, diminutive reetje n)

  1. The roe, Capreolus capreolus.
  2. Any deer of the genus Capreolus.

Etymology 2[edit]

Syncopic form of rede.

Noun[edit]

ree f (plural reeën)

  1. (now literary or dialectal) Alternative form of rede (“anchorage”).
