Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch dronc, from Old Dutch *drunc, from Proto-Germanic *drunkiz (“drink”). More at drink.

Noun

dronk m (plural dronken, diminutive dronkje n)

  1. drink; draught

Etymology 2

Verb

dronk

  1. singular past indicative of drinken
