cabernet (32742)


Etymology

From the Médoc dialect of southwestern France, but of uncertain ultimate origin; possibly Late Latin caput nigrum, in this sense "black vine."

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

cabernet m (plural cabernets)

  1. Cabernet (grape variety)
