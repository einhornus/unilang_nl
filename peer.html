peer (14913)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch pēre, from Latin pira.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /peːr/, [pɪːr]
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: peer
  • Rhymes: -eːr

Noun

peer f (plural peren, diminutive peertje n)

  1. A pear, a fruit of the pear tree.
  2. A light bulb.

Noun

peer m (plural peren, diminutive peertje n)

  1. A pear tree, Pyrus communis.
