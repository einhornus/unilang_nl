lem (30428)



Etymology

From Dutch lijm, from Middle Dutch lijm, from Old Dutch *līm, from Proto-Germanic *līmaz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈlɛm]
  • Hyphenation: lèm

Noun

lem or lèm

  1. glue, any sticky adhesive substance.
    Synonym: perekat

Synonyms

  • gam (Brunei, Malaysia, Singapore)
  • perekat (Brunei, Malaysia, Singapore)
