moeras (4825)


Etymology[edit]

Alteration (by assimilation to moer (“moor”)) of Middle Dutch marasch, from Old French mareis (“marsh”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /muˈrɑs/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: moe‧ras
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

moeras n (plural moerassen, diminutive moerasje n)

  1. swamp, morass
    Synonym: zwamp
