vakbond (5630)


Etymology[edit]

Compound of vak +‎ bond.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɑk.bɔnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vak‧bond

Noun[edit]

vakbond m (plural vakbonden, diminutive vakbondje n)

  1. trade union
    Synonym: syndicaat

Related terms[edit]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

vakbond
