bekogelen (36311)

<br>

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bəˈkoːɣələ(n)/
  • Hyphenation: be‧ko‧ge‧len

Etymology 1[edit]

From be- +‎ kogelen.

Verb[edit]

bekogelen

  1. (transitive) to bombard, to launch (e.g. shoot or throw]) a hail of projectiles at a target
    Synonym: bombarderen
  2. (transitive) to besiege, to systematically attack or taunt by any (e.g. verbal) means
    Synonym: belagen

Etymology 2[edit]

A rare alternate form of begoochelen, perhaps parallel to bekokkelen.

Verb[edit]

bekogelen

  1. (transitive, rare) to delude, play tricks on someone
