Etymology[edit]

From mythe +‎ -logie. From Middle French mythologie, from Latin mythologia, from Ancient Greek μυθολογία (muthología, “legend”) μυθολογέω (muthologéō, “I tell tales”), from μυθολόγος (muthológos, “legend”), from μῦθος (mûthos, “story”) + λέγω (légō, “I say”).

Noun[edit]

mythologie f (plural mythologieën, diminutive mythologietje n)

  1. mythology (collection and study of myths)
