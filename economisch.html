economisch (18822)



Etymology

From economie (“economics, economy”) + -isch (“-ish, -ic(al)”). Ultimately from Ancient Greek.

Pronunciation

  • economisch
    		(file)

Adjective

economisch (comparative economischer, superlative meest economisch or economischt)

  1. economic, relating to the economy
  2. economic, relating to economics
  3. economical, relating to economizing
    Ideale besparingen zijn economische alternatieven met dezelfde performantie
    Ideal spending cuts are economical alternatives with equal performance
