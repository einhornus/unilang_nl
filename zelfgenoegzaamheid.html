zelfgenoegzaamheid (66453)



Etymology

From zelfgenoegzaam +‎ -heid.

Noun

zelfgenoegzaamheid f (uncountable)

  1. self-satisfaction, smugness, complacency (quality of being overly pleased with oneself and one's actions)
