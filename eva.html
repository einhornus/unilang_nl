eva (4130)


Etymology

From Eva (“Eve”), referring to the fig leaves the postlapsarian Eve wore to cover her vulva in the Biblical story.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈeː.vaː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: eva

Noun

eva f (plural eva's, diminutive evaatje n)

  1. A half-apron.
