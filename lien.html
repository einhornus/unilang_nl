Etymology 1[edit]

From Old Dutch *līan, from Proto-Germanic <small>[Term?].

Verb[edit]

liën

  1. (transitive) to admit
  2. (transitive) to acknowledge, to be convinced
  3. (transitive) to declare
  4. (intransitive) to assent
