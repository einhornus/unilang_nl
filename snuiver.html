Noun

snuiver m (plural snuivers, diminutive snuivertje n)

  1. One who sniffs (smells, fragrances) or insufflates (tobacco, cocaine, etc.).
  2. An exhaust or ventilation duct.
    1. (obsolete) A small chimney.
