Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch marmer, from Latin marmor, from Ancient Greek μάρμαρος (mármaros).

Noun

marmer n (uncountable)

  1. marble (type of stone).
Related terms
  • marmeren
  • marmergroeve

Verb

marmer
