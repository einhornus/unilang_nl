rente (6360)


Etymology[edit]

From Old French rente (1230–1231),[1] cognate with Icelandic renta and Danish rente.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɛn.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ren‧te

Noun[edit]

rente f (plural rentes or renten, diminutive rentetje n)

Synonyms[edit]
