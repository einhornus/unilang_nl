toverstaf (26890)


Etymology

From toveren (“to perform magic”) +‎ staf (“staff, stick”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtoː.vərˌstɑf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: to‧ver‧staf

Noun

toverstaf m (plural toverstaven, diminutive toverstafje n)

  1. magic wand, stick or staff used to perform magic
    Synonym: toverstok
