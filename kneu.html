kneu (104246)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch cnuut, cnuyt (“jackdaw, crow”), from cnoteren, cnueteren, cneuteren (“to twitter”). Equivalent to an apocope of kneuter.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /knøː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kneu
  • Rhymes: -øː

Noun

kneu f or m (plural kneus, diminutive kneutje n)

  1. common linnet, Linaria cannabina
    Synonyms: heikneuter, kneuter

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kneu v/m
