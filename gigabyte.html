gigabyte (117253)



Etymology

giga- + byte

Pronunciation

  • (Uk) IPA(key): /ˈɡɪɡəbaɪt/
  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈɡɪɡəbaɪt/, /ˈdʒɪɡəbaɪt/

Noun

gigabyte (plural gigabytes)

  1. (Si) 109, one billion (1,000,000,000) bytes. Si symbol: GB
    • 1981, IBM, IBM 3380 Direct Access Storage Description and User's Guide, page 1
      The IBM 3380 Direct Access Storage is a disk storage device with a storage capacity of 2.5 gigabytes (billion bytes) per unit, an increase of almost four times the capacity of the IBM 3350 Direct Access Storage.
  2. (computing) a gibibyte or 10243 (1,073,741,824) bytes.

Synonyms

  • GB, ㎇
  • gig

Related terms

  • gibibyte
  • gigabit

Zelfstandig naamwoord

gigabyte m

  1. (informatica) 1.000.000.000 bytes, oftewel 1.000 megabyte, weergegeven met symbool GB
  2. (informatica) (verouderd) 1024³ bytes, tegenwoordig gibibyte geheten, weergegeven met symbool Gib
