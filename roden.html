Etymology[edit]

From Old High German rātan, from Proto-Germanic *rēdaną. Cognate with German raten, Dutch raden, English read, Icelandic ráða.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʀoːden/, [ˈʀoːdən]

Verb[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="lb">roden (third-person singular present réit or rot, past participle geroden, auxiliary verb hunn)

<ol>
  • (transitive) to recommend, to advise
  • (intransitive) to guess
