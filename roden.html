roden (34735)


Etymology

From High German">Old High German goh">rātan, from Proto-Germanic *rēdaną. Cognate with German raten, Dutch raden, English read, Icelandic ráða.

Verb

roden (third-person singular present réit or rot, past participle geroden, auxiliary verb hunn)

  1. (transitive) to recommend, to advise
  2. (intransitive) to guess
