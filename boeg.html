boeg (7239)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch boech (“forearm”), from Old Dutch *buog, from Proto-Germanic *bōguz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂ǵʰus (“arm”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bux/
  • boeg
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boeg
  • Rhymes: -ux

Noun

boeg m (plural boegen, diminutive boegje n)

  1. (nautical) bow (front of a boat or ship)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

boeg m
