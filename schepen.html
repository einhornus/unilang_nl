Pronunciation[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch schēpen, from Old Dutch scepeno, possibly a derivate of Proto-Germanic *skapjaną (“to shape, create”). Cognate with German Schöffe.

Noun[edit]

schepen m (plural schepenen, diminutive schepentje n)

  1. alderman
