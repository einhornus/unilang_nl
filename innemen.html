innemen (3927)


Etymology

in +‎ nemen

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈɪˌneːmə(n)/

Verb

innemen

  1. to swallow (a medicine)
  2. to confiscate
  3. to conquer
  4. to get in a means of transportation
  5. to charm
