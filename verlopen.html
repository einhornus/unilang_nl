verlopen (4792)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch verlôpen. Equivalent to ver- +‎ lopen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /vərˈloː.pə(n)/, /vɛrˈloː.pə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧lo‧pen
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

verlopen

  1. to progress
  2. to expire, to become invalid
  3. (colors) to fade
  4. (clocks) to go fast/slow, to go out of pace
    Klokken van standaard computersystemen verlopen wel 2 seconden per 6 tot 7 minuten. [1]
    Standard computer system clocks can be up to two seconds off per six to seven minutes.
