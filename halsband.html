halsband (8755)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch halsbant. Equivalent to hals +‎ band.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɑls.bɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hals‧band

Noun

halsband m (plural halsbanden, diminutive halsbandje n)

  1. collar (as worn by pets)
  2. collar, bond placed around the neck to restrain people
  3. (obsolete) necklace
    Synonym: halsketting

Zelfstandig naamwoord

halsband m
