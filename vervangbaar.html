vervangbaar (17937)


Etymology[edit]

vervang +‎ -baar

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

vervangbaar (comparative vervangbaarder, superlative vervangbaarst)

  1. replaceable
  2. (finance and commerce) fungible
    Synonym: fungibel
