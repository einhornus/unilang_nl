gifmenger (117259)



Etymology

Compound of gif (“poison”) + menger (“mixer, one who mixes”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɪfˌmɛ.ŋər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gif‧men‧ger

Noun

gifmenger m (plural gifmengers)

  1. One who mixes poison into food or drinks, a poisoner.
    • 1723 May 18, Jacob Campo Weyerman, Den Amsterdamschen Hermes, Vol. 2, No. 34, page 267.
      Ik myn allerliefste (antwoorde de geduldige Gifmenger) die verzoek om te mogen binnen komen?
