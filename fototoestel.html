Etymology[edit]

Compound of foto (“photograph”) +‎ toestel (“device, machine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈfoː.toː.tuˌstɛl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fo‧to‧toe‧stel

Noun[edit]

fototoestel n (plural fototoestellen, diminutive fototoestelletje n)

  1. A still camera; a camera used to shoot still pictures.

Synonyms[edit]

  • camera
  • fotocamera
