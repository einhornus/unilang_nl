sofa (10959)


Etymology

Borrowed from French sofa, perhaps via Turkish sofa, ultimately from Arabic صُفَّة‎‎ (ṣuffa).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsoː.faː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: so‧fa

Noun

sofa m (plural sofa's, diminutive sofaatje n)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) A couch, a sofa.
    Synonyms: bank, zitbank
