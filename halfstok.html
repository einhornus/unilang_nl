halfstok (54340)

Compound of half + stok.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɦɑlfˈstɔk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: half‧stok

Adverb

halfstok

  1. half-staff, half-mast

Bijwoord

halfstok
