snotneus (14090)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Compound of snot (“snot”) +‎ neus (“nose”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsnɔt.nøːs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: snot‧neus

Noun[edit]

snotneus m (plural snotneuzen, diminutive snotneusje n)

  1. snotty nose, often symptomatic of a cold
  2. (metonymically, derogatory) brat, whippersnapper, rather helpless/irresponsible youngster
    • 2015 April 14, Fons de Poel, Brandpunt.
      Snotneus! Dit was Brandpunt, goedenavond.
      You moralistic brat! This was Brandpunt, have a good night.
  3. a specific kind of oil lamp

Synonyms[edit]

  • (brat): snotaap
