Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch ploech, from Old Dutch *pluog, from Proto-Germanic *pl≈çgaz.

Noun[edit]

ploeg m or f (plural ploegen, diminutive ploegje n)

  1. team; crew
  2. plough; coulter
