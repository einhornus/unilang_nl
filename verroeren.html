verroeren (28978)



Etymology

ver- + roeren 'to move'

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vəˈrurə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -urən

Verb

verroeren

  1. To move, to cause to change place or posture; to set in motion
  2. To arouse, move emotionally
