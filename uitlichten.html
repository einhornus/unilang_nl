uitlichten (98273)



Etymology

Compound of uit + lichten.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈœy̯tˌlɪx.tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: uit‧lich‧ten

Verb

uitlichten

  1. (transitive) to lift
  2. (transitive) to select, to focus on
  3. (transitive, information science, of data carriers) to read out
  4. (transitive) to lighten, to cover in light
