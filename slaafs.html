slaafs (72903)



Etymology

From slaaf + -s.

Adjective

slaafs (comparative slaafser, superlative meest slaafs or slaafst)

  1. slavish, obsequious, subservient
  2. slavish, unthinking, uncritically following orders or examples
  3. (uncommon) pertaining to slaves or slavery
