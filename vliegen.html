Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch vliegen, from Old Dutch fliegan, from Proto-Germanic *fleuganą, from Proto-Indo-European *plewk-.

Verb

vliegen

  1. (intransitive) to fly
  2. (transitive) to steer an aircraft, to fly
  3. (intransitive) to soar, to rush
