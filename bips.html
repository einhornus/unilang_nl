bips (25442)



Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Perhaps a shortening of bibbers, itself a humourous euphemism for billen (“buttocks, behind”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bɪps/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bips
  • Rhymes: -ɪps

Noun

bips f (plural bipsen, diminutive bipsje n)

  1. (slightly euphemistic, chiefly childish) butt, buttocks
    • 1967, Carla Lipp, "De twips", Ja zuster, nee zuster.
      Twips twips twips. Eerst de handen op de heupen, dan de handen op de bips.
      Twips twips twips. First put your hands on your hips, then put your hands on your butt.

Synonyms

  • billen
  • kont
  • reet
