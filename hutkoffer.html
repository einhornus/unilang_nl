hutkoffer (27114)



Etymology

Compound of hut +‎ koffer.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦʏtˌkɔ.fər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hut‧kof‧fer

Noun

hutkoffer m (plural hutkoffers, diminutive hutkoffertje n)

  1. A trunk, a large chest used to move luggage.
    Synonyms: koffer, reiskist
