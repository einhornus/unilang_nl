bijzetten (41029)


Etymology

From bij +‎ zetten.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛi̯zɛtə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bij‧zet‧ten

Verb

bijzetten

  1. (transitive) to add, to set beside something else

Usage notes

  • This verb is especially used to mean adding human remains to a cemetery or tomb.
