bloes (12949)



Etymology

Borrowed from French blouse.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /blus/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: bloes
  • Rhymes: -us

Noun

bloes f (plural bloesen or bloezen, diminutive bloesje n)

  1. A blouse (an outer garment, usually loose, that is similar to a shirt).
  2. (now chiefly Suriname) A women's blouse.
