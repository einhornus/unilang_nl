Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch boerde.

Noun[edit]

boert f (plural boerten, diminutive boertje n) (obsolete)

  1. farce
    Synonym: klucht
  2. joke, prank
    Synonyms: geintje, grap, grol, lolletje
  3. jest, mockery

Related terms[edit]

  • boerten

Verb[edit]

boert

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of boeren
  2. (archaic) plural imperative of boeren
