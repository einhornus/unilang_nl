kavel (32552)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch cavele.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkaː.vəl/
  • Hyphenation: ka‧vel
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

kavel m or n (plural kavels or kavelen)

  1. A plot, a lot, a tract of land.
    Synonyms: kaveling, perceel
  2. A lot in an auction sale.
    Synonym: kaveling
  3. A lot, something apportioned by lottery.
