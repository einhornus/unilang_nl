uitlekken (21198)


Etymology

From uit +‎ lekken

Pronunciation

  • uitlekken
    		(file)

Verb

uitlekken

  1. to leak out
  2. (figuratively) to divulge (a secret), to reveal
  3. (Southern) to lick something clean
