marine (2630)


Etymology

French marine, from Latin marinus, derived from mare (“sea”).

Pronunciation

  • marine
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /maːˈri.nə/
  • Hyphenation: ma‧ri‧ne
  • Rhymes: -inə

Noun

marine f (plural marines, diminutive marinetje n)

  1. (navigation) A navy
  2. (military) An armed navy (naval branch of armed forces)

Synonyms

  • (military) zeemacht

Related terms

  • marinier

Zelfstandig naamwoord

marine
