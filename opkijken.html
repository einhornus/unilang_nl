opkijken (9632)


Etymology

From op (“up”) +‎ kijken (“to look”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

opkijken

(intransitive)

  1. (literally) To look (in an) up(ward manner); to lift one's eyes
  2. (figuratively) (used with 'naar ...': to ...) To admire, respect, look up to someone in a higher position
