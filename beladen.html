beladen (21130)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch belāden. Equivalent to be- +‎ laden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈlaː.də(n)/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧la‧den
  • Rhymes: -aːdən

Verb

beladen

  1. (transitive) to burden, to charge, to lade, to freight, to load, to encumber
  2. (transitive) to pack, to pile

beladen (not comparable)
