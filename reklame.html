reklame (100632)



Etymology

From Dutch reclame, from French réclame, réclamer, from Old French reclamer, borrowed from Latin reclāmō, reclāmāre, from clāmō.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [rɛˈkla.mə]
  • Hyphenation: rè‧kla‧mê

Noun

rèklamê (plural, first-person possessive reklameku, second-person possessive reklamemu, third-person possessive reklamenya)

  1. advertisement.
    Synonym: iklan
