Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch sulte (“brine, salted food”), from Old Dutch sulta (“salt water”), from Proto-Germanic *sultijō (“salt water, brine”). Cognate with Old Saxon sultia, Old High German sulza (German Sülze).

Noun

zult m (uncountable)

  1. brawn, head cheese
