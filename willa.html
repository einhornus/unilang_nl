Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Germanic *wiljô. Cognate with Old Saxon willio, Old Dutch willo (Dutch wil), Old High German willo (German Wille), Old Norse vili (Swedish vilja).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈwil.lɑ/, [ˈwiɫ.ɫɑ]
