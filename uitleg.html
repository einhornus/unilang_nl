Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch utelech. Equivalent to uitleggen.

Noun

uitleg m (plural uitleggen, diminutive uitlegje n)

  1. (chiefly uncountable) explanation
  2. (historical) expansion, esp. the expansion of a city outside its previous walls
