Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch tellen, from Old Dutch tellen, from Proto-Germanic *taljaną.

Verb

tellen

  1. (transitive) to count, to enumerate
  2. (transitive) to determine the number of
  3. (intransitive) to matter, to be of significance
