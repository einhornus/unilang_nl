patronus (73626)



Etymology

From pater (“father”).

Pronunciation

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /paˈtroː.nus/, [paˈt̪roː.nʊs]
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /paˈtro.nus/, [paˈt̪rɔː.nus]

Noun

patrōnus m (genitive patrōnī); second declension

  1. a protector, patron

Expecto patronum. I await my protector.
