grasland (42220)

Compound of gras +‎ land.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣrɑs.lɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gras‧land

Noun

grasland n (plural graslanden, diminutive graslandje n)

  1. grassland; may also refer to a pasture or lawn
