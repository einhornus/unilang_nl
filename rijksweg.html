rijksweg (56145)



Etymology

Compound of rijk + -s- + weg.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɛi̯ks.ʋɛx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rijks‧weg

Noun

rijksweg m (plural rijkswegen)

  1. (chiefly Netherlands) A road built and maintained under national authority.
