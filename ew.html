ew (70185)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɪʊ̯/, [ɪ̃ʉ̃͡u]
  • Audio (Us)
    		(file)

Etymology

Imitative, ideophonic. Compare oh, ugh.

Interjection

ew (with as many extra ‘e’s and/or ‘w’s as needed for emphasis)

  1. Expression of disgust or nausea.
    Ew! There’s a fly in my soup.
    Ew! This peanut butter tastes disgusting!
