borrelen (32751)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɔ.rə.lə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: bor‧re‧len
  • Rhymes: -ɔrələn

Etymology 1

From borrel +‎ -en.

Verb

borrelen

  1. (intransitive) to drink spirits
  2. (intransitive) to have a drink, to hobnob [+ met (with)]
