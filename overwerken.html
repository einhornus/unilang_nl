overwerken (12278)


Etymology 1[edit]

Compound of over +‎ werken.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈoː.vərˌʋɛr.kə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: over‧wer‧ken

Verb[edit]

overwerken

  1. (intransitive) to work overtime
  2. (transitive) to rework, to work on (something) that has already been done
Related terms[edit]
  • overwerk
