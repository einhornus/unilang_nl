blinde (4137)

Etymology 1

From blind.

Adjective

blinde

  1. Inflected form of blind

Noun

blinde m or f (plural blinden)

Etymology 2

From blinden.

Noun

blinde f (plural blinden, diminutive blindetje n)

Synonyms
