tuimelaar (58878)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch tumelaer. Equivalent to tuimelen +‎ -aar.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtœy̯.məˌlaːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: tui‧me‧laar

Noun

tuimelaar m (plural tuimelaars, diminutive tuimelaartje n)

  1. One who tumbles.
  2. A common bottlenose dolphin, Tursiops truncatus.
  3. A rocker (implement or machine working with a rocking motion).
  4. A tumbler pigeon (domesticated pigeon, variety of Columba livia).

Zelfstandig naamwoord
