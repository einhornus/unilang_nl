Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch vrage. Compare Old High German fraga. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun[edit]

vraag f or m (plural vragen, diminutive vraagje n)

  1. (countable) question, query
  2. (countable) question, issue, problem
    Synonym: vraagstuk
  3. (countable) interrogative, question (sentence form formulating a query)
  4. (uncountable, economics) demand
    Coordinate term: aanbod

vraag

  2. imperative of vragen
