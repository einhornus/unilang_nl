baard (4249)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch baert, from Old Dutch *bart, from Proto-West Germanic *bard, from Proto-Germanic *bardaz, Proto-Indo-European *bʰardʰ-eh₂.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /baːrt/
  • audio (Belgium)
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: baard
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

baard m (plural baarden, diminutive baardje n)

  1. beard (mass of facial hair that includes chin hair)
  2. key bit
    Synonym: sleutelbaard
  3. (botany) awn, beard
  4. baleen, whalebone
    Synonyms: balein, walvisbaard
