branding (23894)


Etymology

From branden (“burn”) +‎ -ing, compare aestus, which means both fire and tide.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbrɑn.dɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bran‧ding

Noun

branding f (plural brandingen, diminutive brandinkje n)

  1. breakers (foaming waves), surf

Zelfstandig naamwoord

branding v
