krenken (24897)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch crenken, crinken; related to krank (“sick, ill”). Cognate with German kränken.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛŋkən

Verb

krenken

  1. to damage, hurt
  2. to miff, insult
