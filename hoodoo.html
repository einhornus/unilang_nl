hoodoo (45360)


Etymology

Apparently an alteration of voodoo.

Some hoodoos (spires of rock).
A hoodoo, showing why it is also called a tent rock.

Noun

hoodoo (countable and uncountable, plural hoodoos)

  1. (chiefly United States">US) A practitioner of voodoo.
  2. (chiefly United States">US) Supernatural bad luck, or something or someone believed to bring bad luck.
  3. (geology) A tall thin spire of rock that protrudes from the bottom of arid basins and badlands.
