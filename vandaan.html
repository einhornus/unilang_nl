vandaan (551)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -aÀên

Adverb

vandaan

  1. (postpositional) from, away
    Ga van dat raam vandaan!
    Go away from that window!

Usage notes

Mostly used in combination with verbs of motion like komen, as in waar kom je vandaan.
