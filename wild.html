wild (2324)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch wilt, from Old Dutch wildi, from Proto-Germanic *wilþijaz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʋɪlt/
  • Audio

Adjective[edit]

Noun[edit]

wild n (uncountable)

  1. game (food; animals hunted for meat)
  2. wildlife
  3. wilderness
