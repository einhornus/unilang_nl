Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch halle, from Old Dutch *halla, from Proto-Germanic *hallō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to hide, cover, conceal”). Cognate with Low German Hall, German Halle, English hall, Danish hall.
