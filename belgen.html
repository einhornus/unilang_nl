belgen (23233)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch belgen, from Old Dutch belgan, from Proto-West Germanic *belgan, from Proto-Germanic *belganą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛl.ɣə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: bel‧gen
  • Rhymes: -ɛlɣən

Verb

belgen

  1. (reflexive, dated) to become angry
  2. (transitive, archaic) to anger
