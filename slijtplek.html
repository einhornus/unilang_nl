slijtplek (99770)



Etymology

Compound of slijten + plek.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈslɛi̯t.plɛk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: slijt‧plek

Noun

slijtplek f (plural slijtplekken, diminutive slijtplekje n)

  1. A wear, a damaged spot.
