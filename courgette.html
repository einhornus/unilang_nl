courgette (45681)


Etymology

Borrowed from French courgette.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kurˈʒɛt/, /ˌkurˈʒɛ.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: cour‧get‧te
  • Rhymes: -ɛt, -ɛtə

Noun

courgette f (plural courgettes)

  1. A courgette, a zucchini (fruit).
  2. The courgette plant, a variety of Cucurbita pepo
