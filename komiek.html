komiek (9175)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French comique, from Middle French comique, from Latin cōmicus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /koːˈmik/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ko‧miek
  • Rhymes:

Adjective[edit]

komiek (comparative komieker, superlative komiekst)

  1. comical, funny
    Synonym: komisch

Related terms[edit]

  • komisch

Noun[edit]

komiek m (plural komieken, diminutive komiekje n)

  1. A comedian.
  2. A jokester, a funny person.
