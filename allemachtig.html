allemachtig (3447)


Etymology[edit]

From almachtig with shwa insertion.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑ.ləˈmɑx.təx/
  • allemachtig
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: al‧le‧mach‧tig

Interjection[edit]

allemachtig

  1. An expression of surprise or excitement: oh my God!, holy macaroni, God Almighty!
    Allemachtig! Achtentachtig krachtige vrachtwagens brachten prachtige, zachte vachten.
    Oh my! Eighty-eight powerful trucks brought beautiful, soft furs.

Adjective[edit]

allemachtig (comparative allemachtiger, superlative allemachtigst)

  1. (colloquial) Alternative form of almachtig.
