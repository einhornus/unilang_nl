Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch geliken, liken, from Old Dutch *gilīkon, from Proto-Germanic *galīkōną.

Verb[edit]

lijken

  1. (copulative) to seem, appear
    Dat lijkt me leuk!
    That seems fun (to me)!
  2. (intransitive) to look like, resemble (depict well what it is intended to)
    Deze tekening lijkt sprekend!
    This drawing looks just like the real thing.

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch liken, from Old Dutch līcon, from Proto-Germanic *līkāną.

Verb[edit]
