Etymology 1[edit]

From dood +‎ -e.

Noun[edit]

dode m or f (plural doden)

  1. A deceased person
    Synonym: overledene
    Synonyms: levende, ondode
  2. A casualty, victim of a fatal killing, illness, accident etc.
