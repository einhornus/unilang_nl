Etymology

From Middle Dutch wisselen, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch *wissalon, from Proto-Germanic *wihslōną. Equivalent to wissel +‎ -en.

Verb

wisselen

  1. (transitive) to swap, to exchange
  2. (intransitive) to vary, to change (over time, by circumstances etc.)
