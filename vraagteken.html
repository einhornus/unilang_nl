vraagteken (27872)


Etymology

From vraag +‎ teken, perhaps a calque of German Fragezeichen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvraːxˌteː.kə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vraag‧te‧ken

Noun

vraagteken n (plural vraagtekens, diminutive vraagtekentje n)

  1. a question mark (?) [from late 16th c.]
  2. something that is unknown or unclear, something unanswered [from 19th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord
