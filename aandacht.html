aandacht (1222)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch aendachte, from High German">Middle High German andaht, from High German">Old High German goh">anadāht. Cognate to German Andacht. Equivalent to aan (“on”) +‎ dacht (“thought”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːn.dɑxt/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧dacht

Noun

aandacht f (plural aandachten, diminutive aandachtje n)

  1. (uncountable) attention
    Synonyms: aandachtigheid, omzicht, attentie
  2. (uncountable) respect
    Synonyms: eerbied, omzicht, respect
  3. (uncountable) affection
    Synonyms: genegenheid, affectie
  4. (obsolete, countable) meditation (contemplative discourse)
    Synonyms: bedenking, meditatie, overdenking, overpeinzing, contemplatie, reflectie
