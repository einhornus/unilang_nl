Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch riddere, a variant form of ridere, from Old Dutch *rīdere, from rīdan +‎ -ere (equivalent to modern rijder).

Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">ridder m (plural ridders, diminutive riddertje n)

<ol>
  • A knight.
  • (obsolete) One of certain butterflies of the family Papilionidae. <ol>
  • (obsolete) In particular, the swallowtail, Papilio machaon.
