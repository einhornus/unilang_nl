avondster (85058)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch âvontsterre. Equivalent to avond + ster.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaː.vɔntˌstɛr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: avond‧ster

Noun

avondster f (plural avondsterren, diminutive avondsterretje n)

  1. (astronomy) Evening star; the planet Venus as seen in the western sky in the evening.
  2. (astronomy) Evening star; any star seen in the evening, especially the planets Mercury and Jupiter.
