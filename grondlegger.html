grondlegger (39487)


Etymology

grond (“ground, base”) +‎ legger (“someone who lays, 'layer'”)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: grond‧leg‧ger

Noun

grondlegger m (plural grondleggers, diminutive grondleggertje n)

  1. founder, progenitor

Synonyms

  • oprichter

Zelfstandig naamwoord

grondlegger m
