nuchter (4451)


Etymology

Germanic, cognate with German nüchtern

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ʏxtər

Adjective

nuchter (comparative nuchterder, superlative nuchterst)

  1. prosaic, matter of fact
  2. sober, not drunk
  3. (of a person) having neither eaten nor drunk anything
  4. (of a body) empty as a result of fasting
  5. (of an animal) newborn
