administratie (7654)

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch administracie, from Old French administration, from Latin administrātiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑt.mi.niˈstraː.(t)si/, /ˌɑt.mi.nɪsˈtraː.(t)si/
  • Hyphenation: ad‧mi‧nis‧tra‧tie
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">administratie f (plural administraties)

<ol>
  • administration, registration, in particular with respect to record keeping
  • (chiefly religion) act of administering, e.g. of a ritual or sacrament
  • (Belgium) civil service
