opperhoofd (9547)


Etymology

From opper- +‎ hoofd.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔ.pərˌɦoːft/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: op‧per‧hoofd

Noun

opperhoofd n (plural opperhoofden, diminutive opperhoofdje n)

  1. A tribal chief, a chieftain.
    Synonym: stamhoofd
  2. A pope.
  3. (historical) A leader of a colony.
  4. (historical) A military commander.
  5. (archaic, nautical) A captain.
    Synonym: kapitein
  6. (obsolete) A leader of any group.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
