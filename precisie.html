precisie (13544)



Etymology

Ultimately from Latin praecīsiō, probably borrowed via Middle French precision. Synchronically formed as precies (“precise”) +‎ -ie (“-ion, -y”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /prəˈsi.zi/, /ˌpreːˈsi.zi/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pre‧ci‧sie
  • Rhymes: -izi

Noun

precisie f (uncountable)

  1. accuracy, precision

Synonyms
