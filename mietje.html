mietje (3292)



Etymology

Originally a diminutive of miet, from sodomiet. Later also influenced by Mie, and the senses “sissy, wuss” and “effete man” have become more dominant.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmitjə/, [ˈmicə]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: miet‧je
  • Rhymes: -itjə

Noun

mietje n (plural mietjes)

  1. (derogatory) male homosexual
  2. (derogatory) sissy, wuss, pansy
  3. (derogatory) effete man
