prijsschieten (44821)



Etymology

Compound of prijs +‎ schieten.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈprɛi̯sˌsxi.tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: prijs‧schie‧ten

Noun

prijsschieten n (uncountable)

  1. A shooting game in which a prize is awarded to the best performer(s).
  2. An occasion where people (often haphazardly) gang up to engage in uncharitable criticism.

Related terms

  • prijsgeschoten
