Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Middle French basse, from Italian basso, from Late Latin bassus.

Noun[edit]

bas f (plural bassen, diminutive basje n)

  1. bass (instrument)
  2. bass (low frequencies of sound)
  3. bass (singing voice)
