cijfer (8860)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɛifər/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch cifer, from Medieval Latin cifra.

Noun[edit]

cijfer n (plural cijfers, diminutive cijfertje n)

  1. digit
  2. statistic
  3. a grade given for an assignment or class, particularly in primary school

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

cijfer

  1. first-person singular present indicative of cijferen
  2. imperative of cijferen
