tegenpartij (17952)


Etymology

Compound of tegen (“against, counter”) +‎ partij (“party, group”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈteː.ɣə(n).pɑrˌtɛi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: te‧gen‧par‧tij

Noun

tegenpartij f (plural tegenpartijen)

  1. opposing team, opposing party (group of competitive, military, political or legal opponents)

tegenpartij v
