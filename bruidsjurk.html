Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈbrœy̯ts.jʏrk/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

bruidsjurk f (plural bruidsjurken, diminutive bruidsjurkje n)

  1. (chiefly Netherlands) wedding dress

Synonyms[edit]

  • trouwkleed (Belgium)
