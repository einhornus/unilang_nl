Noun[edit]

cliënt m (plural cliënten, diminutive cliëntje n, feminine cliënte)

  1. (male) client, customer

Usage notes[edit]

  • The difference between Dutch klant and cliënt is that the former buys goods and the latter pays for services.
  • Not to be confused with Dutch client.
