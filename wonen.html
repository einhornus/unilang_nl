wonen (738)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch wōnen, from Old Dutch wonon, from Proto-Germanic *wunāną, from Proto-Indo-European *wenh₁- (“to wish, love”).

Pronunciation[edit]

Verb[edit]

wonen
