ongedierte (7024)


Etymology[edit]

From on- +‎ gedierte.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔn.ɣəˌdiːr.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧ge‧dier‧te

Noun[edit]

ongedierte n (uncountable)

  1. (collective) vermin, pests, harmful or undesirable animals
  2. (figuratively, derogatory, offensive) undesirable people

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ongedierte
