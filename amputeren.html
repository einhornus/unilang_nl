Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Middle French amputer.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɑmpyˈteːrə(n)/
  • amputeren

Verb[edit]

amputeren

  1. (transitive, medicine) to amputate, surgically remove a body part
  2. (transitive, figuratively) to remove something essential, to maim, weaken greatly
