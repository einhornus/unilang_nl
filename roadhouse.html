roadhouse (plural roadhouses)

  1. (chiefly United States">US) An inn or similar establishment situated beside a road beyond the jurisdiction of a town or city.
  2. (theater) A receiving house.

the current "inn" definition does not match the use of the word in Australia where a roadhouse is something like a truckstop or petrol station with attached restaurant along a highway in a rural area.">
