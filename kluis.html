kluis (1783)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch cluse, from Medieval Latin clūsa, variant of clausa.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /klœy̯s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kluis
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

kluis f (plural kluizen, diminutive kluisje n)

  1. A safe.
  2. (in the diminutive) A cashbox.
  3. (often diminutive) A locker.
  4. A hermit's cell.
  5. A hermitage.

Synonyms[edit]

  • (safe): brandkast
  • (locker): locker
  • (hermit's cell): cel
  • (hermitage): hermitage, kluizenarij
