pastor (13546)


Etymology

From Dutch pastoor, from Middle Dutch pastōor, from Latin pāstor, from pāscō (“to feed, maintain, pasture, graze”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“to protect”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pastor/
  • Hyphenation: pas‧tor

Noun

pastor (plural, first-person possessive pastorku, second-person possessive pastormu, third-person possessive pastornya)

  1. (Christianity, Roman Catholicism) parish priest

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pastor m

  1. (religie) (beroep) zielenherder, zielzorger, pastoor (bij rooms-katholieken), dominee (bij protestanten)
Verwante begrippen
  • geestelijke, predikant, priester, voorganger
