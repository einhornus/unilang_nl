kinderfeest (128586)



Etymology

Compound of kind + feest.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɪn.dər.feːst/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

kinderfeest n (plural kinderfeesten, diminutive kinderfeestje n)

  1. any party or holiday celebrated primarily by children
    Sinterklaas is een bekend Nederlands kinderfeest.
    Sinterklaas is a well-known Dutch holiday for children.
  2. (specifically, usually in the diminutive) a child's birthday party
    Ik word morgen negen, kom je ook naar mijn kinderfeestje?
    I'm turning nine tomorrow, are you coming to my birthday party too?
