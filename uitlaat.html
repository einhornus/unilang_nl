uitlaat (13246)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈœy̯tlaːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: uit‧laat

Etymology 1[edit]

From uit- +‎ laat. Compare West Frisian útlaat, English outlet.

Noun[edit]

uitlaat m (plural uitlaten, diminutive uitlaatje n)

  1. exhaust, system through which burned gases, steam etc. are vented or otherwise discharged; especially used for a car exhaust
  2. any outlet, to relieve pressure, literally and figuratively

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

uitlaat

  1. first-, second- and third-person singular present indicative of uitlaten (when using a subclause)
