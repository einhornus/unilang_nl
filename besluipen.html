besluipen (13762)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch beslupen. Equivalent to be- +‎ sluipen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈslœy̯pə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧slui‧pen
  • Rhymes: -œy̯pən

Verb

besluipen

  1. (transitive) to sneak up on, stalk, to approach using stealth
  2. (transitive, figuratively) to bother constantly, to harass
