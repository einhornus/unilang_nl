Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch behouden, from Old Dutch *bihaldan, from Proto-Germanic *bihaldaną (“to keep”). Equivalent to be- +‎ houden.

Verb

behouden

  1. (transitive) to save, keep
  2. (transitive) to preserve
