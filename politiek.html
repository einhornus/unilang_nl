politiek (1975)


Etymology

Borrowed from French politique. By analogy with Aristotle’s "τα πολιτικά" ('affairs of state').

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /poːliˈtik/
  • Audio

  • Rhymes: -ik

Noun

Adjective

  1. political
