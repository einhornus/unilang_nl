politiek (1975)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French politique. By analogy with Aristotle’s "τα πολιτικά" ('affairs of state').

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /poːliˈtik/

politiek (comparative politieker, superlative politiekst)
