debutante (39680)



Etymology

Borrowed from French débutante or from debutant + -e.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌdeː.byˈtɑn.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: de‧bu‧tan‧te
  • Rhymes: -ɑntə

Noun

debutante f (plural debutantes, masculine debutant)

  1. A debutante, a woman who debuts.

Related terms

  • debuteren
  • debuut
