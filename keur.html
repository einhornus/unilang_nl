Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch core, cuere, from Old Dutch kuri, from Proto-Germanic *kuziz.

Noun[edit]

keur f (plural keuren, diminutive keurtje n)

  1. choice
    Synonyms: keus, keuze
  2. selection
    Synonyms: uitkiezing, selectie, verkiezing
  3. elite
    Synonym: elite
  4. seal
    Synonym: keurmerk
  5. a local ordinance

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

keur

  1. first-person singular present indicative of keuren
  2. imperative of keuren
