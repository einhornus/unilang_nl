Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Middle French buste, from Italian busto, from Latin bustum.

Noun[edit]

buste f (plural busten or bustes, diminutive bustetje n)

  1. bust (a sculptural portrayal of a person's head and shoulders)
  2. breast, of a woman
Synonyms[edit]
  • borstbeeld
