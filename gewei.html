Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Geweih, from Middle High German gewīge. Doublet of gewicht.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈʋɛi̯/
  • gewei
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧wei
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

gewei n (plural geweien, diminutive geweitje n)

  1. An antler or a set of antlers, the bony structure(s) on the head of deer, moose or other antilope species.
    Synonym: gewicht

Zelfstandig naamwoord

gewei
