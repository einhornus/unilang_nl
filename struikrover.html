struikrover (46369)



Etymology

From struik + rover.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: struik‧ro‧ver

Noun

struikrover m (plural struikrovers, diminutive struikrovertje n)

  1. highwayman, literally one who ambushes their victims from behind the bushes
