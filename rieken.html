rieken (113507)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch rieken, from Old Dutch riecan, from Proto-Germanic *reukaną. Doublet of ruiken. Cognate to English reek.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ikən

Verb

rieken

  1. (formal) to stink
  2. (Belgium, informal) to smell (i.e. to produce, give off a smell)
  3. (Belgium, informal) to smell (i.e. to sense a smell)
