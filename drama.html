drama (4372)


Etymology

Borrowed from Late Latin drāma, from Ancient Greek δρᾶμα (drâma).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdraː.maː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dra‧ma
  • Rhymes: -aːmaː

Noun

drama n (plural drama's, diminutive dramaatje n)

  1. drama (theatrical work; anything involving play acting).
  2. Something tragic, a tragedy.
