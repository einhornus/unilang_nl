gilde (7300)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch gilde, ghilde, gulde, from Old Dutch *gilda, from Proto-Germanic *geldaną (“to pay”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɪl.də/
  • Rhymes: -ɪldə
  • Hyphenation: gil‧de

Noun[edit]

gilde n or f (plural gilden or gildes)

  1. guild

Verb[edit]

gilde

  1. singular past indicative and subjunctive of gillen
