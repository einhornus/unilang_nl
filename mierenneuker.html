mierenneuker (63992)



Etymology

From mierenneuken +‎ -er. Literally “antscrewer”; “one who has sexual intercourse with ants”.[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmiːrənøːkər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mie‧ren‧neu‧ker

Noun

mierenneuker m (plural mierenneukers, diminutive mierenneukertje n or mierenneukerke n)

  1. (derogatory, vulgar) a petty person who makes a pointless fuss about insignificant details or issues; a nitpicker
    de mierenneukers van de Vlaamse overheid
    the "ant screwers" of the Flemish Government
    Synonyms: azijnpisser, haarklover, haarsplitter, kommaneuker, beuzelaar, chicaneur, criticaster, haarklover, kanarieneuker, krentenweger, muggenzifter, peuteraar, pezewever, pietepeuter, pietepeuteraar, pietjesneuker, pietlut, scherpslijper, vitter
