neuro (9034)


Etymology[edit]

Blend of noorden +‎ euro or noordelijk +‎ euro, probably also influenced by neuro-.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈnøː.roː/
  • Hyphenation: neu‧ro

Noun[edit]

neuro c (plural neuro's, diminutive neurootje n)

  1. (politics) a (putative) common currency for northern Europe
