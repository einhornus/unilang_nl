Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈmaːlən/
  • Hyphenation: ma‧len
  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch mālen, from Old Dutch *malan, from Proto-West Germanic *malan, from Proto-Germanic *malaną.

Verb[edit]

malen

  1. to grind

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch mâlen, from Old Dutch *mālon, denominative of *māl (“spot, stain”) (modern maal).

Verb[edit]

malen

  1. (obsolete) to paint
