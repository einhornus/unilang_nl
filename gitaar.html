gitaar (4299)


Etymology[edit]

Either borrowed from French guitare (from Spanish) or directly from Spanish guitarra, from Arabic قِيثَارَة‏‎ (qīṯāra), from Latin cithara, from Ancient Greek κιθάρα (kithára).
