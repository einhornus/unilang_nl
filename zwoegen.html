zwoegen (17711)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch *swoegen (attested in derived forms), from Old Dutch *swuogan, from Proto-Germanic *swōganą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzʋu.ɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zwoe‧gen
  • Rhymes: -uɣən

Verb

zwoegen

  1. to heave, to breathe heavily
  2. (figuratively) to toil, to drudge
    Synonyms: hosselen, knoeften, ploeteren

Werkwoord

zwoegen
