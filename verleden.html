verleden (1062)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch verleden, the past participle of verliden (“to pass by”) (modern verlijden).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

verleden (not comparable)

  1. (dated) gone by, past
  2. previous, last
