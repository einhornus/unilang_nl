werkman (55416)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch wercman. Equivalent to werken (“to work”) + man (“man”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛrk.mɑn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: werk‧man

Noun

werkman m (plural werkmannen or werklieden or werklui, diminutive werkmannetje n, feminine werkvrouw)

  1. workman, (employed) working man; commoner (Can we clean up(+) this sense?)
  2. worker, laborer

Synonyms

  • arbeider
  • werker
  • werknemer
