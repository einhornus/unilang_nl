werpen (5655)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch werpen, from Old Dutch werpan, from Proto-Germanic *werpaną.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛrpə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wer‧pen
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

werpen

  1. (transitive) to throw
  2. (transitive, of animals) to give birth

Synonyms[edit]

  • gooien
