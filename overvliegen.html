overvliegen (27489)


Etymology

From over +‎ vliegen.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

overvliegen

  1. to fly over
  2. to fly in, to bring in by aircraft
  3. to pass from one department or level to the next

overvliegen

  1. ergatief ergens overheen vliegen
