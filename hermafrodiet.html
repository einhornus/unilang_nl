hermafrodiet (57271)



Etymology

Borrowed from French hermaphrodite, from Latin Hermaphroditus, from Ancient Greek Ἑρμαφρόδῑτος (Hermaphródītos).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɦɛr.maː.froːˈdit/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: her‧ma‧fro‧diet
  • Rhymes: -it

Noun

hermafrodiet m (plural hermafrodieten, diminutive hermafrodietje n)

  1. A hermaphrodite. (see usage note below)
    Synonym: manwijf

Adjective

hermafrodiet (not comparable)

  1. Hermaphrodite, hermaphroditic.
    Synonyms: hermafroditisch, tweeslachtig

Usage notes

For humans interseks or interseksueel is now often preferred.
