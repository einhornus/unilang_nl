Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">entree f (plural entrees, diminutive entreetje n)

<ol>
  • the entry to a house, i.e. the room beyond the front door, from which doors to other rooms will typically open.
  • the right of entrance to a building or land where there is no general right of entrance.
  • (dining) (formerly) a savoury served after the fish course and before the main course; (now usually) the starter or the main course itself.

    • Synonyms[edit]

    • (entry): hall, vestibule
    • (entrance): ingang
    • (starter): voorgerecht, amuse
