roekeloos (8251)


Etymology[edit]

From the now obsolete Dutch verb roeken (“to care, to worry (about)”) +‎ -loos. Compare German ruchlos, English reckless.

Pronunciation[edit]

    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

roekeloos (comparative roekelozer, superlative meest roekeloos or roekeloost)

  1. reckless
