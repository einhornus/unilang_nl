dusdanig (45584)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch dusdanich, from dusgedanich, from dusgedaen (literally “thus done, made in such a way”). Equivalent to dus + gedaan + -ig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /dʏsˈdaːnəx/
