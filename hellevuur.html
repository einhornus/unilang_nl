Noun[edit]

hellevuur n (plural hellevuren, diminutive hellevuurtje n)

  1. (uncountable) the fire of Hell; hellfire
  2. (uncountable) fire produced by the Devil, or a similar supernatural creature connected to Hell
  3. (countable) a fire that burns with unusual heat or ferocity; an inferno
