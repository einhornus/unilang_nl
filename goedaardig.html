Etymology[edit]

goed +‎ -aardig

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio

Adjective[edit]

goedaardig (comparative goedaardiger, superlative goedaardigst)

  1. (of tumour or swelling) benign, not malignant
  2. benign, good-natured
  3. minor, unimportant
