middelbaar (18760)


Etymology

middel +‎ -baar

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

middelbaar (not comparable)

  1. secondary

Usage notes

  • This term is used specifically to refer to secondary schooling. It commonly collocates with words such as onderwijs (secondary education) or simply school (secondary school). In combination with 'leeftijd;', however, it just means 'middle'. Van middelbare leeftijd: middle-aged.
