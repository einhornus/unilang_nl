larve (35921)



Etymology

From Latin larva, from Old Latin larua (“demon, spirit of the dead”).[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɑr.və/
  • larve
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lar‧ve
  • Rhymes: -ɑrvə

Noun

larve f (plural larven, diminutive larfje n or larvetje n)

  1. grub, larva (juvenile stage of metabolous insects)

Related terms

  • larvaal
