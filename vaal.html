vaal (55647)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch vāle, from Old Dutch *falo, from Frankish *falu, from Proto-Germanic *falwaz

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vaːl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vaal
  • Rhymes: -aːl

Adjective

vaal (comparative valer, superlative vaalst)

  1. (of skin) sallow, pallid
  2. (of color, lighting etc) pale, light
