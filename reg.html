reg (12669)


Etymology

From Dutch recht, from Middle Dutch recht, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch reht, from Proto-Germanic *rehtaz (adjective) and *rehtą (noun), from Proto-Indo-European *h₃reǵtós.

Adjective

reg (attributive regte, comparative regter, superlative regste)

  1. right
  2. real; true; actual

Noun

reg (plural regte)

  1. justice
  2. right
