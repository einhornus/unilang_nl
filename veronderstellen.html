veronderstellen (17097)



Etymology

Blend of vermoeden + onderstellen from onder- + stellen, calque of Latin suppōnō. Compare German unterstellen (“to assume, to allege”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

veronderstellen

  1. to presume, guess, suppose
