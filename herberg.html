herberg (9908)



Etymology

From Old Dutch *heribergo, from Proto-Germanic *harjaz (“army”) + *bergô (“shelter”). Corresponds to heir, heer +‎ berg.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛr.bɛrx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: her‧berg

Noun

herberg m or f (plural herbergen, diminutive herbergje n)

  1. inn, lodging

Verb

herberg

  1. first-person singular present indicative of herbergen
  2. imperative of herbergen

Zelfstandig naamwoord
