Noun[edit]

schakel m or f (plural schakels, diminutive schakeltje n)

  1. shackle (chain shackle)
  2. link (chain)
  3. link, connection
  4. association, contact

Synonyms[edit]

  • (shackle): sluiting
  • (chain link): schalm
  • (connection):, band, link

Verb[edit]

schakel

  2. imperative of schakelen
