kruisvaarder (33267)


Etymology

From kruis +‎ vaarder.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkrœy̯sˌfaːr.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kruis‧vaar‧der

Noun

kruisvaarder m (plural kruisvaarders, diminutive kruisvaardertje n)

  1. (historical) A crusader, a fighter in the mediaeval crusades.
    Synonym: kruisridder

Related terms

  • kruisvaart
