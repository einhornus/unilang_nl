moslim (7148)


Etymology[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɔslɪm/
  • Audio

Noun[edit]

moslim m (plural moslims, diminutive moslimmetje n, feminine moslima)

  1. Muslim; a Muslim person, adherent to Islam
    Synonyms: islamiet, mohammedaan, muzelman
