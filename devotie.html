devotie (47559)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch devotie, from Old French devotion, from Latin dēvōtiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌdeːˈvoː.(t)si/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: de‧vo‧tie
  • Rhymes: -oːtsi

Noun[edit]

devotie f (plural devoties)

  1. devotion, piety
    Synonyms: godsvrucht, piëteit, vroomheid
  2. an act of worship, a religious mass or service

Related terms[edit]
