postbode (6686)


Etymology

Compound of post (“mail”) +‎ bode (“messenger”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɔstˌboː.də/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: post‧bo‧de

Noun

postbode m (plural postbodes, diminutive postbodetje n)

  1. postperson, mailperson (a person who delivers the mail)

Synonyms

Zelfstandig naamwoord
