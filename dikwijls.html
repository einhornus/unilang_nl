dikwijls (10733)

From Middle Dutch dicwijls. Equivalent to dikwijl +‎ -s.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɪkʋəls/, /ˈdɪkʋɛls/, /ˈdɪkʋɛi̯ls/, /ˈdɪkəls/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dik‧wijls

Adverb

dikwijls

  1. often (frequently, many times)

Synonyms

  • veel
Synoniemen

Verwante begrippen
