vaardig (24009)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch vardich, verdich, vaerdich. Equivalent to vaart +‎ -ig. Originally, the word meant "ready for departure".

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvaːrdəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

vaardig (comparative vaardiger, superlative vaardigst)

  1. skillful
  2. (archaic) ready
