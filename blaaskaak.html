blaaskaak (25023)



Etymology

Compound of blaas (“blowhard, boaster”) +‎ kaak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈblaːs.kaːk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: blaas‧kaak

Noun

blaaskaak m (plural blaaskaken, diminutive blaaskaakje n)

  1. blowhard, braggart
