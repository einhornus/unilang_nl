Noun[edit]

big bang (plural big bangs)

  1. (astrophysics) An explosion giving rise to a universe.
    Millions of big bangs may be happening as we speak.
  2. (Can we clean up(+) this sense?) In project management, a project that has no staged delivery. The customer must wait, sometimes months, before seeing anything. At the end of the wait comes a "big bang".
