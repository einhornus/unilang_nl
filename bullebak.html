bullebak (17231)


Etymology

Compound of bul +‎ -e- +‎ bak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbʏ.ləˌbɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bul‧le‧bak

Noun

bullebak m (plural bullebakken, diminutive bullebakje n)

  1. bully, jerk, churl (rude and nasty man) [from early 18th c.]
  2. (archaic, now dialectal) bogeyman, bugbear [from early 17th c.]
    Synonyms: bietebauw, boeman
  3. (obsolete) evil spirit, imp, demon [early 17th to 19th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bullebak m
