doorsnijden (11886)


Etymology 1[edit]

From door (“through”) +‎ snijden (“to cut”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoːrˌsnɛi̯.də(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

doorsnijden

  1. (intransitive) to keep cutting
  2. (transitive) to cut in two
Synonyms[edit]
  • doorknippen
