doorschuiven (61256)



Etymology

From door + schuiven.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoːrˌsxœy̯və(n)/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: door‧schui‧ven

Verb

doorschuiven

  1. to move, to pass
  2. to delay, to postpone, to defer, to put off
  3. to continue shoving
