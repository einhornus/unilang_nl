drachtig (43882)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch drachtich. Equivalent to dracht +‎ -ig. Compare German trächtig, Middle Low German drachtich, from which latter Danish drægtig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdrɑx.təx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: drach‧tig
  • Rhymes: -ɑxtəx

Adjective

drachtig (not comparable)

  1. (now exclusively of animals, formerly also of women) gestating, pregnant
