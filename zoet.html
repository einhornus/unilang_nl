Etymology 2

From the adjective zoet.

Noun

zoet n (uncountable)

  1. sweets
  2. (figuratively) good things in life
  3. an endearment similar to English sweetie or sweetheart.

Etymology 3

From Middle Dutch soet, from Proto-Germanic *sōtą.

Noun

zoet n (uncountable)

  1. (dated) soot
    Synonym: roet
