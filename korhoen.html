korhoen (56964)



Etymology

Compound of korren + hoen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɔr.ɦun/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kor‧hoen

Noun

korhoen n (plural korhoenders or korhoenderen or korhoenen, diminutive korhoentje n)

  1. black grouse, blackgame, Lyrurus tetrix (syn. Tetrao tetrix).
    Synonyms: berkhoen, moerhoen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

korhoen o
