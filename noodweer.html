noodweer (14116)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnoːt.ʋeːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nood‧weer

Etymology 1

From nood (“emergency”) +‎ weer (“weather”).

Noun

noodweer n (plural noodweren, diminutive noodweertje n)

  1. extremely bad weather (e.g torrential rainfall)

Etymology 2

From nood (“emergency”) +‎ weer (“defence, resistance”).

Noun

noodweer f (uncountable)

  1. self-defence in case of emergency
  2. (law) the right to self-defense

Zelfstandig naamwoord

noodweer o

Verwante begrippen
