vervallen (10950)


Etymology[edit]

ver- +‎ vallen

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

vervallen

  1. to decline, reject
  2. to worsen
  3. to decay, rot
  4. to recede, pull away
  5. (law) to cease to be valid
  6. (of food) to expire

Participle[edit]

vervallen

  1. past participle of vervallen

Adjective[edit]

vervallen (comparative vervallener, superlative vervallenst)

  1. dilapidated, ruinous
  2. (law) having no legal force or cogency, invalid
  3. (of food) expired
