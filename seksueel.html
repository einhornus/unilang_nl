seksueel (4773)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French sexuel, from Latin sexuālis.

Adjective[edit]

seksueel (comparative seksueler, superlative seksueelst)

  1. sexual, of or relating to sexuality in the wide sense
  2. related to sex or gender
