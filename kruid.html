kruid (14225)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch cruut, from Old Dutch krūd, from Proto-Germanic *krūdą.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /krœy̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kruid
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

kruid n (plural kruiden, diminutive kruidje n)

  1. herb; a plant without a wooden stem
    Synonym: keukenkruid
  2. spice
    Synonym: specerij
