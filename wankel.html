wankel (32898)



Etymology

From Old Dutch *wankal, from Proto-West Germanic *wankul.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

wankel (comparative wankeler, superlative wankelst)

  1. unsteady, unstable, tottering
  2. shaky, insecure
