onzin (719)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch onsin (“absurd”), a calque of French non-sens (“nonsense”), equivalent to on- (“un-, not”) +‎ zin (“sense”).

Compare English nonsense, German Unsinn (“nonsense”).
