ziek (638)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch siec, from Old Dutch *siok, from Proto-Germanic *seukaz.

Adjective[edit]

ziek (comparative zieker, superlative ziekst)

  1. sick, ill
  2. (slang) excellent

Adverb[edit]

ziek

  1. (slang) very
