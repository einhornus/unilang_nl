premie (12210)


Etymology

From Latin praemium possibly through English language">English premium.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpreː.mi/
  • Hyphenation: pre‧mie
  • Rhymes: -eːmi

Noun

premie f (plural premies, diminutive premietje n)

  1. A prize, a reward.
  2. A premium, money paid for e.g. an insurance.
  3. A bounty, a reward for killing or capturing a suspect, convict or animal at large.
