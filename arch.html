arch (16721)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Old Dutch *arg, from Proto-Germanic *argaz.

Adjective[edit]

arch

  1. bad, depraved
  2. wrong, evil
  3. shameful
  4. bad, worthless, of low quality

Etymology 2[edit]

Substantive form of the adjective arch.

Noun[edit]

arch n

  1. evil
  2. disaster, misfortune
