muizenval (28618)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch muyseval, from Old Dutch *mūsfalla, from Proto-West Germanic *mūsfallā. Equivalent to muis (“mouse”) + -en- + val (“trap”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmœy̯.zə(n)ˌvɑl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mui‧zen‧val

Noun

muizenval m (plural muizenvallen, diminutive muizenvalletje n)

  1. mouse trap (conctraption for capturing or killing mice)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

muizenval v/m [1]

  1. apparaat om muizen te vangen
