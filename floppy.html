    • Noun[edit]

    <strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">floppy (plural floppies)

    <ol>
  • (computing) A floppy disk.
  • (military slang, Rhodesia, South Africa) An insurgent in the Rhodesian Bush War, called as such for the way they "flop" when shot. [1]

  • (informal) A comic book.
