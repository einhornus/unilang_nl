Adjective

floppy (comparative floppier, superlative floppiest)

  1. Limp, not hard, firm, or rigid; flexible.
    • 2005, Alan Hollinghurst">Alan Hollinghurst, The Line of Beauty">The Line of Beauty, Bloomsbury Publishing, p. 3,

Noun

floppy (plural floppies)

  1. (computing) A floppy disk.
  2. (military slang, Rhodesia, South Africa) An African National Liberation Army">insurgent in the Bush War">Rhodesian Bush War, called as such for the way they "flop" when shot. [1]

  3. (informal) A comic book.
