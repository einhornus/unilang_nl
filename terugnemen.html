terugnemen (11921)


Etymology

From terug (“back”) +‎ nemen (“to take”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /təˈrʏxˌneːmə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

terugnemen

  1. (transitive) to take back (after having lost), to retake
  2. (transitive) to take back (word)
    Synonym: herroepen
