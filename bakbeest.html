bakbeest (85032)



Etymology

Compound of bak (“container, crate”) +‎ beest (“beast”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɑkbeːst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bak‧beest

Noun

bakbeest n (plural bakbeesten, diminutive bakbeestje n)

  1. (Netherlands) whopper, hulk (any particularly large object, person or animal)
  2. (Belgium) drunk, drunkard

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bakbeest o

  2. (scheepvaart) zwaar anker
