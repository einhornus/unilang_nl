pretpakket (89131)



Etymology

Compound of pret (“fun”) +‎ pakket (“package, boxset”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈprɛt.pɑˌkɛt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pret‧pak‧ket

Noun

pretpakket n (plural pretpakketten, diminutive pretpakketje n)

  1. (Netherlands, derogatory) Any curriculum in secondary education with no or relatively mathematics and science subjects; currently the nickname of the cultuur en maatschappij curriculum.
  2. A recreational boxset containing various items.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pretpakket o
