vuurmond (121092)



Etymology

Compound of vuren or vuur +‎ mond.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvyːr.mɔnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vuur‧mond

Noun

vuurmond m (plural vuurmonden, diminutive vuurmondje n)

  1. the muzzle and chamber of a gun, especially of a piece of artillery
  2. a cannon, artillery piece
