vrijwillig (4916)


Etymology[edit]

16th century, from vrij (“free”) +‎ wil (“will”) +‎ -ig (“-y”), probably after somewhat earlier German freiwillig.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

vrijwillig (not comparable)

  1. voluntary, of one's own free will
