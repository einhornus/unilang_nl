Etymology[edit]

school +‎ meester (“master, teacher”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio

Noun[edit]

schoolmeester m (plural schoolmeesters, diminutive schoolmeestertje n)

  1. A schoolmaster, male schoolteacher
  2. (figuratively) A pedantic person

Synonyms[edit]

  • (schoolmeester) meester m, leraar m, schoolvos m
  • (pedantic) pedant m, schoolvos m, betweter m
