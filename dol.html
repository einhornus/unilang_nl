Etymology 1[edit]

From Old Dutch *dol, from Proto-Germanic *dulaz.

Adjective[edit]

dol (comparative doller, superlative dolst)

  1. crazy, silly, mad
  2. mindless, reckless; irate
  3. out of control, gone wild, notably said of a tool or machine
