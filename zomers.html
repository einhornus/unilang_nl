Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch somersch. Equivalent to zomer +‎ -s.

Adjective[edit]

zomers (comparative zomerser, superlative meest zomers or zomerst)

  1. summery
    Antonym: winters
  2. (meteorology, officially) 25 °C or more
