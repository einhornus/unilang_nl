Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch schermen, from Old Dutch *skirmen, from Proto-West Germanic *skirmijan.

Verb[edit]

schermen

  1. (intransitive) to fence
  2. (transitive, obsolete) to protect, to shield
