onvindbaar (16058)


Etymology[edit]

From on- +‎ vindbaar.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔnˈvɪnt.baːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧vind‧baar

Adjective[edit]

onvindbaar (comparative onvindbaarder, superlative onvindbaarst)

  1. impossible to find, untraceable
