leeuw (3566)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch leeuwe, from Old Dutch lewo, from Latin leō, from Ancient Greek λέων (léōn).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /leːu̯/
  • audio

Noun[edit]

leeuw m (plural leeuwen, diminutive leeuwtje n, feminine leeuwin)

  1. lion
  2. (heraldry) lion
    Nederlandse leeuw - Dutch lion
    Vlaamse leeuw - Flemish lion
