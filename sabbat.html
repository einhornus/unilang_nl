Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin sabbatum (“Sabbath”), from Ancient Greek σάββατον (sábbaton, “Sabbath”), from Hebrew שַׁבָּת‎ (shabát, “Sabbath”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɑ.bɑt/

sabbat

  2. (meer algemeen:) rustdag, rusttijd[6]
