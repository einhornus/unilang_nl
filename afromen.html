afromen (77474)



Etymology

From af +‎ romen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfˌroː.mə(n)/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧ro‧men

Verb

afromen

  1. to skim
    1. to remove cream
    2. to scrape off; remove (something) from a surface
    3. (euphemistic) to economise, to cut budget
