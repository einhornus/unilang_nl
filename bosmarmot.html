bosmarmot (27230)


Etymology

Compound of bos +‎ marmot.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɔs.mɑrˌmɔt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bos‧mar‧mot

Noun

bosmarmot f (plural bosmarmotten)

  1. A groundhog, Marmota monax.
