kookkunst (24486)


Etymology

Compound of koken +‎ kunst.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkoː.kʏnst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kook‧kunst

Noun

kookkunst f (plural kookkunsten)

  1. (uncountable) culinary art
  2. (countable) culinary skill
  3. (uncountable) cuisine
    Synonym: keuken
