Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch rië, from Old Dutch *rīa.

Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">rij f (plural rijen, diminutive rijtje n)

<ol>
  • row, line
    Synonym: reeks
  • row (entries in a table going left to right)
    Antonym: kolom
  • line, queue of people waiting for something
    Is dit de achterkant van de rij?
    Is this the back of the line?
  • (mathematics) series
    rij van Fibonacci
    Fibonacci series
    Synonym: reeks
  • (chess) rank
