zelfmoord (1144)


Etymology

From zelf- (“self-”) +‎ moord (“murder”). A calque of German Selbstmord, from New Latin">New Latin suīcīdium.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɛlf.moːrt/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zelf‧moord

Noun

zelfmoord m (plural zelfmoorden)

  1. suicide [from 18th c.]
  2. (figuratively) self-destruction, self-destructive behaviour

Synonyms

  • (suicide): suïcide, zelfdoding

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zelfmoord m/v
