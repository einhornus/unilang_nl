garnaal (15822)


Etymology[edit]

Unclear. Possibly from Middle Dutch grane (“gag”) referring to its feelers looking like the hair of a beard, or from the name Geernaert.

Noun[edit]

garnaal m (plural garnalen, diminutive garnaaltje n)

  1. shrimp, arthropod of the suborder Pleocyemata, especially of the infraorder Caridea
