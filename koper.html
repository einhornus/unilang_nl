Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch coper, ultimately from Latin cuprum.

Noun[edit]

koper n (uncountable)

  1. copper (metal, element)

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch coper, copere. Equivalent to kopen +‎ -er.

Noun[edit]

koper m (plural kopers, diminutive kopertje n)

  1. buyer
