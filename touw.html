touw (2267)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch touwe, from Old Dutch *tou, from Proto-Germanic *tawwą. Cognate with German Tau, West Frisian tou, English tow, Old Norse tó (Norwegian to).

Pronunciation

  • audio

  • Rhymes: -ɑu̯
