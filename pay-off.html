pay-off (89461)



Etymology

pay +‎ off

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpeɪ.ɒf/, American: IPA(key): /ˈpeɪ.ɔːf/

Noun

pay-off (plural pay-offs)

  1. A payment.
  2. A reward.
  3. A bribe.
  4. (colloquial) A resolution or justification of an event that has already occurred, especially in fiction.
