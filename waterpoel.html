waterpoel (58599)



Etymology

Compound of water (“water”) + poel (“pool, puddle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋaː.tərˌpul/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wa‧ter‧poel

Noun

waterpoel m (plural waterpoelen, diminutive waterpoeltje n)

  1. waterhole (place with water where (wild) animals come to drink)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

waterpoel m

  1. ondiep, stilstaand waterbekken van geringe omvang.
