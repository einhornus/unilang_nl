schandalig (8514)


Etymology[edit]

From schandaal +‎ -ig

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

schandalig (comparative schandaliger, superlative schandaligst)

  1. scandalous, shameful
  2. outrageous

Synonyms[edit]

  • schandelijk
