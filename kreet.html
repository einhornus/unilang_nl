kreet (18407)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -eːt

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch crēte, a verbal noun to criten (Dutch krijten). Compare the derivation of beet and bijten.

Noun

kreet m (plural kreten, diminutive kreetje n)

  1. a cry (shout)

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

kreet
