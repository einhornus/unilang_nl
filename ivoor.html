ivoor (19636)



Pronunciation

  • ivoor
    		(file)

Noun

ivoor n (plural ivoren, diminutive ivoortje n)

Moorish ivory pyxis, 968 Ce (Louvre)
  1. ivory, a hard natural material from elephant tusks and some other mammals' teeth
  2. an ivory artifact
