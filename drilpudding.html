drilpudding (39643)


Etymology

Compound of dril (“jelly”) or drillen (“to vibrate, to tremble”) +‎ pudding (“puddding”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdrɪlˌpʏ.dɪŋ/
  • Hyphenation: dril‧pud‧ding

Noun

drilpudding m (plural drilpuddingen, diminutive drilpuddinkje n)

  1. A jelly pudding.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

drilpudding m
