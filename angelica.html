angelica (15719)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio (Us)
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

angelica (countable and uncountable, plural angelicas)

  1. A tall plant, with hollow stems, genus Angelica, especially the garden angelica (Angelica archangelica).
    Synonym: archangel
  2. Candied stems of the plant, used to decorate cookies.
  3. Species of Aralia.
  4. Synonym of angélique (“musical instrument”)
