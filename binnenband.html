binnenband (95155)



Etymology

From binnen + band.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɪ.nə(n)ˌbɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bin‧nen‧band

Noun

binnenband m (plural binnenbanden, diminutive binnenbandje n)

  1. inner tyre
  2. inner tube

binnenband m
