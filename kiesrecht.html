kiesrecht (21582)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From kiezen (“to choose, to vote”) +‎ recht (“right”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

kiesrecht n (plural kiesrechten, diminutive kiesrechtje n)

  1. The legislation governing the electoral process
  2. The right to vote, suffrage

Synonyms[edit]

  • (elctoral legislation): kieswetgeving
  • (right to vote): stemrecht

Related terms[edit]

  • kiezer
  • kiesgerechtigde
  • kiesplicht
