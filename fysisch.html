fysisch (36120)


Etymology

Borrowed from German physisch, ultimately from Ancient Greek φυσικός (phusikós), cognate with English physical.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈfi.zis/
  • fysisch
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fy‧sisch

Adjective

fysisch (not comparable)

  1. (now chiefly Belgium) physical, having to do with matter, nature, the material world
  2. having to do with physics, the natural science
