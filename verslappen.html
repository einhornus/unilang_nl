verslappen (41355)



Etymology

From ver- +‎ slap +‎ -en.

Pronunciation

  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: vers‧lap‧pen
  • Rhymes: -ɑpən

Verb

verslappen

  1. to extenuate (make thin or slender)
  2. to wilt, to fatigue
  3. to relax, sag, slack (to lose firmness)
  4. to relent, slacken, abate
  5. to fade
