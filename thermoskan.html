thermoskan (37690)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Thermoskanne. Equivalent to Thermos +‎ kan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtɛr.mɔsˌkɑn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ther‧mos‧kan

Noun

thermoskan f (plural thermoskannen, diminutive thermoskannetje n)

  1. A thermos (vacuum flask).
    Synonym: thermosfles

Zelfstandig naamwoord

thermoskan v / m
