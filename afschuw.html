afschuw (17254)


Etymology

From afschuwen (literally “to shoo off, shoo away”). Compare German Abscheu, Swedish avsky, Swedish afsky.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑf.sxyu̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧schuw

Noun

afschuw m (uncountable)

  1. revulsion, abhorrence, repugnance, horror, abomination (strong aversion)

Related terms

  • afschuwen
  • afschuwelijk
