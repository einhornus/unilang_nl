eetlust (9278)


Etymology[edit]

From eten (“to eat”) +‎ lust (“taste, appetite, pleasure”). First attested in the early 17th century.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈeːt.lʏst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: eet‧lust

Noun[edit]

eetlust m (uncountable)

  1. An appetite, lusting to eat

Synonyms[edit]

  • appetijt
  • trek

Related terms[edit]

  • eetfestijn n

  • goklust
