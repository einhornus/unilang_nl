Etymology[edit]

From plaat (“record”) +‎ -en- +‎ zaak (“shop”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈplaː.tə(n)ˌzaːk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pla‧ten‧zaak

Noun[edit]

platenzaak m (plural platenzaken, diminutive platenzaakje n)

  1. record shop, record store

Synonyms[edit]

platenzaak /
