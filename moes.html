Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch moes, from Old Dutch muos, from Proto-Germanic *mōsą.

Noun[edit]

moes f or n (uncountable)

  1. mush, pulp (of food)

Etymology 2[edit]

Compare moeder, moe.

Noun[edit]

moes f (uncountable, diminutive moesje n)
