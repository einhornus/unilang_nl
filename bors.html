bors (45762)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Dutch borst, from Middle Dutch borst, from Old Dutch brust, from Proto-Germanic *brusts, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrews- (“to swell, blow, inflate”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bɔ(ː)rs/

Noun[edit]

bors (plural borste, diminutive borsie)

  1. chest
  2. breast
