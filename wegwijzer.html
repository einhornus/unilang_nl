wegwijzer (38679)


Etymology

Compound of weg (“road”) +‎ wijzer (“pointer, indicator”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛxˌʋɛi̯.zər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: weg‧wij‧zer

Noun

wegwijzer m (plural wegwijzers, diminutive wegwijzertje n)

  1. A road sign.
  2. Any other sign giving directions where to go and/or which path/trail/route to follow, e.g. within a building or gardens.
  3. (figuratively) Something giving ample directions in a more theoretical field, e.g. a publication with legal advice, administrative procedures.
