provinciehuis (89044)



Etymology

Compound of provincie +‎ huis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /proːˈvɪn.siˌɦœy̯s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pro‧vin‧cie‧huis

Noun

provinciehuis n (plural provinciehuizen, diminutive provinciehuisje n)

  1. The seat of a provincial government.
  2. (uncommon) A provincial, rural house.
