brouwerij (12706)


Etymology[edit]

From brouwen (“to brew”) +‎ -erij (“-ery”) or alternatively brouwer (“brewer”) +‎ -ij (“-y”). See also the usage notes at -erij.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌbrɑu̯əˈrɛi̯/

Noun[edit]

brouwerij f (plural brouwerijen, diminutive brouwerijtje n)

  1. brewery
  2. (dated) the act of brewing
