saucijzenbroodje (73072)



Etymology

Compound of saucijs + -en + broodje.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sɑu̯ˈsɛi̯.zə(n)ˌbroː.tjə/, /soːˈsɛi̯.zə(n)ˌbroː.tjə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sau‧cij‧zen‧brood‧je

Noun

saucijzenbroodje n (plural saucijzenbroodjes)

  1. A sausage roll, a roll made of puff pastry containing spiced mince.
