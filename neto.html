neto (23081)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Dutch netto, from Vulgar Latin *nittus &lt; *nit'dus, from syncopation of classical Latin nitidus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈneto]
  • Hyphenation: né‧to

Adjective[edit]

neto or néto

  1. net, free from extraneous substances, remaining after expenses or deductions.
    Synonym: bersih
