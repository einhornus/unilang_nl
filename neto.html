neto (23083)



Etymology

From Dutch netto, from Vulgar Latin *nittus < *nit'dus, from syncopation of classical Latin nitidus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈneto]
  • Hyphenation: né‧to

Adjective

neto or néto

  1. net, free from extraneous substances, remaining after expenses or deductions.
    Synonym: bersih
