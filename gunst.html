gunst (2747)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch gunst, gonst, prefixed from ge- + onst, onste, the latter from Old Dutch anst, from Proto-Germanic *unstiz. Equivalent to gunnen +‎ -st.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɣʏnst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gunst
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

gunst f (plural gunsten, diminutive gunstje n)

  1. favour

Interjection[edit]

gunst

  1. (formal, somewhat dated) good grief, gosh

Zelfstandig naamwoord

gunst
