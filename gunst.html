gunst (2747)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gunst, gonst, prefixed from ge- + onst, onste, the latter from Old Dutch">Old Dutch anst, from Proto-Germanic *unstiz. Equivalent to gunnen +‎ -st.

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gunst
  • Rhymes: -ʏnst

gunst f (plural gunsten, diminutive gunstje n)

gunst
