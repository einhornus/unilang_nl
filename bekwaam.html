Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch bequaem, from Proto-Germanic *bi-quēmi-, *bikwemaną. Doublet of bekomen.

Adjective[edit]

bekwaam (comparative bekwamer, superlative bekwaamst)

  1. capable, competent
