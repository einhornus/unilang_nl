Pronunciation

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Yiddish שמועס‎ (shmues, “talk, schmooze”).

Noun

smoes m (plural smoezen, diminutive smoesje n)

  1. excuse, pretext
    Smoezen! Je wilde gewoon met mij praten! ― Excuses! You just wanted to talk with me!
    Synonyms: excuus, uitvlucht

smoes
