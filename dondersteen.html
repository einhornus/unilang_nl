dondersteen (35218)


Etymology

Compound of donder (“thunder”) +‎ steen (“stone”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: don‧der‧steen

Noun

dondersteen m (plural donderstenen, diminutive dondersteentje n)

  1. thunderstone, fulgurite or one of several objects (historically) thought to originate from lightning
  2. rascal, scamp

Zelfstandig naamwoord
