plant (4226)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch plante, from Latin planta.[1] Doublette with clan (“clan, tribe”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /plɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: plant
  • Rhymes: -ɑnt

Noun

plant f (plural planten, diminutive plantje n)

  1. plant, any member of the kingdom Plantae
  2. (potentially offensive) cabbage, vegetable (person with severe brain damage)
