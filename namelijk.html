namelijk (2403)



Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

namelijk

  1. namely
  2. Used to indicate that a statement explains an earlier one.
    Franske is eten gaan kopen. Ik had dat namelijk gevraagd.
    Franske went to buy food because I asked him to.

Synonyms

  • (explanation): immers
