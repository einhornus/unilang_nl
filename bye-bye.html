bye-bye (38459)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From bye, goodbye.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /baɪˈbaɪ/, /ˈbaɪbaɪ/

Interjection[edit]

bye-bye

  1. (informal, often childish) Goodbye.

Noun[edit]

bye-bye (plural bye-byes)

  1. (colloquial, often childish) A goodbye.
  2. (colloquial, often childish) Bedtime for a toddler, going to sleep, going to bed.
    Brush your teeth, it's time to go bye-bye.
    Synonym: beddy-bye
