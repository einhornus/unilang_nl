klaverjassen (82149)



Etymology

From klaver (“club; clover”) + jas (“jack, knave”) + -en.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌklaː.vərˈjɑ.sə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kla‧ver‧jas‧sen
  • Rhymes: -ɑsən

Noun

klaverjassen n (uncountable)

  1. a type of trick-taking card game popular in the Netherlands, akin to belote and tarabish

Verb

klaverjassen

  1. (intransitive) to play the above card game
