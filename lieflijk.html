lieflijk (19827)

Synchronically, from lief +‎ -lijk. Diachronically, from Proto-Germanic *leubalīkaz. Cognate with German lieblich, Gothic 𐌻𐌹𐌿𐌱𐌰𐌻𐌴𐌹𐌺𐍃 (liubaleiks).
