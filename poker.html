Etymology 1

American English, perhaps from first element of German Pochspiel, from German pochen, perhaps from French poque

Noun

poker n (uncountable)

  1. poker (card game)

Etymology 2

From poken +‎ -er.

Noun

poker m (plural pokers, diminutive pokertje n)

  1. somebody who pokes a fire

poker
