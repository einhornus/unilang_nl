zwelgen (44257)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch swelgen, from Old Dutch *swelgan, from Proto-Germanic *swelganą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzʋɛlɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zwel‧gen
  • Rhymes: -ɛlɣən

Verb

zwelgen

  1. to eat greedily, gulp down, gobble up, guzzle
  2. to indulge, revel in
