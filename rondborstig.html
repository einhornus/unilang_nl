rondborstig (73188)



Etymology

From rond (“round”) +‎ borst (“breast”) +‎ -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌrɔntˈbɔr.stəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rond‧bor‧stig

Adjective

rondborstig (comparative rondborstiger, superlative rondborstigst)

  1. busty, round-breasted
  2. honest, candid, open-hearted, straightforward, frank
    Synonyms: frank, openhartig
