gedicht (3563)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch gedichte. Equivalent to an agent noun derived from dichten, from Latin dictare. Compare German Gedicht, Old English gediht.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈdɪxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧dicht
  • Rhymes: -ɪxt

Noun

Related terms
