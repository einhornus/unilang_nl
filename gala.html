gala (17670)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French gala, from Spanish gala, from Old Spanish gala.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈɣaː.laː/
  • Hyphenation: ga‧la

Noun[edit]

gala n (plural gala's, diminutive galaatje n)

  1. A ceremonial celebration, originally a ball (formal dance), now often a prom.
  2. Formal dress.
    Synonyms: galakleding, staatsiegewaad, staatsiekleding
