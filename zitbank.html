zitbank (110644)



Etymology

Compound of zitten + bank.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɪt.bɑŋk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zit‧bank

Noun

zitbank f or m (plural zitbanken, diminutive zitbankje n)

  1. A couch, a sofa.
    Synonyms: bank, sofa
