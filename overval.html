overval (2673)


Etymology

From overvallen (verb). Equivalent to over- +‎ val.

Pronunciation

  • Hyphenation: over‧val

Noun

overval m (plural overvallen, diminutive overvalletje n)

  1. robbery, hold-up

Verb

overval

  1. first-person singular present indicative of overvallen
  2. imperative of overvallen
