grootvader (2805)


Etymology

From groot- +‎ vader, calque of Middle French [Term?].

Pronunciation

  • audio

Noun

grootvader m (plural grootvaders, diminutive grootvadertje n, feminine grootmoeder)

  1. (slightly formal) grandfather [from 16th c.]
    Synonyms: bompa, opa
