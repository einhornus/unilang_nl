vredig (6234)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch vredich. Equivalent to vrede +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

vredig (comparative vrediger, superlative vredigst)

  1. peaceful
    Synonym: vreedzaam
  2. calm
