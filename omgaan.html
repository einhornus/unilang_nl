omgaan (2492)


Etymology 1[edit]

From om +‎ gaan.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔmˌɣaːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: om‧gaan

Verb[edit]

omgaan

  1. (ergative) to go around
  2. (ergative, of time) to pass
  3. (used with met) to handle, to cope, to deal
  4. (used with met) to associate socially
