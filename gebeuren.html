Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch geburen, from Old Dutch geburien (“to happen, occur”), from Proto-Germanic *gaburjaną. Equivalent to ge- +‎ beuren. Cognate with German Low German geböhren (“to happen”), West Frisian barre (“to happen”), Old English ġebyrian (“to happen”).

Verb[edit]

gebeuren

  1. (intransitive) to happen
