arrenslee (95686)



Etymology

From earlier narrenslee (nar + -en- + slee) through aphaeresis. Later reinterpreted as a compound of ar + slee, but the former element is in fact a back-formation from this word.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑrə(n)ˌsleː/
  • Audio

Noun
