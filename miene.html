Etymology[edit]

From Old Frisian mēna, from Proto-Germanic *mainijaną. Cognate with English mean and Dutch menen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈmiə̯nə/

Verb[edit]

miene

  1. to think
  2. to mean
