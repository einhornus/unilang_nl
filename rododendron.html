rododendron (113465)



Etymology

Borrowed from Latin rhododendron (“rosebay”), from Ancient Greek ῥοδόδενδρον (rhodódendron), from ῥόδον (rhódon, “rose”) + δένδρον (déndron, “tree”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌroː.doːˈdɛn.drɔn/
  • Often incorrectly pronounced as IPA(key): /roː.dɔnˈdɛn.drɔn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ro‧do‧den‧dron

Noun

rododendron m (plural rododendrons, diminutive rododendronnetje n)

  1. A rhododendron, plant of the genus Rhododendron.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

rododendron m

  1. (plantkunde) plant uit het geslacht Rhododendron (Familie: Ericaceae)
