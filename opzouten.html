Etymology[edit]

Verb[edit]

opzouten

  1. (transitive) to salt up, to pickle
  2. (transitive) to bottle up, to hold in, to not express
  3. (intransitive, vulgar) to get lost, to go away, to piss off
    Synonyms: aftaaien, opdonderen, opflikkeren, ophoepelen, opkankeren, oprotten, opsodemieteren, optyfen
