prat (37849)


Etymology

Germanic, cognate with praten (“to talk”), pret (“fun”) and English prat (“trick, prank”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

prat (comparative pratter, superlative pratst)

  1. (used with op) focused, bent, fixated
  2. (obsolete) proud, haughty, arrogant
