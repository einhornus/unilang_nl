ansjovis (19055)


Etymology

Borrowed from Spanish anchovas, plural of anchova. The word was phonetically adapted to Dutch vis (“fish”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɑnˈʃoː.vɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: an‧sjo‧vis

Noun

ansjovis f (plural ansjovissen, diminutive ansjovisje n)

  1. (also collective) An anchovy (small, edible saltwater fish species, especially Engraulis encrasicolus)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ansjovis m

  1. (vissen) een klein haringachtig visje
