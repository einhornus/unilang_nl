muur (1078)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /myr/, [myːr]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: muur
  • Rhymes: -yr

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch muur, mure, from Old Dutch *mūr, *mūra, from Latin mūrus.

Noun

muur m (plural muren, diminutive muurtje n)

  1. A wall.
  2. A type of fast-food vending machine with compartments arranged similar to a wall that contain snacks (chiefly in the phrase "uit de muur trekken").

Etymology 2

Noun

muur m (plural muren, diminutive muurtje n)

  1. chickweed
