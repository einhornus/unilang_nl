potvis (59564)



Etymology

Compound of pot (“pot”) +‎ vis (“fish”). The part pot refers to the sperm whale's blunt head due to its melon and spermaceti organ.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɔtfɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pot‧vis

Noun

potvis m (plural potvissen, diminutive potvisje n)

  1. sperm whale, Physeter catodon (syn. Physeter macrocephalus)
  2. sperm whale, any member of the family Physeteridae, including the above and several prehistoric species
