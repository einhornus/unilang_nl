invalide (10366)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French invalide, from Latin invalidus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɪn.vaːˈli.də/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: in‧va‧li‧de
  • Rhymes: -idə

Noun[edit]

invalide m or f (plural invaliden, diminutive invalidetje n)

  1. A disabled or handicapped person, an invalid.
    Synonym: gehandicapte

Adjective[edit]

invalide (comparative invalider, superlative meest invalide or invaliedst)

  1. disabled, handicapped

Synonyms[edit]

  • gehandicapt
  • mindervalide, andersvalide
  • beperkt, gebrekkig, behinderd

Related terms[edit]

  • mindervalide, andersvalide
  • invalideren, invalidatie
  • revalideren, revalidatie
  • valide, valideren, validatie
