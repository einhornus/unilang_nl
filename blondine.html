blondine (13679)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowing from French blondine, from blondin (“blond one”), from blond (“fair-haired”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /blɔnˈdiːnə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: blon‧di‧ne
  • Rhymes: -iːnə

Noun[edit]

blondine f (plural blondines, diminutive blondinetje n)

  1. A blonde (fair-haired) female.

Synonyms[edit]
