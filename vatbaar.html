vatbaar (16759)



Etymology

From vatten + -baar.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɑt.baːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vat‧baar

Adjective

vatbaar (comparative vatbaarder, superlative vatbaarst)

  1. susceptible, receptive, liable
    1. prone to certain behaviours
    2. easily damaged or afflicted by disease
    3. well-disposed to certain things, notion or actions
  2. (dated) comprehensible, understandable
