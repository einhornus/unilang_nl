weeral (24323)


Etymology

Compound of weer (“again”) +‎ al (“already”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋeːr.ɑl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: weer‧al

Adverb

weeral

  1. (Belgium) (once) again
    Weeral dat gedoe met die paspoorten!
    Once again the mess with those passports!

Synonyms

  • alweer (Netherlands)
Synoniemen
  • alweer, opnieuw, nogmaals, wederom, weer
