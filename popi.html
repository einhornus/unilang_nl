popi (53485)



Etymology

From Dutch popje, pop, from New Latin pupa. Doublet of pop and pupa.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈpo.pi]
  • Hyphenation: po‧pi

Noun

popi (plural, first-person possessive popiku, second-person possessive popimu, third-person possessive popinya)

  1. (colloquial) doll.
    Synonyms: boneka, pop
