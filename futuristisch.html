futuristisch (49524)



Etymology

From futurist + -isch.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌfy.tyˈrɪs.tis/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fu‧tu‧ris‧tisch
  • Rhymes: -ɪstis

Adjective

futuristisch (comparative futuristischer, superlative meest futuristisch or futuristischt)

  1. futuristic (resembling something from the distant future; resembling science-fiction; looking fantastically future-like) [from mid 20th c.]
  2. (art) futuristic (pertaining to futurism) [from 1910s]
