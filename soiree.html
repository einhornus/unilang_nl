soiree (41539)


Etymology

Borrowed from French soirée (“evening activity”).

Pronunciation

  • (British English">UK, American English">US) IPA(key): /ˌswɑːˈɹeɪ/
  • Rhymes: -eɪ

Noun

soiree (plural soirees)

  1. A formal evening party.
    • 1987, “Hope Road”, in Hopeless Cases, performed by Anne Clark:
