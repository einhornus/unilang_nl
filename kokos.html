kokos (47082)


Etymology

Borrowed from Spanish coco or Portuguese coco. The final s was originally a plural ending.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkoː.kɔs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ko‧kos

Noun

kokos n or c (uncountable)

  1. the white flesh of the coconut, often in a desiccated form

Noun

kokos c (plural kokossen)

  1. coconut

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kokos o

  1. (stofnaam) de eetbare substantie in het binnenste deel van de kokosnoot
    • Misschien is het wel lekker om hier wat kokos door te doen.
