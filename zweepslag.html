zweepslag (66389)



Etymology

Compound of zweep (“whip”) +‎ slag (“blow, hit”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzʋeːp.slɑx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zweep‧slag

Noun

zweepslag m (plural zweepslagen, diminutive zweepslagje n)

  1. A whip lash, notably as (part of) corporal punishment.
  2. (figurative) Any hard or hurtful blow, e.g. verbal.
  3. Acute pain in the calf as a result of spraining a muscle.
