rocko (34736)


Etymology 1

From Proto-West Germanic *roggō, whence also Saxon">Old Saxon roggo, Old Dutch *roggo, and Old Frisian rogga. Old English ang">ryġe and Old Norse non">rugr are also closely related. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wrugʰyo- (compare Russian рожь (rožʹ) and Old Prussian rugis).
