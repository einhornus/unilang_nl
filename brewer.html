brewer (32013)


Etymology

From Middle English brewere; cognate with Dutch brouwer, Swedish bryggare.

Pronunciation

  • (British English">UK) IPA(key): /ˈbɹu.ə(ɹ)/
  • Rhymes: -uə(ɹ)

Noun

brewer (plural brewers)

  1. Someone who brews, or whose occupation is to prepare malt liquors.
