Etymology[edit]

From onder +‎ tussen.

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔn.dərˈtʏ.sə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

  • Rhymes:

Adverb[edit]

ondertussen

  1. meanwhile, in the meantime

Usage notes[edit]

Ondertussen is sometimes incorrectly claimed to be a false contamination of intussen and onderwijl, but its usage dates back to at least 1548. In contrast, the earliest mention of intussen was from 1722.

Synonyms[edit]
