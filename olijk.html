olijk (114810)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch odelijc, from ode (“easy, light”) + -lijc. The first element is also found in ootmoed and derives from Proto-Germanic *auþuz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈoːˌlək/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

olijk (comparative olijker, superlative olijkst)

  1. cheeky, cheerful
