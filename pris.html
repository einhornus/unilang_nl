pris (46623)


Etymology

From Dutch prijs, from Middle Dutch prijs, prise, from Old French pris, preis, from Latin pretium (“worth, price, money spent, wages, reward”). Cognate to Afrikaans">Afrikaans prys.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈprɪs]
  • Hyphenation: pris

Noun

pris (plural, first-person possessive prisku, second-person possessive prismu, third-person possessive prisnya)

  1. (colloquial) prize, honor or reward striven for in a competitive contest or that which may be won by chance.
    Synonym: hadiah
