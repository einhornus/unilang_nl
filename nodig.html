nodig (129)


Etymology

From Proto-Germanic *naudigaz (compare English needy and German nötig); synchronically analyzable as nood +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnoːdəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: no‧dig
  • Rhymes: -oːdəx

Adjective

nodig (comparative nodiger, superlative nodigst)

  1. necessary, needed
    Dat is niet nodig.
    That's not necessary.
  2. (only in attribute position) some amount or degree of, a certain level of, some
