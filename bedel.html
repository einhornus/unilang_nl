Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">bedel m (plural bedels, diminutive bedeltje n)

<ol>
  • A charm (small trinket or pendant on a bracelet).
  • The practice of begging, mendicancy.
    • Usage notes[edit]

    In the sense “charm” most commonly used in the diminutive form bedeltje. When used as the first component in a compound noun, however, as in bedelarmband = <strong class="selflink">bedel +‎ armband, the main form is used.

    Synonyms[edit]
    • (begging): gebedel
    • (begging): bedelen
