doorzetten (11211)


Etymology[edit]

From door +‎ zetten.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoːrzɛtə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

doorzetten

  1. (intransitive) to persevere, pull through
  2. (intransitive) to persist
  3. (transitive) to continue, to push on with
