Etymology[edit]

From uit +‎ lopen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

uitlopen

  1. to walk out
  2. to originate, to arise
  3. to sprout, to stem
  4. to turn out, to end
  5. to take more time than planned
