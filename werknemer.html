werknemer (6678)


Etymology

From werk (“work”) +‎ nemen (“to take”) +‎ -er (agentive suffix).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛrkˌneː.mər/, /ˌʋɛrkˈneː.mər/
  • audio

  • Hyphenation: werk‧ne‧mer

Noun

werknemer m (plural werknemers, diminutive werknemertje n, feminine werkneemster)

  1. an employee
