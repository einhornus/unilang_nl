praktisch (4265)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German praktisch. The adverb meaning “almost always” is a semantic loan from English practically.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈprɑk.tis/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: prak‧tisch
  • Rhymes: -ɑktis

Adjective[edit]

praktisch (comparative praktischer, superlative meest praktisch or praktischt)

  1. practical

Synonyms[edit]

  • handig

Adverb[edit]

praktisch

  1. practically, virtually (almost always)

Synonyms[edit]

  • vrijwel
  • zo goed als
