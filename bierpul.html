bierpul (119275)



Etymology

Compound of bier (“beer”) +‎ pul (“mug”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbiːr.pʏl/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

bierpul f (plural bierpullen, diminutive bierpulletje n)
