raadslid (11664)


Etymology

Compound of raad +‎ -s- +‎ lid.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈraːts.lɪt/
  • Hyphenation: raads‧lid

Noun

raadslid n (plural raadsleden)

  1. A councillor, a council member; in particular a member of a municipal council.

Related terms

  • gemeenteraadslid

Zelfstandig naamwoord

raadslid o
