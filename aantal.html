aantal (1328)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German Anzahl. Equivalent to aan (“on”) +‎ tal (“number”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːn.tɑl/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧tal

Noun[edit]

aantal n (plural aantallen, diminutive aantalletje n)

  1. countable amount
  2. a number, a plurality
  3. a few

Related terms[edit]

  • getal
