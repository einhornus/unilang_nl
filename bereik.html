bereik (3323)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈrɛi̯k/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧reik
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯k

Etymology 1

From bereiken.

Noun

bereik n (plural bereiken, diminutive bereikje n)

  1. reach (a continuous extent of water)
  2. compass (area)
Related terms

  • bereiken
