hoererij (64753)



Etymology

From hoer +‎ -erij.

Pronunciation

Noun

hoererij f (plural hoererijen)

  1. (somewhat derogatory) whoredom (sexual immorality; prostitution)
  2. (biblical, figurative) idolatry
