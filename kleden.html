kleden (4939)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kle‧den
  • Rhymes: -eːdən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch clêden. Equivalent to kleed +‎ -en.

Verb

kleden

  1. (transitive) to dress
  2. (transitive) to fit someone, to suit someone (of a piece of clothing)
