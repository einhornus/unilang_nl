grijs (5333)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch grijs, from Old Dutch *grīs, from Proto-Germanic *grīsaz.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ɣrɛi̯s/
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯s

Adjective

grijs (comparative grijzer, superlative meest grijs or grijst)

  1. (literally) grey, grey-colored
  2. (figuratively, referring to grey hair) old
  3. weathered, tarnished, affected by wear and tear
    Gozer, ik heb die ille plaat van Jantje Smitje helemaal grijs gedraaid.
    Dude, I wore that wicked record by Jantje Smitje completely out.
  4. partially illegal
