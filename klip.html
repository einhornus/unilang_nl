klip (31132)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch clippe, from Old Dutch *klippa, from Proto-Germanic *klippō.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /klɪp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klip
  • Rhymes: -ɪp

Noun

klip f (plural klippen, diminutive klipje n)

  1. rock or cliff in or around sea water
    Synonym: klif
  2. (dialectal) dune, sandy hill
  3. (archaic) rock on land, e.g. used as a habitat by animals
  4. (obsolete) boulder, piece of rock

Related terms

  • klif
