politie (306)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch policie, from Middle French policie.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /poː.ˈli.(t)si/
  • audio (Netherlands)
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: po‧li‧tie
  • Rhymes: -itsi

Noun[edit]

politie f (uncountable)

  1. police
  2. (obsolete) policy, governance

Synonyms[edit]

  • skotoe
  • popo
