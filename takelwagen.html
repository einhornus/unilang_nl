Etymology[edit]

Compound of takel or takelen +‎ wagen.

  • IPA(key): /ˈtaː.kəlˌʋaː.ɣə(n)/
  • Audio

Noun[edit]

takelwagen m (plural takelwagens, diminutive takelwagentje n)

  1. A tow truck, a breakdown lorry.

takelwagen
