holland (13292)


Etymology

Back-formation from Hollandia. Ultimately from Old Dutch holt lant (“wood land”).[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ ˈholːɒnd]
  • Hyphenation: hol‧land
  • Rhymes: -ɒnd

Adjective

holland (not comparable)

  1. Dutch (of or relating to the Netherlands, its people or language)

Noun

holland (plural hollandok)

  1. Dutch (person)
  2. (singular only) Dutch (language)

Related terms

  • Hollandia
