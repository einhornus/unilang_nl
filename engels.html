Etymology 1[edit]

From the Engels (“English”) weights esterling or pennysterling.

Noun[edit]

engels n (plural engelsen, diminutive engelsje n)

  1. (historical) A unit of mass equal to one twentieth of an ounce, approximately 1.5 grams
  2. (historical) A physical weight used for weighing gold or silver

Synonyms[edit]

  • esterling
