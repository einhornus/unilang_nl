knipperlichtrelatie (69244)



Etymology

Compound of knipperlicht (“flashing light”) +‎ relatie (“relation(ship)”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈknɪ.pər.lɪxt.reːˌlaː.(t)si/
  • Hyphenation: knip‧per‧licht‧re‧la‧tie

Noun

knipperlichtrelatie f (plural knipperlichtrelaties)

  1. A relationship with repeated interruptions because of falling outs and/or breaks; an on-and-off relationship.
