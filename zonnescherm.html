zonnescherm (71564)



Etymology

From zon (“sun”) +‎ -e- +‎ scherm (“screen”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɔ.nəˌsxɛrm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zon‧ne‧scherm

Noun

zonnescherm n (plural zonneschermen, diminutive zonneschermpje n)

  1. An awning.
  2. (dated) An umbrella used to block sunlight.
    Synonym: parasol
  3. Any screen that blocks sunlight.

Related terms

  • regenscherm
  • windscherm

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zonnescherm o
