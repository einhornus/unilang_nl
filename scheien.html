scheien (100140)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʃɑɪ̯ən/
  • Rhymes: -ɑɪən

Etymology 1

From Old High German skiuhen, from Proto-Germanic *skiuhwijaną. Cognate with German scheuen, Dutch schuwen, English eschew and shy.

Verb

scheien (third-person singular present scheit, past participle gescheit, auxiliary verb hunn)

  1. (transitive) to eschew, to shun, to shy away from
  2. (intransitive, of horses) to shy

Etymology 2

Adjective

scheien

  1. masculine nominative of schei
  2. masculine accusative of schei
  3. masculine dative of schei
  4. neuter dative of schei
  5. plural dative of schei
