schoon (1379)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch schône, from Old Dutch scōni, from Proto-Germanic *skauniz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sxoːn/, (Belgium) [sxoːn], (Netherlands) [sxoʊ̯n]
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schoon
  • Rhymes: -oːn

Adjective

schoon (comparative schoner, superlative schoonst)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) beautiful
    Synonyms: fraai, mooi, knap, prachtig, schitterend
  2. (Netherlands) clean; cleansed
    Synonyms: kuis, net, proper, rein
