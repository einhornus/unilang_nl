wolkenkrabber (33649)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From wolk (“cloud”) +‎ -en- (“scratcher, scraper”) +‎ krabber, a calque of German Wolkenkratzer, itself a calque of English skyscraper.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɔl.kə(n)ˌkrɑ.bər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wol‧ken‧krab‧ber

Noun[edit]

wolkenkrabber m (plural wolkenkrabbers, diminutive wolkenkrabbertje n)

  1. skyscraper
    Synonym: torenflat
