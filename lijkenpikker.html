lijkenpikker (69009)



Etymology

Compound of lijk + -en- + pikker.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɛi̯.kə(n)ˌpɪ.kər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lij‧ken‧pik‧ker

Noun

lijkenpikker m (plural lijkenpikkers, diminutive lijkenpikkertje n)

  1. (derogatory, figurative) Someone who benefits from others' misery.
  2. (derogatory, now uncommon) Someone who makes a living from funerals or tending to the dead in any other way.
