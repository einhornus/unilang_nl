Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch lesse, from Latin lēctiō.

Noun[edit]

les f (plural lessen, diminutive lesje n)

  1. course, lesson

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

les

  2. imperative of lessen
