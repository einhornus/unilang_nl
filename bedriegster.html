bedriegster (26785)


Etymology

From bedriegen (“to cheat”) +‎ -ster.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈdrix.stər/

Noun

bedriegster m (plural bedriegsters, diminutive bedriegstertje n, masculine bedrieger)
