alien (11104)


Etymology

Borrowed from English alien (“stranger, foreigner”), from Middle English alien, from Old French alien, from Latin aliēnus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈeː.li.ən/
  • Hyphenation: ali‧en

Noun

alien m (plural aliens)

  1. An alien, an extraterrestrial.
    Synonym: ruimtewezen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

alien m
