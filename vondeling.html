vondeling (42797)

Etymology[edit]

From vond an old deverbal form of vinden (“to find”). Equivalent to vinden +‎ -e- +‎ -ling.

Pronunciation[edit]

Noun[edit]

vondeling m (plural vondelingen, diminutive vondelingetje n, feminine vondelinge)

  1. foundling, an abandoned and/or orphaned but timely found and raised child
