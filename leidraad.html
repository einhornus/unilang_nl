leidraad (34083)


Etymology

Formerly spelled leiddraad, composed of the stem of leiden (“to guide”) +‎ draad (“wire”)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lei‧draad

Noun

leidraad m (plural leidraden, diminutive leidraadje n)

  1. guiding principle
  2. guidebook
