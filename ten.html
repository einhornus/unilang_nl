ten (1729)


Etymology

A contraction of te + den.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /t…õn/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Usage notes

ten is part of many fossilized idiomatic expressions. Being derived in part from te, it is followed by the (similarly fossilized) dative case.
ten is commonly used in Dutch family names such as ten Boom">Corrie ten Boom, ten Brink">Bernhard ten Brink, Marti ten Kate">Marti ten Kate, and ten Holt">Simeon ten Holt.

Related terms

  • ter

Voorzetsel

ten
