nachtwacht (45015)



Etymology

Compound of nacht + wacht.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnɑxt.ʋɑxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nacht‧wacht

Noun

nachtwacht f (plural nachtwachten, diminutive nachtwachtje n)

  1. night guard, night watchman or night watchwoman (person belonging to a night watch, standing guard at night)
    Synonym: nachtwaker
  2. night watch, night guard (group of people who stand guard at night)
  3. guard duty for the night watch (look-out duty at night)
