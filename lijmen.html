lijmen (23773)


Pronunciation

  • lijmen
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lij‧men
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯mən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch limen. Equivalent to lijm (“glue”) +‎ -en, with many Indo-European cognates (e.g. leem (“loam”), possibly slijm (“slime”)), apparently from a root meaning “to smear”.

Verb

lijmen

  1. To glue, join (as) with glue
  2. (figuratively) To convince, win over
    Hij lijmde de bengels met snoep
    He won the rascals over with candy
