neen (2722)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch neen, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch *nēn (“none, not one”), from *ne ēn, from Proto-Germanic *ne + *ainaz. Cognate with German nein.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /neːn/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: neen
  • Rhymes: -eːn

Adverb

neen

  1. no; stressed or formal form of nee.

Usage notes

Neen is the stressed form productively used in Flanders in both spoken and written language. It is archaic in both the spoken as well as written language in the Netherlands.
