echtbreekster (107252)



Etymology

From echtbreken 'wreck a marriage, commit adultery' (from echt 'marriage' + breken 'to break [cognate], wreck') +‎ -ster

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: echt‧breek‧ster

Noun

echtbreekster f (plural echtbreeksters, diminutive echtbreekstertje n, masculine echtbreker)

  1. An female adulterer, who breaks his (or her) marital bond
  2. A female homewrecker

Synonyms

  • overspelige m or f

Related terms

  • echtbreuk
  • echtgenote f
  • ijsbreker m
