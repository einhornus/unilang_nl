Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch billen, probably ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bíþla- (“axe”), instrumental noun from *bītaną (“to bite”). Related to Old Norse non">bíldr (“bloodletting knife”).

Verb

billen

  1. (transitive) to sharpen, to apply grooves to a millstone
