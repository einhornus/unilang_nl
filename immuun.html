immuun (9346)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French immun, from Latin immūnis. The medical sense is a semantic loan from English immune.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /iˈmyn/, /ɪˈmyn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: im‧muun
  • Rhymes: -yn

Adjective[edit]

immuun (not comparable)

  1. immune (not susceptible to disease)
  2. immune (not liable)
