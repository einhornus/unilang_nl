Etymology[edit]

From vrijwillig +‎ -er. First attested in the 18th century.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌvrɛi̯ˈʋɪ.lə.ɣər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vrij‧wil‧li‧ger
  • Rhymes: -ɪləɣər

Noun[edit]

vrijwilliger m (plural vrijwilligers, diminutive vrijwilligertje n, feminine vrijwilligster)

  1. volunteer (one engaged in volunteering work) [from mid 20th c.]
  2. voluntary (para)military recruit or soldier, volunteer [from 18th c.]

Adjective[edit]

vrijwilliger

  1. Comparative form of vrijwillig
