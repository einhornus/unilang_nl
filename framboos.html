Etymology

Noun

framboos m (plural frambozen, diminutive framboosje n)

  1. (botany) raspberry, plant of the species Rubus idaeus
  2. raspberry, a fruit of this plant
  3. (uncountable) raspberry, the reddish color of raspberries
