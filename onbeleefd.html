onbeleefd (4101)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch onbeleeft. Equivalent to on- +‎ beleefd.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔn.bəˈleːft/

Adjective[edit]

onbeleefd (comparative onbeleefder, superlative onbeleefdst)

  1. uncivil, rude
  2. (obsolete) cruel, callous
