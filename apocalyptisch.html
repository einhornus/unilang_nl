apocalyptisch (71179)



Etymology

Borrowed from Latin apocalypticus, from Ancient Greek ἀποκαλυπτικός (apokaluptikós). Equivalent to Apocalyps +‎ -isch; some senses refer to apocalyps instead.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌaː.poː.kaːˈlɪp.tis/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: apo‧ca‧lyp‧tisch

Adjective

apocalyptisch (not comparable)

  1. (Judaism, Christianity) apocalyptic (pertaining to the Book of Revelations or the religious genre in general)
  2. apocalyptic (pertaining to a future cataclysm)
