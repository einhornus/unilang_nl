benoemen (12951)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch benoemen. Equivalent to be- +‎ noemen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈnumə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧noe‧men
  • Rhymes: -umən

Verb

benoemen

  1. (transitive) to appoint, to nominate
    Synonyms: aanstellen, nomineren
  2. (transitive) to name, to call by someone's or something's name
