Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch schure, from Old Dutch skūra, from Proto-Germanic *skūrō. Cognate with German Scheuer.

Noun[edit]

schuur f (plural schuren, diminutive schuurtje n)

  1. barn
  2. shed
Synonyms[edit]
  • barak

schuur
