schuur (3345)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sxyr/, [sxyːr]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schuur
  • Rhymes: -yr

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schure, from Old Dutch skūra, from Proto-Germanic *skūrō. Cognate with German Scheuer.

Noun

schuur f (plural schuren, diminutive schuurtje n)

  1. barn
  2. shed
Synonyms
  • barak

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

schuur

  1. first-person singular present indicative of schuren
  2. imperative of schuren
