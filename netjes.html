netjes

Adverb[edit]

netjes

  1. Diminutive of net (only neatly, properly)

Adjective[edit]

netjes (used only predicatively, comparative netter, superlative netst)

  1. tidy, neat
    Ik moet mijn huis netjes houden. — I've got to keep the house tidy.
  2. decent, proper

Interjection[edit]

netjes!

  1. Nice!
    Je hebt je examens gehaald? Netjes!
    You passed your exams? Nice!
