praatprogramma (44826)


Etymology

Compound of praten +‎ programma. Possibly a Calque of English talk show.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpraːt.proːˌɣrɑ.maː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: praat‧pro‧gram‧ma

Noun

praatprogramma n (plural praatprogramma's, diminutive praatprogrammaatje n)

  1. (television) talk show

Zelfstandig naamwoord

praatprogramma o
