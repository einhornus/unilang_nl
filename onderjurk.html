onderjurk (63706)



Etymology

Compound of onder +‎ jurk.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔn.dərˈjʏrk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧der‧jurk

Noun

onderjurk f (plural onderjurken, diminutive onderjurkje n)

  1. A women's undergarment worn under a dress; a slip, a petticoat.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
