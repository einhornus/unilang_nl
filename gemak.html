Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch gemac, from Old Dutch *gimak, from Proto-Germanic *gamaką, a substantivised form of *gamakaz.

Noun[edit]

gemak n (plural gemakken, diminutive gemakje n)

  1. convenience
  2. ease, comfort
  3. (euphemistic) toilet
