speelhol (99565)



Etymology

Compound of speel + hol.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈspeːl.ɦɔl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: speel‧hol

Noun

speelhol n (plural speelholen, diminutive speelholletje n)

  1. illegal casino, gambling den
  2. (uncommon) a secluded area where children can play
