Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch hert, from Old Dutch hirot, from Proto-Germanic *herutaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂-.

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">hert n (plural herten, diminutive hertje n)
