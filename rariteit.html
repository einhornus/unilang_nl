rariteit (50875)



Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French rareté, from Latin rāritās. The spelling -i- is due to influence from Latin.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌraː.riˈtɛi̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ra‧ri‧teit
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯t

Noun

rariteit f (plural rariteiten, diminutive rariteitje n)

  1. curiosity, curiosum, something exotic or unusual
  2. rarity, something rare
