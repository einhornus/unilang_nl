bultrug (70726)



Etymology

Either from bult (“hump”) +‎ rug (“back”) or a shortening of bultrugwalvis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbʏlt.rʏx/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bult‧rug

Noun

bultrug m (plural bultruggen, diminutive bultruggetje n or bultrugje n)

  1. A humpback whale (Megaptera novaeangliae). [from ca. 1950s]
  2. (dated) A humpback or arched back. [from early 20th c.]
