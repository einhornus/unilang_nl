vriendschap (2380)


Etymology

Derived Middle Dutch vrientschap, from Old Dutch friundscap, from Proto-West Germanic *friundskapi. Equivalent to vriend +‎ -schap.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvrint.sxɑp/
  • audio

  • Hyphenation: vriend‧schap

Noun

vriendschap f (plural vriendschappen, diminutive vriendschapje n)

  1. friendship
