Etymology[edit]

Compound of binnen (“inner, interior, inside”) +‎ zak (“bag, pocket”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɪ.nə(n)ˌzɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bin‧nen‧zak

Noun[edit]

binnenzak m (plural binnenzakken, diminutive binnenzakje n)

  1. An inside pocket, inner pocket

Synonyms[edit]

  • binnenbeurs (obsolete)

Related terms[edit]
