concierge (29390)


Etymology

Borrowed from French concierge.

Pronunciation

  • (British English">UK) IPA(key): /ˌkɒn.siˈɛə̯ʒ/
  • (American English">US) IPA(key): /ˌkɑn.siˈɛɹʒ/

Noun

concierge (plural concierges)

  1. One who attends to the wishes of hotel guests.
  2. (Great Britain">Britain) One who attends to the maintenance of a building and provides services to its tenants and visitors.

Synonyms

  • caretaker
  • custodian
  • janitor
