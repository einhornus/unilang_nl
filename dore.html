dore (93989)



Etymology 1

From Old Dutch thuro, from Proto-Germanic *þurhw.

Preposition

dōre

  1. through
  2. by, through the action of
  3. because of
  4. within (a time)

Adverb

dōre

  1. through
  2. very, thoroughly
  3. throughout (a space, time)

Etymology 2

From Old Dutch duri, from Proto-Germanic *durz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwer- (“doorway, door, gate”).

Noun

dōre f

  1. door
