salto (18351)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian it">salto, from Latin saltus. First attested in the 18th century.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɑl.toː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sal‧to
  • Rhymes: -ɑltoː

Noun

salto m (plural salto's, diminutive saltootje n)

  1. somersault, flip (jump where one makes a 360° rotation) [from 19th c.]
