granaatwerper (47293)


Etymology

Compound of granaat +‎ werpen +‎ -er. Likely a calque.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣraːˈnaːtˌʋɛr.pər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gra‧naat‧wer‧per

Noun

granaatwerper m (plural granaatwerpers, diminutive granaatwerpertje n)

  1. A grenade launcher. [from ca. 1900]
