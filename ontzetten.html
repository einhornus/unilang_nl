ontzetten (33955)


Etymology

From ont- (“de-, away”) +‎ zetten (“to set, put”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔntˈzɛtə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ont‧zet‧ten
  • Rhymes: -ɛtən

Verb

ontzetten

  1. (transitive) to shock, to horrify
  2. (transitive) to discharge, to release from function or service
  3. (military, transitive) to relieve a town or fortress from a siege
