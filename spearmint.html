spearmint (72816)



Etymology

So named from its spiry, not capitate, inflorescence.

spear +‎ mint

Pronunciation

  • (Uk) IPA(key): /ˈspɪəˌmɪnt/
  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈspɪɹˌmɪnt/

Noun

spearmint (plural spearmints)

  1. Mentha spicata, A herb of the mint family, commonly used in herb tea, candy and to treat mild stomach ache.
