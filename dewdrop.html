dewdrop (84022)



Etymology

From Middle English deu drop, dew droppe, dewe droppe, equivalent to dew + drop. Compare Dutch dauwdruppel (“dewdrop”), German Tautropfen (“dewdrop”), Danish dugdråbe (“dewdrop”), Swedish daggdroppe (“dewdrop”), Faroese døggdropi (“dewdrop”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio (Au)
    		(file)

Noun

dewdrop (plural dewdrops)

  1. A droplet of water formed as dew.
    Hypernym: drop
  2. (baseball, slang, dated, 1800s) A slow pitch.
