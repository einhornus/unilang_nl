rouw (9340)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch rouwe, ruwe, from Old Dutch *reuwa, from Proto-Germanic *hrewwōn, *hrewwō (“pain, sadness, regret, repentance”). Cognate with Old English ang">hrēow, English rue, German Reue, Old Norse non">hryggr. More at rue. The noun rouw belongs to the verb rouwen.[1]
