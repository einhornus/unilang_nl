lichtsabel (103724)



Etymology

A loan translation of English lightsaber. A compound of licht (“light”) + sabel (“saber”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɪxtˌsaː.bəl/
  • Hyphenation: licht‧sa‧bel

Noun

lichtsabel m (plural lichtsabels, diminutive lichtsabeltje n)

  1. lightsaber

Synonyms

  • (lightsaber): lichtzwaard
