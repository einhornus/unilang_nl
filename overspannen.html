overspannen (16349)


Etymology[edit]

From over- +‎ spannen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌoː.vərˈspɑ.nə(n)/
  • Audio

Verb[edit]

overspannen

  1. to overstress, to overstrain
  2. to span (of a structure)

Participle[edit]

overspannen

  1. past participle of overspannen

Adjective[edit]

overspannen (comparative overspannener, superlative overspannenst)

  1. overstrained, overstressed
  2. (psychology) emotionally exhausted
