lof (9554)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lɔf/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: lof
  • Rhymes: -ɔf

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch lof, from Old Dutch lof, from Proto-Germanic *lubą, from Proto-Indo-European *lewbʰ- (“love”).

Noun

lof m (uncountable)

  1. praise
    Antonym: blaam

Etymology 2

Clipping of witlof, related to loof.

Noun

lof n (uncountable)

  1. Clipping of witlof (“chicory”).
