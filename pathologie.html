pathologie (26530)


Etymology

Borrowed from French pathologie, from Ancient Greek παθολογία (pathología).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pa‧tho‧lo‧gie

Noun

pathologie f (plural pathologieën)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pathologie v
