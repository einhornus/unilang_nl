reisgenoot (36731)


Etymology

From reis (“trip, journey”) +‎ genoot (“fellow, companion”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: reis‧ge‧noot

Noun

reisgenoot m (plural reisgenoten, diminutive reisgenootje n)

  1. A travel companion, who shares one's company on a journey

Synonyms

  • tochtgenoot m

Related terms

  • reisagent m
  • reisbureau n
  • reisleider m
  • disgenoot m
  • genootschappelijk (adjective)
  • huisgenoot m
