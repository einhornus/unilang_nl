Pronunciation

Determiner

hunne

  1. non-attributive form of hun (English: theirs)
    Normally used in conjunction with the definite article de or het depending on the gender of what is being referred to.
    Die auto is de hunne. — That car is their one. That car is theirs.
    Dat huis is het hunne. — That house is their one. That house is theirs.
    Dat is de/het hunne. — That is their one. That is theirs.
  2. (archaic) inflected form of hun
