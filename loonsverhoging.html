loonsverhoging (19823)

    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: loons‧ver‧ho‧ging

Noun

loonsverhoging f (plural loonsverhogingen, diminutive loonsverhoginkje n)

  1. A raise, a wage increase.

loonsverhoging v [1]
