Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch bereiken. Equivalent to be- +‎ reiken.

Verb[edit]

bereiken

  1. (transitive) to arrive at, to reach, to get to
  2. (transitive) to achieve, to attain

bereiken

Werkwoord

bereiken
