snijboon (67426)



Etymology

Compound of snijden (“to cut”) + boon (“bean”), named because they were usually cut into small pieces before cooking or eating.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsnɛi̯.boːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: snij‧boon

Noun

snijboon f (plural snijbonen, diminutive snijboontje n)

  1. A flat bean, a long variety of Phaseolus vulgaris, with flat pods and fully matured seeds.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

snijboon v/m

  1. (groente) (plantkunde) Phaseolus vulgaris , een plantensoort met brede platte peulen die gesneden worden gegeten
Verwante begrippen
  • boon, herenboon, prinsessenboon, pronkboon, slaboon, sperzieboon, staakboon, suikerboon
