kunstvorm (29838)



Etymology

Compound of kunst (“art”) +‎ vorm (“form”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkʏnst.fɔrm/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

kunstvorm m or f (plural kunstvormen, diminutive kunstvormpje n)

  1. form of art, art form
  2. (language) artificial form
