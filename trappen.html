Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch trappen. Equivalent to trap +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

trappen

  1. (intransitive) to step, to tread
  2. (intransitive) to pedal
  3. (transitive) to kick
    Synonym: schoppen
