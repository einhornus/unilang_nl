Etymology[edit]

Compound of aan +‎ vliegen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːnˌvli.ɣə(n)/
  • Audio

  • Hyphenation: aan‧vlie‧gen

Verb[edit]

aanvliegen

  1. (intransitive) to fly near, to arrive or approach by flying
  2. (transitive) to start a conversation in person with, to accost
