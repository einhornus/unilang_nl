Noun

scheetje n (plural scheetjes)

  1. Diminutive of scheet
  2. (informal, endearing) Someone or something cute.
    Zijn deze puppy's geen scheetjes?
    Aren't these puppies adorable?

Usage notes

Also used to describe something or someone cute like a small child.
