misdadiger (5939)



Etymology 1

Replaced earlier misdader, probably by analogy with misdadig. Thus, now analysable as misdadig + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɪsˌdaːdəɣər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mis‧da‧di‧ger

Noun

misdadiger m (plural misdadigers, diminutive misdadigertje n)

  1. criminal
