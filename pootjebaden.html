pootjebaden (63387)



Etymology

Compound of pootje (“paw; foot, leg”, diminutive) +‎ baden (“to bath”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpoːt.jəˌbaː.də(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: poot‧je‧ba‧den

Verb

pootjebaden

  1. (intransitive) to paddle, to wet one's feet in water either by wading or by dabbling while seated
