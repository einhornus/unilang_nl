bloemrijk (95053)



Etymology

Compound of bloem +‎ rijk.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Adjective

bloemrijk (comparative bloemrijker, superlative bloemrijkst)

  1. having many flowers, flowery (in a literal sense)
    Synonym: bloemenrijk
  2. (figurative) flowery, ornate, florid (lavishly decorated or elaborate)
