nijd (44991)


Etymology

From Old Dutch *nīth, from Proto-Germanic *nīþą. Cognate with German Neid

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /nɛi̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nijd
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯t

Noun

nijd m (uncountable)

  1. envy, jealousy
  2. envious or general anger
