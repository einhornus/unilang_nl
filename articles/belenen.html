belenen (71016)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch belênen. Equivalent to be- + lenen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈleːnə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧le‧nen
  • Rhymes: -eːnən

Verb

belenen

  1. (transitive) to pledge, to impawn
  2. (transitive, historical) to enfeoff

belenen
