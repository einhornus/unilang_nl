bezighouden (7922)


Etymology

From bezig + houden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeːzəxɦɑu̯də(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧zig‧hou‧den

Verb

bezighouden

  1. (transitive) to keep busy, to occupy
  2. (reflexive) to occupy oneself, to be productive
