formulier (6367)


Etymology

Borrowed from French formulaire, from Latin formularium (“form (collection)”), itself from formula (diminutive of forma (“form, shape”)) + -arium (“-ary”). The current sense was borrowed from German Formular.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌfɔr.myˈliːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: for‧mu‧lier
  • Rhymes: -iːr

Noun

formulier n (plural formulieren, diminutive formuliertje n)

  1. A form, a formally standardized (often printed) document to fill out in order to file a specific request, declaration etc.

Derived terms

  • bestelformulier n
