snik (8880)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /snɪk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: snik
  • Rhymes: -ɪk

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch snic. Equivalent to a deverbal from snikken.

Noun

snik m (plural snikken, diminutive snikje n)

  1. A sob, a weep with a convulsive gasp.

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

snik

  1. first-person singular present indicative of snikken
  2. imperative of snikken
