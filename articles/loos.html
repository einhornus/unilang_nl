Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch loos, from Old Dutch *lōs, from Proto-Germanic *lausaz.

Adjective

loos (comparative lozer, superlative meest loos or loost)

  1. blank, empty
  2. idle
  3. amiss, wrong, problematic
  4. sly, cunning
  5. (obsolete) clever, insightful
