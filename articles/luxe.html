Etymology 2

The first part of typical compounds with luxe (e.g. luxe-editie (“luxury edition”), luxevilla (“luxury-mansion”), ...) is reinterpreted as an adjective, instead of a noun.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lyks/

Adjective

  1. (Netherlands) luxurious
  2. (Netherlands) deluxe
