kleingeld (6709)


Etymology

Compound of klein (“small”) + geld (“money”): cognate to German Kleingeld.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklɛi̯n.ɣɛlt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klein‧geld

Noun

kleingeld n (uncountable)

  1. small change
