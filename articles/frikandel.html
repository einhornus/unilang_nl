frikandel (106697)


Etymology

Blend of frikadel + fricandeau.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌfri.kɑnˈdɛl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fri‧kan‧del
  • Rhymes: -ɛl

Noun

frikandel f (plural frikandellen, diminutive frikandelletje n)

  1. A deep-fried snack mainly containing ground meat and bread crumbs, to some degree resembling a sausage.
  2. Archaic form of frikadel.

Derived terms

  • frikandelbroodje

Zelfstandig naamwoord

frikandel v/m
