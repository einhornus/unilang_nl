opvreten (16790)


Etymology

From op (“up”) + vreten (“to eat greedily”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔpvreːtə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

opvreten

  1. (transitive) to eat up something in a savage manner
  2. (transitive) to corrode
