gordeldier (45421)


Etymology

From gordel (“belt”) + dier (“animal”), calque of German Gürteltier. First attested in the 1790s.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɔrdəlˌdiːr/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gor‧del‧dier

Noun

gordeldier n (plural gordeldieren, diminutive gordeldiertje n)

  1. armadillo
