natuurmens (102569)


Etymology

Compound of natuur + mens.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /naːˈtyːrˌmɛns/
  • Hyphenation: na‧tuur‧mens

Noun

natuurmens m (plural natuurmensen, diminutive natuurmensje n)

  1. (philosophy) natural man, the archetypical human in a state of nature held to be a representative agent for all humanity in some philosophical systems
  2. nature lover (person who loves to be in the wild)
