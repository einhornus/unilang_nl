hak (5490)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɦɑk/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hak
  • Rhymes: -ɑk

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch hacke, from Middle Low German hakke. Cognate with German Hacke.

Noun

hak m (plural hakken, diminutive hakje n)

  1. heel of a shoe
  2. heel
    Synonym: hiel
Derived terms
  • naaldhak
  • op de hak nemen

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch hac.

Noun

hak m (plural hakken, diminutive hakje n)

  1. hoe (agricultural tool, gardening tool)

Etymology 3

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

hak

  2. imperative of hakken

Zelfstandig naamwoord

hak m

  3. (gereedschap) werktuig om de grond mee open te hakken [4]
