afname (50030)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfˌnaː.mə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧na‧me

Etymology 1

Derived from afnemen on the model of German Abnahme. The same is true of other nouns in -name.

Noun

afname f (plural afnames)

  1. decrease, decline, diminution, shrinkage
  2. purchase, acquisition
  3. sale, take-up
Related terms
  • afnemen
  • afnemend

Etymology 2

Verb

afname

  1. (archaic) singular past subjunctive of afnemen (when using a subclause)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

afname v/m

  1. het minder worden
    • De afname van zuurstof was groot.
