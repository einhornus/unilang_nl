dijenkletser (61291)


Etymology

Compound of dij (“thigh”) + -en- + kletsen (“to smack, to slap”) + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɛi̯.ə(n)ˌklɛt.sər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dij‧en‧klet‧ser

Noun

dijenkletser m (plural dijenkletsers, diminutive dijenkletsertje n)

  1. A hilarious joke that evokes a strong response such as knee-slapping.
