longontsteking (10502)


Etymology

From long (“lung”) + ontsteking (“infection, inflammation”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈlɔŋɔn(t)ˌsteːkɪŋ/
  • Hyphenation: long‧ont‧ste‧king

Noun

longontsteking f (plural longontstekingen, diminutive longontstekingetje n or longontstekinkje n)

  1. The disease pneumonia, a serious infection of the lung(s)

Synonyms

  • pneumonie
