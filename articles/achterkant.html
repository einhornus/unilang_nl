achterkant (3640)


Etymology

From achter (“behind”) + kant (“side”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑxtərˌkɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ach‧ter‧kant

Noun

achterkant m (plural achterkanten, diminutive achterkantje n)

  1. The backside, rear-end of an object etc.
  2. The reverse of a roughly symmetrical object which has a distinct front

Synonyms

  • achterste n
  • (reverse) keerzijde, rugzijde

Related terms

  • achteraan
  • achterruit
  • achtersteven
  • achterwerk n
