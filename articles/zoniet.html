zoniet (14175)


Etymology

Univerbation of zo niet (“if not”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /zoːˈnit/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zo‧niet
  • Rhymes: -it

Adverb

zoniet

  1. (proscribed) or else, otherwise

Usage notes

The spelling zoniet is not recognised by official spelling guidelines, by whom zo niet is recommended instead.
