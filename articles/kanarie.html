kanarie (18784)


Etymology

Borrowed from French canari, from Spanish canario.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌkaːˈnaː.ri/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ka‧na‧rie
  • Rhymes: -aːri

Noun

kanarie m (plural kanaries, diminutive kanarietje n)

  1. A canary, bird of the Serinus or specifically Serinus canaria. [from mid 16th c.]
    Synonyms: kanariepiet, kanarievogel
