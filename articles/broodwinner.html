broodwinner (76844)


Etymology

Compound of brood + winner. Cf. English breadwinner

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbroːtˌʋɪ.nər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: brood‧win‧ner

Noun

broodwinner m (plural broodwinners, diminutive broodwinnertje n, feminine broodwinster)

  1. financial provider for the entire family, the person who brings home the bacon; breadwinner [from early 18th c.]

Synonyms

  • kostwinner
