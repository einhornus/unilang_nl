kruisraket (47059)


Etymology

From kruisen + raket, calque of English cruise missile.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkrœy̯s.raːˌkɛt/
  • Hyphenation: kruis‧ra‧ket

Noun

kruisraket f or m (plural kruisraketten, diminutive kruisraketje n)

  1. cruise missile
