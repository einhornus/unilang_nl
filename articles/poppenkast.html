poppenkast (17989)


Etymology

Compound of pop (“puppet”) + -en- + kast (“cupboard, casing”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɔ.pə(n)ˌkɑst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pop‧pen‧kast
  • Rhymes: -ɔpənkɑst

Noun

poppenkast f (plural poppenkasten, diminutive poppenkastje n)

  1. (literally) A portable puppet theatre and puppets for use in it.
  2. A puppet-show, notably of the Punch and Judy type.
  3. (figuratively) A bad, excessive or hilarious 'show', pointlessly theatrical conduct.

Synonyms

  • (puppet-show) poppenspel
  • (figurative) misbaar, poeha, theater, vertoning

Derived terms

  • poppenkasterij

Related terms

  • poppenspeler m
