opbeuren (28576)


Etymology

From op- + beuren. From Middle Dutch bueren, from Old Dutch *burien, from Proto-Germanic *burjaną.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: op‧beu‧ren
  • Rhymes: -øːrən

Verb

opbeuren

  1. (transitive) to lift up, to raise
  2. (transitive, figuratively) to cheer up, to comfort
