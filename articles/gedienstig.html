gedienstig (83371)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gedienstich. Equivalent to ge- + dienst (“service”) + -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈdin.stəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧dien‧stig

Adjective

gedienstig (comparative gedienstiger, superlative gedienstigst)

  1. helpful, willing to help
  2. (obsolete) useful, utile
    Synonym: nuttig
