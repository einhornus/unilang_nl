dagloon (47591)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dachloon. Equivalent to dag + loon.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɑx.loːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dag‧loon

Noun

dagloon n (plural daglonen, diminutive dagloontje n)

  1. daily pay, daily wages, as opposed to hourly or long-term remuneration

Derived terms

  • dagloner

Related terms

  • dagwerk
  • jaarloon
  • maandloon
  • uurloon
  • weekloon

Zelfstandig naamwoord

dagloon o

  1. dagloon is de aanduiding voor arbeidsloon dat wordt berekend per dag.
