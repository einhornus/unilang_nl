jongensdroom (75103)


Etymology

Compound of jongen + -s- + droom.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈjɔ.ŋə(n)sˌdroːm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: jon‧gens‧droom

Noun

jongensdroom m (plural jongensdromen)

  1. A boy's dream, a boyhood dream.
