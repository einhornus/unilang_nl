narcisme (41869)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Narzissmus. Equivalent to Narcissus + -isme.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌnɑrˈsɪs.mə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nar‧cis‧me
  • Rhymes: -ɪsmə

Noun

narcisme n (uncountable)

  1. narcissism [from late 1910s]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

narcisme o [3]
