enigste (7557)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Pronoun

enigste

  1. (de enigste) only one
    Jij bent de enigste die er bezwaar tegen heeft.
    You are the only one who has objections.
  2. (het enigste) only thing
    Het enigste dat ik zie is bomen.
    The only thing I see is trees.

Usage notes

This word is often used instead of enige, but people may see it as wrong. The form is correct, but the use of it is discouraged. Its use is only considered correct when used in a certain style. See http://taaladvies.net/taal/advies/vraag/262/ (in Dutch) for more information.

Adjective

enigste

  1. Inflected form of enigst, the superlative of enig
