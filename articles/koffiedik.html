koffiedik (54090)


Etymology

Compound of koffie (“coffee”) + dik (“ground”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɔ.fiˌdɪk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kof‧foe‧dik

Noun

koffiedik n (uncountable)

  1. coffee grounds (sediment in coffee)

Derived terms
