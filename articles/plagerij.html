plagerij (114020)


Etymology

From the stem of plagen (“to plague; to tease”) + -erij (“-ery”). Or alternatively, from plager (“plaguer; teaser”) + -ij (“-y”). See also the usage notes at -erij.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

plagerij f (plural plagerijen, diminutive plagerijtje n)

  1. worry, concern
  2. teasing, chaff

Zelfstandig naamwoord
