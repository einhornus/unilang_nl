loodgieterij (64161)


Etymology

From loodgieten (“to plumb; lit. to pour lead”) + -erij (“-ery”). Or alternatively, from loodgieter (“plumber; lit. lead pourer)”) + -ij (“-y”). See also the usage notes at -erij.

Pronunciation
