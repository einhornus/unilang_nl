draagband (118292)


Etymology

Compound of dragen (“to carry”) + band (“strap, strip, belt”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdraːx.bɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: draag‧band

Noun

draagband m (plural draagbanden, diminutive draagbandje n)

  1. (clothing) carrying strap, shoulder strap
    Synonym: draagriem
