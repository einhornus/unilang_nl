dekmantel (5277)


Etymology

From dekken (“to cover”) + mantel (“cloak”)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dek‧man‧tel

Noun

dekmantel m (plural dekmantels, diminutive dekmanteltje n)

  1. coverup (only used figuratively)
  2. guise, facade

Synonyms

  • schijn
  • voorwendsel
