hospik (12013)


Etymology

blend of hospitaal 'hospital' (from Latin) + pik 'dick, pecker, penis' (as crude pars pro toto for '[army] boy, man')

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

hospik m (plural hospikken, diminutive hospikje n)

  1. (army slang) A military (hospital) orderly
  2. (by extension) A medic

Synonyms

  • (hospital orderly) hospitaalsoldaat m

Derived terms

  • hospikken (verb)

Verb

hospik

  1. first-person singular present indicative of hospikken
  2. imperative of hospikken
