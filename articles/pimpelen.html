pimpelen (101312)


Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɪmpələn

Etymology 1

From pimpeltje.

Verb

pimpelen

  1. (of strong alcoholic drinks, dated) to drink with small sips
  2. (Netherlands, of any alcoholic drinks) to drink (heavily)
