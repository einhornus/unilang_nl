zelfzucht (77878)


Etymology

From zelf + zucht.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɛlf.sʏxt/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: zelf‧zucht

Noun

zelfzucht f (uncountable, diminutive zelfzuchtje n)

  1. egoism (tendency to think of self)

Derived terms
