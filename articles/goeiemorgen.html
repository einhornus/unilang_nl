goeiemorgen (3154)


Etymology

From goeie + morgen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɣujəˈmɔrɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Interjection

goeiemorgen

  1. (proscribed) good morning

Usage notes

Although commonly used in everyday writing, this form is considered incorrect by some, who favour the more formal goedemorgen instead.
