kraanwater (28670)


Etymology

Compound of kraan (“tap”) + water (“water”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkraːnˌʋaː.tər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kraan‧wa‧ter

Noun

kraanwater n (uncountable)

  1. tap water, running water
    Synonym: leidingwater
