arrestatie (4001)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch [Term?], from Middle French arrestation. Equivalent to arresteren + -atie.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑ.rɛˈstaː.(t)si/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ar‧res‧ta‧tie
  • Rhymes: -aːtsi

Noun

arrestatie f (plural arrestaties, diminutive arrestatietje n)

  1. arrest (the act of arresting a criminal, suspect etc.)
  2. (archaic) (temporary) confiscation of possessions
