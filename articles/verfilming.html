verfilming (66880)


Etymology

From verfilmen + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vərˈfɪl.mɪŋ/, /vɛrˈfɪl.mɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧fil‧ming

Noun

verfilming f (plural verfilmingen)

  1. A film that has been based on or adapted from another medium.
  2. The process of adapting another medium into a movie.
  3. The process of storing a medium on microform.
