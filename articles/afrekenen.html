afrekenen (7376)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch afrekenen. Equivalent to af + rekenen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfreːkənə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧re‧ke‧nen

Verb

afrekenen

  1. (transitive) to pay (a purchase, an owed amount)
  2. (transitive) to settle
  3. (transitive) to kill
