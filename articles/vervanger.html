vervanger (7163)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vervanger. Equivalent to vervangen (“replace”) + -er (agentive suffix).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vərˈvɑ.ŋər/, /vɛrˈvɑ.ŋər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧van‧ger

Noun

vervanger m (plural vervangers, diminutive vervangertje n)

  1. substitute, replacement

Derived terms
