patriarchaal (125574)


Etymology

Probably ultimately from Latin patriarchālis. Equivalent to patriarch + -aal.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌpaː.tri.ɑrˈxaːl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pa‧tri‧ar‧chaal
  • Rhymes: -aːl

Adjective

patriarchaal (comparative patriarchaler, superlative patriarchaalst)

  1. patriarchal, pertaining to the Biblical patriarchs
  2. (Christianity) patriarchal, pertaining to an Orthodox or Roman Catholic patriarch
  3. (feminism) patriarchial, pertaining to patriarchy
