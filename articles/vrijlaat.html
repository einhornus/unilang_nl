vrijlaat (18690)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvrɛi̯.laːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vrij‧laat

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch vrilâet.

Noun

vrijlaat m (plural vrijlaten)

  1. (historical) A freed serf, a freedman.

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

vrijlaat

  1. first-, second- and third-person singular present indicative of vrijlaten (when using a subclause)
