atol (49981)


Etymology

Borrowed from English atoll, from Dhivehi އަތޮޅު (atoḷu) and Malayalam അതൊലു (atolu).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /aːˈtɔl/, /ˈaː.tɔl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: atol

Noun

atol n or m (plural atollen, diminutive atolletje n)

  1. atoll
    Synonym: koraaleiland

Zelfstandig naamwoord

atol v/m en o
