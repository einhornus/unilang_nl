tal (59040)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɑl

Determiner

tal

  1. (~ van) numerous, many, lots
    Je hebt tal van mogelijkheden - You have lots of possibilities

Noun

tal n (plural tallen)

  1. number

Usage notes

Tal is almost never used to say 'number', getal and nummer are used instead.
