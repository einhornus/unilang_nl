boogschieten (29423)


Etymology

From boog (“bow”) + schieten (“to shoot”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈboːxˌsxitə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boog‧schie‧ten

Noun

boogschieten n (uncountable)

  1. (the practice of) archery

Werkwoord
