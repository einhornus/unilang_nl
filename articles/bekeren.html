bekeren (14568)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch bekeren, from Old Dutch bekeren. Equivalent to be- + keren.

  • Hyphenation: be‧ke‧ren
  • Rhymes: -eːrən

Verb

bekeren

bekeren
