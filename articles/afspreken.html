afspreken (3730)


Etymology

From af + spreken.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfspreːkə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧spre‧ken

Verb

afspreken

  1. (transitive) to agree, to make an agreement
    Afgesproken!
    Deal!
  2. (intransitive) to make an appointment
    Laten we morgen afspreken.
    Let's make an appointment (for) tomorrow.
