theeservies (78995)


Etymology

Compound of thee + servies.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈteː.sərˌvis/, /ˈteː.sɛrˌvis/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: thee‧ser‧vies

Noun

theeservies n (plural theeserviezen, diminutive theeserviesje n)

  1. tea service (set of cups and dishes) [from 18th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

theeservies o [1]
