scheen (10854)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schene, from Old Dutch *skina, from Proto-Germanic *skinō. Cognate with German Schiene, English shin.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sxeːn/
  • audio (Belgium)
    		(file)
  • audio (Netherlands)
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: scheen
  • Rhymes: -eːn

Noun

scheen f (plural schenen, diminutive scheentje n)

  1. (chiefly plural) shin
  2. strip of wood or metal

Related terms

  • scheenbeen
  • scheenbeschermer
