ambras (52459)


Etymology

Borrowed from French embarras, introduced in Napoleontic times in the Low Countries.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɑm.brɑs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: am‧bras

Noun

ambras m (uncountable, diminutive ambraske n)

  1. (Belgium) quarrel, fight
