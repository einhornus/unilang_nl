loopneus (60202)


Etymology

Compound of lopen (“to run, to walk, to stream”) + neus (“nose”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈloːp.nøːs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: loop‧neus

Noun

loopneus f or m (plural loopneuzen, diminutive loopneusje n)

  1. A runny nose.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

loopneus m
