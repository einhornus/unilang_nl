graancirkel (49427)


Etymology

From graan (“grain, corn, cereal”) + cirkel (“circle”), calque of English crop circle. First attested in the early 1990s.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣraːnˌsɪr.kəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: graan‧cir‧kel

Noun

graancirkel m (plural graancirkels, diminutive graancirkeltje n)

  1. crop circle (geometric figure in crop fields)
