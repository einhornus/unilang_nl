entrecôte (107083)


Etymology

Borrowed from French entrecôte, from entre (“between”) + côte (“rib”), equivalent to Latin inter (“between”) + costa (“rib”). Doublet of intercostaal.

Noun

entrecôte f (plural entrecôtes, diminutive entrecôtetje n)

  1. entrecôte

Synonyms

  • ribstuk, tussenribstuk
