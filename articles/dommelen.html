Etymology 1

Unknown. May be the same as dommelen (“to buzz”), with influence from dom (“dumb”).

Verb

dommelen

  1. (intransitive) to doze, to snooze

From Middle Dutch dommelen, from dommen.

Verb

dommelen

  2. (intransitive) to thump (sound)
