pronkstuk (41682)


Etymology

From pronken (“to display”) + stuk (“piece”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

pronkstuk n (plural pronkstukken, diminutive pronkstukje n)

  1. showpiece, centerpiece
  2. (figuratively) prize, gem, jewel
