penisnijd (43234)


Etymology

From penis + nijd (“penis”), calque of German Penisneid.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pe‧nis‧nijd

Noun

penisnijd m (uncountable)

  1. (psychoanalysis, literally) Penis envy, the alleged desire by a woman to possess male genitalia
  2. (figuratively) Macho rivalry

Related terms

  • nijdas m
  • nijdig (adjective)
  • peniskoker

Zelfstandig naamwoord
