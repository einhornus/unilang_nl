luchtzak (36966)


Etymology

Compound of lucht + zak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlʏxt.sɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lucht‧zak

Noun

luchtzak m (plural luchtzakken, diminutive luchtzakje n)

  1. (aviation) A downdraught, a downdraft.
  2. (aviation) The ballonet of a blimp.
    Synonym: ballonet
  3. A bag of air.
  4. A pocket (cavity) of air.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
