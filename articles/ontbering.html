ontbering (40409)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ontberinge. Equivalent to ontberen + -ing

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔntˈbeː.rɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ont‧be‧ring
  • Rhymes: -eːrɪŋ

Noun

ontbering f (plural ontberingen, diminutive ontberinkje n)

  1. (countable) hardship, ordeal, adversity (difficult event or timespan)
  2. (uncountable) deprivation, hardship (state of want)
