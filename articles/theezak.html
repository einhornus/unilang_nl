theezak (122897)


Etymology

Compound of thee + zak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈteː.zɑk/
  • Hyphenation: thee‧zak

Noun

theezak m (plural theezakken, diminutive theezakje n)

  1. (chiefly diminutive) A teabag.
    Ze deed een theezakje in haar glas heet water.
    She put a teabag in her glass of hot water.
