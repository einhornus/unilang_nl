mazzel (3324)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish מזל (mazl, “luck”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɑ.zəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: maz‧zel
  • Rhymes: -ɑzəl

Interjection

mazzel

  1. (Netherlands, slang) see you later, bye
    Synonym: de mazzel

Noun

mazzel m (uncountable)

  1. (Netherlands) luck
    Hij had mazzel. ― He was lucky.

Synonyms

  • geluk

Derived terms

  • mazzelen
  • mazzelpik

Related terms

  • bemazzel

Zelfstandig naamwoord

mazzel m

Verwante begrippen
  • mazzel oen brooche, mazzel tov
  • Hebreeuws: mazal

Werkwoord

mazzel
