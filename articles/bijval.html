bijval (54968)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛi̯.vɑl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bij‧val

Etymology 1

Calque of German Beifall. Equivalent to a deverbal from bijvallen.

Noun

bijval m (uncountable)

  1. approval, endorsement
  2. accolade, applause

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

bijval

  1. first-person singular present indicative of bijvallen (when using a subclause)
