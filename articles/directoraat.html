directoraat (83977)


Etymology

directeur + -aat

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

directoraat n (plural directoraten, diminutive directoraatje n)

  1. A directorate, the position of directeur (director), a term as such
  2. A directorate, department run by (a) director(s)
