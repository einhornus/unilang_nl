doorbreken (10569)


Etymology 1

From door + breken.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoːrbreːkə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

doorbreken

  1. (ergative) to break, break through, break in half
    Toen de dikke man op zijn stoel ging zitten, brak de stoel door.
    When the fat man sat down on his chair, the chair broke.
