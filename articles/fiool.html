fiool (70119)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch fiole, ultimately from Latin phiala, from Ancient Greek φιάλη (phiálē). Related to fiaal.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fiˈoːl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fi‧ool
  • Rhymes: -oːl

Noun

fiool m or f (plural fiolen, diminutive fiooltje n)
