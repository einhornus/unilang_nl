spuitgast (112765)


Etymology

From spuit (“spout, hose, nozzle”) + gast (“guest, guy, dude”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈspœy̯t.xɑst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: spuit‧gast

Noun

spuitgast m (plural spuitgasten, diminutive spuitgastje n)

  1. A firefighter, especially a man, who operates a firehose.

Usage notes

Rarely this job title may be interpreted gender-neutrally and be used to refer to a female firefighter. However, due to the association of gast (“guy”) with males, typically brandweervrouw will be used in such cases. Brandweermens is a fully gender-neutral alternative.

Synonyms

  • brandspuitgast
  • brandweerman
  • brandweervrouw
  • brandweermens

Related terms

  • spuitmeester
