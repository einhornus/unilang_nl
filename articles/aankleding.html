aankleding (61965)


Etymology

From aankleed (“to dress”) + -ing (“-ation”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːnˌkleː.dɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧kle‧ding

Noun

aankleding f (plural aankledingen, diminutive aankledinkje n)

  1. decoration, furnishing
  2. stage setting

Zelfstandig naamwoord
