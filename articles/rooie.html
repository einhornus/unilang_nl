rooie (4709)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /roːi̯ə/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

rooie

  1. Inflected form of rood

Noun

rooie m or f (plural rooien)

  1. A redheaded person
  2. A socialist, a communist
  3. (informal) A thousand-guilder bill, short for rooie rug 'red back' (the colour of the underside of the bill).

Verb

rooie

  1. (archaic) singular present subjunctive of rooien

Synonyms

  • (adj) rode
  • (noun) roodharige
