bergrug (42558)


Etymology

From berg + rug.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛrxrʏx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: berg‧rug

Noun

bergrug m (plural bergruggen, diminutive bergruggetje n)

  1. A mountain-ridge, (line/view formed by) a (chain of) mountain top(s)
  2. (metonymically) The top section of a mountain, where its slopes meet

Synonyms

  • (ridge): bergkam

Derived terms

  • bergruggrens
