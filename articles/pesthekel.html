pesthekel (80443)


Etymology

From pest (“pestilence”) + hekel (“dislike, hatred”); the former element is used as an intensifier, per the common Dutch practice of using diseases as intensifiers in compound words (compare kanker-).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

pesthekel m (uncountable)

  1. (colloquial) an intense hatred or dislike
