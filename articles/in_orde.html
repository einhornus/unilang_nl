Adjective

in orde (used only predicatively, not comparable)

  1. in order, correct, right, okay, as it should be
    Ik heb mijn auto laten controleren, en alles was in orde.
    I had my car checked, and everything was in order.
    Volgens mij is er iets niet in orde met deze website.
    I think something isn't right about this website.
