giraf (29296)


Etymology

From German Giraffe, from French girafe, from Arabic زَرَافَة (zarāfa).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ʒiˈrɑf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gi‧raf
  • Rhymes: -ɑf

Noun

giraf f (plural giraffen, diminutive girafje n)

  1. A giraffe, mammal of the genus Giraffa.

Synonyms

  • giraffe
