oogbol (73837)


Etymology

Compound of oog (“eye”) + bol (“sphere, ball”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈoːx.bɔl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: oog‧bol

Noun

oogbol m (plural oogbollen, diminutive oogbolletje n)

  1. eyeball
    Synonym: oogbal

Zelfstandig naamwoord

oogbol m [1]
