leukemie (13565)


Etymology

From German Leukämie, from Ancient Greek λευκός (leukós, “white”) and αἷμα (haîma, “blood”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌløː.kəˈmi/, (uncommon, classicising) /ˌlœy̯.kəˈmi/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: leu‧ke‧mie

Noun

leukemie f (plural leukemieën)

  1. (pathology, oncology) leukemia
