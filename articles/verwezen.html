Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch verwesen. Equivalent to ver- + wees + -en.

Verb

verwezen

  1. (intransitive) to become orphaned
  2. (transitive, chiefly figuratively) to orphan, to bereave of parents
Usage notes

May be qualified by half and heel to specify the loss of one or two parents respectively.
