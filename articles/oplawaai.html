oplawaai (43271)


Etymology

Compound og op (“on”) + lawaai (“noise”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔp.laːˌʋaːi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: op‧la‧waai

Noun

oplawaai m (plural oplawaaien, diminutive oplawaaitje n)

  1. A sounding blow, punch, whack, as with a fist
  2. (figuratively) A hard hit, bad blow, inadversity such as a setback

Synonyms

  • dreun
  • lel
  • opdoffer
  • opdonder
  • opduvel
  • oplababbel
  • oplazer
  • opsodemieter
