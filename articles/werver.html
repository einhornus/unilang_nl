werver (120728)


Etymology

the root of werven 'to recruit' + -er

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

werver m (plural wervers, diminutive wervertje n)

  1. A recruiter of staff, notably:
    1. of troops
    2. of sailors
  2. A political or other propagandist

Synonyms

  • ronselaar m
  • (troops) recruteringsofficier m

Related terms

  • aanwerven (verb)
  • wervend
  • werving
