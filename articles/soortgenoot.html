soortgenoot (88040)


Etymology

Compound of soort + genoot.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsoːrt.xəˌnoːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: soort‧ge‧noot

Noun

soortgenoot m (plural soortgenoten, diminutive soortgenootje n)

  1. An organism belonging to the same species (in usage sometimes translatable as congener).
  2. (figurative) An object of the same type or category.
