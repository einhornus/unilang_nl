ergeren (16276)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch argeren, ergeren. Equivalent to erger + -en.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Verb

ergeren

  1. (transitive) to annoy, to irritate
  2. (reflexive) to be annoyed, to be irritated
