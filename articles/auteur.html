auteur (9124)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French autheur, from Old French autor, from Latin auctor.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɑu̯ˈtøːr/, /oːˈtøːr/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: au‧teur
  • Rhymes: -øːr

Noun

auteur m (plural auteurs, diminutive auteurtje n)

  1. author
  2. composer, artist, creator of a work

Derived terms

  • auteurschap
  • auteursrecht
