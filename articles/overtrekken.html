overtrekken (89606)


Etymology 1

From over + trekken.

Pronunciation 1

  • IPA(key): /ˈoːvərˌtrɛ.kə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: over‧trek‧ken

Verb

overtrekken

  1. (transitive) to pull again
  2. (transitive) to trace / copy an existing drawing
  3. (intransitive, aeronautics) To exceed the critical angle of attack, resulting in total loss of lift.
