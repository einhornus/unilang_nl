kost (945)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kɔst/
  • Hyphenation: kost
  • Rhymes: -ɔst

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch cost, from Old French cost.

Noun

kost m (plural kosten, diminutive kostje n)

  1. cost, price
  2. (in the plural) expenses
  3. (used absolutely, with definite article) board, livelihood, meals and lodgings
  4. food, nourishment
