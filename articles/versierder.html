versierder (18695)


Etymology

root of versieren "to ornate; pick up" + -der '-er'

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

versierder m (plural versierders, diminutive versierdertje n)

  1. (literally) A decorator
  2. A male who smoothly picks up dates, such as a womanizer

Related terms

  • versiering
