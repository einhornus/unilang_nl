snoet (44635)


Etymology

Variant of snuit.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /snut/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: snoet
  • Rhymes: -ut

Noun

snoet m (plural snoeten, diminutive snoetje n)

  1. (endearing) The face, especially mouth and nose, of a human or some animals.
  2. (uncommon) Synonym of snuit.
