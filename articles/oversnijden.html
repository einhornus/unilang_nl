oversnijden (51015)


Etymology 1

From over + snijden.

Pronunciation 1

  • IPA(key): /ˈoːvərˌsnɛi̯.də(n)/
  • Hyphenation: over‧snij‧den

Verb

oversnijden

  1. (transitive) to cut again
  2. (transitive) to cut over, to cut across
