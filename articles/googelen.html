googelen (69807)


Etymology

Borrowed from English google, from the website name (and company) Google.

Pronunciation

  • (Netherlands) IPA(key): /ˈɡu.ɡə.lə(n)/, /ˈɡuː.ɡə.lə(n)/
  • (Belgium) IPA(key): /ˈɣu.ɣə.lə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

googelen

  1. (intransitive) To google, search for Internet info using search engine (market leader) Google
  2. (transitive) To google, research Internet info on something using search engine Google
