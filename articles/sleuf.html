sleuf (88125)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sløːf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -øːf

Noun

sleuf f (plural sleuven, diminutive sleufje n)

  1. slit (narrow cut or opening; a slot)
  2. any long, narrow opening: trench, etc.

Synonyms

  • (slit): gleuf

Derived terms

  • sleufmachine
