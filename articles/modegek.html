modegek (102880)


Etymology

From mode + gek.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

modegek m (plural modegekken, diminutive modegekje n)

  1. A fashion freak, someone who obsesses about fashion 'like mad'
    De industrie verdient fortuinen aan modegekken
    The industry earns fortunes from fashion freaks
