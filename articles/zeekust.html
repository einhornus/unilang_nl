zeekust (85845)


Etymology

Compound of zee + kust.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.kʏst/
  • zeekust
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zee‧kust

Noun

zeekust f (plural zeekusten, diminutive zeekustje n)

  1. sea coast (marine shoreline) [from 17th c.]

Derived terms

  • Afrikaans: seekus

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zeekust v/m
