kruisridder (128173)


Etymology

Compound of kruis (“cross”) + ridder (“knight”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkrœy̯sˌrɪ.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kruis‧rid‧der

Noun

kruisridder m (plural kruisridders, diminutive kruisriddertje n)

  1. A crusader, a knight who participated in the crusades.
    Synonym: kruisvaarder

Related terms

  • kruistocht
  • kruisvaart
  • tempelridder
