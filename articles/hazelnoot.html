hazelnoot (45384)


Etymology

Compound of hazel (“hazel”) + noot (“nut”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦaːzəlˌnoːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ha‧zel‧noot

Noun

hazelnoot m or f (plural hazelnoten, diminutive hazelnootje n)

  1. A hazel nut
  2. A hazel, a hazel nut tree or shrub; tree of the genus Corylus
    Synonyms: hazel, hazelaar

  • hazelnootbederver m
  • hazelnootschil

  • hazelnotepit

Related terms
