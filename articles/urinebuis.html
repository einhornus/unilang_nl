urinebuis (86943)


Etymology

Compound of urine (“urine”) + buis (“tube, duct”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: uri‧ne‧buis

Noun

urinebuis f (plural urinebuizen, diminutive urinebuisje n)
