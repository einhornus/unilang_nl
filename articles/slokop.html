slokop (99755)


Etymology

From opslokken (“to swallow”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈslɔk.ɔp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: slok‧op

Noun

slokop m (plural slokoppen, diminutive slokopje n)

  1. a glutton, a pig

Synonyms

Zelfstandig naamwoord

slokop m
