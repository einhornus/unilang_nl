engte (93720)


Etymology

From eng + -te. Compare Gothic 𐌰𐌲𐌲𐍅𐌹𐌸𐌰 (aggwiþa).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛŋ.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: eng‧te

Noun

engte f (plural engten or engtes)

  1. any narrow passage
  2. narrowness
  3. (obsolete) difficulty
