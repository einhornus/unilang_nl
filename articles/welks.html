welks (120786)


Etymology

Neuter and masculine genitive singular of welk.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Pronoun

welks

  1. (relative, dated) whose
    Wee het land, welks koning een kind is
    Woe to the country whose king is a child

Usage notes

Usually only used to refer to neuter words in the singular. For masculine words in the singular, wiens is used; wier for feminine words in the singular and for all plurals.
