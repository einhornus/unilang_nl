boerenjongen (33512)


Etymology

Compound of boer (“farmer”) + -en- + jongen (“boy”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌbuː.rə(n)ˈjɔ.ŋən/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boe‧ren‧jon‧gen

Noun

boerenjongen m (plural boerenjongens, diminutive boerenjongetje n)

  1. (common) farmboy, country-boy
    Bart was een echte boerenjongen, geen inwijkeling uit de stad.
    Bart was a real farmboy, no immigrant from the city.
  2. a farmer's boy, i.e. son
  3. an alcoholic beverage with brandy and raisins
  4. raisin drenched in brandy
