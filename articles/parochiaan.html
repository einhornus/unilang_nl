parochiaan (89508)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch parochiaen, from Latin parochianus. Equivalent to parochie + -aan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌpaː.rɔ.xiˈaːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pa‧ro‧chi‧aan
  • Rhymes: -aːn

Noun

parochiaan m (plural parochianen)

  1. A parishioner, a member of a Catholic parish.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
