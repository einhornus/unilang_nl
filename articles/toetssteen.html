toetssteen (98670)


Etymology

From toetsen + steen, calque of Middle French pierre de touche.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtut(s).steːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: toets‧steen

Noun

toetssteen m (plural toetsstenen, diminutive toetssteentje n)

  1. touchstone [from 16th c.]
