ekster (76186)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch egestre, exter, from Old Dutch *agastra, from Proto-West Germanic *agastrijā.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛk.stər/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ek‧ster

Noun

ekster m (plural eksters, diminutive ekstertje n)

  1. Eurasian magpie, Pica pica.
  2. A magpie, any bird of the genus Pica.

Derived terms

  • eksternest
  • eksteroog
  • scholekster

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ekster v/m
