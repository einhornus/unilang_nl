beraad (21129)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈraːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧raad
  • Rhymes: -aːt

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch berâet. Equivalent to a deverbal from beraden.

Noun

beraad n (plural beraden, diminutive beraadje n)

  1. consideration, consultation (the process of considering)
Derived terms
  • crisisberaad
Related terms
  • zich beraden
  • beraden

beraad
