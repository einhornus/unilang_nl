Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch gevoelen, from Old Dutch *gefuolen, from Proto-Germanic *gafōlijaną. Equivalent to ge- + voelen.

Verb

gevoelen

From Middle Dutch gevoelen. Gerund of the verb gevoelen.
