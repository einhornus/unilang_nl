kebab (23794)


Etymology

Borrowed from Turkish kebap, from Arabic كَبَاب‎ (kabāb).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kəˈbɑp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ke‧bab
  • Rhymes: -ɑp

Noun

kebab m (uncountable)

  1. kebab (seasoned meat grilled on a spit), esp. doner kebab

Derived terms

  • kebabtent
  • kebabzaak
