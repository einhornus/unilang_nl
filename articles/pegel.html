pegel (125560)


Etymology

From Old Dutch *pegil, from Proto-Germanic *pagilaz. Doublet of peil.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpeː.ɣəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pe‧gel
  • Rhymes: -eːɣəl

Noun

pegel m (plural pegels, diminutive pegeltje n)

  1. icicle
  2. (informal, Netherlands, historical) a guilder
  3. (informal, Netherlands) a euro

Synonyms

  • (icicle): ijspegel

Derived terms

  • ijspegel
