dwarszitten (21090)


Etymology

From dwars + zitten.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdʋɑrsɪtə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

dwarszitten

  1. (transitive) to hinder, to impede
  2. (transitive) to bother, to irritate
    Nou, wat zit je dwars?
    Well, what's bothering you?
    Ik weet niet wat hem dwarszit.
    I don't know what's bothering him.
