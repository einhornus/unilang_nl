troffel (98493)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtrɔfəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: trof‧fel

Etymology 1

Modified from truweel, by analogy with schoffel and gaffel. Cognate with English trowel.

Noun

troffel m (plural troffels, diminutive troffeltje n)

  1. trowel
  2. scoop of resembling shape, as used for gardnening
Synonyms
  • truweel
  • (scoop): schep
Derived terms
  • troffelen
  • troffelbink
  • troffelegge

  • troffelschop

  • troffelstok
  • troffelzaag

Etymology 2

Akin to troeven.

Noun

troffel m (plural troffels, diminutive troffeltje n)

  1. blow, hit
  2. beating

Zelfstandig naamwoord

troffel m
