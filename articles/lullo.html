Etymology

1980s or early 1990s. Popularised in 1996 by Jiskefet. From lul (“dick, cock”) + -o (typical denominative suffix in several slangs, particularly Turbotaal).

Noun

lullo m (plural lullo's, diminutive lullootje n)

  1. (slang, derogatory, Netherlands) A dick, a dickhead, an arse (generic insult, usually for males).
    Hé lullo, heb je nog geneukt? ― Hey dickhead, had any sex?
  2. (slang, derogatory, Netherlands) A posh frat boy studying in Leiden.
