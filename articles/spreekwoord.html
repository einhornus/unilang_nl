spreekwoord (22096)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch spreecwort. Equivalent to spreken + woord. Compare German Sprichwort.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈspreːk.ʋoːrt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: spreek‧woord

Noun

spreekwoord n (plural spreekwoorden, diminutive spreekwoordje n)

  1. saying, proverb

Related terms
