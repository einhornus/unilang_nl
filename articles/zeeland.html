zeeland (96649)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch seelant. Equivalent to zee (“sea”) + land (“land”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.lɑnt/
  • Hyphenation: zee‧land
  • Rhymes: -eːlɑnt

Noun

zeeland n (plural zeelanden)

  1. (uncommon) land adjacent to the sea, coastal land
