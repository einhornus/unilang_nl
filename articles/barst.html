Etymology 1

From barsten.

Noun

barst f (plural barsten, diminutive barstje n)

  1. A crack, rip, tear
  2. (figuratively) A little, a tiny amount; notably in: geen barst ("not a bit")
  3. (obsolete) An outburst, eruption
Related terms
  • barsten
  • barstig

Interjection
