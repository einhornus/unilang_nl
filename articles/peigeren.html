peigeren (80475)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish פּגרן (peygern), from Yiddish פּגר (peyger, “carcass, cadaver”), from Hebrew פֶּגֶר (péger, “corpse, carcass”).

Verb

peigeren

  1. (slang, intransitive) to die
  2. (slang, intransitive) to kill
