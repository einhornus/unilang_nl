Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch enge, from Old Dutch *engi, from Proto-Germanic *anguz. Cognate with German eng, from Old High German engi.

Adjective

eng (comparative enger, superlative engst)

  1. narrow
  2. small
  3. scary, creepy
