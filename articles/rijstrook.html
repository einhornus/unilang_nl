rijstrook (53382)


Etymology

From rijden (“to drive”) + strook (“lane”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɛi̯.stroːk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rij‧strook

Noun

rijstrook f (plural rijstroken, diminutive rijstrookje n)

  1. (Netherlands) Each of several parallel driving lanes on a (broad) road

Related terms

  • rijweg
