flauwte (65165)


Etymology

From flauw + -te.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈflɑu̯.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: flauw‧te

Noun

flauwte f (plural flauwten or flauwtes, diminutive flauwtetje n)

  1. feebleness, weakness; the quality of lacking strength or vigour
  2. a sudden loss of consciousness; syncope
  3. (economics) low demand in markets
