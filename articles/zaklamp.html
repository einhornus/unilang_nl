zaklamp (7806)


Etymology

From zak (“pocket”) + lamp (“lamp”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɑklɑmp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zak‧lamp

Noun

zaklamp f (plural zaklampen, diminutive zaklampje n)

  1. flashlight, torch (battery-powered hand-held lightsource)

Synonyms

  • (flashlight): zaklantaarn, pillamp, pillicht

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zaklamp v
