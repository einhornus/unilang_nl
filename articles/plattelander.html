plattelander (89300)


Etymology

From platteland + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌplɑ.təˈlɑn.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: plat‧te‧lan‧der
  • Rhymes: -ɑndər

Noun

plattelander m (plural plattelanders, diminutive plattelandertje n)

  1. A country-dweller, a rustic, someone who lives outside (large) inhabited areas.

Synonyms

  • dorpeling (“villager”)
  • dorpsbewoner (“inhabitant of a village”)

Zelfstandig naamwoord
