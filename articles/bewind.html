bewind (17885)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bewint. Equivalent to a deverbal from bewinden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈʋɪnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧wind
  • Rhymes: -ɪnt

Noun

bewind n (uncountable)

  1. reign
  2. government, regime, administration
