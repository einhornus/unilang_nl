aftaaien (55166)


Etymology

Unknown. May be from nautical jargon borrowed from English tie up or tie off.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑftaːi̯ə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧taai‧en

Verb

aftaaien

  1. (intransitive, rare) to stop
  2. (intransitive, informal) to go away
