linkmiegel (81789)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Linkmichel. Equivalent to link + Michel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɪŋkˌmi.ɣəl/
  • Hyphenation: link‧mie‧gel

Noun

linkmiegel m (plural linkmiegels)

  1. (Bargoens) A sly, deceptive and untrustworthy man.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

linkmiegel m [1]

  1. (scheldwoord) sluw, verradelijk persoon
