heengaan (6783)


Etymology

Compound of heen + gaan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦeːn.ɣaːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: heen‧gaan

Verb

heengaan

  1. (formal) to go away
    Ga heen in vrede.
    Depart in peace.
  2. (euphemistic) to pass away, to die
