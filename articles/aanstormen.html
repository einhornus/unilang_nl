aanstormen (110128)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch aenstormen. Equivalent to aan + stormen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːnˌstɔrmə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧stor‧men

Verb

aanstormen

  1. (intransitive) to approach quickly, to go straight towards (someone) with great speed
    Na de wedstrijd kwam zij op mij aangestormd. - She quickly came up to me after the match.
  2. (intransitive) to storm, to assault
    Synonym: bestormen
