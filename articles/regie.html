regie (22731)


Etymology

From French régie

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /rəˈɣi/, /reːˈɣi/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -i
  • Hyphenation: re‧gie

Noun

regie f (plural regies, diminutive regietje n)

  1. production, creative management (of a film, theatre work or other creative work)
  2. (figuratively) the management, being in charge

Related terms

  • regisseur
  • regisseren

Zelfstandig naamwoord

regie v

  2. het leiding geven, aansturen
