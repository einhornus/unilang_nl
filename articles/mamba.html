mamba (41967)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from English mamba or Afrikaans mamba, from Zulu imamba or Swazi mamba.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɑm.baː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mam‧ba
  • Rhymes: -ɑmbaː

Noun

mamba m (plural mamba's)

  1. A mamba, venomous snake of the genus Dendroaspis.

Derived terms

  • groene mamba
  • zwarte mamba
