lijfeigene (81806)


Etymology

Substantive form of lijfeigen.

Pronunciation

Noun

lijfeigene m or f (plural lijfeigenen)

  1. serf (person whose very bodily person is legally owned by a master, yet enjoying some legal (and religious-moral) protection)
