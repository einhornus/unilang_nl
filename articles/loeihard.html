loeihard (103563)


Etymology

Compound of loeien (“to low”) + hard (“hard, loud”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lui̯ˈɦɑrt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: loei‧hard
  • Rhymes: -ɑrt

Adjective

loeihard (not comparable)

  1. extremely loud, deafening
  2. (less common) very hard, with a lot of force or vigour
