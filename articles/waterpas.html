waterpas (46098)


Etymology

From water (“water”) + pas (“fit, measure”).

Pronunciation

Noun

waterpas n (plural waterpassen, diminutive waterpasje n)

  1. A level, tool for finding whether a surface is leveled perfectly horizontally
