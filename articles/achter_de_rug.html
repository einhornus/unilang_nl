achter_de_rug (4135)


Etymology

Literally, “behind the back”.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑx.tər də ˈrʏx/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

achter de rug (used only predicatively, not comparable)

  1. (idiomatic) having ended in the recent past, especially for unpleasant things.
    We hebben de negen uur durende reis achter de rug, maar nu zijn we in Frankrijk.
    The nine-hour journey is behind us, we're in France now.
