ontginning (73880)


Etymology

From ontginnen + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔntˈxɪ.nɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ont‧gin‧ning

Noun

ontginning f (plural ontginningen, diminutive ontginninkje n)

  1. development into agricultural land; a tract of land being developed
  2. excavation of mines; a tract of land being excavated
