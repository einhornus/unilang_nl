verstrooiing (86622)


Etymology

From verstrooien (“to scatter”) + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

verstrooiing f (plural verstrooiingen, diminutive verstrooiinkje n)

  1. scattering
  2. leisure

Related terms

  • verstrooien
