tuniek (48279)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French tunique, from Latin tunica.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /tyˈnik/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: tu‧niek
  • Rhymes: -ik

Noun

tuniek f (plural tunieken, diminutive tuniekje n)

  1. tunic
    1. A sports uniform.
    2. A long women's overgarment similar to a long shirt or sweater or a short dress.
    3. A short overgarment, often one worn as part of a uniform.

Related terms

  • tunica
