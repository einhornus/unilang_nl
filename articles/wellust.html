wellust (22353)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wellust, from Old Dutch welalust. Equivalent to wel- + lust.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛ.lʏst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wel‧lust

Noun

wellust f or m (uncountable)

  1. pleasure, lust
  2. lust, lechery, sensual pleasure
  3. desire
