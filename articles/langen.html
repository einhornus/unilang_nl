langen (91357)


Etymology

From Old Dutch *langon, from Proto-Germanic *langijaną.

Verb

langen

  1. to be/become long
  2. to lengthen, to make longer
  3. to touch
  4. to hand, to pass, to reach in order to give
  5. (impersonal) to long [+dative = subject]
