bedenking (109317)


Etymology

From bedenken + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈdɛŋ.kɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧den‧king
  • Rhymes: -ɛŋkɪŋ

Noun

bedenking f (plural bedenkingen, diminutive bedenkinkje n)

  1. (countable) doubt, reservation
    Synonym: twijfel
  2. (countable, archaic) consideration, contemplation, deliberation
    Synonym: overweging
