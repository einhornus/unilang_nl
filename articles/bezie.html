bezie (34379)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch besie. Cognate to bes and English berry.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeːzi/
  • Hyphenation: be‧zie

Noun

bezie f (plural bezies or beziën, diminutive bezietje n)

  1. (dated) berry
