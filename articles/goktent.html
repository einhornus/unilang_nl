goktent (51806)


Etymology

From gokken (“to gamble”) + tent (“a tent, joint”)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gok‧tent

Noun

goktent m or f (plural goktenten, diminutive goktentje n)

  1. A gambling joint, smaller or less mundane then a casino

Synonyms

  • speelhol n

Related terms

  • feesttent m or f
