doofpot (11326)


Etymology

Compound of doven (“to extinguish”) + pot (“pot”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoːf.pɔt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: doof‧pot

Noun

doofpot m (plural doofpotten, diminutive doofpotje n)

  1. an old-fashioned metal canister with a tight lid, in which remaining coals and other fuel were put to die down.
  2. a metaphorical place to put ideas or facts that one wishes to keep out of the public discussion, e.g. in an attempt to evade penalties; (metonymically) cover-up
    De onderzoeksresultaten eindigden in de doofpot
    The results of the investigation were eventually covered up.

Derived terms

  • doofpotaffaire
  • in de doofpot stoppen
