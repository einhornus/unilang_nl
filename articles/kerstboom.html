kerstboom (9607)


Etymology

From kerst + boom.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɛrstˌboːm/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

kerstboom m (plural kerstbomen, diminutive kerstboompje n)

  1. A Christmas tree, decorated tree placed during the Christmas holiday season
  2. A conifer used to be thus ornamented
