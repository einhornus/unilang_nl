dolheid (107536)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dulheit. Equivalent to dol (“crazy; mad, insane”) + -heid (“-ness”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɔl.ɦɛi̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dol‧heid

Noun

dolheid f (plural dolheden, diminutive dolheidje n)

  1. (pathology) Madness, insanity
  2. Mental craziness, folly
  3. Any strong, even excessive emotional state:
    1. extasis
    2. passion
    3. fury, rage
  4. (Nick)name of various diseases which cause excessive behavior such as rage

Derived terms

Related terms
