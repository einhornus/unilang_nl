bijhouden (7546)


Etymology

From bij + houden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛi̯ɦɑu̯də(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bij‧hou‧den

Verb

bijhouden

  1. (transitive) to keep up with (the pace)
  2. (transitive) to maintain, upkeep
