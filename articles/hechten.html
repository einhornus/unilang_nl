hechten (10616)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hechten, from Old Dutch heften, from Proto-Germanic *haftijaną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛx.tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hech‧ten
  • Rhymes: -ɛxtən

Verb

hechten

  1. (transitive) to attach, to bind
  2. (transitive) to suture, to stitch
