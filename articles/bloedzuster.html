bloedzuster (119156)


Etymology

Compound of bloed + zuster.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈblutˌsʏs.tər/
  • Hyphenation: bloed‧zus‧ter

Noun

bloedzuster f (plural bloedzusters, diminutive bloedzustertje n)

  1. blood sister (sister to whom one is related)
  2. blood sister (intimate female friend, as if an adopted sister)
