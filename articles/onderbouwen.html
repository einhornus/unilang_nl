onderbouwen (48819)


Etymology

From onder- + bouwen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔn.dərˈbɑu̯.ə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɑu̯ən

Verb

onderbouwen

  1. to support, to buttress
    Synonym: ondersteunen
  2. to substantiate (a claim)
