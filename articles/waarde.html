Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch waerdi, a contraction of waert gi (modern waart gij).

Contraction

waarde

  1. (Brabant) Contraction of waart gij.
Usage notes

The contraction is sometimes reinforced with an additional gij, giving waarde gij.
