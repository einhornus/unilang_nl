bromfiets (34351)


Etymology

From brommen (“to hum”) + fiets (“bicycle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbrɔm.fits/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: brom‧fiets

Noun

bromfiets f (plural bromfietsen, diminutive bromfietsje n)

  1. moped, limited to 50 cc engine and 45 km/h speed
    Synonym: brommer
  2. (Belgium) snorfiets moped, limited to 25 km/h speed

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bromfiets v / m
