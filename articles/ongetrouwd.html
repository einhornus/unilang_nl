ongetrouwd (18382)


Etymology

on- (“un-”) + getrouwd (“married”)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

ongetrouwd (not comparable)

  1. (of a couple) unmarried, not joined by marriage; may mean 'living in sin'
  2. (of a person) unwed, single, having no spouse; often taken for widowed
