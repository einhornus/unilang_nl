arrestatiebevel (10084)

From arrestatie (“arrest”) + bevel (“order, command”).

Pronunciation

Noun

arrestatiebevel n (plural arrestatiebevelen, diminutive arrestatiebevelletje n)

  1. (law) arrest warrant
