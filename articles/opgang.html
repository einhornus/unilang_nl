opgang (63612)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch opganc. Equivalent to op- + gang and a deverbal from opgaan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔp.xɑŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: op‧gang

Noun

opgang m (plural opgangen, diminutive opgangetje n)

  1. rising of a heavenly body (such as the sun or moon)
  2. rising (of a country's or a person's power)
  3. staircase
  4. path leading upwards

Synonyms

  • opkomst

Derived terms

  • zonsopgang
  • maansopgang
