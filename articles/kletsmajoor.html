kletsmajoor (64413)


Etymology

From kletsen (“to chatter”) + majoor (“major”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklɛts.maːˌjoːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klets‧ma‧joor

Noun

kletsmajoor m (plural kletsmajoors, diminutive kletsmajoortje n)

  1. babbler, chatterbox, all too talkative person

Synonyms

  • babbelaar
  • kletser
  • praatvaar
  • tateraar
