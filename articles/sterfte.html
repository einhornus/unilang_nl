sterfte (53154)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sterfte, steerfte. Equivalent to sterven + -te.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɛrf.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sterf‧te
  • Rhymes: -ɛrftə

Noun

sterfte f (plural sterftes or sterften)

  1. death, dying
  2. mortality
  3. (now chiefly in compounds) mass death

Derived terms

  • bijensterfte
  • kindersterfte
  • kraamsterfte
  • oversterfte
  • veesterfte
  • vissterfte
