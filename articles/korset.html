korset (18028)


Etymology

From French corset.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [kɔrˈsɛt]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

korset n or m (plural korsetten or korsets, diminutive korsetje n)

  1. A corset, tight undergarment
  2. A medieval precursor
  3. (figuratively) A bothersome constraint, limitations

Synonyms

  • (garment) rijglijf n
  • (constraint) keurslijf n

Derived terms

  • (corsets by type) badkorset n or m, buikkorset n or m, gezondheidskorset n or m, lijfkorset n or m, reformkorset n or m

Zelfstandig naamwoord

korset o
