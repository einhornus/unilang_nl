meermin (31083)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch meerminne. Equivalent to meer (“lake”) + min, formed from meerman + -in.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmeːrmɪn/, [ˈmɪːr.mɪn]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: meer‧min

Noun

meermin f (plural meerminnen, diminutive meerminnetje n)

  1. mermaid

Synonyms

  • zeemeermin

Related terms

  • meerman

Zelfstandig naamwoord
