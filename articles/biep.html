biep (25888)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bip/
  • Hyphenation: biep
  • Rhymes: -ip

Etymology 1

Either borrowed from English beep or a recent parallel onomatopoeia.

Noun

biep m (plural bieps or biepen, diminutive biepje n)

  1. A beep or bling, such sound, notably made as a signal by a beeper or any automatic - or control device
Related terms
  • biepen
  • bieper

Verb

biep

  2. imperative of biepen
