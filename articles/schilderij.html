schilderij (2656)


Etymology

From the stem of schilderen (“to paint”) + -erij (“-ery”). Or alternatively, from schilder (“painter”) + -ij (“-y”). See also the usage notes at -erij.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sxɪl.dəˈrɛi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schil‧de‧rij
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯

Noun

schilderij n or f (plural schilderijen, diminutive schilderijtje n)

  1. painting

Synonyms

Zelfstandig naamwoord

schilderij o of v
