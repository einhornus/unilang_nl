salpeter (53324)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch salpeter, from Medieval Latin salpetra.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌsɑlˈpeː.tər/
  • Hyphenation: sal‧pe‧ter
  • Rhymes: -eːtər

Noun

salpeter m or n (uncountable)

  1. saltpetre (UK), saltpeter (US).

Derived terms

  • chilisalpeter
  • salpeterzuur

Zelfstandig naamwoord

salpeter o
