roetmop (34732)


Etymology

Compound of roet (“soot”) + mop (“chunk”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrut.mɔp/
  • Hyphenation: roet‧mop

Noun

roetmop m (plural roetmoppen)

  1. (ethnic slur, derogatory, offensive) An offensive term of abuse for a black person: nigger. [from late 19th c.]
    Synonyms: blauwe, nikker
  2. (derogatory) A nickname or insult for a chimney sweep.
  3. (derogatory, chiefly in Limburgish contexts) A nickname or insult for a coal miner.
  4. (dated, ethnic slur, derogatory, offensive) An offensive term of abuse for a native Indonesian person. [late 19th - mid 20th c.]
    Synonym: blauwe
