foton (49541)


Etymology

From English photon, constructed from Ancient Greek φῶς (phôs, “light”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈfoːtɔn/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

foton n (plural fotonen, diminutive fotontje n)

  1. A photon, quantum of light
  2. The unit of electromagnetic radiation
