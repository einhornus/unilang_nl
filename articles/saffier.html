saffier (34719)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch saffier, from Latin sapphirus, from Ancient Greek σάπφειρος (sáppheiros).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sɑˈfiːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: saf‧fier
  • Rhymes: -iːr

Noun

saffier m (plural saffieren, diminutive saffiertje n)

  1. sapphire (transparent blue corundum, or a clear corundum of another colour)

Noun

saffier n (uncountable)

  1. sapphire ((blue) corundum)
  2. sapphire (blue colour)

Derived terms

  • saffieren
