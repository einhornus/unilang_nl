appelsien (133933)


Etymology

Compound of appel + Sina (old form of China).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑpəlˈsin/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ap‧pel‧sien

Noun

appelsien f (plural appelsienen, diminutive appelsientje n)

  1. (Belgium, Limburg, archaic elsewhere) orange (fruit)
    Synonym: sinaasappel

Derived terms

  • appelsiensap

Related terms

  • sinaasappel
