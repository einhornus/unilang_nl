poëet (35723)

From Middle Dutch poëte, from Latin poēta, from Ancient Greek ποιητής (poiētḗs).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: po‧eet
  • Rhymes: -eːt

Noun

poëet m (plural poëten, diminutive poëetje n)

  1. (formal, literary) poet

Related terms
