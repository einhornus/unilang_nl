mensenmassa (68762)


Etymology

Compound of mens (“human”) + -en- + massa (“mass, crowd”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɛn.sə(n)ˌmɑ.saː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: men‧sen‧mas‧sa

Noun

mensenmassa f (plural mensenmassa's)

  1. A crowd of people, a multitude of humans.
