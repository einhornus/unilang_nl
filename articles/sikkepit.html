sikkepit (53262)


Etymology

Compound of sik (“goatee”) + -e- + pit (“kernel, seed, dropping”). Literally, “goat dropping”.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɪ.kəˌpɪt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sik‧ke‧pit

Noun

sikkepit f (uncountable)

  1. (informal) very little, next to nothing

Usage notes

Mostly used in a negative form, i.e. geen sikkepit.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
