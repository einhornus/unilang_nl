Etymology 1

Noun

kolder m (uncountable)

  1. (obsolete) a brain disease (or perhaps multiple conflated brain diseases) in livestock
  2. madness, insanity, craziness
    Synonym: gekte
  3. nonsense
    Synonyms: nonsens, onzin
