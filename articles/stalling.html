stalling (79345)


Etymology

From stallen (“to park, put aside”) + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

stalling f (plural stallingen, diminutive stallinkje n)

  1. A place where gear can be stored, or where vehicles (especially bicycles) can be parked.
