tovermiddel (112133)


Etymology

Compound of toveren + middel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtoː.vərˌmɪ.dəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: to‧ver‧mid‧del

Noun

tovermiddel n (plural tovermiddelen, diminutive tovermiddeltje n)

  1. magical concoction or object
  2. talisman, fetish
    Synonyms: fetisj, talisman
  3. miracle cure, any cure with drastic good effects, also used of less than scientific folk medicine
    Synonym: wondermiddel
