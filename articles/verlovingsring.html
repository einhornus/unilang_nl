verlovingsring (14885)


Etymology

From verloving (“engagement”) + -s- + ring (“ring”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧lo‧vings‧ring

Noun

verlovingsring m (plural verlovingsringen, diminutive verlovingsringetje n)

  1. engagement ring
