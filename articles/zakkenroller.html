zakkenroller (32872)


Etymology

From zakkenrollen + -er or from zak + -en- (“pickpocket”) + roller.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɑ.kə(n)ˌrɔ.lər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zak‧ken‧rol‧ler

Noun

zakkenroller m (plural zakkenrollers, diminutive zakkenrollertje n, feminine zakkenrolster)

  1. pickpocket

Derived terms

  • zakkenrollerij

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zakkenroller m
