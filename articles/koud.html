koud (818)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch cout, from Old Dutch *kalt, from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gel-.

  • audio

Adjective

koud (comparative kouder, superlative koudst)

  1. cold (temperature)
    Antonyms: warm, heet
  2. cold (unfriendly)
    Antonym: warm
