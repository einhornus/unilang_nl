kennisgeving (60447)


Etymology

From kennis + geven + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɛ.nɪsˌxeː.vɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ken‧nis‧ge‧ving

Noun

kennisgeving f (plural kennisgevingen)

  1. The act of informing, notifying; notification.
  2. A notice, a message, a notification.
