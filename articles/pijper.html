pijper (68051)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch piper. Equivalent to pijpen + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɛi̯.pər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pij‧per
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯pər

Noun

pijper m (plural pijpers)

  1. A piper, a flautist.
  2. (slang) Someone who performs fellatio. [from late 20th c.]

Related terms
