zelfstandig (9973)


Etymology

From zelf- + stand + -ig, calque of German selbstständig. First attested in the late sixteenth century.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌzɛlfˈstɑn.dəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zelf‧stan‧dig

Adjective

zelfstandig (comparative zelfstandiger, superlative zelfstandigst)

  1. independent, autonomous, sovereign
  2. self-employed
  3. self-reliant, self-supporting
