volslank (111223)


Etymology

From vol (“full”) + slank (“slender”), calque of German vollschlank.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

volslank (comparative volslanker, superlative volslankst)

  1. slender and curvaceous
  2. (by extension, ironic, play on words) well-rounded, well-fed, as euphemism for chubby or fat
    Rubens' volslanke figuren etaleren de Vlaamse weelde in een vaak hongerig Europa
    Rubens's well-rounded figures display Flemish wealth in an often hungry Europe
