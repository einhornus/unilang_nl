oermens (63753)


Etymology

From oer- + mens, likely a calque of German Urmensch.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈuːr.mɛns/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: oer‧mens

Noun

oermens m (plural oermensen)

  1. A prehistoric human, especially an apeman. [from late 1860s]
  2. (dated) A primitive human, considered uncivilised. [from late 19th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

oermens m
