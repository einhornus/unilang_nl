spugen (9446)


Etymology

Reformed from spuwen, by analogy with its older strong past tense spoog.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈspy.ɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: spu‧gen
  • Rhymes: -yɣən

Verb

spugen

  1. to spit
  2. (euphemistic) to vomit

spugen
