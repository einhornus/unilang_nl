duisternis (2714)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch duusternesse, from Old Dutch thiusternussi. Equivalent to duister + -nis. Cognate to German Düsternis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdœy̯stərˌnɪs/
  • audio

  • Hyphenation: duis‧ter‧nis

Noun

duisternis f (plural duisternissen)

  1. darkness
