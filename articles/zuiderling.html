zuiderling (27297)


Etymology

From zuider- + -ling.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zui‧der‧ling

Noun

zuiderling m (plural zuiderlingen, diminutive zuiderlingetje n)

  1. southerner, someone living in, coming from or associated with the south of the world, a country etc.
