keet (21315)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /keːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -eːt

Noun

keet m or f (plural keten, diminutive keetje n)

  1. a shack or stand
  2. a barrack, shed, shanty
  3. a row, unruliness, especially in keet schoppen; rumble, frolicking
  4. a salt container; a salt boiling installation

Derived terms

  • bouwkeet

Zelfstandig naamwoord

  4. plezier
