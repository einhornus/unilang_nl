nakie (27026)


Etymology

From naakt (“naked, nude”) + -ie (colloquial Hollandic diminutive suffix).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnaː.ki/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: na‧kie

Noun

nakie n (plural nakies)

  1. (slang) The nude; a naked state

Synonyms
