gauw (1165)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gouwe, gauwe (“quick, fast, hasty”), alteration of gâ, from Old Dutch gā, from Proto-Germanic *ganhuz, *ganhwaz (“sudden, quick”), of unknown origin. Cognate with Low German gau (“quick, fast”), German jäh (“sudden, abrupt”). More at gay.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣɑu̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɑu̯
  • Homophone: gouw

Adverb

gauw

  1. quickly
  2. soon

Bijwoord

gauw

  1. binnen een kort tijdsbestek
    • Kleed je gauw om.
  2. zeer binnenkort
    • De bus zal wel gauw komen.
