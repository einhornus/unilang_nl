ven (46224)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch venne, from Old Dutch *feni, from Proto-Germanic *fanją. Doublet of veen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vɛn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛn

Noun

ven n (plural vennen, diminutive vennetje n)

  1. mere, a small shallow lake or pond

Derived terms

  • bosven
  • heideven

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ven o
