samenscholing (88524)


Etymology

From samenscholen + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsaː.mə(n)ˌsxoː.lɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sa‧men‧scho‧ling

Noun

samenscholing f (plural samenscholingen, diminutive samenscholinkje n)

  1. (countable) An assembly, a group of people gathered, usually in public.
  2. (uncountable) The act or process of assembling, of gathering together, usually in public.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

samenscholing v

  1. het samenscholen
  2. de samengeschoolde menigte
