vet (2967)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vet, from Old Dutch fētit, fet, from Proto-Germanic *faitidaz, originally a past participle.

Pronunciation

  • audio

Adjective

vet (comparative vetter, superlative vetst)

  1. fat
  2. greasy
  3. (informal) cool
