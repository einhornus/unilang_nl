akkerland (95855)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ackerlant, ackerland. Equivalent to akker + land.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑ.kərˌlɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ak‧ker‧land

Noun

akkerland n (plural akkerlanden, diminutive akkerlandje n)

  1. farmland used for crops

Zelfstandig naamwoord

akkerland o [1]
