stoorzender (41497)


Etymology

From storen + zender.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstoːrˌzɛn.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stoor‧zen‧der

Noun

stoorzender m (plural stoorzenders, diminutive stoorzendertje n)

  1. A jammer, a jamming station.
  2. (derogatory, figuratively) A person who obstructs regular operation; an obstructionist, a saboteur.
