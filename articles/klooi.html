klooi (56994)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kloːi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klooi
  • Rhymes: -oːi̯

Etymology 1

Lenited form of kloot (“bollock”). The verb klooien may be (un)related.

Noun

klooi m (plural klooien, diminutive klooitje n)

  1. A dork, jerk, nobody
  2. A bumbler, loser
Synonyms
  • (dork) sul m, clown m, nul(liteit), oelewapper m, oen n, onnozelaar m
  • (bumbler) knoeier m, stuntel m, stuntelaar m, mislukkeling m
Derived terms
  • klojo
  • klooiig
Related terms
  • geklooi n
  • kloten

klooi
