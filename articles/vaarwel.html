vaarwel (1820)


Etymology

From varen (“to fare, to go, to travel”) + wel (“well”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vaːrˈʋɛl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vaar‧wel
  • Rhymes: -ɛl

Interjection

vaarwel

  1. (poetic) goodbye, farewell (connotation is somewhat sad, almost poetic)
    Vaarwel, wrede wereld! ― Farewell, cruel world!

Noun

vaarwel n (uncountable)

  1. A farewell, an occasion of saying goodbye; a departure.
