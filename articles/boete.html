Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch boete, from Old Dutch *bōta, from Proto-Germanic *bōtō (“recompense”). Cognate to English bote, boot (“profit, plunder”).

Noun

boete f (plural boetes or boeten)

  1. fine, financial penalty
    Synonyms: bekeuring, geldboete
  2. penance, paining atonement
    Synonym: boetedoening
Related terms
  • beboeten
  • boeten
  • boeteling
  • boetepij, boetekleed
  • uitboeten
