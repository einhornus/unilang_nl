Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch âer, from Old Dutch *ār, from Proto-Germanic *ahaz.

Noun

aar f (plural aren, diminutive aartje n)

  1. ear (of corn, grain etc.)
Derived terms
  • korenaar

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch āer.

Noun

aar m (plural aren, diminutive aartje n)

  1. (archaic) eagle
Synonyms

Noun
