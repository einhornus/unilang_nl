babbelen (8240)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch babbelen, from Old Dutch *babbalon, from Proto-Germanic *babalōną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɑbələ(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bab‧be‧len
  • Rhymes: -ɑbələn

Verb

babbelen

  1. (intransitive) to chatter, to chit-chat, to talk casually
  2. (archaic, dialectal) to nibble, to bite on something
