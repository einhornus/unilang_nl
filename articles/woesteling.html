Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

From woest + -e- + ling.

Noun

woesteling m (plural woestelingen, diminutive woestelingetje n)

  1. A barbaric, savage ruffian, gross boor
  2. A brute, crude and dangerous person
  3. A savage and indomitable beast
    Hondsdolheid verandert de liefste huisdieren in woestelingen
    Rabies turns the sweetest pets into brutal beasts
