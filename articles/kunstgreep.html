kunstgreep (104042)


Etymology

From kunst- + greep, calque of German Kunstgriff.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkʏnst.xreːp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kunst‧greep

Noun

kunstgreep m (plural kunstgrepen, diminutive kunstgreepje n)

  1. artificial device, ploy, trick
