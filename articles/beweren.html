beweren (4646)


Etymology

A merger of two verbs:

  • Middle Dutch beweren, an eastern variant of bewaren (“to prove”), from waer (“true”) (modern waar). Equivalent to be- + waar + -en.
  • Middle Dutch beweren, a variant of bewaren (“to uphold, to stand one's ground”) (modern bewaren). Equivalent to be- + waren.

Pronunciation

Verb

beweren

  1. (transitive) to claim, to assert, to allege
