belemmeren (19245)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch belemmeren. Equivalent to belemmen + -eren. Compare German belämmert.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈlɛmərə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧lem‧me‧ren
  • Rhymes: -ɛmərən

Verb

belemmeren

  1. (transitive) to obstruct, to hinder, to hamper
