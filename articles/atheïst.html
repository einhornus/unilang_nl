atheïst (18862)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French atheiste.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌaː.teːˈjɪʃt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: a‧the‧ist
  • Rhymes: -ɪst

Noun

atheïst m or f (plural atheïsten, diminutive atheïstje n)

  1. atheist [from 16th c.]

Related terms

  • atheïsme

Zelfstandig naamwoord

atheïst m
