vertier (20916)


Etymology

From vertieren.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vərˈtir/, [vərˈtiːr]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧tier
  • Rhymes: -ir

Noun

vertier n (uncountable)

  1. (slightly dated and formal) entertainment, amusement
  2. (dated) activity, social intercourse (the presence of many interacting people)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

vertier o

  1. zaken die de zinnen verzetten

Verwante begrippen
