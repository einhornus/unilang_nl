Adjective

executieve

Noun

executieve m or f (plural executieven)

  1. (often capitalized: Executieve) An executive organ
    1. historically, the name of the three regions' and three linguistic communities' councils of ministers during the early years of federal Belgium (later changed to the equivalent of government)
    De Vlaamse Executieve was de voorloper van de huidige Vlaamse Regering
    The Flemish 'Executive (Council)' was the precursor of the present Flemish Government
