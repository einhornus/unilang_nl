stilstand (20221)


Etymology

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɪl.stɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stil‧stand

Noun

stilstand m (uncountable)

  1. standstill, stop, stoppage, cessation
