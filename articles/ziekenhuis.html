ziekenhuis (728)


Etymology

From zieke (“sick person, patient”) + -n- + huis (“house, building, institution”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzi.kə(n).ɦœy̯s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zie‧ken‧huis

Noun

ziekenhuis n (plural ziekenhuizen, diminutive ziekenhuisje n)

  1. A hospital, medical care facility
  2. (by extension) An infirmary

Synonyms
