politieker (80325)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌpoː.liˈti.kər/
  • Audio
    		(file)

  • Rhymes: -ikər

Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Politiker, from Latin polīticus, from Ancient Greek πολιτικός (politikós). Equivalent to politiek + -er.

Noun

politieker m (plural politiekers, diminutive politiekertje n)

  1. (Belgium, informal) politician [from late 19th c.]
    Synonym: politicus

See the etymology of the main entry.

Adjective

politieker
