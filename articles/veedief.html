veedief (52923)


Etymology

Compound of vee (“livestock”) + dief (“thief”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈveː.dif/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vee‧dief

Noun

veedief m (plural veedieven, diminutive veediefje n)

  1. abactor, someone who steals livestock [from 16th c.]
