Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch rijm, rīme, from Old French rime, of uncertain origin. More at rhyme.

Noun

rijm n (plural rijmen, diminutive rijmpje n)

  1. rhyme, rhyming
  2. (now chiefly diminutive) A rhyme, a poem that uses rhyme.
Derived terms
  • binnenrijm
  • eindrijm

  • rijmwoord
