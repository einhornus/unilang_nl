klemtoon (45240)

  • (Limburg) IPA(key): [ˈklæmtoː(ə)n ~ klæmˈtoː(ə)n]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

klemtoon m (plural klemtonen, diminutive klemtoontje n)

  1. (linguistics) stress

Zelfstandig naamwoord

klemtoon m
