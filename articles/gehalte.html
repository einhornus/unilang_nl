gehalte (21634)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Gehalt.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣə.ˈɦɑltə/
  • audio (Belgium)
    		(file)
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧hal‧te

Noun

gehalte n (plural gehalten or gehaltes, diminutive gehaltetje n)

  1. content (numerical, as a fraction of a whole)
    Als het CO2-gehalte in het bloed daalt, spreken we van hypocapnie.[1] — If the CO2 content in the blood falls, we speak of hypocapnia.
  2. value, purity (if higher fractional content (of a valuable metal in an alloy) equals higher purity, less impurity; thus, higher value (of the alloy as a whole, per unit of weight))
    Goudgehalte wordt in karaat uitgedrukt, waarbij 24 karaat 100% is.[2] — The purity of gold is expressed in carat, where 24 carat is 100%.
