cola (4302)


Etymology

From English cola, from the drink Coca Cola.

Pronunciation

  • (Netherlands) IPA(key): /ˈkoː.laː/
  • (Belgium) IPA(key): /ˈkoː.lɑ/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

cola m (plural cola's, diminutive colaatje n)

  1. cola (drink)
  2. kola (nut)
