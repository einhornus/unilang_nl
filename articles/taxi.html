taxi (1349)


Etymology

Borrowed from French taxi, from German Taxameter.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtɑk.si/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ta‧xi
  • Rhymes: -ɑksi

Noun

taxi m (plural taxi's, diminutive taxietje n)

  1. A taxi.

Derived terms

Related terms

  • taximeter

Zelfstandig naamwoord

taxi m

taxi
