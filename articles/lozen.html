lozen (10187)


Etymology

From Old Dutch lōsen, from Proto-Germanic *lausijaną.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -oːzən

Verb

lozen

  1. to drain, to discharge (e.g. waste water)
  2. (figuratively) to drive away, to force to leave
