belagen (39809)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch belagen, from lagen, from lage (modern laag).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈlaːɣə(n)/
  • belagen
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧la‧gen
  • Rhymes: -aːɣən

Verb

belagen

  1. (transitive) to beleaguer, waylay
  2. (transitive) to vex, harass or beset
