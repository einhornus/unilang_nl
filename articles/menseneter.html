menseneter (35852)


Etymology

Compound of mens + -en- + eter.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɛn.sə(n)ˌeː.tər/
  • Hyphenation: men‧sen‧eter

Noun

menseneter m (plural menseneters)

  1. A man-eater, one who eats humans, in particular a human cannibal.
