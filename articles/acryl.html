acryl (77535)


Etymology

From Latin acer (“sharp, biting”) + -yl.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɑˈkril/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: acryl
  • Rhymes: -il

Noun

acryl n (uncountable)

  1. The acidic compound acryl.
  2. A wool-like artificial fiber.

Derived terms

  • acrylverf
  • acrylzuur
