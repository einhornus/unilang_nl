dobbelspel (83956)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dobbelspel. Equivalent to dobbelen + spel.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dob‧bel‧spel

Noun

dobbelspel n (plural dobbelspelen, diminutive dobbelspelletje n)

  1. A die game (game played with dice), often a gambling game.
