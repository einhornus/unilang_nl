visboer (55539)


Etymology

From vis (“fish”) + boer (“farmer, merchant”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

visboer m (plural visboeren, diminutive visboertje n)

  1. fishmonger

Zelfstandig naamwoord

visboer m
