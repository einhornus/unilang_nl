regering (1229)


Etymology

From regeren + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /rəˈɣeː.rɪŋ/
  • regering
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: re‧ge‧ring
  • Rhymes: -eːrɪŋ

Noun

regering f (plural regeringen, diminutive regerinkje n)

  1. The act of governing, ruling a state or similar political entity
    Synonyms: bewind, gouvernement, regime
  2. (law) A government, a governing council: especially the executive branch of government
    Synonyms: gouvernement, kabinet, regime
  3. The rule, reign of a prince etc.
    Synonyms: bewind, regime
