beschieting (47785)


Etymology

From beschieten + -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈsxi.tɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧schie‧ting

Noun

beschieting f (plural beschietingen)

  1. (countable, uncountable) A shooting, an act of shooting at someone.
  2. (countable) A cover of planks.
    Synonym: beschot

Zelfstandig naamwoord

beschieting v
