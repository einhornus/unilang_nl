afstompen (134114)


Etymology

Compound of af + stompen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfˌstɔm.pə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧stom‧pen

Verb

afstompen

  1. (transitive, literally) to blunt, to make blunt (of sharp objects)
  2. (transitive) to dull, to numb (of other objects or faculties)
  3. (transitive) to numb, to make apathetic (of minds and people)
