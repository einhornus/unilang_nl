avance (77293)


Etymology

Borrowed from French avance.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /aːˈvɑn.sə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: avan‧ce

Noun

avance m or f (plural avances)

  1. (chiefly plural) overture, approach, advance (act of accosting)
    Synonym: toenadering
  2. (dated) progress
    Synonyms: voortgang, vooruitgang
  3. (dated) stock appreciation
  4. (obsolete) profit
    Synonym: winst
  5. (dated) advantage, benefit
    Synonym: voordeel

Related terms

  • avanceren

Zelfstandig naamwoord

avance m/v

  1. poging tot toenadering
  2. voorschot
  3. (verouderd) voordeel, winst
