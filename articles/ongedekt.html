ongedekt (41817)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ongedect. Equivalent to on- + gedekt.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔŋ.ɣəˈdɛkt/
  • Hyphenation: on‧ge‧dekt
  • Rhymes: -ɛkt

Adjective

ongedekt (not comparable)

  1. uncovered, not protected against attacks or the elements
  2. uncovered, not wearing headwear
  3. naked, not clothed
  4. (of female animals) uncovered, not having been covered by a male animal
  5. (finance) unsecured
  6. (linguistics) unchecked, being in an open syllable
  7. (of tables) not covered by a tablecloth
