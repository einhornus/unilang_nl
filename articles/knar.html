knar (27078)


Etymology

Variant of knor.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /knɑr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: knar
  • Rhymes: -ɑr

Noun

knar m (plural knarren, diminutive knarretje n)

  1. (informal, often with pleonastic attribute "oude") old geezer, oldtimer
    krasse knar ― vivacious oldtimer
  2. (informal) bonce, head
