kapsel (5822)


Etymology

From kappen + -sel. The meaning “membrane” derives from Latin capsula.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɑp.səl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kap‧sel

Noun

kapsel n (plural kapsels, diminutive kapseltje n)

  1. haircut, hairstyle, hairdo
  2. (physiology) capsule (enveloping membrane)
  3. (archaic) headdress, headwear (in particular for women)

Derived terms

  • bloempotkapsel

Related terms
