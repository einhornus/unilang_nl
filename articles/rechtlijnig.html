rechtlijnig (46586)


Etymology

From recht (“straight”) + lijn (“line”) + -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

rechtlijnig (comparative rechtlijniger, superlative rechtlijnigst)

  1. rectilinear
  2. (figuratively) stiff, rigid, inflexible
