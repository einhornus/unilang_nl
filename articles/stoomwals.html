stoomwals (72673)


Etymology

Compound of stoom + wals.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstoːm.ʋɑls/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stoom‧wals

Noun

stoomwals f (plural stoomwalsen, diminutive stoomwalsje n)

  1. steamroller (often also used of non-steam-operated rollers)
