inwerken (21595)


Etymology

Compound of in + werken.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɪnˌʋɛr.kə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: in‧wer‧ken

Verb

inwerken

  1. (intransitive) to impact, to act [+ op (on)]
  2. (ergative) to familiarise or become familiarised with a new work activity, usually through on-the-job training [+ in (object)]
