  • Rhymes: -eːldən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch bēelden, from Old Dutch bilithen. Equivalent to beeld + -en.

Verb

beelden

  1. (obsolete) to shape, form
  2. (obsolete) to form an image in the mind, to picture, imagine
  3. (poetic) to give an impression of something
