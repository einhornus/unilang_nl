bespuiten (58049)


Etymology

From be- + spuiten (“to spout”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌbəˈspœy̯tə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: be‧spui‧ten
  • Rhymes: -œy̯tən

Verb

bespuiten

  1. (transitive) to besprinkle, to spray on/at
  2. (transitive) to spout on/at
