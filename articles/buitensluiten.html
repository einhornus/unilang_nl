buitensluiten (25003)


Etymology

From buiten + sluiten.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbœy̯tə(n)ˌslœy̯tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bui‧ten‧slui‧ten

Verb

buitensluiten

  1. (transitive) to shut out, to lock out
  2. (transitive) to exclude, to shun
