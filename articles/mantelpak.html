mantelpak (103286)


Etymology

Compound of mantel + pak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɑn.təlˌpɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: man‧tel‧pak

Noun

mantelpak n (plural mantelpakken, diminutive mantelpakje n)

  1. (chiefly diminutive) A ladies' suit with a skirt. [from early 20th c.]
