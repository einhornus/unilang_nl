melig (53888)


Etymology

From meel (“flour”) + -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

melig (comparative meliger, superlative meligst)

  1. mealy
  2. hokey, sappy, cheesy
  3. (of mood) being bored (and) silly, cheerful
  • Zij is in een melige bui vandaag. Geen wonder, ze veveelt zich te pletter.
    She is in a silly cheerful mood today. Little surprise, she is bored stiff.
