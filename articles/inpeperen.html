inpeperen (104976)


Etymology

From in + peperen.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

inpeperen

  1. to pepper, to cover with pepper
  2. to rub in, to make (a point) annoyingly obvious
  3. to get back at
  4. to rub snow into, particularly in the face (during snowball fights)
