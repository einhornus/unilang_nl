onderbaas (33172)


Etymology

From onder (“under”) + baas (“boss”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Hyphenation: on‧der‧baas

Noun

onderbaas m (plural onderbazen, diminutive onderbaasje n, feminine onderbazin)

  1. a generic term referring to a person that acts on behalf of their superior in the event of the said superior's absence, such as an acting manager of a firm; a deputy, a lieutenant
  2. an underboss

onderbaas m (plural onderbazen, diminutive onderbaasje n)

  1. base of a pillar
