  • Rhymes: -ɑpən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch stappen, from Old Dutch *stappen, *steppen, from Proto-Germanic *stapjaną.

Verb

stappen

  1. to march
  2. to step
  3. (Netherlands) to go out (for a drink)
    Synonym: uitgaan
  4. (chiefly Belgium) to walk
    Synonyms: gaan, lopen, wandelen
