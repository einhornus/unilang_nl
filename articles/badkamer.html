badkamer (1999)


Etymology

From bad + kamer.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɑtˌkaː.mər/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bad‧ka‧mer

Noun

badkamer f (plural badkamers, diminutive badkamertje n)

  1. bathroom

Usage notes

In English, bathroom may mean a room with only a toilet, but badkamer always refers to a room with a bath and/or a shower (and possibly a toilet).

Zelfstandig naamwoord
