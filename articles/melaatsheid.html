melaatsheid (68778)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch malaetscheit. Equivalent to melaats (“leprose”) + -heid (“-ity, -ness”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌmeːˈlaːts.ɦɛi̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: me‧laats‧heid

Noun

melaatsheid f (plural melaatsheden)

  1. (pathology) skin disease; either leprosy (the infectious skin-disease caused by the bacteria Mycobacterium leprae) or (historical) another disease historically conflated with leprosy

Synonyms

  • lepra (some contexts)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

melaatsheid v
