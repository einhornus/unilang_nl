Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch cassijn, from Old French cassin.

Noun

kozijn n (plural kozijnen, diminutive kozijntje n)

  1. window frame
Derived terms
  • deurkozijn
  • raamkozijn

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch cosin, from Old French cosin.

Noun

kozijn m (plural kozijnen, diminutive kozijntje n)

  1. (now chiefly Southern) A male cousin.
    Synonym: neef
  2. (now chiefly Southern) A nephew.
    Synonym: neef
