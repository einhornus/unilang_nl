gamba (106650)


Pronunciation

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Italian viola da gamba, from gamba (“leg”).

Noun

gamba f (plural gamba's, diminutive gambaatje n)

  1. viola da gamba
    Synonym: knieviool
Derived terms
  • gambist

Etymology 2

Borrowed from Catalan gamba, Portuguese gamba or French gamba.

Noun

gamba f (plural gamba's, diminutive gambaatje n)

  1. scampi, prawn

Zelfstandig naamwoord

gamba v / m

  1. (muziekinstrument) knieviool
  2. (voeding) (kreeftachtigen) grote garnaal
