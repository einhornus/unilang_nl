bevuilen (31367)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bevulen. Equivalent to be + vuil + en.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈvœy̯.lə(n)/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧vui‧len
  • Rhymes: -œy̯lən

Verb

bevuilen

  1. to sully (to corrupt or damage)
  2. to dirty, to soil, to defile, to stain, to sully (to make dirty or impure)
