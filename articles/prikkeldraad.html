prikkeldraad (11415)


Etymology

Compound of prikkel (“barb, point”) + draad (“wire”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈprɪ.kəlˌdraːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: prik‧kel‧draad

Noun

prikkeldraad m (plural prikkeldraden, diminutive prikkeldraadje n)

  1. (chiefly uncountable, also countable) barbed wire
  2. (uncountable) A pain stimulus created by twisting the skin of the arm in two different directions.
