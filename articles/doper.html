doper (30603)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dôpere, from Old Dutch dōperi. Equivalent to dopen + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoː.pər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: do‧per
  • Rhymes: -oːpər

Noun

doper m (plural dopers)

  1. baptiser, one who baptises
  2. (historical) Anabaptist [from 16th c.]
    Synonyms: anabaptist, doperse, herdoper, wederdoper

Derived terms

  • dopers
