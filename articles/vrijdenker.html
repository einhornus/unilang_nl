vrijdenker (86318)


Etymology

From vrij + denken + -er, calque of English freethinker.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvrɛi̯ˌdɛŋ.kər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vrij‧den‧ker

Noun

vrijdenker m (plural vrijdenkers, diminutive vrijdenkertje n, feminine vrijdenkster)

  1. freethinker, someone critical of organised religion

Derived terms

  • vrijdenkersbeweging
