bovenkaak (61557)


Etymology

From boven- (“upper”) + kaak (“jaw”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈboː.və(n)ˌkaːk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bo‧ven‧kaak

Noun

bovenkaak f (plural bovenkaken, diminutive bovenkaakje n)

  1. An upper jaw.
