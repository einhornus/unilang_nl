wildbraad (78029)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wiltbraet, from Old Dutch wiltbrāt. Equivalent to wild + braad, though note the difference in gender.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɪlt.braːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wild‧braad

Noun

wildbraad n (uncountable)

  1. prepared game meat
