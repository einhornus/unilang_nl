kerstvakantie (35016)


Etymology

Compound of kerst + vakantie.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɛrst.faːˌkɑn.si/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kerst‧va‧kan‧tie

Noun

kerstvakantie f (plural kerstvakanties)

  1. A school holiday covering Christmas and New Year's Day, typically lasting two weeks.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kerstvakantie v
