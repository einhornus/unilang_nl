bloem (3789)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bloeme, from Old Dutch *bluomo, from Proto-Germanic *blōmô.

Pronunciation

  • audio

Noun

bloem f (plural bloemen, diminutive bloempje n or bloemetje n)

  1. flower of flowering plant
  2. flour of wheat or corn (uncountable)
