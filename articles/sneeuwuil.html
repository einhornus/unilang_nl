sneeuwuil (99689)


Etymology

Compound of sneeuw (“snow”) + uil (“owl”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsneːu̯.œy̯l/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sneeuw‧uil

Noun

sneeuwuil m or f (plural sneeuwuilen, diminutive sneeuwuiltje n)

  1. snowy owl, Bubo scandiacus.
