viskraam (86501)


Etymology

Compound of vis + kraam.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɪs.kraːm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vis‧kraam
  • Rhymes: -ɪskraːm

Noun

viskraam f (plural viskramen, diminutive viskraampje n)

  1. fish stall (stall where fish is sold)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

viskraam v/m of o
