telen (32253)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch tēlen, from Old Dutch tilon, from Proto-Germanic *tilōną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈteː.lən/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: te‧len
  • Rhymes: -eːlən

Verb

telen

  1. (transitive) to cultivate, grow plants, notably crops
    Synonyms: verbouwen, kweken
  2. (transitive) to raise, breed animals or (figurative, notably a type of) men
    Synonym: kweken
