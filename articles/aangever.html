aangever (37485)


Etymology

From aangeven + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːnˌɣeː.vər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧ge‧ver

Noun

aangever m (plural aangevers, diminutive aangevertje n, feminine aangeefster)

  1. One who hands something to someone, one who passes something on.
  2. informant
  3. declarant
  4. (comedy) stooge, straight man
  5. betrayer
