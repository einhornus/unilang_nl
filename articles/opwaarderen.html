opwaarderen (89688)


Etymology

From op (“up”) + waarderen (“to appreciate”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

opwaarderen

  1. to revalue, increase in value
  2. to top up (to extend the credit of something)

opwaarderen

  1. verbeteren, bijwerken
