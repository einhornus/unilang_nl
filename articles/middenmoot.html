middenmoot (102997)


Etymology

Compound of midden + moot.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɪ.də(n)ˌmoːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mid‧den‧moot

Noun

middenmoot f (uncountable)

  1. the middle slice of a fish
  2. (figurative) middle, the average or mediocre section of a certain reference class

Derived terms

  • middenmoter
