boos (746)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bôse, from Old Dutch bōsi, from Proto-Germanic *bausuz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /boːs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boos
  • Rhymes: -oːs

Adjective

boos (comparative bozer, superlative meest boos or boost)

  1. mad, angry
  2. upset, frustrated
  3. evil
    Er zijn boze geesten in deze bossen. - There are evil spirits in these forests.
