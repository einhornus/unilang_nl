bankzitter (58119)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bank‧zit‧ter

Noun

bankzitter m (plural bankzitters)

  1. (sports, games) A benched player.
  2. Someone sitting on a bench, pew or (Netherlands) couch.
