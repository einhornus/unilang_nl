kindertjes (17652)


Etymology

Formed as the plural diminutive of kinderen, the standard but irregular plural of kind, as if the latter was the regular plural of the (nonexistent) singular kinder. The standard plural of the diminutive of kind is kindjes, but this alternative form is common.
