klopgeest (49162)


Etymology

From kloppen (“to knock”) + geest (“ghost, spirit”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklɔp.xeːst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klop‧geest

Noun

klopgeest m (plural klopgeesten, diminutive klopgeestje n)

  1. poltergeist
    Synonym: poltergeest
  2. (humorous) someone who knocks or drops by a lot
