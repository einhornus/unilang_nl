antilichaam (109652)


Noun

From anti- + lichaam, calque of German Antikörper.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑn.tiˌlɪ.xaːm/
  • antilichaam
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: an‧ti‧li‧chaam

Noun

antilichaam n (plural antilichamen, diminutive antilichaampje n)

  1. (biochemistry) antibody, protein that binds to a specific antigen to counter it
    Synonym: antistof

Zelfstandig naamwoord

antilichaam o

Synoniemen
