bezinnen (44067)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch besinnen. Equivalent to be- + zinnen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈzɪnə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧zin‧nen
  • Rhymes: -ɪnən

Verb

bezinnen

  1. (intransitive) to reflect (i.e. think about)
  2. (reflexive) to reconsider, to think carefully
