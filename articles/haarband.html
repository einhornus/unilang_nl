haarband (60798)


Etymology

Compound of haar + band.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦaːr.bɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: haar‧band

Noun

haarband m (plural haarbanden, diminutive haarbandje n)

  1. A hair tie, a hairband, a headband (elastic band wrapped around the head to keep hair in place).
