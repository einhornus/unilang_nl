verschansen (58731)


Etymology

From ver- + schans + -en.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌvɛrˈsxɑn.sə(n)/, /ˌvərˈsxɑn.sə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧schan‧sen
  • Rhymes: -ɑnsən

Verb

verschansen

  1. (transitive) to fortify; (literally) to surround with a defensive wall, to protect with a bastion
  2. (reflexive) to dig oneself in, to seek cover, to fortify one's position, to take a fortified position
