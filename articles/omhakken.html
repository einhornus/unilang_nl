omhakken (32430)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ommehacken. Equivalent to om + hakken.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔmˌɦɑ.kə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: om‧hak‧ken

Verb

omhakken

  1. (transitive) to chop down, to fell (chiefly of trees, plants, etc.)
