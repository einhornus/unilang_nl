doeg (19895)


Etymology

Derived from the Dutch dialect Zaans, where doeg means dag (“day”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /dux/
  • Hyphenation: doeg
  • Rhymes: -ux

Interjection

doeg

  1. (Netherlands) bye

Usage notes

This word is mostly used in the Netherlands, it is less common in Belgium.

Synonyms

  • doei
  • dag
  • toedeloe
  • houdoe
Synoniemen
  • doei, toedeloe
