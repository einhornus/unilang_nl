plasseks (101259)


Etymology

Compound of plassen + seks.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈplɑ.sɛks/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: plas‧seks

Noun

plasseks m (uncountable)

  1. pee sex (any sexual activity that involves urination or urine in noticeable quantities)
    Ik heb geen plasseks met die vrouw gehad.
    I did not have pee sex with that woman.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

plasseks m

  1. (seksualiteit) prikkeling van de geslachtsdrift met behulp van urine of urineren
