misbaksel (21550)


Etymology

From mis- (“miss-, faulty”) + baksel (from the root of bakken (“to bake”) + -sel (“-ing”)).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

misbaksel n (plural misbaksels, diminutive misbakseltje n)

  1. (literally) A failed baking product
  2. (figuratively) A failure, any faulty product
  3. (figuratively) An ugly one, a mongrel
