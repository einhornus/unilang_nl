sutler (53103)


Etymology

From Dutch soeteler (modern zoetelaar).

Pronunciation

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˈsʌtlə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /ˈsʌtləɹ/
  • Rhymes: -ʌtlə(ɹ)

Noun

sutler (plural sutlers)

  1. A person who follows an army, selling provisions.

Derived terms

  • sutlership

Related terms

  • sutle
  • sutling
