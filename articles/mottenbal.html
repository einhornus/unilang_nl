mottenbal (102769)


Etymology

Compound of mot (“moth”) + -en- + bal (“ball”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɔ.tə(n)ˌbɑl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mot‧ten‧bal

Noun

mottenbal m (plural mottenballen, diminutive mottenballetje n)

  1. A mothball, bug-repelling ball.
  2. (figurative, in the plural) Long-term storage; unsavory state (of conservation)

Related terms

  • mottenbakkes
  • mottenkruid
  • mottenpapier
  • mottenplaag

Zelfstandig naamwoord

mottenbal m
