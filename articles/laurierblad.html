laurierblad (128012)


Etymology

Compound of laurier + blad.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lɑu̯ˈriːrˌblɑt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lau‧rier‧blad

Noun

laurierblad n (plural laurierbladeren or laurierbladen or laurierblaren, diminutive laurierblaadje n)

  1. A bay leaf, a laurel leaf, often used as a culinary herb.
    Synonym: lauwerblad
