Numeral

één

  1. one
    Eén tel met jou is mooier dan een eeuwigheid alleen.
    One count with you is more beautiful than an eternity alone. (Marco Borsato ft. Sita – Lopen Op Het Water)

Usage notes

  • A variant spelling of een, usually to emphasize the numeral aspect of the usage, as opposed to the indefinite article (which is pronounced [ən]).
  • When the first letter is capitalized, the accent is usually dropped: Eén.
Verbuiging
  • Verbogen vorm: ene.
