plezierjacht (101230)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pləˈziːrˌjɑxt/
  • Hyphenation: ple‧zier‧jacht

Etymology 1

Compound of plezier + jacht.

Noun

plezierjacht n (plural plezierjachten, diminutive plezierjachtje n)

  1. luxurious yacht

Etymology 2

Compound of plezier + jacht.

Noun

plezierjacht f (uncountable)

  1. recreational hunting, pleasure hunting
