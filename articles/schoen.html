schoen (3804)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch schoe, from Old Dutch skuo, from Proto-Germanic *skōhaz. The original Middle Dutch plural was schoen, which was reanalysed as a singular form.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

  • Rhymes: -un

Noun
