koelte (40613)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch coelde. Equivalent to koel + -te.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkul.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: koel‧te
  • Rhymes: -ultə

Noun

koelte f (plural koeltes, diminutive koeltje n)

  1. (uncountable, literally) coolness, a cold, but not freezing temperature, possibly pleasant
  2. (countable, nautical) A breeze; any kind of wind.
    Er stak een harde koelte op. ― A strong breeze began to blow.
    Synonyms: bries, wind
  3. (uncountable, rare) cool, unshakable attitude
