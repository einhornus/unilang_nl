dagelijks (4293)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dagelics, from Old Dutch dagawelikis, a compound of dag (“day”) + the genitive of welik (“which, whichever, any”) (modern welk). Equivalent to dag +‎ -e- +‎ -lijks.
