gefortuneerd (117689)



Etymology

From Middle French fortuné (“fortunate, favored by fate”), from Latin fortūnātus.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

gefortuneerd (comparative gefortuneerder, superlative gefortuneerdst)

  1. (obsolete) fortunate, favored by fate
  2. possessing a fortune, very affluent
