weinig (722)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch weinich, wenich, from Old Dutch *weinag, *wēnag, from Proto-Germanic *wainagaz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛi̯nəx/
  • audio
    		(file)

Pronoun[edit]

weinig (comparative minder, superlative het minst or het minste)

  1. little, not much, not a lot
    Je hebt vandaag maar weinig gezegd. Voel je je niet goed?
    You've only said a little today. Are you not feeling well?

Adverb[edit]

weinig (comparative minder, superlative minst)

  1. little, not much
  2. not often

Synonyms[edit]

  • (not often): zelden
Synoniemen
  • gering, beetje, miniem
Verwante begrippen
  • hoeveelheid, kwantiteit, minimaal, klein
