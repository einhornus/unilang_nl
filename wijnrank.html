wijnrank (66544)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch wijnranke. Equivalent to wijn +‎ rank.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛi̯n.rɑŋk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wijn‧rank

Noun

wijnrank f (plural wijnranken, diminutive wijnrankje n)

  1. grapevine
  2. branch of a grapevine
