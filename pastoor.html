pastoor (4170)


Etymology[edit]

Noun[edit]

pastoor m (plural pastoors, diminutive pastoortje n)

  1. (Christianity, especially Roman Catholicism) parish priest
  2. (Christianity, dated) pastor, one who provides spiritual guidance
