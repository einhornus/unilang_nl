heler (17349)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦeː.lər/
  • Hyphenation: he‧ler
  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From helen (“to heal, to cure”) +‎ -er (agent noun suffix).

Noun[edit]

heler m (plural helers, diminutive helertje n, feminine heleres)

  1. healer

Etymology 2[edit]

From helen (“to deal in stolen items, to fence”) +‎ -er (agent noun suffix).

Noun[edit]

heler m (plural helers, diminutive helertje n, feminine heleres)

  1. fence (middleman for transactions of stolen goods)

  • helm
