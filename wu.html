wu (18875)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Compare German wo, Yiddish וווּ‎ (vu), Dutch waar, English where.

Adverb[edit]

wu

  1. (interrogative, relative) where
    Wu iss die Kuh?
    Where is the cow?
    Nau muss er sei Actions schtelle wu sei grosse Maul iss!
    Now he has to put his actions where his big mouth is!
