imbeciel (8033)


Etymology

Borrowing from French imbécile.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɪm.bəˈsil/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: im‧be‧ciel
  • Rhymes: -il

Adjective

imbeciel (comparative imbecieler, superlative imbecielst)

  1. (derogatory) stupid, idiotic
  2. (dated, now offensive) mentally retarded, imbecilic, idiotic (mentally disabled with the mental age of a young child)
    • 1974, Willem Wilmink, "Frekie".
      't Was een imbeciele jongen. / Een mongool.
      He was an imbecile boy. / A retard.
