Adjective[edit]

dwars (comparative dwarser, superlative meest dwars or dwarst)

  1. in a crosswise direction, transverse, at right angles
  2. slantwise, diagonal, askew
  3. rebellious, stubbornly disobedient

Synonyms[edit]

  • (rebellious): weerbarstig
  • (slantwise): scheef
