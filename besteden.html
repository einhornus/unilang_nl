besteden (8085)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch besteden, bestaden. Equivalent to be- +‎ steden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈsteːdə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧ste‧den
  • Rhymes: -eːdən

Verb

besteden

  1. (transitive) to spend
    Besteed je veel geld aan kleren?
    Do you spend much money on clothes?
  2. (transitive, obsolete) to match for a job
