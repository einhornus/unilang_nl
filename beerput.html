beerput (21414)


Etymology

Compound of beer (“liquid waste”) +‎ put (“pit, well”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeːr.pʏt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: beer‧put

Noun

beerput m (plural beerputten, diminutive beerputje n)

  1. cesspool, storage for effluent waste

Zelfstandig naamwoord
