publiceren (7935)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch publiceren, from Middle French publicer, from Latin pūblicō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌpy.bliˈseː.rə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pu‧bli‧ce‧ren
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

publiceren

  1. (transitive) to publish
    Synonym: uitgeven
  2. (transitive, obsolete) to proclaim, to announce

Related terms[edit]

  • publicatie
  • publiek

Werkwoord

publiceren
