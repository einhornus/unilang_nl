Adjective[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">achterbaks (comparative achterbakser, superlative meest achterbaks or achterbakst)

<ol>
  • shrewd, sneaky, underhand, furtive
    Hij is echt een achterbakse hond.
    He really is a sneaky bastard.

    • Usage notes[edit]

    Acting "achterbaks" is to act in a way you wouldn't expect from well-behaved people, implied is little profit or benefit for the one doing it and big loss for the one it's done to.
