zwanger (1129)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch swanger.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzʋɑ.ŋər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zwan‧ger
  • Rhymes:

Adjective[edit]

zwanger (not comparable)

  1. pregnant
    Zij is zwanger van de behanger.
    She is pregnant from the wallpaperer.

Synonyms[edit]

  • (formal) in verwachting
  • (of female animals) drachtig

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

zwanger
