staaf (16771)



Etymology

From the inflected forms of Dutch staf.[1]

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /staːf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: staaf
  • Rhymes: -aːf

Noun

staaf m (plural staven, diminutive staafje n)

  1. A bar, rod (solid metal object).
  2. A rod cell (in the retina).

Related terms

  • boekstaaf

Zelfstandig naamwoord

staaf v/m
