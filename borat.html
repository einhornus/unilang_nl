borat (36263)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch burat, from Old French bourras, from Latin burra.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /boːˈrɑt/
  • Hyphenation: bo‧rat
  • Rhymes: -ɑt

Noun

borat n (uncountable)

  1. a type of woven woollen cloth, often made from yarn of combed wool and sometimes including silk; borato

Zelfstandig naamwoord

borat o [2]

  1. (textielindustrie) grove wollen stof
Synoniemen
  • brat, sajetgaren, breigaren
