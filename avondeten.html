avondeten (4934)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch avonteten. Equivalent to avond (“evening”) +‎ eten (“meal, food”).

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈaːvɔntˌeːtə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: avond‧eten

Noun

avondeten n (uncountable)

  1. supper, dinner, evening meal

Synonyms

  • avondmaal
  • diner (more formal)

avondeten o
