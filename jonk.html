jonk (51568)



Etymology

From Dutch jong, from Old Dutch jung, from Proto-Germanic *jungaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂yuh₁n̥ḱós.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /jɔŋk/

Adjective

jonk (attributive jong, comparative jonger, superlative jongste)

  1. young
  2. recent

Zelfstandig naamwoord

jonk m
