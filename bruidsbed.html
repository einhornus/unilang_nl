bruidsbed (84525)



Etymology

Compound of bruid (“bride”) + -s- + bed (“bed”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbrœy̯ts.bɛt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bruids‧bed

Noun

bruidsbed n (plural bruidsbedden, diminutive bruidsbedje n)

  1. marriage bed, nuptial bed

Synonyms
