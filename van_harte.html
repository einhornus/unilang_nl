van_harte (9059)


Etymology[edit]

Literally, “from the heart”. The form harte derives from the old dative case form of hart.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /vɑn ˈɦɑr.tə/

Adverb[edit]

van harte

  1. with love, from the bottom of one's heart

Interjection[edit]

van harte

  1. best wishes, congratulations
