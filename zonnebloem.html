zonnebloem (36420)


Etymology

From zon +‎ -e- +‎ bloem. Named because the flower turns to keep facing the sun; compare English language">English sunflower, Spanish language">Spanish girasol. The sense "50 guilder banknote" is derived from the large image of a sunflower found on such notes.

Pronunciation

Noun

zonnebloem f (plural zonnebloemen, diminutive zonnebloemetje n or zonnebloempje n)

  1. A sunflower, flower of the genus Helianthus.
  2. A 50 guilder banknote.
