inlichten (6495)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch inlichten, from Old Dutch *inliuhten, from Proto-Germanic *inliuhtijaną. Equivalent to in (“in”) +‎ licht (“light”) +‎ -en.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɪnˌlɪx.tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: in‧lich‧ten

Verb

inlichten
