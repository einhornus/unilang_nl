Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch betogen, from togen, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch tōgen, from Proto-Germanic *ataugijaną, a derivation of *augijaną, from *augô (“eye”) (modern oog).

Verb

betogen

  1. (transitive) to demonstrate, to show
  2. (transitive) to (attempt to) produce evidence or proof
