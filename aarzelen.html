aarzelen (12869)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch erselen, arselen (“to step back, to retreat”), frequentative of ersen, from aers (“buttocks”) (modern aars). Compare English arsle.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːrzələ(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aar‧ze‧len

Verb

aarzelen

  1. (intransitive) to hesitate
