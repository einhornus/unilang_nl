bekendmaken (14661)


Etymology[edit]

From bekend +‎ maken, c.f. German bekanntmachen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bəˈkɛntmaːkə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧kend‧ma‧ken

Verb[edit]

bekendmaken

  1. (transitive) to announce, to make known
  2. (transitive) to denounce
