woning (3812)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch woninge. Equivalent to wonen (“to live, reside”) +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋoː.nɪŋ/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wo‧ning
  • Rhymes: -oːnɪŋ

Noun[edit]

woning f (plural woningen, diminutive woninkje n)

  1. flat, apartment (Can we verify(+) this sense?)
  2. house, abode, residence, dwelling

Related terms[edit]

  • wonen
  • woonst

Zelfstandig naamwoord

woning
