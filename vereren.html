vereren (15741)



Etymology

ver- +‎ eren

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -eːrən

Verb

vereren

  1. to worship
    Zij vereren er de geesten van hun voorouders. — There they worship the spirits of their ancestors.
  2. to honour
  3. to idolize
