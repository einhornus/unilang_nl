brandweerkazerne (25010)


Etymology

From brandweer (“fire department”) +‎ kazerne (“barracks, (military) quarter”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbrɑnt.ʋeːr.kaːˌzɛr.nə/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

brandweerkazerne m or f (plural brandweerkazernes or brandweerkazernen, diminutive brandweerkazernetje n)

  1. A fire station, housing a fire brigade
