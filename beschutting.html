Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch beschuttinge. Equivalent to beschutten +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bəˈsxʏ.tɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧schut‧ting
  • Rhymes: -ʏtɪŋ

Noun[edit]

beschutting f (plural beschuttingen, diminutive beschuttinkje n)

  1. shelter; protection, especially from the elements or from harm
  2. shelter; object or place that offers protection
