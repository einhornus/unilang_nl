veearts (22681)



Etymology

Compound of vee (“cattle”) +‎ arts (“-iater, medical doctor”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈveː.ɑrts/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vee‧arts

Noun

veearts m (plural veeartsen, diminutive veeartsje n)

  1. A veterinarian, mainly used for one treating farm animals

Zelfstandig naamwoord

veearts m
