Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch schricken, from Old Dutch *skrikken, from Proto-Germanic *skrikjaną.

Verb[edit]

schrikken

  1. (intransitive) to be startled, to get a scare
    Niet schrikken!
    Don't be startled!
  2. (transitive) to quench (cool rapidly by immersion)
