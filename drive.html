drive (16557)


Etymology 1

From Old Norse non">drífa, from Proto-Germanic *drībaną, cognate with Swedish driva, English drive, Dutch drijven, German treiben.

Pronunciation

Verb

drive (imperative driv, present driver, past drev, past participle drevet, c dreven, definite and plural drevne)

  1. drive
  2. run
  3. force
  4. power
  5. drift
