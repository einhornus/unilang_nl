werkster (9059)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛrks.tər/

Etymology

From werken + -ster.

Noun

werkster f (plural werksters, diminutive werkstertje n, masculine werker)

  1. maid (house cleaner)
  2. female worker

Synonyms
