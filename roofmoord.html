roofmoord (37798)


Etymology

Compound of roof (“robbery”) +‎ moord (“murder”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈroːf.moːrt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: roof‧moord

Noun

roofmoord m (plural roofmoorden)

  1. A crime involving murder and (attempted) robbery.
