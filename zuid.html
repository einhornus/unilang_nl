zuid (7753)


Etymology[edit]

From Old Dutch *sūth, from Proto-Germanic *sunþrą. Compare West Frisian súd, German Süd, English south, Danish syd.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /zœy̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zuid
  • Rhymes:

Adverb[edit]

zuid

  1. (only in compounds) south
  2. southwards

Synonyms[edit]
