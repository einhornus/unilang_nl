zuid (7753)


Etymology

From Old Dutch *sūth, from Proto-Germanic *sunþrą. Compare West Frisian súd, German Süd, English south, Danish syd.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /zœy̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zuid
  • Rhymes: -œy̯t

Adverb

zuid

  1. (only in compounds) south
  2. southwards

Synonyms

  • zuidwaarts
Verwante begrippen
  • oost, west, oosten, westen
