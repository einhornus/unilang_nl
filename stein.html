stein (17285)


Etymology

From a regional use[1] of German Stein (“stone”). Probably a clipping of Steingut (“stoneware”) or Steinkrug (“stone pitcher”). Compare Old English stǣna (“stone jug, a pot of stone or earth”). More at stean.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /staɪn/

      		(file)
    Rhymes: -aɪn

Noun

stein (plural steins)
