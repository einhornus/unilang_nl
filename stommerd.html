stommerd (16614)


Etymology[edit]

From stom (“mute, stupid”) +‎ -erd (“-ard”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɔ.mərt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stom‧merd

Noun[edit]

stommerd m (plural stommerds, diminutive stommerdje n)

  1. (chiefly childish) A dumbo, a stupid person, a stupid-head.

Synonyms[edit]

  • stommeling
  • stommerik
