onrust (9031)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch onruste, equivalent to on- (“un-”) +‎ rust (“rest”). Compare English unrest.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔn.rʏst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧rust

Noun[edit]

onrust f or m (plural onrusten, diminutive onrustje n)

  1. (uncountable) unrest (state of agitation)
  2. unrest, upheaval (agitated situation)
  3. a constantly moving piece in a mechanical clock

Synonyms[edit]

  • (unrest): rumoer, oproer
