stalknecht (30911)



Etymology

From stal (“stable”) +‎ knecht (“servant, employee”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɑl.knɛxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stal‧knecht

Noun

stalknecht m (plural stalknechten, diminutive stalknechtje n)

  1. stablehand, hostler, employee working in a stable
    Een goede stalknecht weet alles van paarden en is daarmee goud waard.
    A good stablehand knows everything about horses and is therefore as good as gold.
    Synonym: palfrenier

Zelfstandig naamwoord

stalknecht m

  1. (beroep) iemand die paarden verzorgt en de stal netjes houdt
Synoniemen
  • groom
  • paardenknecht
  • stalbediende
