onnozel (8824)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch onnosel, from on- (“not”) +‎ nosel (“dangerous”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɔˈnoː.zəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧no‧zel

Adjective

onnozel (comparative onnozeler, superlative onnozelst)

  1. (originally now rare) innocent
  2. naive, gullible
  3. silly, goofy
  4. dumb, stupid
  5. senseless, out of one's mind
