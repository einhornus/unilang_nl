lader (34990)


Etymology

From laden +‎ -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlaː.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: la‧der
  • Rhymes: -aːdər

Noun

lader m (plural laders)

  1. One who loads something.
  2. An electrical charger for a battery.
    Synonyms: batterijlader, oplader

Zelfstandig naamwoord

lader m [1]
