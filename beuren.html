beuren (22307)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bueren, from Old Dutch *burien, from Proto-Germanic *burjaną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbøːrə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: beu‧ren
  • Rhymes: -øːrən

Verb

beuren

  1. (transitive) to lift, to raise
  2. (transitive) to collect (debt, owed money)
  3. (transitive, poetic) to receive
  4. (intransitive) to happen, to occur
  5. (transitive) to befall
