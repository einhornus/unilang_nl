shampoo (11122)

Borrowed from English shampoo, from Hindi चाँपो (cā̃po).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʃɑm.poː/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

shampoo m (plural shampoos, diminutive shampootje n)
