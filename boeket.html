boeket (14656)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch boeckeetken, from Middle French bouquet, from Old French bochet.[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /buˈkɛt/
  • Audio

Noun[edit]

boeket n or m (plural boeketten, diminutive boeketje n)

  1. bouquet (of flowers)
    Synonyms: handruiker, ruiker
  2. aroma (e.g. of wine or cigar)
