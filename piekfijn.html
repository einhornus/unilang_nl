Pronunciation

Etymology

Borrowed from German piekfein, from Low German püükfein, piekfein, wherein the former element is itself borrowed from Dutch puik.

Adjective

piekfijn (comparative piekfijner, superlative piekfijnst)

  1. mint, brand new
  2. elegant, very neat
