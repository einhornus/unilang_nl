gezet (1134)



Etymology

Adjective

gezet (comparative gezetter, superlative gezetst)

  1. fixed, set, determined (said of mortgage terms, timeframe of a plan, etc)
  2. stout, stocky; (euphemistic or ironic) obese, overweight
