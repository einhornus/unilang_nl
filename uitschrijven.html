uitschrijven (15599)


Etymology

uit +‎ schrijven

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

uitschrijven

  1. to disqualify, invalidate (e.g. membership)
  2. to completely write down (e.g. a lesson, a passage of text)
  3. to write out (a cheque)
  4. to call out (a meeting), summon
  5. to give one the opportunity to participate in
