Etymology 1

Uncertain. Perhaps related to Dutch sudderen (“to simmer”), or to Middle Dutch sōren (“to sour, become acidic”).

Verb

zeuren

  1. (intransitive) to whine, to complain in an unpleasant manner
  2. (intransitive) to persist
