hulpeloos (7719)


Etymology[edit]

hulp (“help”) +‎ -e- +‎ -loos (“-less”)

Pronunciation[edit]

Adjective[edit]

hulpeloos (comparative hulpelozer, superlative meest hulpeloos or hulpeloost)

  1. helpless

hulpeloos
