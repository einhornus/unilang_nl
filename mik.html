Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Medieval Latin mīcha, from Latin mīca (“crumb”).

Noun[edit]

mik m (plural mikken, diminutive mikje n)

  1. loaf of bread
  2. (informal) mouth, mug
  3. (informal) stomach (organ)
    Synonym: maag
  4. (nautical) pump-cheek
