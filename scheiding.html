scheiding (2906)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From scheiden +‎ -ing. Cognate to English shedding.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsxɛi̯.dɪŋ/
  • scheiding
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schei‧ding
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯dɪŋ

Noun[edit]

scheiding f (plural scheidingen, diminutive scheidinkje n)

  1. (between objects) separation
  2. A divorce.
    • 1992, A. F. Th. van der Heijden, Weerborstels, Em. Querido's Uitgeverij, page 36:
      'Ze liggen in scheiding,' hoorde ik geregeld fluisteren in de familie.
      'They are in the process of divorce ', I often heard family members whisper.
  3. A parting, a part (hairstyle).

Synonyms[edit]

  • (divorce): echtscheiding
