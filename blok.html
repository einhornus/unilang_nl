Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch blok, from Old Dutch *blok, from Proto-Germanic *blukką.

Noun[edit]

blok n (plural blokken, diminutive blokje n)

  1. a block (e.g wood)
  2. a street block
  3. a political bloc

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

blok

  2. imperative of blokken
