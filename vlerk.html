vlerk (37602)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vlerc, from Proto-West Germanic *feþerik, *feþerak (“wing”), from *feþru (“feather”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vlɛrk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vlerk
  • Rhymes: -ɛrk

Noun

vlerk m or f (plural vlerken, diminutive vlerkje n)

  1. wing
  2. scoundrel

Synonyms

  • (wing): vleugel

Zelfstandig naamwoord

vlerk m, v

  1. brutaal, onbeleefd en meestal jong persoon

  2. vleugel
  3. (informeel) hand
