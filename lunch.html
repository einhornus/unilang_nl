lunch (1564)


Etymology

Borrowed from English lunch.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lʏnʃ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lunch

Noun

lunch m (plural lunchen or lunches, diminutive lunchje n)

  1. A lunch, a meal eaten around noon

Synonyms

  • middageten n

Related terms

  • brunchen (verb)
  • lunchroom

Verb

lunch

  1. first-person singular present indicative of lunchen
  2. imperative of lunchen
