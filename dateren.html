dateren (32717)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French dater.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /daːˈteːrə(n)/
  • dateren
    		(file)

Verb

dateren

  1. (transitive) to date, determine the age (of)
  2. (intransitive) to date (from), belong to an age

dateren
