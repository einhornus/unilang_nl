kaffer (26183)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Arabic كَافِر‎ (kāfir, “infidel”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɑ.fər/
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

kaffer m (plural kaffers, diminutive kaffertje n)

  1. (originally) A non-believer or an infidel from the Muslim viewpoint
  2. (offensive) A black person, notably a (Southern African) Bantu.
  3. (offensive) A stupid person, numskull

Synonyms[edit]

  • (black person): neger, zwarte, nikker
  • (stupid person): (steen)ezel m, uilskuiken n

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kaffer

  1. (Jiddisch-Hebreeuws) (scheldwoord) (pejoratief) lomperd, stommeling
  2. zwarte Zuid-Afrikaan (vroeger), Bantoe in Zuid-Afrika
