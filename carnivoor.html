carnivoor (42484)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin carnivorus (“flesh-eating”), itself from carō (“flesh, meat”) + vorāre (“to devour”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌkɑrniˈvoːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: car‧ni‧voor
  • Rhymes: -oːr

Noun

carnivoor m (plural carnivoren, diminutive carnivoortje n)

  1. carnivore, meat-eating creature

Synonyms

  • vleeseter

Related terms

  • herbivoor
  • insectivoor
  • omnivoor
