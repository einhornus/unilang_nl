actualiseren (134311)



Etymology

Borrowed from French actualiser.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑk.ty.aː.liˈzeː.rə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ac‧tu‧a‧li‧se‧ren
  • Rhymes: -eːrən

Verb

actualiseren

  1. (transitive) to update (to make something up to date)
    Synonym: bijwerken
  2. (transitive) to modernise (to adapt something to modern times)
  3. (ergative, philosophy) to actualise (to make or become actual, real or realised)
