aftands (55165)



Etymology

From af +‎ tand +‎ -s.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɑfˈtɑnts/
  • Hyphenation: af‧tands
  • Rhymes: -ɑnts

Adjective

aftands (comparative aftandser, superlative meest aftands or aftandst)

  1. run-down, dilapidated
  2. old, (almost) losing one's teeth
  3. (obsolete, of horses) having fully grown teeth
