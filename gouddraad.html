gouddraad (105963)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch goutdraet. Equivalent to goud +‎ draad.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɑu̯t.draːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: goud‧draad

Noun

gouddraad m (plural gouddraden, diminutive gouddraadje n)

  1. gold thread (object)

Noun

gouddraad m or n (uncountable)

  1. gold thread (material)
