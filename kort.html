Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch cort, from Old Dutch kurt, from Proto-West Germanic *kurt, an early borrowing from Latin curtus.

Adjective[edit]

kort (comparative korter, superlative kortst)

  1. short

Verb[edit]

kort
