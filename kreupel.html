kreupel (11489)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch cruepel (“lacking, missing”), from Old Dutch *krupil, from Proto-Germanic *krupilaz. Cognate with German Krüppel, English cripple.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkrøːpəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

kreupel (comparative kreupeler, superlative kreupelst)

  1. crippled; lame
