proviand (16638)



Etymology

Borrowed from German Proviant, in turn borrowed from Italian provianda.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌproː.viˈɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pro‧vi‧and
  • Rhymes: -ɑnt

Noun

proviand f or n (plural provianden)

  1. food supplies, edible provision, in particular when travelling

Synonyms

  • mondvoorraad
