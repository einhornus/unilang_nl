Verb[edit]

ben

  1. first-person singular present indicative of zijn
  2. (dialectal) imperative of zijn

Usage notes[edit]

Ben, as an imperative, is considered non-standard, the standard form being wees.

Synonyms[edit]

  • (imperative) wees
