absolutie (23939)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch absolucie, from Latin absolūtiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑp.soːˈly.(t)si/
  • Hyphenation: ab‧so‧lu‧tie

Noun[edit]

absolutie f (uncountable)

  1. (Roman Catholicism) absolution, absolvement of sins by a clerical authority
  2. (obsolete, law) absolution, pardoning

Zelfstandig naamwoord

absolutie

  1. het vergeven van de zonden
