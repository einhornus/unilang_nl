aandringen (11826)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch aendringen. Equivalent to aan +‎ dringen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːnˌdrɪŋə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧drin‧gen

Verb

aandringen

  1. (intransitive) to insist, to press on
  2. (intransitive, obsolete) to advance, to press on
