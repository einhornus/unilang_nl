Etymology 1[edit]

From blok +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

blokken

  1. (intransitive) to cram, to study hard, to swot
    Het examen is overmorgen, ik zit dus hard te blokken.
    The exam is day after tomorrow, so I am cramming like mad.
