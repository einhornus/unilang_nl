beddegoed (95475)



Etymology

Compound of bed +‎ -e- +‎ goed.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛ.dəˌɣut/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bed‧de‧goed

Noun

beddegoed n (uncountable)

  1. Superseded spelling of beddengoed.: bedding (sheets, blankets etc.)

Usage notes

  • The spelling beddegoed was deprecated in 1996 in the new Groene Boekje (“Little Green Book”) spelling reform.
