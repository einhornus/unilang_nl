chinezen (5523)


Etymology[edit]

From Chinees +‎ -en.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʃiˈneːzə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: chi‧ne‧zen

Verb[edit]

chinezen

  1. (intransitive) to dine out at a Chinese(-Indonesian) restaurant.
  2. (intransitive, slang) to chase the dragon, to inhale the vapour from heated heroin

Werkwoord

chinezen
