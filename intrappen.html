intrappen (17023)


Etymology[edit]

From in (“in”) +‎ trappen (“to kick”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

intrappen

  1. To kick in.
    Trap die deur in! - Kick in that door!
  2. (colloquial) To put in.
    Trap die kussens even in de bus. - Put those cushions in the truck, would you?
