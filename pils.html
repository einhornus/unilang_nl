pils (24812)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Pils, short for Pilsener (Bier).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pɪls/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pils
  • Rhymes: -ɪls

Noun

pils m or n (plural pilsen, diminutive pilsje n)

  1. pilsner beer
  2. (by extension) lager beer

Related terms

  • pilsener

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pils m / o

Synoniemen
