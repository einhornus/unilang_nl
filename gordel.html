gordel (4921)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch gorden, from Old Dutch gurden, from Proto-Germanic *gurdijaną.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɔr.dəl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gor‧del

Noun[edit]

gordel m (plural gordels, diminutive gordeltje n)

  1. belt, a (typically leather) band worn around the waist
  2. belt, roughly belt-shaped area
  3. seat belt
    Synonyms: autogordel, autoriem, riem

Synonyms[edit]

  • (band): riem
