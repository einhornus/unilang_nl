synthetiseren (112472)



Etymology

From French synthétiser (from synthèse (“synthesis”), ultimately from Ancient Greek), cognate with English synthesize and German synthetisieren.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

synthetiseren

  1. (transitive) To synthesize, make a synthesis, summarise
  2. (transitive) To (re)produce synthetically, e.g. using substitute ingredients
