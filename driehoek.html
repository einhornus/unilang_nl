driehoek (13203)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch driehoec, calque of Latin triangulum. Equivalent to drie +‎ hoek.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdri.ɦuk/

Noun[edit]

driehoek m (plural driehoeken, diminutive driehoekje n)

  1. triangle

Zelfstandig naamwoord

driehoek
