straatroof (62852)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch straetroof. Equivalent to straat +‎ roof.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstraːt.roːf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: straat‧roof

Noun

straatroof m (plural straatroven, diminutive straatroofje n)

  1. street robbery
