Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch tāken, from Old Dutch *takan, from Proto-Germanic *takaną (“to touch”).

Verb[edit]

taken

  1. (archaic, dialectal) to take, to grasp
  2. (archaic, dialectal) to touch
