Noun

clarion (plural clarions)

  1. (historical, music) A medieval brass instrument, related to the trumpet, or its sound.
    Synonym: claro
    The clarion's call to action has been heard.
  2. (heraldry) A different type of musical instrument resembling an organ.

clarion (third-person singular simple present clarions, present participle clarioning, simple past and past participle past|and|past|ptcp-form-of" lang="en">clarioned)
