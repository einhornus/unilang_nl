doodsbang (3255)


Etymology[edit]

From dood (“death”) +‎ -s- +‎ bang (“scared”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /doːtsˈbɑŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: doods‧bang

Adjective[edit]

doodsbang (not comparable)

  1. (literally) fearing death, scared of dying
  2. (by extension) scared to death, gravely anguished

Related terms[edit]

  • doodsverachting
  • doodsangst
