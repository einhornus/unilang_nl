rechtvaardig (9026)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌrɛxtˈfaːr.dəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: recht‧vaar‧dig

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch rechtverdich. Equivalent to recht (“justice”) +‎ vaart +‎ ig (“-y”).

Adjective[edit]

rechtvaardig (comparative rechtvaardiger, superlative rechtvaardigst)

  1. fair, just
    De doctrine van de rechtvaardige oorlog — The doctrine of justified war

Verb[edit]

rechtvaardig
