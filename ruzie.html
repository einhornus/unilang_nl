ruzie (1113)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈry.zi/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ru‧zie
  • IPA(key): /yzi/

Etymology 1[edit]

From ruze, Eastern variant of ruis.

Noun[edit]

ruzie f (plural ruzies, diminutive ruzietje n)

  1. quarrel, row, conflict, dispute

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

ruzie

  1. first-person singular present indicative of ruziën
  2. imperative of ruziën
