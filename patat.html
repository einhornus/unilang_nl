patat (8417)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French patate, from Spanish patata, probably from Taíno batata.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /paːˈtɑt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pa‧tat
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

patat f (plural patatten, diminutive patatje n)

  1. (Northern, uncountable) chips, french fries
  2. (Southern, countable) a potato

Synonyms[edit]

  • (chips/fries): friet
  • (potato): aardappel

Related terms[edit]

  • tatta

Zelfstandig naamwoord

patat

  1. (voeding) Noord-Nederlandse benaming voor een gerecht of snack van gefrituurde aardappelreepjes ('patat frites')
  2. (voeding) (elders) aardappel
