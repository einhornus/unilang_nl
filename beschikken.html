beschikken (14065)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch beschicken. Equivalent to be- +‎ schikken.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈsxɪkə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧schik‧ken
  • Rhymes: -ɪkən

Verb

beschikken

  1. (transitive) to direct, have influence over, decide
  2. (intransitive) to have at one's disposal [+ over (object)]
