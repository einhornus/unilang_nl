Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch inval (“inspiration, sudden idea”).

Noun[edit]

inval m (plural invallen, diminutive invalletje n)

  1. inspiration, brain wave
  2. bust, raid
  3. incursion, invasion
  4. incidence
