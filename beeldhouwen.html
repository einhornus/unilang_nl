    Usage notes[edit]

    The verb is a backformation from a noun "beeldhouwer" meaning "sculptor", which is why it follows a weak verb inflection pattern as opposed to class 7 strong verb pattern that the base verb, houwen (hieuw/gehouwen), belongs to.
