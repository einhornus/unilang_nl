renner (16481)


Etymology

From rennen (“to run”) +‎ -er.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

renner m (plural renners, diminutive rennertje n)

  1. runner, one who runs
  2. racer; especially short for a competitive wielrenner 'cyclist'

Synonyms

  • (one who runs) loper m

Zelfstandig naamwoord

renner m
