roddelpers (29689)



Etymology

Compound of roddel or roddelen +‎ pers.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɔ.dəlˌpɛrs/
  • Hyphenation: rod‧del‧pers

Noun

roddelpers f (uncountable)

  1. gossip press (gossip magazines, tabloids, etc.)
