bandeloos (95549)



Etymology

From band (“bound”) +‎ -loos (“less”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɑn.dəˌloːs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ban‧de‧loos

Adjective

bandeloos (comparative bandelozer, superlative meest bandeloos or bandeloost)

  1. unrestrained, boundless
    Synonyms: ongebreideld, teugelloos
  2. immoral, without moral restraints, indulgent

Usage notes

Not to be confused with without tires, without ties.
