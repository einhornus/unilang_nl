Verb[edit]

herleven

  1. (intransitive, literally) To come to life again, revive
  2. (intransitive, figuratively) To reappear
  3. (intransitive, figuratively) To return, rise again
  4. (intransitive, figuratively) To recover strength or vitality
  5. (intransitive, figuratively) To be renewed
  6. (transitive, literally) To relive, experience again
