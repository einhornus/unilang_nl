amulet (8769)


Etymology

Borrowed from French amulette, from Middle French amulete, from Latin amulētum.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌaː.myˈlɛt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: amu‧let
  • Rhymes: -ɛt

Noun

amulet f or m (plural amuletten, diminutive amuletje n)

  1. amulet, talisman

Synonyms

  • talisman

Zelfstandig naamwoord
