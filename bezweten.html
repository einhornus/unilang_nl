bezweten (119284)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch besweten. Equivalent to be- +‎ zweten.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈzʋeː.tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧zwe‧ten
  • Rhymes: -eːtən

Verb

bezweten

  1. (transitive, uncommon) to cover in sweat (usually due to exertion)

Usage notes

Conjugated forms are very rare, apart from the past participle bezweet (“sweaty”).
