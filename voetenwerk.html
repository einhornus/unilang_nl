voetenwerk (21177)


Etymology

From voeten +‎ werk.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

voetenwerk n (uncountable)

  1. Someone's footwork; notably the lower limb 'dexterity' of a football (l.s.) player

Related terms

  • voetkus
  • handwerk
