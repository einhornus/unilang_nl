Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch lucht, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch *luft, from Proto-Germanic *luftuz.

Noun

lucht f (plural luchten, diminutive luchtje n)

  1. air (mixture of gases)
  2. sky
  3. odour, smell
