rationeel (11127)


Etymology

Borrowed from French rationnel, from Latin ratiōnālis.

Pronunciation

  • (Netherlands) IPA(key): /ˌraː.(t)ʃoːˈneːl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ra‧ti‧o‧neel
  • Rhymes: -eːl

Adjective

rationeel (comparative rationeler, superlative rationeelst)

  1. rational, reasonable (pertaining to reason, by means of reason)
  2. rational (pertaining to ratios, fractional)
    Synonym: rationaal
