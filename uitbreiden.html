uitbreiden (10843)


Etymology[edit]

From uit (“out”) +‎ breed (“broad”) +‎ -en.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

uitbreiden

  1. to extend, to expand
  2. (figuratively) to escalate, to become more serious

Related terms[edit]

  • uitgebreid
