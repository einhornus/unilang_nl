knaagdier (22808)


Etymology

From knagen (“to gnaw”) +‎ dier (“animal”), perhaps through German Nagetier, eventually after Latin rodens.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈknaːxdiːr/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: knaag‧dier

Noun

knaagdier n (plural knaagdieren, diminutive knaagdiertje n)

  1. rodent, any member of the order Rodentia
