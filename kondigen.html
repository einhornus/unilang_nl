kondigen (13279)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch condigen, from condich, from cont (modern kond). Equivalent to konde +‎ -igen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɔndəɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

kondigen
