bekopen (34395)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch becôpen. Equivalent to be- +‎ kopen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈkoːpə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧ko‧pen
  • Rhymes: -oːpən

Verb

bekopen

  1. (transitive) to pay for, to suffer punishment for
  2. (transitive, now only in the passive) to swindle, to hustle
