dynamiet (6008)


Etymology

Coined by Nobel, the inventor. Ultimately from Ancient Greek δύναμις (dúnamis, “power”) +‎ -iet, possibly under the influence of dynamo or dynamisch.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dy‧na‧miet

Noun

dynamiet n (uncountable)

  1. dynamite

Related terms

dynamiet o
