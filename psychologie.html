Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French psychologie, from Latin psychologia (coined by Marko Marulić from Ancient Greek ψυχή (psukhḗ, “soul”) + Latin -logia (“study of”)). Equivalent to psycho- +‎ -logie.

psychologie
