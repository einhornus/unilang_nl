zeemacht (44279)


Etymology

Compound of zee +‎ macht.

Pronunciation

  • zeemacht
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.mɑxt/
  • Hyphenation: zee‧macht

Noun

zeemacht f (plural zeemachten, diminutive zeemachtje n)

  1. A navy (sea force).
    Synonym: marine
  2. A naval power, a country or organisation with considerable military power at sea.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zeemacht v/m

  1. (scheepvaart) (militair) krijgsmacht ter zee
  2. zeemogendheid
Synoniemen
  • marine
