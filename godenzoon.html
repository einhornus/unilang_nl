godenzoon (106030)



Etymology

Compound of god + -en- + zoon.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣoː.də(n)ˌzoːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: go‧den‧zoon

Noun

godenzoon m (plural godenzonen, feminine godendochter)

  1. (in polytheistic contexts) A male demigod, hero.
  2. (sports) A male sports champion.
