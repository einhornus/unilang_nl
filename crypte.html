crypte (19034)


Etymology

Ultimately from Latin crypta, from Ancient Greek κρυπτός (kruptós). Doublet of krocht. Doublet of grot. Doublet of gruft.

Pronunciation

Noun

crypte f (plural cryptes, diminutive cryptetje n)

  1. crypt
    Synonyms: krocht, graftombe
