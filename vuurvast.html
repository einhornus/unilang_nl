vuurvast (97216)



Etymology

Compound of vuur (“fire”) + vast (“solid, resistant, steady”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vyːrˈvɑst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vuur‧vast
  • Rhymes: -ɑst

Adjective

vuurvast (comparative vuurvaster, superlative meest vuurvast or vuurvastst)

  1. fire-proof
  2. (by extension) with fairly great heat tolerance; fit for use in cooking, refractory, ovenproof
