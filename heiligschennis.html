heiligschennis (18428)


Etymology

From heilig (“holy”) +‎ schennis (“desecration”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛi̯.ləxˌsxɛ.nɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hei‧lig‧schen‧nis

Noun

heiligschennis f (plural heiligschennissen, diminutive heiligschennisje n)

  1. sacrilege, desecration

Related terms

  • heiligschenner

Zelfstandig naamwoord
