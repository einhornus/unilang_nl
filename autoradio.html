autoradio (39832)


Etymology

Compound of auto (“car”) +‎ radio (“radio, tuner”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑu̯.toːˌraː.di.oː/, /ˈoː.toːˌraː.di.oː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: au‧to‧ra‧dio

Noun

autoradio m (plural autoradio's, diminutive autoradiootje n)

  1. car radio
