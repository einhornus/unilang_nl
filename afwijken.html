afwijken (30086)


Etymology

From af +‎ wijken.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfʋɛi̯kə(n)/
  • audio

Verb

afwijken

  1. (intransitive, dated) to go away, to leave [+ van (from)]
  2. (intransitive) to deviate
  3. (intransitive) to differ
