kornuit (104138)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch cornuut, from Old French cornut, from Latin cornūtus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kɔrˈnœy̯t/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kor‧nuit
  • Rhymes: -œy̯t

Noun

kornuit m (plural kornuiten, diminutive kornuitje n)

  1. chum, companion, mate (usually a man)
  2. (obsolete) cuckold
    Synonym: hoorndrager
