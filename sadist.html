sadist (18923)


Etymology

From the name of the Marquis de Sade; see sadism.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈseɪdɪst/, /ˈsædist/
  • Rhymes: -eɪdɪst, -ædɪst

Noun

sadist (plural sadists)

  1. One who derives pleasure through cruelty or pain to others.

Related terms

  • sadism
  • sadomasochist
