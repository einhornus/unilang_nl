omzien (63719)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch ommesien. Equivalent to om +‎ zien.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔm.zin/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: omzien

Verb

omzien

  1. (intransitive) to look back [+ naar (to)]
  2. (intransitive) to look after [+ naar (object)] (to care for/about)
    Synonym: omkijken
  3. (intransitive, dated) to look around [+ naar (object)]
