muntstuk (24453)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Compound of munt (“coin, currency”) +‎ stuk (“piece”). First attested in the seventeenth century.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈmʏnt.stʏk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: munt‧stuk

Noun[edit]

muntstuk n (plural muntstukken, diminutive muntstukje n)

  1. A coin, metallic piece of cash

Related terms[edit]

  • munteenheid
  • muntslag
  • stukgoed n
