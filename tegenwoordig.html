tegenwoordig (1833)


Etymology[edit]

From tegen (“against”) +‎ worden (“to become”) +‎ -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

tegenwoordig (not comparable)

  1. present, current

Adverb[edit]

tegenwoordig

  1. nowadays, presently

Synonyms[edit]

  • huidig
  • actueel

  2. aanwezig
