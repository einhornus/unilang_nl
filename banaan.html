banaan (7561)

Noun

banaan m or f (plural bananen, diminutive banaantje n)

  1. banana
    Synonym: bacove
  2. (Surinam) plantain
    Synonym: bakbanaan
  3. (offensive, ethnic slur) black person, person of African heritage
