hersenschudding (8856)


Etymology[edit]

hersen +‎ schudding: root of hersenen 'brain(s)' + schudding 'a shake' (from schudden 'to shake' + -ing)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio

  • Hyphenation: her‧sen‧schud‧ding

Noun[edit]

hersenschudding f (plural hersenschuddingen, diminutive hersenschuddingetje n or hersenschuddinkje n)

  1. The medical condition concussion
