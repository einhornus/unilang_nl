zondag (1866)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sondach, sonnendach, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch sunnadag (1151-1200; Reimbibel), from late Proto-Germanic *sunnōniz dagaz (“day of the sun”), a calque of Latin dies Solis. Compare Low German">Low German Sünndag, German Sonntag, West Frisian language">West Frisian snein, English language">English Sunday, Danish da">søndag.
