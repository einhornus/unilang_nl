egel (33428)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch eghel, from Old Dutch *igil, from Proto-Germanic *igilaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁eǵʰis (“hedgehog”). Echel (“leech”) is commonly thought to be related to this term.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈeːɣəl/
  • audio

  • Rhymes: -eːɣəl

Noun

egel m (plural egels, diminutive egeltje n)
