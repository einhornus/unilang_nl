Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French tarif, from Middle French <small>[Term?], from Italian tariffa or Spanish tarifa, from Ottoman Turkish تعرفه‎ (ta'rife), from Persian تعرفه‎ (ta'refe), from Arabic تَعْرِفَة‎ (taʿrifa).

Noun[edit]

tarief n (plural tarieven, diminutive tariefje n)

  1. fee, rate
  2. (somewhat dated) tariff
    Synonym: importheffing
  3. (dated) list of prices or rates
