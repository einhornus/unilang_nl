microdot (68738)



Etymology

micro- +‎ dot

Noun

microdot (plural microdots)

  1. A text or photographic image that has been reduced in size to that of a typographical dot in order to escape detection by unintended recipients.
  2. (informal) A small-sized tablet containing lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD).

Verb

microdot (third-person singular simple present microdots, present participle microdotting, simple past and past participle microdotted)

  1. (transitive) To make a microdot of.
