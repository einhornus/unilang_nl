lacherig (103964)



Etymology

From lachen + -erig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɑ.xə.rəx/
  • Hyphenation: la‧che‧rig

Adjective

lacherig (comparative lacheriger, superlative lacherigst)

  1. Prone to or characterised by laughing, particularly in a ridiculing manner.
