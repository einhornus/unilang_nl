gastvrouw (12776)


Etymology[edit]

Compound of gast +‎ vrouw.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɑst.frɑu̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gast‧vrouw

Noun[edit]

gastvrouw f (plural gastvrouwen, diminutive gastvrouwtje n)

  1. A hostess (at a party or a venue).

Usage notes[edit]

This word is not used for air hostesses; stewardess is used for that.
