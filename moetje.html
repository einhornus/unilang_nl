Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

moetje n (plural moetjes)

  1. a must (necessity)
    Synonym: must
  2. (especially) something that is necessary but not desired
  3. a shotgun wedding (marriage contracted to legitimise a premarital pregnancy)

Noun[edit]
