gewiekst (34208)


Etymology

19th century. Borrowed from German gewichst.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈʋikst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧wiekst

Adjective

gewiekst (comparative gewiekster, superlative meest gewiekst or gewiekstst)

  1. smart, sly, cunning, clever
    Ook op dit terrein hebben gewiekste handelslui een gat in de markt ontdekt.
    Also in this area sly salesmen have found a gap in the market.

Usage notes

  • This word normally implies acting out of self-interest, and often praise for marketing or merchant's skills.
