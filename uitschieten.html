uitschieten (55671)



Etymology

From uit + schieten

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

uitschieten

  1. to shoot out
  2. to eject, to throw (out)
  3. (especially of plants) to grow rapidly
  4. (clothing) to take off quickly
  5. (figurative) to be extraordinary, to excel beyond the average
