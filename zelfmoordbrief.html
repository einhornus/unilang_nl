zelfmoordbrief (37538)



Etymology

Compound of zelfmoord (“suicide”) + brief (“note”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɛlf.moːrtˌbrif/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zelf‧moord‧brief

Noun

zelfmoordbrief m (plural zelfmoordbrieven, diminutive zelfmoordbriefje n)

  1. suicide note
