handel (2280)


Etymology

From handelen. First attested in the 16th century.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɑn.dəl/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: han‧del
  • Rhymes: -ɑndəl

Noun

handel m (uncountable, diminutive handeltje n)

  1. trade (activity of trading)
  2. business, company (commercial entity)
    Synonyms: bedrijf, toko, zaak
  3. goods, things
    Hij liet de hele handel op de grond vallen.
    He dropped everything on the floor.
  4. shop, store (merchant's business)
    Synonyms: toko, winkel

Related terms

  • handelen

Verb

handel

  1. first-person singular present indicative of handelen
  2. imperative of handelen
