grof (4168)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch grof, from Old Dutch *grof, from Proto-Germanic *grubaz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣrɔf/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: grof
  • Rhymes: -ɔf

Adjective

grof (comparative grover or groffer, superlative grofst)

  1. rough, coarse
  2. rude
  3. extreme, gross, grave
