hersenspoeling (34173)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From hersenen +‎ spoeling, calque of English brain-washing, itself a calque of Chinese 洗腦／洗脑 (xǐnǎo).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛr.sə(n)ˌspu.lɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: her‧sen‧spoe‧ling

Noun[edit]

hersenspoeling f (plural hersenspoelingen, diminutive hersenspoelinkje n)

  1. brain-washing
    Ze zijn gehersenspoeld om in hersenspoeling te geloven.
    They have been brainwashed into believing in brainwashing.

Related terms[edit]

  • hersenspoelen
