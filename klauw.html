klauw (10666)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch clauwe, from Old Dutch *klawa, from Proto-Germanic *klawō. Compare German Klaue, English claw, Danish klo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /klɑu̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klauw
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

klauw f (plural klauwen, diminutive klauwtje n)

  1. claw
  2. (informal) hand
