boomkikker (119075)



Etymology

Compound of boom (“tree”) +‎ kikker (“frog”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈboːmˌkɪ.kər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boom‧kik‧ker

Noun

boomkikker m (plural boomkikkers, diminutive boomkikkertje n)

  1. tree frog, any frog of the family Hylidae
    Synonyms: boomkikvors, boomvors
  2. European tree frog, Hyla arborea, as an individual or as a species

Zelfstandig naamwoord

boomkikker m

  1. (amfibieën) Hyla arborea , klein kikkertje dat zich in bomen en heesters kan begeven
