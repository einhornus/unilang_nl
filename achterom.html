Adverb[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">achterom

<ol>
  • around the back
    Heb je al áchterom gekéken? - Have you looked around the house yet?
  • backwards over the shoulder
    Heb je al achterómgekeken? - Have you looked over your shoulder yet?
    Ze keek achterom. - She looked back.

    • Usage notes[edit]

    In the first meaning the word is a separate adverb of location, in the second a separable prepositional adverb that can be attached to certain verbs. Used as a preposition it is written in two parts:

    Achter het huis om - Around the back of the house.
    Loop achter om het huis! - Come around the back!
