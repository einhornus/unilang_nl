Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch sōmer, from Old Dutch *sumar, from Proto-Germanic *sumaraz.

Noun[edit]

zomer m (plural zomers, diminutive zomertje n)

  1. summer

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb[edit]

zomer

  1. first-person singular present indicative of zomeren
  2. imperative of zomeren
