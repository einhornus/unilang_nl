Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch hāmer, from Old Dutch *hamar, from Proto-Germanic *hamaraz.

Noun[edit]

hamer m (plural hamers, diminutive hamertje n)

  1. hammer

hamer

  2. imperative of hameren
