aria (29466)



Etymology

Metathesis from Latin āera, Greek-type accusative of āēr, from Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”). See also aere.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈa.rja/, [ˈäːr̺jä]
  • Audio (l'aria)

  • Audio

Noun

aria f (plural arie)

  1. air
  2. look, appearance, countenance
  3. (plural only) airs
  4. wind (all senses)
  5. (music) aria, song
