Etymology[edit]

Metathesis from Latin āera, Greek-type accusative of āēr, from Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”). See also aere.

Noun[edit]

aria f (plural arie)

  1. air
  2. look, appearance, countenance
  3. (plural only) airs
  4. wind (all senses)
  5. (music) aria, song
