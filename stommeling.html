stommeling (5991)


Etymology

From stom (“stupid”) +‎ -e- +‎ -ling.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɔməlɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stom‧me‧ling

Noun

stommeling m (plural stommelingen, diminutive stommelingetje n)

  1. A dumb-ass, dunce, duffer, stupid person

Synonyms

  • dommerik m
  • domoor m
  • dwaas m
  • idioot m
  • stomkop m

Related terms

  • stomheid
