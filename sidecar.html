sidecar (79628)



Etymology

side + car

Noun

sidecar (plural sidecars)

  1. A one-wheeled attachment to a motorcycle to allow for a separate seat for a passenger or cargo space.
  2. A cocktail made with cognac (or brandy), triple sec liqueur, and lemon juice.
