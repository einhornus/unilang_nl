lijf (2467)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch lijf, from Old Dutch līf, from Proto-Germanic *lībą.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /lɛi̯f/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

lijf n (plural lijven, diminutive lijfje n)

  1. body
    Synonyms: lichaam, lijk
  2. corsage
  3. (algebra, in the Netherlands) field
  4. (algebra, in Belgium) division ring
