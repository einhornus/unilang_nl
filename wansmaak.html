wansmaak (78189)



Etymology

From wan- +‎ smaak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɑn.smaːk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wan‧smaak

Noun

wansmaak m (uncountable)

  1. poor aesthetic taste
  2. (dated) a bad taste in one's mouth
  3. (obsolete) aversion, disgust
    Synonyms: tegenzin, weerzin
