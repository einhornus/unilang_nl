afhaalchinees (85297)



Etymology

Compound of afhalen + Chinees.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑf.ɦaːl.ʃiːˌneːs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧haal‧chi‧nees

Noun

afhaalchinees m (plural afhaalchinezen)

  1. (chiefly Netherlands) Chinese takeaway (restaurant) (in practice usually a Chinese-Indonesian restaurant)
    Synonym: meeneemchinees
