Noun[edit]

bodem m (plural bodems, diminutive bodempje n)

  1. bottom (e.g. of a box or of the sea)
  2. soil, ground
  3. earth surface
  4. territory
    op Hollandse bodem: on Dutch soil
  5. (nautical) ship, vessel
