Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch sorgen, from Old Dutch sorgon, from Proto-Germanic *surgāną. Equivalent to zorg +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

zorgen

  1. (intransitive) to make sure, to ensure
