Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch gebaren. Equivalent to ge- +‎ baren.

Verb[edit]

gebaren

  1. (intransitive, transitive) to gesture, to point, to gesticulate
  2. (intransitive, transitive) to sign (to use sign language)
