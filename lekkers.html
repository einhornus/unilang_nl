lekkers (5678)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1[edit]

Partitive form of lekker (“tasty, good”).

Noun[edit]

lekkers n (uncountable)

  1. what is tasty, sweets, treats
    Na dat harde werken hebben ze wel iets lekkers verdiend.
    After all that hard work they earned themselves something tasty.
    Wie zoet is krijgt lekkers, wie stout is de roe.
    Who is sweet gets treats, who is naughty the rod. (from a traditional Sinterklaas song)
