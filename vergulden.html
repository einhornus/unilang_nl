vergulden (97901)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch vergulden. Equivalent to ver- + goud + -en.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vɛrˈɣʏl.də(n)/, /vərˈɣʏl.də(n)/
  • Hyphenation: ver‧gul‧den
  • Rhymes: -ʏldən

Verb

vergulden

  1. (transitive) to gild, to apply gold leaf to
