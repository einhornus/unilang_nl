dik (1558)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch dicke, from Old Dutch *thicki, from Proto-Germanic *þekuz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /dɪk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dik
  • Rhymes:

Adjective[edit]

dik (comparative dikker, superlative dikst)

  1. thick
  2. fat

Adverb[edit]

dik

  1. fatly, thickly
  2. (modifying "tevreden") well, rather, considerably

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord
