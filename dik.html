dik (1558)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dicke, from Old Dutch *thicki, from Proto-Germanic *þekuz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /dɪk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dik
  • Rhymes: -ɪk

Adjective

dik (comparative dikker, superlative dikst)

  1. thick
  2. fat

Adverb

dik

  1. fatly, thickly
  2. (modifying "tevreden") well, rather, considerably
