zeevaarder (120517)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch sêefaerder, variant of sêefarer. Equivalent to zee +‎ vaarder.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeːˌvaːr.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zee‧vaar‧der

Noun

zeevaarder m (plural zeevaarders, diminutive zeevaardertje n)

  1. seafarer

Related terms

Zelfstandig naamwoord
