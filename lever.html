Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch lēvere, from Old Dutch *levara, from Proto-Germanic *librō.

Noun[edit]

lever f (plural levers, diminutive levertje n)

  1. liver
  2. edible animal liver as a dish or culinary ingredient
