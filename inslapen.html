inslapen (15665)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch inslâpen, from Old Dutch inslāpan; equivalent to in +‎ slapen. Cognate with German einschlafen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɪnˌslaː.pə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: in‧sla‧pen

Verb

inslapen

  1. to fall asleep
  2. (euphemistic) to die peacefully

inslapen
