al (68)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch al, from Old Dutch *al, from Proto-Germanic *allaz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /É‘l/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: al
  • Rhymes:

Related terms[edit]

  • alles

Adverb[edit]

al

  1. already
    Het is al negen uur.
    It's 9 o'clock already.
  2. yet
    Heb je al een kip gekocht?
    Have you bought a chicken yet?

Conjunction[edit]

al

  1. even
    Al zou ik het willen...
    Even if I wanted to...

Synonyms[edit]

  • ook al
