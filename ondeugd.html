Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch ondoget. Equivalent to on- +‎ deugd.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔndøːxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧deugd

Noun[edit]

ondeugd f (plural ondeugden)

  1. A vice (bad habit).
  2. (informal) A naughty person.
