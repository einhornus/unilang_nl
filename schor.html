Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch schorre, from Old Dutch *skora; see scheren (“to shave”).

Noun

schor m or f (plural schorren, diminutive schorretje n)

  1. A plain or marsh formed by silt deposits unprotected by any dykes.
    Synonym: kwelder
