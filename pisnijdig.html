pisnijdig (17134)


Etymology[edit]

Compound of pis (“piss”) +‎ nijdig (“angry”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌpɪsˈnɛi̯.dəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pis‧nij‧dig

Adjective[edit]

pisnijdig (comparative pisnijdiger, superlative pisnijdigst)

  1. (informal) livid, furiously angry
