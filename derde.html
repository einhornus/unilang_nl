derde (985)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch derde, from Old Dutch thriddo, from Proto-Germanic *þridjô.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɛːr.də/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: der‧de
  • Rhymes: -ɛrdə

Adjective[edit]

derde (not comparable)

  1. third

Noun[edit]

derde n (plural derden)

  1. a third (part)

Noun[edit]

derde m or f (plural derden)

  1. third party
