kleermaakster (91701)



Etymology

Compound of kleer (singular of plurale tantum kleren) +‎ maken +‎ -ster.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkleːrˌmaːk.stər/, [ˈklɪːrˌma(ː)k.stər]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kleer‧maak‧ster

Noun

kleermaakster f (plural kleermaaksters, diminutive kleermaakstertje n, masculine kleermaker)

  1. female tailor (woman who makes, repairs, or refashions clothing, often professionally)
