vee (3755)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vêe, from Old Dutch fē, from Proto-Germanic *fehu, from Proto-Indo-European *peḱu- (“livestock”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /veː/
  • Rhymes: -eː

Noun
