zeeslak (77898)



Etymology

Compound of zee +‎ slak. First attested in the sixteenth century.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.slɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zee‧slak

Noun

zeeslak f (plural zeeslakken, diminutive zeeslakje n)

  1. marine snail or slug
