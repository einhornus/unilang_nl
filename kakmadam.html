kakmadam (75076)



Etymology

Compound of kak (“shit; poshness, arrogance”) +‎ madam (“lady, ma'am”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɑk.maːˌdɑm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kak‧ma‧dam

Noun

kakmadam f (plural kakmadammen or kakmadams, diminutive kakmadammetje n)

  1. (derogatory) A posh lady. [from late 19th c.]
