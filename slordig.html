slordig (7433)


Etymology

From slodderen (“to muddle up”) +‎ -ig (“-y”). Compare German schluderig (“botched, slipshod”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

slordig (comparative slordiger, superlative slordigst)

  1. sloppy, careless
  2. undisciplined, haphazard
  3. unkempt, untidy, slovenly
