erwt (34258)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch erwete, from Old Dutch *erwit, from Proto-Germanic *arwīts. Cognate to German Erbse.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɛrt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: erwt
  • Rhymes: -ɛrt

Noun

erwt f (plural erwten, diminutive erwtje n)

  1. A pea (edible seed).
  2. A pea plant, Pisum sativum.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
