dé (7796)


Etymology[edit]

An emphatically stressed variant of the definite article de.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈˈdə/

Usage notes[edit]

  • Like the unstressed version, the article is used with masculine and feminine nouns in the singular as well as plural nouns of all genders.
