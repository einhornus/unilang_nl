step (34658)


Etymology

Borrowed from English step (“footrest on a bicycle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /stɛp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: step
  • Rhymes: -ɛp

Noun

step f (plural steps, diminutive stepje n)

  1. kick scooter
    Synonyms: autoped, trottinette
  2. (dated) A mounting bracket on a bicycle.

  • autoped (verouderd), trottinette, trottinet
