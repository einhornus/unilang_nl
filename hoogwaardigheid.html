hoogwaardigheid (92382)



Etymology

From hoogwaardig (“high-quality; eminent”) + -heid (“-hood”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

hoogwaardigheid f (plural hoogwaardigheden, diminutive hoogwaardigheidje n)

  1. the state of being of high quality
  2. someone of high rank; an eminence
    Synonym: eminentie
