rijkaard (48589)



Etymology

rijk (“rich”) +‎ -aard (“-er”). Parallel to French richard.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

rijkaard m (plural rijkaarden or rijkaards, diminutive rijkaardje n)

  1. A rich, wealthy man

Usage notes

Generally used with a negative connotation, such as sin or envy

Synonyms

  • rijke m

Related terms

  • rijkbedeeld
  • vadsigaard m
