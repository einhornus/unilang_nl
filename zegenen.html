zegenen (8138)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch segenen, from Old Dutch *seginon, from Latin signāre. Related to zegen. Cognate to German segnen, English sain.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.ɣə.nə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ze‧ge‧nen
  • Rhymes: -eːɣənən

Verb[edit]

zegenen

  1. (transitive) to bless

Werkwoord

zegenen
