gemiauw (83272)



Etymology

From ge- +‎ miauwen: (an onomatopoeia)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧mi‧auw

Noun

gemiauw n (uncountable)

  1. The act, an instance or rather an (often annoying) episode of meowing
  2. The sound of meowing

Related terms

  • miauwer m
  • miauwerik m
