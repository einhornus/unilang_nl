kribbe (28114)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch cribbe, from Old Dutch kribba.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkrɪ.bə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: krib‧be
  • Rhymes: -ɪbə

Noun

kribbe f (plural kribben, diminutive kribbetje n)

  1. A manger, crib.
  2. (dated) A simple, unadorned bed.
  3. (Belgium) A nursery, crèche.
  4. (Netherlands) A breakwater, usually in a river or other stream.
  5. (very often as krib) A stall, a compartment in a stable.

Synonyms

(manger):

  • trog
  • voederbak
  • voerbak

(nursery):

  • crèche
  • kinderdagverblijf
  • kinderkribbe
  • kinderopvang

Zelfstandig naamwoord
