vijfje (30805)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɛi̯fjə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vijf‧je

Noun

vijfje n (plural vijfjes)

  1. Diminutive of vijf
  2. A coin or bank note with the denomination of 5 monetary units:
    1. (Netherlands) A 5 guilder coin, issued from 1988 until 2001. [from late 20th c.]
    2. (Netherlands) A 5 guilder bank note. [from second half 20th c.]
    3. A 5 euro bank note, issued from 2002. [from early 21st c.]
  3. (obsolete, Netherlands) A coin with the denomination of 25 guilder cents. [19th - early 20th c.]
    Synonyms: heitje, kwartje
