automatiek (77296)



Etymology

Substantivation of old automatique (“automatic”), borrowed from French automatique. Equivalent to automaat + -iek.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑu̯.toː.maːˈtik/, /ˌoː.toː.maːˈtik/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: au‧to‧ma‧tiek
  • Rhymes: -ik

Noun

automatiek f (plural automatieken, diminutive automatiekje n)

  1. an automat (a vending machine for warm food, usually deep-fried)
