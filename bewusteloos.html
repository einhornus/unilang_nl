bewusteloos (3472)


Etymology[edit]

From bewust +‎ -e- +‎ -loos.

  • IPA(key): /bəˈʋʏs.təˌloːs/

Adjective[edit]

bewusteloos (comparative bewustelozer, superlative meest bewusteloos or bewusteloost)

  1. unconscious

Synonyms[edit]
