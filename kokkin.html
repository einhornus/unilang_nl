kokkin (14341)


Etymology

From kok +‎ -in.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kɔˈkɪn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kok‧kin
  • Rhymes: -ɪn

Noun

kokkin f (plural kokkinnen, diminutive kokkinnetje n, masculine kok)

  1. A female cook, a cookess.
