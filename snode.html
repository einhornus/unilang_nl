snode (22101)



Etymology

From Old Dutch *snōthi, from Proto-Germanic *snauþijaz.

Adjective

snôde

  1. poor, needy
  2. paltry, of little value
  3. bad, worthless
  4. low in standing
  5. lowly, despised
  6. miserable, pitiful
