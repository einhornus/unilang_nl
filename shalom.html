shalom (27426)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Hebrew שָׁלוֹם‎ (shalóm, “peace, well-being; Hello, good-bye”), from Proto-Semitic *šalām- (“peace”). Doublet of salaam, a borrowing from Arabic.

Interjection[edit]

shalom

  1. A traditional Jewish greeting or farewell.

Noun[edit]

shalom (uncountable)

  1. (uncountable) peace
