Etymology[edit]

From ontoerekeningsvatbaar +‎ -heid, calque of German Unzurechnungsfähigkeit.

Noun[edit]

ontoerekeningsvatbaarheid f (uncountable)

  1. (law) the state of being unaccountable, insane, non compos mentis
