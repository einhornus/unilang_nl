medaille (4671)


Etymology

From French médaille

Pronunciation

  • medaille
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: me‧dail‧le

Noun

medaille f (plural medailles, diminutive medailletje n)

  1. medal (stamped metal disc)
  2. such decoration, awarded as a prize or in recognition of merit

Synonyms

  • eremetaal
  • erepenning
  • penning

Zelfstandig naamwoord

medaille v/m
