doctor (2784)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch doctor, from Latin doctor (“teacher, instructor”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /dɔkˈtɔr/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

doctor m (plural doctors or doctoren, diminutive doctortje n)

Synonyms

Related terms
