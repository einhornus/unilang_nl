droevig (5620)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch droevich. Equivalent to droef +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdru.vəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: droe‧vig

Adjective[edit]

droevig (comparative droeviger, superlative droevigst)

  1. sad, melancholy
  2. gloomy

Synonyms[edit]

  • treurig, triest, sip

Related terms[edit]

  • droef
  • droeven
