suikeroom (50590)



Etymology

Compound of suiker + oom.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsœy̯.kərˌoːm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sui‧ker‧oom

Noun

suikeroom m (plural suikerooms, diminutive suikeroompje n)

  1. A wealthy uncle who is generous or whose fortune one expects to inherit.
  2. (slang, uncommon) sugar daddy
