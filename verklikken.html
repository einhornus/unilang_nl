verklikken (20436)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch verclicken. Equivalent to ver- + klikken.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪkən

Verb

verklikken

  1. To denounce, act as informer, rat on
    (Brabant)
    "A ge mij verklikt tege de meester (leraar), dan geef'k a nen toek oep a bakkes da'ge d'r nie mee van onder komt!"
    (please add an English translation of this usage example)
  2. (obsolete) To observe, spy upon, spot, track
  3. (obsolete) To catch, trick
  4. (obsolete) To betray a secret, reveal what is hidden
