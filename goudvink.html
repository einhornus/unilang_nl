goudvink (105954)



Etymology

Compound of goud + vink.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɑu̯t.fɪŋk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: goud‧vink

Noun

goudvink m (plural goudvinken, diminutive goudvinkje n)

  1. A bullfinch, Pyrrhula pyrrhula.
  2. (dated, slang) A military officer of a branch or sub-branch that uses brass or yellow ("gold") rank insignia
  3. (Surinam) A burnished-buff tanager, Tangara cayana
    Synonym: sabeltangare
