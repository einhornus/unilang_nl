Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">confederatie f (plural confederaties, diminutive confederatietje n)

<ol>
  • a confederation; an union or alliance of (largely) independent sovereign states or political organizations
    Synonym: statenbond
    Coordinate terms: bondsstaat, eenheidsstaat, federatie

    • Usage notes[edit]

    In Belgian politics, the word is used by some parties to describe a federation where most decision power rests with the regional governments and parliaments.
