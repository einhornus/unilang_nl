kamerarrest (82309)



Etymology

From kamer (“room”) + arrest (“arrest”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

kamerarrest n (plural kamerarresten, diminutive kamerarrestje n)

  1. A confinement to quarters, imposed as a punishment and/or preventive measure
  2. (education) grounding (punishment)

Synonyms

  • huisarrest
