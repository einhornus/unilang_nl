verpakken (38740)


Etymology

ver- +‎ pakken

Pronunciation

  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧pak‧ken
  • Rhymes: -ɑkən

Verb

verpakken

  1. to wrap up, to package (to fold and secure something to be the cover or protection)
  2. to box (to place inside a box)
  3. to bag (to put into a bag)
  4. to tarp (to cover with a tarpaulin)
