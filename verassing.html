verassing (8813)


Etymology

From verassen +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vɛrˈɑ.sɪŋ/, /vərˈɑ.sɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧as‧sing

Noun

verassing f (plural verassingen)

  1. cremation
    Synonyms: crematie, lijkverbranding
  2. incineration, burning to ashes
