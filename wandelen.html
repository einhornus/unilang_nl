wandelen (2251)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wandelen, from Old Dutch *wandalon, from Proto-Germanic *wandlōną.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

wandelen

  1. to stroll, be on a walk (‘to go for a walk’ = ‘gaan wandelen’)
  2. to walk

wandelen
