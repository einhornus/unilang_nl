beteren (13494)


Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch bēteren, from Old Dutch *beteron, from Proto-Germanic *batizōną. Equivalent to beter +‎ -en.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeːtərə(n)/
  • béteren
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧te‧ren

Verb[edit]

beteren

  1. (ergative) to improve, to become/make better
Synonyms[edit]
  • verbeteren

From be- +‎ teren.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Hyphenation: be‧te‧ren
