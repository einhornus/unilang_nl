sandman (24776)


Etymology

sand +‎ -man

Pronunciation

  • (Uk, Us) IPA(key): /ˈsænd.mæn/
  • Audio (Uk and Us)
    		(file)

Noun

sandman (plural sandmen)

  1. A legendary figure who is said to bring good sleep and dreams by sprinkling magical sand into people's eyes.
  2. Used as a symbol of the passage of time toward death.
