zwart (1211)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch swart, from Old Dutch swart, from Proto-Germanic *swartaz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /zʋɑrt/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zwart
  • Rhymes:

Adjective[edit]

zwart (comparative zwarter, superlative zwartst)

  1. black
    Antonym: wit
  2. having dark skin
    Antonym: blank
  3. illegal, without payment or taxation
    Antonym: wit

Noun[edit]

zwart n (uncountable)

  1. the colour black
