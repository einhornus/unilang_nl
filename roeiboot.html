roeiboot (24063)



Etymology

Compound of roeien (“to row”) +‎ boot (“boat”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrui̯.boːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: roei‧boot

Noun

roeiboot m or f (plural roeiboten, diminutive roeibootje n)

  1. A rowing boat.

Related terms

  • roeier m
  • bootsman m
  • veerboot m or f

Zelfstandig naamwoord
