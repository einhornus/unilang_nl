lukraak (41977)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Uncertain, plausibly from luk (“luck”) or lukken (“to succeed, be fortunate”) + raak (“spot-on”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /lyˈkraːk/

Adverb[edit]

lukraak

  1. haphazardly, at random, hit or miss, indiscriminately, without proper planning

Adjective[edit]

lukraak (comparative lukraker, superlative lukraakst)

  1. random, indiscriminate, haphazard, without proper planning
  2. uncertain, dubious
