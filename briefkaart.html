briefkaart (39752)



Etymology

Compound of brief + kaart.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbrif.kaːrt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: brief‧kaart

Noun

briefkaart f (plural briefkaarten, diminutive briefkaartje n)

  1. A postcard, a postal card. [from ca. 1870]
    Synonym: kaartbrief

Related terms
