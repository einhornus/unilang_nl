flauwekul (5105)



Etymology

Compound/univerbation of flauwe (“tasteless, insipid”) +‎ kul (“testicle”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌflɑu̯ʋəˈkʏl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: flau‧we‧kul
  • Rhymes: -ʏl

Noun

flauwekul m (uncountable, diminutive flauwekulletje n)

  1. nonsense (meaningless words)

Synonyms

  • lariekoek
  • onzin
  • nonsens
