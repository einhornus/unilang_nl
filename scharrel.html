Noun[edit]

scharrel m or f (plural scharrels, diminutive scharreltje n)

  1. (action, especially in "aan de scharrel") picking up, seeking a casual sex partner.
  2. fuckmate, person with whom one has an irregular, noncommittal, superficial love relationship.

Usage notes[edit]

The grammatical gender of this word does not depend on the biological gender of the person referred to.
