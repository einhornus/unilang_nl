duizelig (5137)


Etymology[edit]

From duizelen (“to be/make dizzy”) +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio

Adjective[edit]

duizelig (comparative duizeliger, superlative duizeligst)

  1. dizzy, experiencing equilibrium-trouble
