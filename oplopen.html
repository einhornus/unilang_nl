oplopen (11133)



Etymology

op +‎ lopen

Pronunciation

  • oplopen
    		(file)

Verb

oplopen

  1. to incur, to receive (an injury)
    De drie anderen liepen lichtere verwondingen op. — The three others incurred lighter injuries.
  2. to contract (a disease)
  3. to increase

oplopen

  1. ergatief naar boven hellen
