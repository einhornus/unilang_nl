Etymology[edit]

From bond +‎ genoot, calque of German Bun­des­ge­nos­se.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɔnt.xəˌnoːt/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bond‧ge‧noot

Noun[edit]

bondgenoot m (plural bondgenoten, diminutive bondgenootje n, feminine bondgenote)

  1. ally (member of the same alliance; one who offers support when needed)
  2. (obsolete) fellow member of a federation, confederacy, association, etc.
