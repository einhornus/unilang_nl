biografie (15343)



Etymology

Probably borrowed from French biographie.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌbi(j)oːɣraːˈfi/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bio‧gra‧fie
  • Rhymes: -i

Noun

biografie f (plural biografieën, diminutive biografietje n)

  1. biography [from late 18th c.]
