tafelzilver (44548)


Etymology

Compound of tafel +‎ zilver.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtaː.fəlˌzɪl.vər/
  • Hyphenation: ta‧fel‧zil‧ver

Noun

tafelzilver n (uncountable)

  1. table silver (silver tableware)
