lauw (26606)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch lâeu, from Old Dutch *lāo, from Proto-Germanic *hlēwaz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lɑu̯/
  • lauw
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lauw
  • Rhymes: -ɑu̯
  • Homophone: louw

Adjective

lauw (comparative lauwer, superlative lauwst)

  1. lukewarm (temperature)
  2. cold, indifferent
  3. (informal) nice, cool, chill
