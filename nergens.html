nergens (594)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch nergens, nergen, from Old Dutch niergin, nieuuergin, equivalent to n- +‎ ergens.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnɛr.ɣəns/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ner‧gens
  • Rhymes: -ɛrɣəns

Adverb

nergens

  1. nowhere
    Mijn tas is nergens te vinden. ― My bag is nowhere to be found.
  2. As part of a pronominal adverb, nothing (niets)
    Jij bent echt nergens goed voor. ― You are truly good for nothing.
