Etymology 1[edit]

From Old Dutch *helm, from Proto-Germanic *helmaz. Compare West Frisian helm, Low German Helm, German Helm, Danish hjelm.

Noun[edit]

helm m (plural helmen, diminutive helmpje n)

  1. helmet
  2. (heraldry) helmet
