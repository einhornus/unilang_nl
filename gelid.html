gelid (15677)


Etymology

From ge- +‎ lid.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈlɪt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧lid
  • Rhymes: -ɪt

Noun

gelid n (plural gelederen)

  1. row of a formation, battle line
  2. an organizational rank, especially a military rank

Noun

gelid n (plural geleden)

  1. a joint, a point of articulation
