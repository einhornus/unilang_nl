Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch deech, from Old Dutch *dēg, from Proto-Germanic *daigaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to build, mold, form, knead”).

Noun

deeg n (uncountable, diminutive deegje n)

  1. dough

Adjective

deeg (not comparable)
