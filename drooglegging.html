drooglegging (32695)


Etymology

From droogleggen +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdroːxˌlɛ.ɣɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: droog‧leg‧ging

Noun

drooglegging f (plural droogleggingen)

  1. drainage of a polder
  2. (historical) alcohol ban, such as the Prohibition
