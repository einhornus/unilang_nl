zwijm (58407)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch swijm. This strong masculine noun may be original, but has no cognates elsewhere. It is therefore more likely a strengthening of the weak Old Dutch *swīmo, from Proto-Germanic *swīmô, which has cognates in other West Germanic languages.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /zʋɛi̯m/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zwijm
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯m

Noun

zwijm m (uncountable)

  1. weakened or lost consciousness

Related terms

  • zwijmen
