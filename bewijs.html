Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch bewijs. Equivalent to a deverbal from bewijzen.

Noun[edit]

bewijs n (plural bewijzen, diminutive bewijsje n)

  1. proof
  2. evidence
  3. the totality of proof and/or evidence used to make a case; argumentation
    Synonym: bewijsvoering
  4. attesting document, permit
  5. licence, permit (document allowing one to use certain vehicles)
    Synonym: brevet
  6. ticket, pass entitling one to use, admission or possession
  7. (obsolete) legal apportionment of an inheritance
