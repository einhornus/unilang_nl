banketbakker (42584)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ban‧ket‧bak‧ker

Noun

banketbakker m (plural banketbakkers, diminutive banketbakkertje n)

  1. baker specialised in baker's confectionery

Related terms
