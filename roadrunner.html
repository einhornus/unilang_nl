roadrunner (43141)


Etymology

road +‎ runner, perhaps literally translated from correcaminos.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɹoʊdˌɹʌnə(ɹ)/
  • Audio (Uk)
    		(file)

Noun

roadrunner (plural roadrunners)

  1. Either of two species of bird in the genus Geococcyx of the cuckoo family, native to North and Central America. They are fast runners.
