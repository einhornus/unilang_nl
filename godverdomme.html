godverdomme (1586)


Etymology

Univerbation of God + verdoeme. Literally meaning “God dammit, may God damn”. It expresses the wish that God damns something. The -me is not the pronoun me, but is the Dutch suffix to indicate a subjunctive.

Pronunciation
