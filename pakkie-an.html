pakkie-an (33928)


Etymology

Borrowed Malay bagian, likely modified under influence of aanpakken, aanpak.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌpɑ.kiˈɑn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pak‧kie-an
  • Rhymes: -ɑn

Noun

pakkie-an n (uncountable)

  1. (Netherlands, colloquial) task, concern, chore, business (that which someone is tasked to do, that which is of concern to someone)
    Zij kunnen optyfen, dat is hun pakkie-an niet!
    They can sod off, it isn't their business at all!
  2. (Netherlands, colloquial) forte, one's strength (something in which one excels)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pakkie-an o

  1. wat iemand ligt
    • Ik geloof niet dat grammatica je pakkie-an is.
