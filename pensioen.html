pensioen (2354)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch pensioen, recorded since 1362, from Old French pension (“payment, rent”), from Latin pensio (“payment, rent”), from pensus, the past participle of pendere (“to weigh, to pay”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pɛnˈʃun/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pen‧si‧oen
  • Rhymes: -un

Noun

pensioen n (plural pensioenen, diminutive pensioentje n)

  1. pension
