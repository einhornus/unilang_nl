afstraffing (38579)


Etymology

From afstraffen (“to punish (severely)”) +‎ -ing (“-ing, -ion, -ment”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfˌstrɑ.fɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧straf‧fing

Noun

afstraffing f (plural afstraffingen, diminutive afstraffinkje n)

  1. (literally) A severe punishment, usually as retribution.
  2. A memorable lesson (Can we verify(+) this sense?)
    Die afstraffing zullen ze niet gauw vergeten!
    They won't forget that lesson in a long time!
  3. A painful, embarrassing failure or defeat, a beating.
    Na de vierde afstraffing keerden zelfs hun fans zich tegen de ploeg.
    After the fourth mortal loss even their fans turned on the team.

Synonyms

  • (punishment): kastijding
  • (lesson): lesje

Related terms

  • afranseling
  • aftuigen (verb)
  • bestraffing
  • strafbaar (adjective)
