heldendaad (29909)


Etymology

Compound of held (“hero”) +‎ -en- +‎ daad (“deed, doing, act”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛl.də(n)ˌdaːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hel‧den‧daad

Noun

heldendaad f (plural heldendaden, diminutive heldendaadje n)

  1. A heroic deed, act, feat. [from 17th c.]
