schouw (33840)



Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

schouw m (plural schouwen, diminutive schouwtje n)

  1. fireplace

Synonyms

  • open haard
  • stookplaats (stookplaats is broader term encompassing both schouw (fireplace with open fire), haard (hearth, fireplace with closed fire for cooking) and, to some extent, kachel (closed fireplace for room heating)

Verb

schouw

  1. first-person singular present indicative of schouwen
  2. imperative of schouwen
