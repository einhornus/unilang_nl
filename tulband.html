tulband (25995)


Etymology

Borrowed from Turkish tülbent, from Ottoman Turkish دلبند‎ (dülbend), from Persian دلبند‎ (dolband). The second element was adapted to band by folk etymology.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtʏl.bɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: tul‧band

Noun

tulband m (plural tulbanden, diminutive tulbandje n)

  1. A turban.
  2. A cake baked in the shape of a turban.
    Synonym: tulbandcake

Related terms

  • tulp
