Adjective[edit]

gezind (not comparable)

  1. inclined, of a certain disposition
    De goden zijn vandaag gunstig gezind.
    The gods are favourably inclined today.

Usage notes[edit]

This word is rarely used alone. It is usually preceded by an adverb to specify what kind of disposition.

Synonyms[edit]

  • geluimd
