luchtkasteel (115483)



Etymology

From lucht (“air”) + kasteel (“castle”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

luchtkasteel n (plural luchtkastelen, diminutive luchtkasteeltje n)

  1. (literally) A castle built in the air, as in fiction
  2. (figurative) castle in the air

Synonyms

  • (illusion): zinsbegoocheling

Related terms

  • zandkasteel n
