zeevis (85833)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch seevisch. Equivalent to zee (“sea”) +‎ vis (“fish”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.vɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zee‧vis

Noun

zeevis m (plural zeevissen, diminutive zeevisje n)

  1. A marine fish (fish that lives at sea).

Synonyms

  • zoutwatervis

Related terms

  • riviervis
  • zeevisser
  • zeevisserij
