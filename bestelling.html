bestelling (5026)


Etymology

From bestellen (“to order”) +‎ -ing (“-ing, -ance, -tion”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈstɛ.lɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧stel‧ling
  • Rhymes: -ɛlɪŋ

Noun

bestelling f (plural bestellingen, diminutive bestellinkje n)

  1. order (requesting a product or service)

Related terms

  • bestellen
