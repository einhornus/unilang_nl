op_de_been (8785)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Literally: “on the legs”, with an old endingless plural of been (instead of contemporary benen). Compare the same construction in German auf den Beinen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔp də ˈbeːn/

Adverb[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">op de been

<ol>
  • standing; upright
  • up; awake; having got up
