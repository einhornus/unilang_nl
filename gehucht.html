gehucht (25367)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch ghehochte, ghehuchte, from Old Dutch *gihufti. Ultimately a combination of ge- +‎ hof +‎ -te. Compare German Gehöft, which is perhaps of Dutch origin.
