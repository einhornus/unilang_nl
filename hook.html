hook (11082)


Etymology[edit]

From Dutch hoek (“corner, angle”). The hyper-correction influenced by the cognate English hook or German Hook.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈhʊk̚]

Noun[edit]

hook (plural, first-person possessive hookku, second-person possessive hookmu, third-person possessive hooknya)

  1. (colloquial) land or building at the corner.
