luchtig (20056)


Etymology

From lucht (“air”) +‎ -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

luchtig (comparative luchtiger, superlative luchtigst)

  1. (literally) airy
  2. light, lite, notably said of food
  3. lighthearted
