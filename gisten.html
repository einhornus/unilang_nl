gisten (64936)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɪs.tə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gis‧ten
  • Rhymes: -ɪstən

Etymology 1

Formed as gist (“yeast”) +‎ -en.

Verb

gisten

  1. (figurative) to be likely to cause commotion and restlessness; to be prone to rebellion
  2. to ferment (more often vergisten)
