lollipop (68962)



Etymology

Multiple theories abound. One is that it is borrowed from Angloromani lollipobbul (“candy apple”). Another is that it is derived from loll ("to dangle the tongue") + pop.

Pronunciation

  • (Uk) IPA(key): /lɒl.i.pɒp/
  • (Us) IPA(key): /lɑ.li.pɑp/

  • Rhymes: -ɒp

Noun

lollipop (plural lollipops)

  1. An item of confectionery consisting of a piece of candy/sweet attached to a stick.

Synonyms

  • lolly, sucker (Us)
