billentikker (133169)



Etymology

From bil (“buttock”) + -en- + tikken (“to tick”) + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɪ.lə(n)ˌtɪ.kər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bil‧len‧tik‧ker

Noun

billentikker m (plural billentikkers, diminutive billentikkertje n)

  1. shortie, bum-coat (a coat design with tails at the back)
