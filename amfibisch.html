amfibisch (85184)



Etymology

From amfibie +‎ -isch.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑmˈfi.bis/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

amfibisch (not comparable)

  1. amphibian, pertaining to amphibians
  2. amphibian, capable of function on both land and in water
