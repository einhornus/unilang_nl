baker (5671)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbaː.kər/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1

Short for bakermoeder.

Noun

baker f (plural bakers, diminutive bakertje n)

  1. (historical) A midwife; one who helps women in childbirth with deliveries.

baker
