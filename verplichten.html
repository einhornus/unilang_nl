verplichten (22973)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch verplichten. Equivalent to ver- +‎ plichten and ver- +‎ plicht +‎ -en.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vərˈplɪxtən/, /vɛrˈplɪxtən/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧plich‧ten
  • Rhymes: -ɪxtən

Verb

verplichten

  1. (transitive) to oblige
  2. (reflexive) to affiliate, to unite
