doorbakken (17863)


Etymology 1[edit]

Compound of door- +‎ bakken.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /doːrˈbɑkə(n)/
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

doorbakken

  1. (transitive) to bake thoroughly
  2. (transitive) to bake completely, bake from beginning till end
