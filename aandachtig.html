aandachtig (13511)



Etymology

From aandacht (“attention, care”) + -ig (“y, -ive”); perfectly parallel with German andächtig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌaːnˈdɑx.təx/
  • aandachtig
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧dach‧tig
  • Rhymes: -ɑxtəx

Adjective

aandachtig (comparative aandachtiger, superlative aandachtigst)

  1. (of a person) attentive, paying care/attention
  2. (of an object) careful, enjoying care/attention (clarification of this definition is needed)
