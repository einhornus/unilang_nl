onderling (15032)


Etymology[edit]

From onder +‎ -ling.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

onderling (not comparable)

  1. under each other
  2. mutual, reciprocal
    Synonyms: wederzijds, wederkerig
