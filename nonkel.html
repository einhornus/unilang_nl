nonkel (16987)


Etymology

From French oncle, with a prothetic n- via metanalysis

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɔŋkəl

Noun

nonkel m (plural nonkels, diminutive nonkeltje n)

  1. (Belgium) uncle (brother of someone’s father or mother)
    Synonym: oom
