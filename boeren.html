Etymology 1[edit]

From boer (“farmer”) +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

boeren

  1. (intransitive) to farm, practice agriculture
  2. (intransitive) to do (well, badly...) in some business

Etymology 2[edit]

From boer (“burp”) +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

boeren

  1. (intransitive) to burp, to belch
