bierkelder (119276)



Etymology

Compound of bier (“beer”) +‎ kelder (“cellar”). The sense “bierkeller” is a calque of German Bierkeller.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbiːrˌkɛl.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bier‧kel‧der

Noun

bierkelder m (plural bierkelders, diminutive bierkeldertje n)

  1. cellar where beer is stored
  2. bierkeller (mostly in reference to Germany)
