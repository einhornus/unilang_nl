onderwerping (29149)



Etymology

From onderwerpen + -ing.

Pronunciation

  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: on‧der‧wer‧ping

Noun

onderwerping f (plural onderwerpingen, diminutive onderwerpinkje n)

  1. submission; subjection; subjugation
