wellicht (2135)


Etymology[edit]

Perhaps from Middle Low German villicht, villîcht (modern German Low German villicht). Cognate with German vielleicht.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb[edit]

wellicht

  1. (sentence-initial) possibly, perhaps
    Wellicht heeft hij de trein gemist. ― Perhaps he missed the train.
  2. probably, likely
    Dit zal wellicht helpen. ― This will probably help.

Usage notes[edit]

Used as the opening word of a sentence, the term tends to indicate a lower degree of confidence.

Synonyms[edit]

  • (sentence-initial): misschien
  • waarschijnlijk
