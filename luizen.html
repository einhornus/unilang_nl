luizen (6840)


Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -œy̯zən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch lusen, from luus (modern luis).

Verb

luizen

  1. (transitive) To pick the lice (from ...)
  2. (transitive) To acquire, obtain
  3. (transitive) To cut the shoots from a tobacco plant
