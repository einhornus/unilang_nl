Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈproː.pər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pro‧per

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch proper, from Old French propre, from Latin proprius.

Adjective[edit]

proper (comparative properder, superlative properst)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) clean

Synonyms[edit]

  • netjes, rein, zindelijk.
  • (Netherlands) schoon.
