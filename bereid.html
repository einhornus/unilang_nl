Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch bereit. Part participle of bereiden.

Adjective[edit]

bereid (not comparable)

  1. ready, prepared
  2. willing

Participle[edit]

bereid

  1. past participle of bereiden

Verb[edit]

bereid

  1. first-person singular present indicative of bereiden
  2. imperative of bereiden
