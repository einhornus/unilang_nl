Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch stōnen, from Old Dutch *stunōn, *stonōn, from Frankish *stunōn, from Proto-Germanic *stunōną (“to groan”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tenh₂- (“to thunder”), compare English stun.
