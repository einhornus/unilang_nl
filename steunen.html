steunen (2983)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch stōnen, from Old Dutch *stunōn, *stonōn, from Frankish *stunōn, from Proto-Germanic *stunōną (“to groan”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tenh₂- (“to thunder”), compare English stun.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -øːnən

Verb

steunen

  1. (transitive) to support, keep up, back up, assist
  2. (intransitive) to rest, be based or founded on
  3. (intransitive) to moan, groan
