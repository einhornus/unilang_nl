belijden (39805)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch beliden. A reanalysis of belien, a weak verb, based on its past tense belide. Synchronically equivalent to be- +‎ lijden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈlɛi̯də(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧lei‧den
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯dən

Verb

belijden

  1. (transitive) to confess
  2. (transitive) to profess
