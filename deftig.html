deftig (14753)


Etymology[edit]

From Proto-West Germanic *daftī (“fitting, suitable”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

deftig (comparative deftiger, superlative deftigst)

  1. stylish, distinguished, genteel (showing high social class)
  2. pompous

Synonyms[edit]

  • (pompous): statig

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

deftig

  1. waardig, voornaam, statig, van goede komaf, erg netjes, bekakt
    • De deftige man sprak heel bekakt.
