rookwaar (79928)



Etymology

Compound of roken (“to smoke”) + waar (“ware, product”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈroːk.ʋaːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rook‧waar

Noun

rookwaar f (plural rookwaren)

  1. (collective) smoking products (tobacco products, sometimes including other smoked drugs and smoking miscellanea)
    Haar rookwaar ging in rook op. ― Her smoking products went up in smoke.
