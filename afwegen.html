afwegen (27792)


Etymology

From af +‎ wegen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfʋeːɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -eːɣən

Verb

afwegen

  1. (transitive) to weigh, to determine the weight of.
  2. (transitive) to consider, to weigh the importance of.
    de voor- en nadelen tegen elkaar afwegen
    To consider the pros and cons.
