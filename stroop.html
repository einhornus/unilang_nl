Etymology 1[edit]

From siroop.

Noun[edit]

stroop f (plural stropen, diminutive stroopje n)

  1. syrup, especially a viscous variety of syrup
Related terms[edit]

Etymology 2[edit]

From stropen.

Noun[edit]

stroop m (uncountable)

  1. poaching (the act of hunting illegally)
