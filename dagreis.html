dagreis (84127)



Etymology

Compound of dag +‎ reis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɑx.rɛi̯s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dag‧reis

Noun

dagreis f (plural dagreizen, diminutive dagreisje n)

  1. day trip
  2. distance travelled in one day (often used as a crude unit of measure)
