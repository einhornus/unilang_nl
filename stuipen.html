stuipen (11064)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch stupen (“to bend over; to bow”). Cognate to English stoop.

Verb[edit]

stuipen

  1. (archaic) to bend the upper part of the body forward and downward; to stoop

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch stupen (“to flog”), from stupe (“pillory; flogging”).

Verb[edit]

stuipen

  1. (archaic) to flog
