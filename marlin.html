marlin (16516)



Etymology

A contraction of marlinspike, named so because of its pointed snout.

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɑː(r)lɪn

Noun

marlin (plural marlin or marlins)

  1. Any species of game fish belonging to either of the genera Tetrapturus or Makaira. Every such species has a pointed, spear-like projection of the upper jaw.

Synonyms

  • spearfish
