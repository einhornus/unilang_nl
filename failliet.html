failliet (5286)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed form French faillite, from Italian fallito.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /faːˈjit/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fail‧liet
  • Rhymes:

Adjective[edit]

failliet (comparative faillieter, superlative faillietst)

  1. bankrupt

Noun[edit]

failliet n (plural faillieten)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) bankruptcy
  2. (figuratively) bankruptcy, failure

Synonyms[edit]

  • bankroet
  • faillissement
  • faling

Noun[edit]

failliet m (plural faillieten)

  1. bankrupt person

Synonyms[edit]

  • bankroetier

Related terms[edit]

  • faillissement
