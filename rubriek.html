rubriek (31606)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch rubrike (“red pigment”), from Old French rubrique, from Latin rubrīca (“red ochre”), the substance used to make red letters, from ruber (“red”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rewdʰ-.
