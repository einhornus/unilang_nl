Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch cruden. Equivalent to kruid +‎ -en.

Noun[edit]

kruiden n (uncountable)

  1. the act of spicing up

Verb[edit]

kruiden

  1. (transitive) to season, to spice
    Synonym: bekruiden
