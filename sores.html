sores (22418)



Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish צרות‎ (tsores), plural of צרה‎ (tsore, “trouble, problem”), from Hebrew צָרָה‎ (tsará, “trouble, tragedy, calamity”).

Pronunciation

Noun
