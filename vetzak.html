vetzak (8409)


Etymology

Equivalent to vet (“fat”) +‎ zak (“sack, bag”).

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɑk
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈvɛtzɑk/
  • Hyphenation: vet‧zak

Noun

vetzak m (plural vetzakken, diminutive vetzakje n)

  1. (derogatory) fatso, lard-ass

Synonyms

  • pafzak

vetzak m
