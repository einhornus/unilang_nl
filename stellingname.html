stellingname (99357)



Etymology

From stelling +‎ name, cf. stelling nemen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɛ.lɪŋˌnaː.mə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: stel‧ling‧na‧me

Noun

stellingname f (plural stellingnames)

  1. the act of taking a position (i.e. a military position or an opinion)
