reisbureau (17304)


Etymology

Compound of reis (“voyage”) +‎ bureau (“office, agency”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɛi̯s.byˌroː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: reis‧bu‧reau

Noun

reisbureau n (plural reisbureaus, diminutive reisbureautje n)

  1. travel agency
    Het reisbureau was dicht tijdens de zomervakantie.
    The travel agency was closed during the summer holidays.
