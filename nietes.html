nietes (7439)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈnitəs/
  • Hyphenation: nie‧tes

Interjection

nietes

  1. (negation, informal, childish) Used to contradict a positive assertion.
    "Jullie waren weer te laat vandaag." "Nietes!"
    "You were late again today." "We were not!"
    "Jíj hebt mijn glas gebroken." "Nietes!" "Welles!"
    "It was you who broke my glass." "It was not!" "It was, too!"
