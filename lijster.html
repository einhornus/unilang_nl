Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch lijstre (“songbird”), from Proto-West Germanic *lī(h)strō, possibly from Proto-Germanic *laikaną (“to move about”). Or, possibly related to Ancient Greek λιγύς (ligús, “shrill, piercing”).
