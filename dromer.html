dromer (12856)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch drômere. Equivalent to dromen +‎ -er.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdroː.mər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dro‧mer
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

dromer m (plural dromers, diminutive dromertje n)

  1. A dreamer, who has (a) dream(s)
  2. A dreamy person, focused especially on dreaming, even at the expense of reality
  3. (obsolete) A seer who sees visions in dreams.
