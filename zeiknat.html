zeiknat (32124)


Etymology

From zeiken (“to pour, to piss”) +‎ nat (“wet”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zeik‧nat
  • Rhymes: -ɑt

Adjective

zeiknat (not comparable)

  1. (informal) soaked, wet
