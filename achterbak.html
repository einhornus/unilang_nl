achterbak (15089)


Etymology[edit]

Compound of achter +‎ bak.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑx.tərˌbɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ach‧ter‧bak

Noun[edit]

achterbak m (plural achterbakken)

  1. A boot, a trunk; a luggage compartment at the back of a car.
    Synonyms: koffer, kofferbak
