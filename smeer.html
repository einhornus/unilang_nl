Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch smeer, from Old Dutch smero, from Old Dutch *smerwą. Cognate with English smear.

Noun[edit]

smeer m or n (plural smeren, diminutive smeertje n)

  1. smear, a fat substance
