opstijgen (6454)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch opstigen. Equivalent to op +‎ stijgen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔpˌstɛi̯.ɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: op‧stij‧gen

Verb

opstijgen

  1. (intransitive) to ascend, to rise
  2. (intransitive) to take off (with an aircraft)
