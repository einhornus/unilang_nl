knapps (82111)



Etymology

From Middle High German knap. Cognate with German knapp, Dutch knap.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /knɑps/

Adjective

knapps (masculine knappsen, neuter knappst, comparative méi knapps, superlative am knappsten)

  1. scarce

Adverb

knapps

  1. barely, narrowly, hardly
