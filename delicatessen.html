delicatessen (35228)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

First attested 1642 [1]. Plural of delicatesse (“fine food”), from French délicatesse, from délicat (“fine”), from Latin delicatus (“alluring”).

Noun[edit]

delicatessen

  1. delicacies, fine food
  2. Plural form of delicatesse

Synonyms[edit]

  1. lekkernijen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

delicatessen
