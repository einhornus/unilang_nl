tehuis (7456)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch te huus; equivalent to te +‎ huis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /təˈɦœy̯s/
  • Rhymes:
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

tehuis n (plural tehuizen, diminutive tehuisje n)

  1. home, asylum

Usage notes[edit]

Whereas thuis refers to one's home as a place of residence, tehuis refers to an asylum or refuge where one may or may not feel at home.

Adverb[edit]

tehuis

  1. (archaic) Alternative form of thuis
