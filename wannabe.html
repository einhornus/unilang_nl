Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">wannabe (plural wannabes)

<ol>
  • (slang, derogatory, often attributive) Someone who wishes to be or do something, but lacks the qualifications or talent; an overeager amateur; an aspirant.
    Hollywood's restaurants are full of wannabe actors waiting to be discovered.
  • (slang, derogatory) Someone who wishes to be part of, or to assimilate to, a majority group of which they are not a member.
