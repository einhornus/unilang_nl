levendig (9075)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch levendich (“alive”). Equivalent to levend +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈleːvəndəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: le‧ven‧dig

Adjective

levendig (comparative levendiger, superlative levendigst)

  1. lively, vivacious
    Synonyms: energiek, kwiek
  2. (now dialectal) living, alive
    Synonym: levend
