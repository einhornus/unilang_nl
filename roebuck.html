roebuck (53369)



Etymology

From Middle English roobukke; equivalent to roe +‎ buck (“male deer”).

Pronunciation

  • (Uk) IPA(key): /ˈɹəʊ.bʌk/
  • Audio (Us)
    		(file)

Noun

roebuck (plural roebucks)

  1. A male roe deer.
