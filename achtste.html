achtste (6970)


Etymology

From acht +‎ -ste (superlative).

Displaced Middle Dutch achtende, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch *ahtundo, which had -n- by analogy with *sivondo (“seventh”) and *nigundo (“ninth”). An even older form was Middle Dutch achte, achtte, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch *ahtodo, from Proto-Germanic *ahtudô.
