afdrogen (19480)


Etymology

From af +‎ drogen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfdroːɣə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧dro‧gen

Verb

afdrogen

  1. (transitive) to dry off, in particular of dishes by means of a tea towel
  2. (transitive) to defeat overwhelmingly
  3. (transitive, informal) to beat up
