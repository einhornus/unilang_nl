Verb

opspatten

  1. (intransitive, of fluids) to disperse and be projected in droplets; to spatter, to splash, to scatter (usually in haphazard or random directions with a vertical component)
    Tijdens het braden spatte wat boter op en er landden druppels op haar arm.
