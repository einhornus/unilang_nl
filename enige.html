enige (289)


Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Pronoun

enige

  1. (masculine, feminine, with de) only one
    Jij bent de enige die er bezwaar tegen heeft.
    You are the only one who has objections.
  2. (neuter, with het) only thing
    Het enige dat ik zie is bomen.
    The only thing I see is trees.

Usage notes

The superlative form enigste is commonly used, but people may see it as wrong. The form is correct, but the use of it is discouraged. See http://taaladvies.net/taal/advies/vraag/262/ (in Dutch) for more information.

Adjective

enige

  1. Inflected form of enig

enige
