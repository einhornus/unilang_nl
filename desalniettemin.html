Etymology[edit]

From des (“of this/that”) +‎ al (“all”) +‎ niettemin (“nevertheless”); translatable as in spite of all this/that, a more emphatic version of niettemin.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɛzɑlnitəˈmɪn/
