zielig (2369)


Etymology[edit]

From ziel +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈziləx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zie‧lig

Adjective[edit]

zielig (comparative zieliger, superlative zieligst)

  1. sad, pitiful
  2. pathetic
