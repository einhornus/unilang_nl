herkomst (14539)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hercompst, a derivative of Middle Dutch hercōmen (“to come from, originate from”), equivalent to her- +‎ komen +‎ -st.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: her‧komst

Noun

herkomst f (plural herkomsten)

  1. origin, descent

Synonyms

  • oorsprong
  • afkomst
