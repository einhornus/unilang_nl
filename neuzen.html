neuzen (11187)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈnøː.zə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: neu‧zen

Etymology 1[edit]

From neus +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

neuzen

  1. (intransitive) to browse, to peruse [+ in (inside)]
  2. (intransitive) to snoop, to peek, to peep

Noun[edit]

neuzen
