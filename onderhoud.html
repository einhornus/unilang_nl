onderhoud (6247)


Etymology[edit]

Conjugated form of onderhouden, turned into a noun.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

onderhoud n (uncountable)

  1. maintenance
  2. overhaul
  3. audience (formal meeting)
  4. basic needs
    Je moet in je eigen onderhoud kunnen voorzien.
    You have to be able to support your own basic needs.

Verb[edit]

onderhoud

  1. first-person singular present indicative of onderhouden (1).
  2. imperative of onderhouden (1).
  3. first-person singular present indicative of onderhouden (when using a subclause) (2).
