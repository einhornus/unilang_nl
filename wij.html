wij (138)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch wi, from Old Dutch wī, from Proto-Germanic *wīz, from Proto-Indo-European *wéy-, plural of *éǵh₂. Compare Low German wi, West Frisian wy, German wir, English we, Danish vi.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʋɛi̯/ (stressed), IPA(key): /ʋə/ (unstressed)
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Homophone: wei

Pronoun[edit]

wij

  1. the first-person plural personal pronoun; refers to the speakers

Synonyms[edit]

  • we
