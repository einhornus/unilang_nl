fine (39585)


Etymology

From Old Frisian">Old Frisian finda, from Proto-Germanic *finþaną, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pent-

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈfinə/

Verb

fine

  1. to find
  2. to decide that, to form the opinion that
    Ik fyn dyn freon moai.
    I find your friend nice.
