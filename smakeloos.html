smakeloos (18536)


Etymology

From smaken (“to taste”) +‎ -loos

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

smakeloos (comparative smakelozer, superlative meest smakeloos or smakeloost)

  1. tasteless (said of food and drink)
  2. (figuratively) obscene, vulgar, tasteless
