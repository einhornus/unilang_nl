vijand (1158)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch viant, from Old Dutch fīand, from Proto-Germanic *fijandz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɛi̯ɑnt/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

vijand m (plural vijanden, diminutive vijandje n, feminine vijandin)

  1. enemy

Zelfstandig naamwoord

vijand m
