schaambeen (88462)



Etymology

From schamen (“to shame”) +‎ been (“bone”).

Pronunciation

  • Hyphenation: schaam‧been

Noun

schaambeen n (plural schaambeenderen or schaambenen, diminutive schaambeentje n)

  1. pubis
