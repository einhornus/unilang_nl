cavalerie (7177)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from French cavalerie, from Italian cavalleria.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌkaː.vaː.ləˈri/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ca‧va‧le‧rie
  • Rhymes: -i

Noun

cavalerie f (plural cavaleries)

  1. cavalry (part of the military that is on horseback)
    Synonym: ruiterij

Related terms
