bijkomen (11589)



Etymology

From bij + komen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛi̯koːmə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bij‧ko‧men

Verb

bijkomen

  1. (intransitive) to regain consciousness, to come round
  2. (intransitive) to recuperate, to recover, to catch one's breath
  3. (intransitive) to gain weight
