veteraan (11225)


Etymology

From Latin veterānus.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -aːn
  • IPA(key): /veː.tə.ˈraːn/

Noun

veteraan m (plural veteranen, diminutive veteraantje n)

  1. veteran
  2. (sports) a player of a higher age (depending on which branch of sports)

Synonyms

  • (soldier): oud-strijder
  • (veteran): oudgediende
