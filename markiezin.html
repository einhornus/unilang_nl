markiezin (24870)


Etymology

From markies +‎ -in.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌmɑr.kiˈzɪn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: mar‧kie‧zin
  • Rhymes: -ɪn

Noun

markiezin f (plural markiezinnen, diminutive markiezinnetje n)

  1. marchioness (female marquess; wife of a marquess)
    Synonym: markgravin

markiezin v
