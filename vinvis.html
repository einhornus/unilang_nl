vinvis (58685)



Etymology

17th century. Compound of vin (“fin”) +‎ vis (“fish”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɪn.vɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vin‧vis

Noun

vinvis m (plural vinvissen, diminutive vinvisje n)

  1. Any cetacean of the family Balaenopteridae, rorqual.
  2. The fin whale, Balaenoptera physalus, in particular.
