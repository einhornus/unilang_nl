aktentas (58211)



Etymology

Compound of akte + -en- + tas.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑk.tə(n)ˌtɑs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ak‧ten‧tas

Noun

aktentas f (plural aktentassen, diminutive aktentasje n)

  1. Superseded spelling of aktetas.

Usage notes

  • The spelling aktentas was deprecated in 1996 in the new Groene Boekje (“Little Green Book”) spelling reform.
