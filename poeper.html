poeper (113949)



Etymology

From poepen (“to defecate, to shit, to poo”) + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpu.pər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: poe‧per
  • Rhymes: -upər

Noun

poeper m (plural poepers, diminutive poepertje n)

  1. (informal, Netherlands) A butthole, arse/ass, anus.
    Wie het als eerste ruikt, heeft zijn poepertje gebruikt.
    He who was the first one to smell it, has used his shitter. (Dutch equivalent of "He who smelt it dealt it.")
  2. (informal, Netherlands) A shitter, a pooper, one who defecates.
  3. (Belgium) See met de poepers zitten.
