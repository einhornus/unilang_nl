gemeenzaam (106365)



Etymology

Adjective

gemeenzaam (comparative gemeenzamer, superlative gemeenzaamst)

  1. (literary) familiar, intimate
  2. (literary) ordinary, common
  3. Of speech: colloquial, informal

    Gemeenzaam klinken. — To sound familiar.
