mayonaise (9416)



Etymology

From French mayonnaise.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌmaː.joːˈnɛː.zə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ma‧yo‧nai‧se
  • Rhymes: -ɛːzə

Noun

mayonaise f (plural mayonaises, diminutive mayonaisetje n)

  1. mayonnaise, a sauce or dressing made from raw egg yolks and oil

mayonaise v
