voogdij (7680)


Etymology[edit]

From voogd +‎ -ij.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /voːɣˈdɛi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

voogdij f (plural voogdijen)

  1. (law) guardianship under which the ward is a minor

Synonyms[edit]
