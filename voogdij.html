voogdij (7680)


Etymology

From voogd +‎ -ij.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /voːɣˈdɛi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

voogdij f (plural voogdijen)

  1. (law) guardianship under which the ward is a minor

Synonyms

  • voogdijschap, zeggenschap
