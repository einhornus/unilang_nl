hupsakee (21335)



Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Interjection

hupsakee

  1. hey presto, ta-da (when the result of one's work becomes apparent)
  2. up you go (when helping a child a climb onto something)
  3. upsadaisy (when throwing a small child in the air during play)
  4. More generally, an exclamation of giving someone a mild and/or pleasant surprise.
