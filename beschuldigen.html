beschuldigen (4935)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch beschuldigen, replacing older beschulden by influence of schuldich (modern schuldig). Equivalent to be- + schuld + -igen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈsxʏldəɣə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧schul‧di‧gen
  • Rhymes: -ʏldəɣən

Verb

beschuldigen

  1. (transitive) to accuse, blame
