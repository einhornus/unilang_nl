piste (12676)


Etymology 1

From French piste.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpistə/

Noun

piste f (plural pistes, diminutive pistetje n)

  1. (skiing) piste
  2. (circus) circus ring
  3. (Belgium) trail, track
