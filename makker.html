Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch macker, probably from *gemacke (“companion”). More at make, match.

Noun[edit]

makker m (plural makkers, diminutive makkertje n)

  1. A mate, fellow, colleague, companion, (not very close) friend, ally etc.
Synonyms[edit]
