nylonkous (114848)



Etymology

Compound of nylon + kous.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ny‧lon‧kous

Noun

nylonkous f (plural nylonkousen, diminutive nylonkousje n)

  1. A nylon stocking.
