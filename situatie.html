situatie (1037)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch situacie, from Middle French situation, ultimately from Latin situātiō: equivalent to situeren +‎ -atie.

Noun[edit]

situatie f (plural situaties or situatiën, diminutive situatietje n)
