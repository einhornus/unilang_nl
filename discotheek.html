discotheek (29991)


Etymology

Borrowed from French discothèque (“record library”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌdɪs.koːˈteːk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dis‧co‧theek
  • Rhymes: -eːk

Noun

discotheek f (plural discotheken, diminutive discotheekje n)

  1. discotheque
  2. (dated) music or sound record library
