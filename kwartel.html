kwartel (27581)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch quattel, quattele, from Old Dutch *kwahtila, from Frankish *kwahtila.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkʋɑr.təl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kwar‧tel
  • Rhymes: -ɑrtəl

Noun

kwartel m (plural kwartels, diminutive kwarteltje n)

  1. quail, bird of the genus Coturnix
    Synonym: kwakkel
  2. common quail, Coturnix coturnix
    Synonym: kwakkel
