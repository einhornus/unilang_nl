kraus (69178)



Etymology

From Middle High German krūs (“frizzy”), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *krusl-, from Pre-Germanic *grus-, contracted from Proto-Indo-European *gurus- (“twist, curl”), same source as Persian گرس (gors, “braid of hair”).

Related to English curl and Dutch crullen (“to curl”).
