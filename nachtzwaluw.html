nachtzwaluw (35817)


Etymology

Compound of nacht (“night”) +‎ zwaluw (“swallow”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnɑxtˌsʋaː.lyu̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nacht‧zwa‧luw

Noun

nachtzwaluw f (plural nachtzwaluwen)

  1. European nightjar, Caprimulgus europaeus
    Synonym: geitenmelker
  2. nightjar, any bird of the family Caprimulgidae
