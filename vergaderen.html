vergaderen (12732)


Etymology[edit]

From ver- +‎ gaderen. See also vergaren, which derives from the same source, but with the later variant garen. Cognate with Low German vergadern.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

vergaderen

  1. (intransitive) to gather, to meet, to assemble
