actieradius (77530)



Etymology

Compound of actie +‎ radius.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑk.siˌraː.di.ʏs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ac‧tie‧ra‧di‧us

Noun

actieradius m (plural actieradii or actieradiussen)

  1. radius of action (radius or range in which something operate, or where a vehicle, vessel or aircraft can reach)
