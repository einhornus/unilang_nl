Etymology[edit]

From ont- +‎ lid +‎ -en.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɔntˈleː.dən/
  • Audio

Verb[edit]

ontleden

  1. to dismember, to analyze
  2. (grammar) to parse
  3. (biology) to dissect
  4. (chemistry) to break down
