ijscowagen (47201)



Etymology

Compound of ijsco + wagen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛi̯s.koːˌʋaː.ɣə(n)/
  • Hyphenation: ijs‧co‧wa‧gen

Noun

ijscowagen m (plural ijscowagens, diminutive ijscowagentje n)

  1. ice cream cart, ice cream van (vehicle used by ice cream vendors)
    Synonym: ijscokar
