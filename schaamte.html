schaamte (6086)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch schaemde. Equivalent to schamen +‎ -te.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsxaːm.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schaam‧te
  • Rhymes: -aːmtə

Noun

schaamte f (plural schaamten or schaamtes, diminutive schaamtetje n)

  1. feeling of shame (uncomfortable or painful feeling)

Related terms

  • beschaamdheid
  • beschamen
  • schande
