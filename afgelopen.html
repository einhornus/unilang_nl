afgelopen (814)


Etymology[edit]

Past participle of aflopen (“to end”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfxəˌloːpə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧ge‧lo‧pen

Adjective[edit]

afgelopen (not comparable)

  1. last, past
    afgelopen maand
    last month
  2. finished, ended
    De film is afgelopen.
    The movie has ended.

Participle[edit]

afgelopen

  1. past participle of aflopen
