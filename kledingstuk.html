kledingstuk (24906)


Etymology

From kleding (“clothing”) +‎ stuk (“piece”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkleːdɪŋˌstʏk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kle‧ding‧stuk

Noun

kledingstuk n (plural kledingstukken, diminutive kledingstukje n)

  1. A garment, a single item/article of clothing.

Related terms

  • aankleden
  • kledij
