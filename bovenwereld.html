bovenwereld (94911)



Etymology

Compound of boven + wereld.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈboː.və(n)ˌʋeː.rəlt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bo‧ven‧we‧reld

Noun

bovenwereld f (uncountable)

  1. The community of law-abiding people, the overworld.
  2. Heaven, an afterlife or domain above the everyday world.
