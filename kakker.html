Noun[edit]

kakker m (plural kakkers, diminutive kakkertje n)

  1. A stereotypical designation of someone, usually a Dutch adolescent, who is elite, posh, preppy, conceited. Commonly associated attributes include polo shirts, field hockey, and the accent (bekakt praten).

kakker

  1. iemand die kakt
  2. (informeel) bal d.w.z. verwaand, bekakt, persoon
