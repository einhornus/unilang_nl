geweld (1566)


Pronunciation

  • Hyphenation: ge‧weld
  • Rhymes: -ɛlt

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch gewelt, influenced by umlauted geweldich (modern geweldig), from Old Dutch giwalt.

Noun

geweld n (uncountable)

  1. violence
  2. strong or violent force; ferocity
Related terms
  • geweldig
