babysitter (9010)



Etymology

From baby + sitter.

Pronunciation

  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈbeɪbiˌsɪtɚ/

Noun

babysitter (plural babysitters)

  1. A person who cares for one or more babies or children for a short period of time in place of their legal guardians.
    My neighbor is sometimes a babysitter for other moms' children.

Related terms

  • babysit
  • house-sitter

Zelfstandig naamwoord

babysitter m

  1. iemand die, gewoonlijk tegen betaling, enige tijd oppast op iemands kind of baby
    • Het eindeloos huilende kind bracht de babysitter tot wanhoop.
Synoniemen
  • kinderoppas, babysit
