bezig (480)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bēsich, from Old Dutch *bisig, from Proto-West Germanic *bisīg.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeː.zəx/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧zig

Adjective

bezig (comparative beziger, superlative bezigst)

  1. busy, occupied
    Hij kan nu niet praten. Hij is bezig. ― He can not talk right now. He is busy.
