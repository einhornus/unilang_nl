Adjective[edit]

reëel (comparative reëler, superlative reëelst)

  1. real, actual
  2. realistic
    Synonym: realistisch
  3. (law) pertaining or relevant to a case; absolute
  4. (mathematics) real, pertaining to real numbers
  5. (economics) real, corrected for inflation, not nominal
