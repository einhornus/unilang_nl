leggen (1055)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch leggen, from Old Dutch leggen, from Proto-Germanic *lagjaną.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɛɣə(n)/

Verb[edit]

leggen

  1. (transitive) to lay
  2. (Holland, colloquial, proscribed) Alternative form of liggen.
