ribbenkast (30279)


Etymology

Compound of rib (“rib”) +‎ -en- +‎ kast (“case, close”). Cf. similar constructions in English language">English rib cage, German Rippenkorb.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɪ.bə(n)ˌkɑst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rib‧ben‧kast

Noun

ribbenkast f (plural ribbenkasten, diminutive ribbenkastje n)

  1. rib cage [from ca. 1840]
    De hongerstakers waren zo mager dat je de ribben onder de douche door hun ribbenkast kon tellen.
    The hunger strikers were so skinny one could count their ribs in the showers trough their rib cages.

Synonyms

  • borstkas

Zelfstandig naamwoord
