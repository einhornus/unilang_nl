beding (22316)


Etymology 1

From bedingen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈdɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧ding
  • Rhymes: -ɪŋ

Noun

beding n (plural bedingen)

  1. condition; often used in the construction onder geen beding, meaning “definitely not”

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch bedinge. Equivalent to beden +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeː.dɪŋ/
  • Hyphenation: be‧ding
  • Rhymes: -eːdɪŋ

Noun

beding n (plural bedingen)

  1. (obsolete) prayer, act of praying
    Synonyms: bede, gebed
