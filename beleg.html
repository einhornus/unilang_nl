Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch belegge, from beleggen (modern beleggen).

Noun[edit]

beleg n (uncountable)

  1. (military) siege on a city
  2. bread topping
Synonyms[edit]

(siege):

  • belegering

(bread topping):

  • broodbeleg
  • broodbelegsel
