lingerie (12290)


Etymology

Borrowed from French lingerie.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌlɪn.ʒəˈri/, /ˌlɛn.ʒəˈri/
  • Hyphenation: lin‧ge‧rie
  • Rhymes: -i

Noun

lingerie f (plural lingeries or lingerieën, diminutive lingerietje n)

  1. lingerie (women's underwear)
  2. (dated) lingerie (linens)
