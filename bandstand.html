bandstand (35336)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

A traditional bandstand in Greenwich Park, London

band +‎ stand

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Uk) IPA(key): /ˈbænd.stænd/

Noun[edit]

bandstand (plural bandstands)

  1. A small, open-air platform or enclosure for bands to play on, usually roofed.
  2. A small, informal stage, usually located in nightclubs, where local and amateur musicians perform.

Related terms[edit]

  • band rotunda
