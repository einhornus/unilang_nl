pastei (17791)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch pasteide.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pɑˈstɛi̯/, /pɑsˈtɛi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pas‧tei
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯

Noun

pastei f (plural pasteien, diminutive pasteitje n)

  1. savoury pie, pasty (often containing meat)
  2. pâté (now chiefly used in compounds)
    Synonym: paté

Related terms

  • paté
