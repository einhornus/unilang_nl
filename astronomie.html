Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch astronomie, from Latin astronomia, from Ancient Greek ἀστρονομία (astronomía), ἄστρον (ástron, “star”) + νόμος (nómos, “law”). Equivalent to astro- +‎ -nomie.
