werpster (96904)



Etymology 1

Compound of werpen (“to throw”) + ster (“star”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛrp.stɛr/
  • Hyphenation: werp‧ster

Noun

werpster m (plural werpsterren, diminutive werpsterretje n)

  1. throwing star, shuriken
    Synonym: shuriken

Etymology 2

From werpen (“to throw”) + -ster (feminine agentive suffix).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛrp.stər/
  • Hyphenation: werp‧ster

Noun

werpster f (plural werpsters, diminutive werpstertje n, masculine werper)

  1. thrower (feminine); a woman who throws
    Synonym: gooister
