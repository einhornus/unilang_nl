braaf (2785)


Etymology

Borrowed from French brave, borrowed from Italian bravo. More at brave.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /braːf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: braaf
  • Rhymes: -aːf

Adjective

braaf (comparative braver, superlative braafst)

  1. well-behaved, obedient
  2. (dated) honorable, virtuous

braaf

  1. bereid de regels in acht te nemen

  2. weinig vernieuwend
