  • Hyphenation: uit‧leg‧gen

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch uteleggen. Equivalent to uit (“out”) + leggen (“to lay”).

Verb

uitleggen

  1. (transitive) to explain
  2. (transitive) to interpret
