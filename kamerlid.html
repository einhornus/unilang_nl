kamerlid (38143)


Etymology

From kamer (“chamber”) +‎ lid (“member”).

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

kamerlid n (plural kamerleden, diminutive kamerlidje n)

  1. (politics) A member of the Dutch Senate (Eerste Kamer) or House of Representatives (Tweede Kamer)
    Synonym: parlementariër
