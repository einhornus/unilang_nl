Etymology 1[edit]

From aanhangen. Equivalent to aan (“on”) +‎ hang (“support”).

Noun[edit]

aanhang m (uncountable)

  1. (collective) support, the totality of supporters
  2. the collection of romantic partners of a group of friends / family
  3. appendix (that which is appended)
