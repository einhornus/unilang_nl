bleekgezicht (58006)



Etymology

Compound of bleek (“pale, bleak”) +‎ gezicht (“(human) face”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbleːk.xəˌzɪxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bleek‧ge‧zicht

Noun

bleekgezicht n (plural bleekgezichten, diminutive bleekgezichtje n)

  1. paleface, an ethnic European as opposed to a native redskin (typically used in fiction as a stereotypical native American idiom)
