republiek (5602)



Etymology

Borrowed from French république, from Latin rēspūblica or rēs pūblica.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌreːpyˈblik/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: re‧pu‧bliek
  • Rhymes: -ik

Noun

republiek f (plural republieken, diminutive republiekje n)

  1. republic (type of state)

Related terms

  • republikein

Zelfstandig naamwoord

republiek v
