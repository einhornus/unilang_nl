Noun[edit]

fontein f (plural fonteinen, diminutive fonteintje n)

  1. (obsolete) A well, spring
  2. A fountain, monumental or small artificial water feature
  3. (obsolete) The water which springs from one of the above; any water
  4. A drink - and/or or wash basin with a tab

Synonyms[edit]

  • (well) bron
  • (water from well or fountain) fonteinwater n, bronwater n
  • (basin) drinkbekken n, wasbekken n
