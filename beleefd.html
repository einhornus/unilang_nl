beleefd (3105)



Etymology

From beleefd, in the older sense "to observe (a custom)". The word, itself a conjugation of beleven, thus originally meant "observing custom or protocol".

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈleːft/

Adjective

beleefd (comparative beleefder, superlative beleefdst)
