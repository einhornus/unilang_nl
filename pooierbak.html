pooierbak (67988)



Etymology

Compound of pooier (“pimp”) +‎ bak (“car, ride”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpoːi̯ərˌbɑk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pooi‧er‧bak

Noun

pooierbak m (plural pooierbakken, diminutive pooierbakje n)

  1. (colloquial) a pimpmobile, a pimped up car.
    • 2012, Dave Nieuwenburg, Een Na Twaalf, self-published, second edition (first edition from 2009), 60.
