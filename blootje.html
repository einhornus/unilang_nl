blootje (30679)


Etymology

Substantivated diminutive from bloot (“(the) bare”) +‎ -je.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbloːt.jə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bloot‧je
  • Homophone: blowtje

Noun

blootje n (plural blootjes)

  1. The bare, a state of total nudity
  2. (figuratively) An embarrassing state of exposure, as to ridicule or criticism

Synonyms

  • (naked state): adamskostuum, bloterik, flikker
