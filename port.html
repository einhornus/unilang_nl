Etymology 2

Borrowed from English language">English port, from port wine. Named for Portuguese pt">Porto, a city in Portugal where the wines were originally shipped from.

Noun

port m (uncountable, diminutive portje n)

  1. (a glass of) port, port wine, Porto

  2. (archaic) plural imperative of porren
