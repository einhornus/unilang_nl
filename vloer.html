Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch vloer, from Old Dutch *fluor, from Proto-Germanic *flōraz.

Noun[edit]

vloer m (plural vloeren, diminutive vloertje n)

  1. A floor
  2. (figuratively) Any ground, surface
  3. (figuratively) The bottom, lowest level
