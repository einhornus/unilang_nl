antistof (77361)



Etymology

Probably borrowed from German Antistoff. Equivalent to anti- + stof.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑn.tiˌstɔf/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: an‧ti‧stof

Noun

antistof m (plural antistoffen, diminutive antistofje n)

  1. (immunology, countable) antibody [from ca. 1900]
    Synonym: antilichaam

Zelfstandig naamwoord

antistof v / m
