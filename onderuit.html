onderuit (5882)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

onderuit

  1. out from underneath (something)
  2. losing support (of one's legs, etc.),
    Op het gladde ijs kun je makkelijk onderuit gaan.
    On the slippery ice you can easily slip and fall. (literally: go from underneath)

Interjection

onderuit

  1. timber!
