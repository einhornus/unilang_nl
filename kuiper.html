kuiper (54055)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch cuper. Equivalent to kuipen + -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkœy̯.pər/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kui‧per
  • Rhymes: -œy̯pər

Noun

kuiper m (plural kuipers, diminutive kuipertje n, feminine kuiperin)

  1. cooper
  2. intriguer

Synonyms

  • tonnenmaker, vatenmaker

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kuiper m
