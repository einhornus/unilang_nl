vissen (1615)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɪ.sə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch visschen, from Old Dutch *fiskon, from Proto-Germanic *fiskōną. Equivalent to vis +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

vissen

  1. to fish, to try to catch fish
  2. to fish (trying to get information)

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun[edit]

vissen

  1. Plural form of vis

Werkwoord

vissen

  1. inergatief vis proberen te vangen
  2. iets proberen te weten te komen, proberen iemand iets te laten zeggen
