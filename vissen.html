vissen (1615)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɪ.sə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪsən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch visschen, from Old Dutch *fiskon, from Proto-Germanic *fiskōną. Equivalent to vis +‎ -en.

Verb

vissen

  1. to fish, to try to catch fish
  2. to fish (trying to get information)
