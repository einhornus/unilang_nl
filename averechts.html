averechts (42591)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch averechts (“backwards, in the wrong direction”), from averecht (“upside down, backwards, wrong”), from ave +‎ recht, equivalent to af +‎ rechts

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaː.vəˌrɛx(t)s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ave‧rechts

Adjective

averechts (comparative averechtser, superlative meest averechts or averechtst)

  1. opposite, backwards, contrary
    Wat je nu doet werkt averechts.
    What you're doing now has the opposite effect of what you're trying to achieve.
