footballer (70092)



Etymology

football + -er

Pronunciation

  • (Uk) IPA(key): /ˈfʊtbɔːlə/
  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈfʊtbɔːləɹ/

Noun

footballer (plural footballers)

  1. One who plays association football.
    • 2004, George Best, Scoring At Half-Time: Adventures On and Off the Pitch, →ISBN, page 259:
      It is footballers though, rather than commentators, presenters or pundits, who when interviewed have provided some of the best Colemanballs.

Synonyms

  • (one who plays football): football player, soccer player (Us, Canada)
