pillendoos (40321)



Etymology

Compound of pil +‎ -en- +‎ doos.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɪ.lə(n)ˌdoːs/
  • Hyphenation: pil‧len‧doos

Noun

pillendoos f (plural pillendozen, diminutive pillendoosje n)

  1. A pillbox (box for pills).
  2. A pillbox (bunker).
