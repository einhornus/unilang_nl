kijken (233)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch kiken, from Old Dutch *kīkan, from Proto-Germanic *kīkaną[1].

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɛi̯kə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯kən

Verb

kijken

  1. (intransitive) to look [+ naar (object) = at]
  2. (transitive) to watch (e.g. a film), to observe

kijken
