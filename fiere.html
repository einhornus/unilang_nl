fiere (57559)



Etymology

From Old Frisian fēra, from Proto-Germanic *fōrijaną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈfiə̯rə/

Verb

fiere

  1. to lead
  2. to carry, to transport
  3. to carry along
  4. to push
