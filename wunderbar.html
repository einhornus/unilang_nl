wunderbar (58492)



Etymology

From Middle High German wunderbære (also wunderberen, wunderberende), equivalent to Wunder + -bar. Akin to German Low German wunnerbaar, Low German wunnerbor, Dutch wonderbaar, Saterland Frisian wunderboar, Swedish underbar.
