perestroika (101397)



Etymology

Pronunciation

  • (Us) IPA(key): /pɛɹ.ɪˈstɹɔi.kə/
  • Audio (original Russian)
    		(file)

Noun

perestroika (plural perestroikas)

  1. (singularity theory) A situation where a small variation of parameters leads to a sudden change in properties.
