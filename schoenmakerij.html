schoenmakerij (88392)



Etymology

From schoenmaker + -ij.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌsxun.maː.kəˈrɛi̯/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schoen‧ma‧ke‧rij

Noun

schoenmakerij f (plural schoenmakerijen)

  1. the shoemaker's profession, the shoe-making sector
  2. a business where shoes are produced, repaired and sold, a shoemaker's shop
