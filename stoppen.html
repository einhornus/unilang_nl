Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch stoppen, from Old Dutch stuppon, from Proto-Germanic *stuppōną. More at stop.

Verb[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">stoppen

<ol>
  • to stop, to halt
    Ze zijn gestopt met werken.
    They have stopped working.
  • to plug
    Het lek is gestopt.
    The leak has been plugged.
  • to stuff, to put, to insert
    Stop dat daar maar ergens tussen.
    Stuff that in between there somewhere.
    Stop de stekker in het stopcontact.
    Insert the plug into the socket.
  • to darn
    Hij heeft zijn sokken gestopt.
    He has darned his socks.
