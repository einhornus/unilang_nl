bommelding (26312)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bom‧mel‧ding

Noun

bommelding f (plural bommeldingen, diminutive bommeldinkje n)

  1. bomb report, bomb threat

bommelding v
