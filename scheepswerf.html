scheepswerf (59305)



Etymology

From scheep (“ship”) +‎ -s- +‎ werf (“wharf”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsxeːps.ʋɛrf/
  • Audio (Belgium)

  • Audio

Noun

scheepswerf f (plural scheepswerven, diminutive scheepswerfje n)

  1. A shipyard, place and/or firm for building and/or repairing ships

Related terms

  • bouwwerf
