vanaf (597)


Etymology[edit]

From van +‎ af. Compare West Frisian fanôf.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • audio
    		(file)

Preposition[edit]

vanaf

  1. starting from, from onwards, as of, as from
    Je rijbewijs is pas vanaf maandag geldig.
    Your driving licence is only valid from Monday onwards.
  2. from on top of
    Er staat iemand vanaf het dak te roepen.
    There's someone shouting from the top of the roof.
