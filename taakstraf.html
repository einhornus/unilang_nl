<br>

Etymology[edit]

taak (“task”) +‎ straf (“punishment”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: taak‧straf

Noun[edit]

taakstraf f or m (plural taakstraffen, diminutive taakstrafje n)

  1. community service as a substitute for a jail sentence.

Synonyms[edit]

  • werkstraf
