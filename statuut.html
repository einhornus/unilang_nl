statuut (37724)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch statute, from Old French statut, from Latin statūtum.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /staːˈtyt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sta‧tuut
  • Rhymes: -yt

Noun

statuut n (plural statuten, diminutive statuutje n)

  1. statute, legislation, regulation; written collection of laws or rules
  2. statute; written rule or principle, especially of a society or other institution
  3. (Belgium) legal status, legal position
  4. (Netherlands) ordinance, law

Related terms

  • status
