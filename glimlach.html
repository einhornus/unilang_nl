Etymology 1

From glimmen (“to glow; shine”) +‎ lach, perhaps euphemistically remodelling an older form, grimlach, which carried a somewhat negative connotation.

Noun

glimlach m (plural glimlachen, diminutive glimlachje n)

  1. smile
