geniaal (3815)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from German genial ("relating to a [mythological] genius"), from genialisch, from Latin genius.

  • IPA(key): /ɣeː.niˈjaːl/

Adjective[edit]

geniaal (comparative genialer, superlative geniaalst)

  1. brilliant, genius, genial
    Synonym: genialisch
