Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch dôpen, from Old Dutch dōpen, from Proto-Germanic *daupijaną.

Verb

dopen

  1. (transitive) to dip, to immerse
  2. (transitive, Christianity) to baptize, to christen
  3. (transitive) to name (give a name to)
    Synonym: noemen
