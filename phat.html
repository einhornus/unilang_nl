phat (68062)



Etymology

phat (comparative phatter, superlative phattest)

  1. (slang, originally African-American Vernacular) Excellent; cool; very good.
  2. (slang) Sexy.
  3. (slang, music) Rich in texture; prominent.
    The song has a phat bass line.

Synonyms

  • (slang: excellent): cool (slang), excellent, fab (dated slang), rad (dated slang), super, wicked (slang)
  • (slang: sexy): foxy (slang), hot (slang)
  • (in music: rich in texture, prominent): prominent, rocking (slang), juicy
