hebbeding (116927)



Etymology

From hebben (“to have”) + ding (“thing”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛbəˌdɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: heb‧be‧ding

Noun

hebbeding n (plural hebbedingen, diminutive hebbedingetje n)

  1. (obsolete) piece of junk
  2. gadget, gimmick, trinket (desirable but often depreciated commodity)
