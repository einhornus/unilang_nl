strontziek (72629)



Etymology

Compound of stront +‎ ziek.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /strɔntˈsik/
  • Hyphenation: stront‧ziek

Adjective

strontziek (not comparable)

  1. (informal) very ill, very sick
  2. (informal, by extension) fed up, sick and tired
