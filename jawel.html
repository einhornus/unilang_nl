jawel (710)


Etymology

Compound of ja (“yes”) +‎ wel (“well, indeed”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /jaːˈʋɛl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ja‧wel
  • Rhymes: -ɛl

Adverb

jawel

  1. An emphatic yes, to indicate strong agreement, or dissent in reply to a negative statement or question
    "Jawel", zei ze, "ik denk wel dat dat juist is."
    "Yes" she said, "I do think that that is true."
Verwante begrippen
  • ja, nee, neen
