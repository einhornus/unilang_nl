Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch stael, from Old Dutch stāl, from earlier *stahal, from Proto-Germanic *stahlą.

Cognate with German Stahl, Danish stål, Icelandic stál, English steel, West Frisian stiel.

Noun[edit]

staal n (uncountable)

  1. steel

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch stael, from Old French estal, from Frankish *stalo, from Proto-Germanic *stalô (“staff”). See also steel.

Noun[edit]

staal n (plural stalen, diminutive staaltje n)

  1. sample
    staal zonder waarde
    worthless sample
