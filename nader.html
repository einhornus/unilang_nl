nader (5009)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnaː.dər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: na‧der
  • Rhymes: -aːdər

Etymology 1

An old comparative of na, from which also naar originates. Compare also English near.

Adjective

nader (not comparable)

  1. closer, more nearby
  2. more precise
    Dat moet nader worden bestudeerd.
    That must be studied in more detail.
