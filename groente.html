Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">groente f (plural groenten or groentes, diminutive groentje n or groentetje n)

<ol>
  • (uncountable, collective) Vegetables.
  • (countable) A (type of) vegetable.

    • Usage notes[edit]

    • Groente is typically not used for a single piece of vegetable; instead that is called (een) stuk groente. However, it is used as a countable noun in both the singular and plural for a particular type (species, genus, cultivar, etc.) of vegetable.
    • While groentetje is the regular form of the diminutive, it is not often used, likely because of its more difficult, less smooth pronunciation. Groentje is the commonly used dimunitive.
