stinky (25160)


Etymology

From English">Middle English stinky, stynky, equivalent to stink +‎ -y. Compare Dutch stinkig (“stinky, foul, rotten”), German stinkig (“stinky”), Hunsrik stinkich.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɪŋki/
  • Rhymes: -ɪŋki

Adjective

stinky (comparative stinkier or more stinky, superlative stinkiest or most stinky)

  1. Having a strong, unpleasant smell; stinking.
  2. (informal) Bad, undesirable.
