Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch hornte, from Old Dutch *hornuta, from Proto-Germanic *hurznutō, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂s- (“hornet”). Compare German Hornisse, English hornet.

Noun

hoorntje n (plural hoorntjes)

  1. Diminutive of hoorn: hornet
