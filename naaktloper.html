naaktloper (90364)



Etymology

From naakt +‎ lopen +‎ -er.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnaːktˌloː.pər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: naakt‧lo‧per

Noun

naaktloper m (plural naaktlopers, diminutive naaktlopertje n, feminine naaktloopster)

  1. A nudist.
    Synonym: nudist
  2. (historical) A 16th-century Anabaptist practicing nudism.
