uitdelen (8381)


Etymology[edit]

From uit +‎ delen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈœy̯ˌdeːlə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

uitdelen

  1. to deal out, to distribute, to share
  2. (mathematics) to divide out
