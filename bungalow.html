Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English bungalow, from Hindi बंगला (baṅglā).

Pronunciation[edit]

Noun[edit]

bungalow m (plural bungalows, diminutive bungalowtje n)

  1. A bungalow (small holiday home, usually single-storey).
