gatlikker (106616)



Etymology

From gat (“hole, arse”) +‎ likker (“licker”).

Pronunciation

Noun

gatlikker m (plural gatlikkers, diminutive gatlikkertje n)

  1. (vulgar) sycophant, arse-kisser, shameless or even slavish flatterer
