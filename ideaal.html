ideaal (6005)



Etymology

From French idéal, from Medieval Latin ideālis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌi.deːˈaːl/, [ˌide(ː)ˈjaːl]
  • ideaal

  • Hyphenation: ide‧aal
  • Rhymes: -aːl

Adjective

ideaal (comparative idealer, superlative ideaalst)

  1. ideal, perfect(ly fit); optimal
  2. immaterial, aetheral
  3. purely cerebral, imaginary in physical terms

ideaal n (plural idealen, diminutive ideaaltje n)

  1. An ideal, perfect standard (of beauty, intellect, etc.)
  2. Something/someone which lives up to it, perfect example
  3. A utopic, unreal perfect aspiration, figment
  4. (algebra) An ideal.

  • (utopic figment) droombeeld, herschenschim
