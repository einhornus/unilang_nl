Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch vergieten. Equivalent to ver- +‎ gieten.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /vərˈɣi.tə(n)/, /vɛrˈɣi.tə(n)/

Verb[edit]

vergieten

  1. (transitive) to spill, to shed
  2. (transitive) to drain, to pour over
  3. (transitive, obsolete) to make liquid, to liquidise
