schoonzus (14314)



Etymology

From schoon +‎ zus, calque of Middle French belle-soeur.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsxoːn.zʏs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schoon‧zus

Noun

schoonzus f (plural schoonzussen, diminutive schoonzusje n)

  1. sister-in-law (brother's wife or spouse's sister)

Synonyms

schoonzus v
