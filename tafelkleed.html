tafelkleed (25153)


Etymology

Compound of tafel (“table”) +‎ kleed (“cloth, cover”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtaː.fəlˌkleːt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ta‧fel‧kleed

Noun

tafelkleed n (plural tafelkleden, diminutive tafelkleedje n)

  1. A tablecloth

Synonyms

  • tafellaken
