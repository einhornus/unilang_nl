aanplakbiljet (71355)



Etymology

Compound of aanplakken (“to affix”) + biljet (“note, piece of paper”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːn.plɑk.bɪlˌjɛt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aan‧plak‧bil‧jet

Noun

aanplakbiljet n (plural aanplakbiljetten, diminutive aanplakbiljetje n)

  1. A placard, a poster containing an announcement or promotional information hung in a public space.
    Synonym: affiche

Zelfstandig naamwoord
