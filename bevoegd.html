bevoegd (10471)



Etymology

Probably borrowed from German befugt. Attested since the late seventeenth century. Similar forms appear earlier in the seventeenth century, which were also likely based on the German root.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈvuxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧voegd
  • Rhymes: -uxt

Adjective

bevoegd (not comparable)

  1. competent
  2. qualified, authorised, having permission
