Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">schoonbroer m (plural schoonbroers, diminutive schoonbroertje n)

<ol>
  • (chiefly Belgium) brother-in-law

    • Usage notes[edit]

    The word schoonbroer is mostly used in Belgium and the southern Dutch provinces Limburg and North Brabant. In the rest of the Netherlands, the more common word for “brother-in-law” is zwager, but schoonbroer is naturally understood from association.
