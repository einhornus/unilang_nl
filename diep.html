diep (1062)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch diep, from Old Dutch *diop, from Proto-Germanic *deupaz, from Proto-Indo-European *d ∞ewb-. Compare Low German deep, West Frisian djip, English deep, German tief, Danish dyb, Norwegian dyp.

Adjective

diep (comparative dieper, superlative diepst)

  1. deep, down low
  2. profound
    Synonyms: diepgaand, diepzinnig
