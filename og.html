og (34869)



Etymology

From Old Norse ok (“and, also”), from Proto-Germanic *auk. Cognate with Swedish och (“and”), ock (“also”), Dutch ook (“also”), and German auch (“also”).

Pronunciation

IPA(key): [ʌ], [ɒw]

Conjunction

og

  1. and

Adverb

og

  1. (archaic, dialect) also
    Synonym: også
