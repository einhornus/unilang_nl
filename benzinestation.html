benzinestation (11101)


Etymology

Compound of benzine +‎ station.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bɛnˈzi.nə.staːˌʃɔn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ben‧zi‧ne‧sta‧ti‧on

Noun

benzinestation n (plural benzinestations, diminutive benzinestationnetje n)

  1. service station or petrol station (Uk); gas station (Us)

Synonyms

  • tankstation
  • pompstation
