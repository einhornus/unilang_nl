Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Germanic *hauhiþō, equivalent to hoog +‎ -te. Compare English height, Norwegian høyde, Swedish höjd, Icelandic hæð, Old High German hohida, Gothic hauhiþa (hauhiþa).

hoogte f (plural hoogtes or hoogten)
