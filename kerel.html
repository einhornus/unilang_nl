Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch kerel, carel, caerl, kerl, from Old Dutch *karl, *karal, *keril, from Proto-Germanic *karilaz.

Cognate with German Kerl, Saxon">Dutch Low Saxon keerl, West Frisian tsjirl, English churl, Danish karl.
