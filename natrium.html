natrium (51179)



Etymology

Borrowed from German Natrium, from New Latin natrium. Coined by Jöns Jacob Berzelius.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnaː.triˌʏm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: na‧tri‧um
  • Rhymes: -aːtriʏm

Noun

natrium n (uncountable)

  1. sodium (a reactive metal) [from 1830s]

Related terms

  • natron
  • nitrisch
