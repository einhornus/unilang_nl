Noun[edit]

spleet f (plural spleten, diminutive spleetje n)

  1. crack, cleft, split
  2. fissure, slit
  3. (childish, especially in the diminutive) vagina
  4. (vulgar) anus

Synonyms[edit]

  • (anus): gat, hol, reet
