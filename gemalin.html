gemalin (75798)



Etymology

From gemaal + -in, calque of German Gemahlin.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˌmaːˈlɪn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧ma‧lin
  • Rhymes: -ɪn

Noun

gemalin f (plural gemalinnen, diminutive gemalinnetje n)

  1. wife with an important role, important female spouse, consort
