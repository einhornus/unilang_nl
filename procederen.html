procederen (30991)



Etymology

from French procéder, from Latin procedere (“to go forward, advance”), itself from pro- (“forward”) + cedere (“to go”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

procederen

  1. (intransitive) To procede, start/follow a procedure, operate
  2. (intransitive) To litigate, resort to a legal procedure
