halsdoek (34186)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch halsdoec. Equivalent to hals + doek. Cognates include German Halstuch.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɑls.duk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hals‧doek

Noun

halsdoek m (plural halsdoeken, diminutive halsdoekje n)

  1. neckerchief

Zelfstandig naamwoord

halsdoek m [1]
