kinkel (38127)


Etymology

From kink (“kink, twist or bend”) +‎ -el.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɪŋ.kəl/
  • Hyphenation: kin‧kel
  • Rhymes: -ɪŋkəl

Noun

kinkel m (plural kinkels)

  1. A rube, a yokel, an unsophisticated person.
