schijt (5374)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sxɛɪt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯t
  • Homophone: scheit

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schite, from Old Dutch skīta, from Proto-Germanic *skītaz, *skitiz. Cognate to Low German Schiet, German Scheiße, English shit, Danish skid, Swedish skit.

Noun

schijt m (uncountable)

  1. (vulgar) shit (solid excretory product evacuated from the bowel)
    Synonym: skitta
Related terms
  • schijten

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

schijt

  1. first-, second- and third-person singular present indicative of schijten
  2. imperative of schijten
