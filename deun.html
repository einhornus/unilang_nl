deun (107693)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch duene, from Old Dutch *duni, from Proto-Germanic *duniz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwen-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /døːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: deun
  • Rhymes: -øːn

Noun

deun m (plural deunen, diminutive deuntje n)

  1. tune
    Synonyms: wijs, wijsje
