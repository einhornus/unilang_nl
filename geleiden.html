Etymology[edit]

Verb[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">geleiden

<ol>
  • (transitive) to escort, to accompany
  • (transitive) to guide (along a certain path)
  • (transitive) to give guidance (especially moral)
  • (intransitive) to conduct (electricity)
  • (transitive, obsolete) to accompany (a piece, musician etc.)
