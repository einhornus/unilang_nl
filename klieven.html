klieven (104315)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch clieven, from Old Dutch *clievan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklivən/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ivən

Verb

klieven

  1. to cleave, to cut
    Synonym: kloven
  2. to cut through (waves)
