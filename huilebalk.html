Noun[edit]

<strong class="Latn headword" lang="nl">huilebalk m (plural huilebalken, diminutive huilebalkje n)

<ol>
  • A crybaby, a child that weeps often and long
    De kinderopvang werd bevolkt door huilebalken.
    The daycare was populated by crybabies.
  • (by extension) Any weepy person, regardless of age
    Wees niet zo'n huilebalk, de tandarts is er niet om je kwaad te doen.
    Don't be such a crybaby, the dentist doesn't intend to harm you.
  • A type of round felt hat with a wide, soft rim and a low crown
    De koetsier van de lijkwagen nam zijn huilebalk af, toen hij langs de nabestaanden reed.
    The driver of the hearse took his broad-brim hat off when he rode past the deceased's survivors.
