hartstikke (2339)


Etymology

Compound of hart (“heart”) +‎ steek (“stab”). Originally used as an intensifier in the phrase hartstikke dood, usage subsequently spread to other constructions.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɑrtˌstɪ.kə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hart‧stik‧ke

Adverb

hartstikke

  1. (informal) very, freaking, hella
    De reactie van de premier was dat het hartstikke gaaf was.
    The prime minister's response was that it was frigging cool.
