achterop (9216)


Etymology

From achter (“behind”) +‎ op (“on”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑx.təˈrɔp/, /ˌɑx.tərˈɔp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ah‧ter‧op
  • Rhymes: -ɔp

Adverb

achterop

  1. on the back/rear, behind

Related terms

  • achterin

Preposition

achterop

  1. on the back/rear of

    The nutritional value is on the back of the box.

Synonyms
