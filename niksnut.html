niksnut (20264)


Etymology

From niks (“nothing”) +‎ nut (“use”), calque of German Nichtsnutz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈnɪksnʏt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: niksnut

Noun

niksnut m (plural niksnutten, diminutive niksnutje n or niksnutteken n)

  1. good-for-nothing [from ca. 1900]

Synonyms

  • nietsnut

Zelfstandig naamwoord
