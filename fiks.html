Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: fiks
  • Rhymes: -ɪks

Etymology 1

Borrowed from Middle French fixe, from Latin fixus.

Adjective

fiks (comparative fikser, superlative meest fiks or fikst)

  1. strong
  2. large, sizable
  3. (obsolete) solid, fixed
