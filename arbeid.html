arbeid (12953)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch arbeit, from Old Dutch arbeit, from Proto-Germanic *arbaidiz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑrbɛi̯t/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ar‧beid
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

arbeid m (uncountable)

  1. labour, work
    werk
  2. labour (act of childbirth)

arbeid

  2. (economie) de primaire productiefactor
