loopy (64159)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈluːpi/
  • Audio (Au)

Etymology

loop + -y

Adjective

loopy (comparative loopier, superlative loopiest)

  1. Having loops.
    A loopy rollercoaster.
  2. (slang) Idiotic, crazy or drunk.
