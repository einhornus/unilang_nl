dorpeling (42387)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From dorp +‎ -e- +‎ ling.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdɔr.pəˌlɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: dor‧pe‧ling

Noun[edit]

dorpeling m (plural dorpelingen, diminutive dorpelingetje n, feminine dorpelinge)

  1. villager
  2. rustic, (simple) countryman
  3. boor, country bumpkin, yokel

Synonyms[edit]

  • (villager): dorper, dorpsbewoner
  • (rustic): boer, landman, plattelander
  • (boor): boerenkinkel, boerenpummel, dorper
