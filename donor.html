donor (10677)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English donor, from Middle English donoure, from Old French doneur.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoː.nɔr/
  • Hyphenation: do‧nor

Noun[edit]

donor m (plural donoren or donors)

  1. A donor.
    Synonyms: donateur, gever, schenker
  2. An organ donor.
    Synonym: orgaandonor

Related terms[edit]

  • donateur
  • donatie
  • doneren

Zelfstandig naamwoord

donor
