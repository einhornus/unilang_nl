brandstichting (11214)


Etymology

From brandstichten +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: brand‧stich‧ting

Noun

brandstichting f (plural brandstichtingen, diminutive brandstichtinkje n)

  1. (law) arson (crime of setting a fire)

Related terms

  • brandstichtster
