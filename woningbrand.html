woningbrand (96766)



Etymology

Compound of woning +‎ brand.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋoː.nɪŋˌbrɑnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wo‧ning‧brand

Noun

woningbrand m (plural woningbranden, diminutive woningbrandje n)

  1. residential fire, housefire (disastrous fire in a residence)
    Synonym: huisbrand
