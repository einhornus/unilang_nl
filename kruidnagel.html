kruidnagel (47060)


Etymology

Compound of kruid +‎ nagel.

Pronunciation

Noun

kruidnagel m (plural kruidnagelen or kruidnagels, diminutive kruidnageltje n)

  1. clove, the dried, often ground flower buds of Syzygium aromaticum
