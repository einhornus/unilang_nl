belastinggeld (17532)


Etymology

Compound of belasting (“tax”) +‎ geld (“money”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈlɑs.tɪŋˌɣɛlt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧las‧ting‧geld

Noun

belastinggeld n (plural belastinggelden)

  1. tax money

Usage notes

Very commonly used as a political spin term for “public money” or “government money”, which would be publiek geld or overheidsgeld respectively. This usage is considered incorrect by economists.
