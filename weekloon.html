weekloon (55446)



Etymology

Compound of week + loon (“pay, wages”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋeːk.loːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: week‧loon

Noun

weekloon n (plural weeklonen, diminutive weeklontje n)

  1. A week's pay, weekly wage.
    Als zijn karig weekloon twee dagen te laat komt hongert zijn gezin.
    When his meager week's pay is two days late, his family goes hungry.

Related terms

  • weekdag m
  • weekeinde n
  • jaarloon n
  • loontrekkend (adjective)
