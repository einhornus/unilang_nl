overal (535)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch overal, from Old Dutch overal.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈoː.vəˌrɑl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: over‧al

Adverb

overal

  1. everywhere, anywhere
    Ik heb hem overal gezocht, maar ik kon hem niet vinden. ― I've looked for him everywhere, but I couldn't find him.
    Het is klein, zo het had overal verborgen kunnen zijn. ― It is small, so it could have been hidden anywhere.
  2. all over, throughout (present all over a place)

Etymology 2

Borrowed from English overall.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌoː.vəˈrɑl/
  • Hyphenation: over‧al

Noun

overal m (plural overals, diminutive overalletje n)

  1. overall
Synoniemen
  • alom, allerwegen, wijd en zijd

Zelfstandig naamwoord

overal m

  1. overall, werkkleding die zoals de naam al aangeeft over andere kleding gedragen wordt.
  2. (scheepvaart) reveille.
Synoniemen
  • [1] overall
  • [2] reveille
