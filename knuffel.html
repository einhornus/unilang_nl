Etymology 1[edit]

Noun[edit]

knuffel m (plural knuffels, diminutive knuffeltje n)

  1. hug, cuddle
  2. stuffed toy, plushie, soft toy (stuffed doll)
Synonyms[edit]
  • (stuffed toy): knuffelbeest, knuffeldier
