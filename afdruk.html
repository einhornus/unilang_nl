afdruk (7035)



Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑvdrʏk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧druk

Etymology 1

From afdrukken.

Noun

afdruk m (plural afdrukken, diminutive afdrukje n)

  1. print (something printed with a printer or printing press)
  2. impression, print (relief or other result from applying pressure)

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

afdruk

  1. first-person singular present indicative of afdrukken (when using a subclause)
