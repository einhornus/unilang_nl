creëren (4351)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin creō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /kreːˈeːrə(n)/
  • Rhymes: -eːrə(n)
  • creëren
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

creëren

  1. (transitive) to create, make, put into existence
  2. (transitive) to design creatively
  3. (transitive) to appoint, invest with an office etc.
  4. (transitive) to establish, erect, found
  5. (transitive) to cause, be responsible for

Synonyms[edit]

(put into existence):

  • maken
  • scheppen

(design):

  • ontwerpen

(appoint):

  • aanstellen

(establish):

  • instellen

(cause):

  • veroorzaken
