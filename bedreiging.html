bedreiging (3113)


Etymology[edit]

From bedreigen +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bəˈdrɛi̯.ɣɪŋ/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧drei‧ging
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯ɣɪŋ

Noun[edit]

bedreiging f (plural bedreigingen, diminutive bedreiginkje n)

  1. threat, menace (something that poses a risk)
  2. threat, menace (that which expresses a threat)

Related terms[edit]
