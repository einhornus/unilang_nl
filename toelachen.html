toelachen (42944)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch toelachen. Equivalent to toe +‎ lachen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtuˌlɑ.xə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: toe‧la‧chen

Verb

toelachen

  1. (transitive) to smile at, to smile toward
  2. (transitive) to smile on, to be beneficial or favourable
