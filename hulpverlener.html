hulpverlener (43655)



Etymology

Compound of hulp + verlener.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦʏlp.fərˌleː.nər/, /ˈɦʏlp.fɛrˌleː.nər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hulp‧ver‧le‧ner

Noun

hulpverlener m (plural hulpverleners, diminutive hulpverlenertje n, feminine hulpverleenster)

  1. aid worker
  2. first responder

Zelfstandig naamwoord

hulpverlener m
