Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch ontbijt, also inbijt, equivalent to ont- +‎ bijt. Cognate with German Imbiss (“snack”).

Noun[edit]

ontbijt n (plural ontbijten, diminutive ontbijtje n)

  1. breakfast

Verb[edit]

ontbijt

  2. imperative of ontbijten
