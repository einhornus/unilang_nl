Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛrvə(n)/
  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch erven, from Proto-Germanic *arbijaną, denominative from *arbiją, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃erbʰ- (“change allegiance or status or ownership”). Equivalent to erf +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

erven

  1. (transitive) to inherit
