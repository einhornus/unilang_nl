doortrapt (28240)


Etymology

Equivalent to door- +‎ getrapt. It appears to be derived from a verb door- +‎ trappen, which is unattested.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

doortrapt (comparative doortrapter, superlative doortraptst)

  1. shrewd, cunning
  2. mean, malevolent
