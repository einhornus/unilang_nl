lankmoedig (81923)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch lancmoedich, equivalent to lang +‎ moed +‎ -ig, calque of Latin longanimis. Compare Old High German lancmuotig (German langmütig), Middle Low German lankmodich, Gothic 𐌻𐌰𐌲𐌲𐌰𐌼𐍉𐌳𐌴𐌹 (laggamōdei, “longanimity”).

Pronunciation

Adjective

lankmoedig (comparative lankmoediger, superlative lankmoedigst)
