geschut (8574)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch geschutte, from Proto-Germanic *gaskutją. Related to schieten.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈsxʏt/
  • (Southern) IPA(key): [ɣəˈsxœt]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧schut
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

geschut n (uncountable)

  1. one or more guns or cannons, generally of heavy calibre
  2. artillery
