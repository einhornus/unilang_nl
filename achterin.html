achterin (2553)

From achter (“behind”) +‎ in (“in”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑx.təˈrɪn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ach‧ter‧in
  • Rhymes: -ɪn

Adverb

achterin

  1. in the back/rear

Related terms

  • achteraan
  • achterop

Preposition

achterin

  1. in the back/rear of

    The groceries are in the back of the car.
