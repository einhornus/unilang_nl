klikspaan (21046)


Etymology

Compound of klikken +‎ spaan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklik.spaːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: klik‧spaan

Noun

klikspaan m or f (plural klikspanen, diminutive klikspaantje n)

  1. (childish) snitch, telltale
    Synonym: klapspaan
  2. (obsolete) gossip, blab, gossiper
