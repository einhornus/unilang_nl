Pronunciation[edit]

  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ant‧woor‧den

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch antworden, from Old Dutch antworden, from Proto-Germanic *andawurdijaną.

Verb[edit]

antwoorden

  1. (intransitive) to answer, reply
    Hij heeft niet geantwoord.
    He has not answered.
