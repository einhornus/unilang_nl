gemeen (1479)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gemêen, from Old Dutch *gimēni, from Proto-Germanic *gamainiz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɣəˈmeːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ge‧meen
  • Rhymes: -eːn

Adjective

gemeen (comparative gemener, superlative gemeenst)

  1. mean, hurtful
  2. intense, extreme
  3. (somewhat dated outside set phrases) common, shared, communal
