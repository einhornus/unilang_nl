symptoom (16611)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • symptoom
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

symptoom n (plural symptomen, diminutive symptoompje n)

  1. A medical symptom, manifestation of a syndrom
  2. An indicator, characteristic of the presence of something else

Synonyms[edit]

  • (medical) ziekteteken n
  • (idicator) aanduiding, aanwijzing, signaal n, verklikker m

Related terms[edit]

  • syndroom n
