heus (1369)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch hovesch, and thus the same word as hoofs (“courtly”).

For the semantic and phonetic development of this word compare also German hübsch (“pretty”) and Central Franconian höösch (“careful, slow”).
