klaver (35955)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch clavere.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklaː.vər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kla‧ver
  • Rhymes: -aːvər

Noun

klaver f or m (plural klavers, diminutive klavertje n)

  1. (botany) clover, plant of the tribe Trifolieae
  2. (card games) clubs

Zelfstandig naamwoord

klaver v / m
