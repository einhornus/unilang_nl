voorwaarde (4647)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch voorwaerde, from vore + waerde, the latter from Old Dutch *warda, from Proto-Germanic *wardō.

Cognate with Middle High German vorwort (“provision, treaty”), Middle English forward, forword (“agreement, covenant”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvoːr.ʋaːr.də/

Noun

voorwaarde f (plural voorwaarden, diminutive voorwaardetje n)
