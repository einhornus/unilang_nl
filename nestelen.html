Pronunciation

  • Audio

Etymology

From Middle Dutch nestelen, from Old Dutch nistilon, from Proto-Germanic *nistlōną (“to build a nest, to nestle”). Equivalent to nest +‎ -elen.

Verb

nestelen

  1. to nest
  2. to nestle

nestelen
