miezerig (20741)


Etymology

From miezelen (“to drizzle”), alternate form of miezeren. Equivalent to miezeren +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

miezerig (comparative miezeriger, superlative miezerigst)

  1. damp, drizzly
  2. measly
