alweer (1161)


Etymology[edit]

From al (“already”) +‎ weer (“again”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɑlˈʋeːr/, (Netherlands) [ɑlˈʋɪːr], (Belgium) [ɑlˈβ̞ɪːr]
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: al‧weer
  • Rhymes:

Adverb[edit]

alweer

  1. (Netherlands) (once) again
    Alweer dit gedoe met die paspoorten!
    Again this hassle with the passports!
    Synonym: weeral
  2. already, already again
    Het is alweer negen uur.
    It’s 9 o'clock already.
    Is het alweer oktober? Wat gaat de tijd snel!
    Is it October already? Time flies!
