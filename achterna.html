achterna (2695)


Etymology[edit]

From achter (“behind”) +‎ na (“after”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑx.tərˈnaː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ach‧ter‧na

Adverb[edit]

achterna

  1. after, behind, following (in movement)
    De hond rende de postbode achterna.
    The dog ran after the postman.
