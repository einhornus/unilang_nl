afzuigen (17252)


Etymology

Compound of af +‎ zuigen.

  • Hyphenation: af‧zui‧gen

Verb

afzuigen

  1. (transitive) to suck off
    1. to exhaust, to drain by means of suction, to extract by suction (of fluids)
    2. to suck clean, to suck in order to remove something
    3. (sex) to perform oral sex
