boerenknecht (42528)



Etymology

Compound of boer (“farmer”) +‎ -en- +‎ knecht (“servant, employee”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌbuː.rə(n)ˈknɛxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boe‧ren‧knecht

Noun

boerenknecht m (plural boerenknechten, diminutive boerenknechtje n)

  1. A farmhand or ranchhand, an agricultural employee under supervision.

Related terms

  • boerenleven n
  • boerenstiel m
  • huisknecht m
  • lansknecht m
