privatiseren (113824)



Etymology

Borrowed from German privatisieren. The sense “to privatise” is likely a semantic loan.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌpri.vaː.tiˈzeː.rə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pri‧va‧ti‧se‧ren
  • Rhymes: -eːrən

Verb

privatiseren

  1. (transitive) to privatise (Uk, Australia), privatize (North America)
  2. (intransitive, obsolete) to live as a private civilian (not holding office or serving in the military), often without an occupation [from early 19th c.]
