Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch pappe.

Noun[edit]

pap m (plural pappen, diminutive papje n)

  1. mush
  2. porridge

Etymology 2[edit]

Shorter form of papa, usually considered more grown-up, whereas papa is considered rather child-like.

Noun[edit]

pap m (uncountable, diminutive paps n)

  1. (colloquial) Pa, dad
