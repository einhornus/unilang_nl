zonnepaneel (66411)



Etymology

From zon +‎ -e- +‎ paneel, calque of English solar panel.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

zonnepaneel n (plural zonnepanelen, diminutive zonnepaneeltje n)

  1. solar panel (photovoltaic panel)
