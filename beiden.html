Etymology 1[edit]

Usage notes[edit]

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch beiden, from Old Dutch beiden, from Proto-Germanic *baidijaną.

Verb[edit]

beiden

  1. (transitive, dated) to await, to bide
  2. (intransitive, archaic) to wait [+ op (for)] [+ naar (for)]
