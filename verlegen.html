verlegen (2814)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch verlegen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vərˈleːɣə(n)/, /vɛrˈleːɣə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ver‧le‧gen
  • Rhymes: -eːɣən

Adjective

verlegen (comparative verlegener, superlative verlegenst)

  1. shy, bashful, timid
  2. embarrassed
  3. lacking, having a shortage
  4. (obsolete) spoiled, rotten
