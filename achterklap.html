achterklap (71323)



Etymology

From achter (“behind”) + klappen (“to talk”), literally “talk behind one's back”.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑx.tərˌklɑp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ach‧ter‧klap

Noun

achterklap m (uncountable, diminutive achterklapje n)

  1. (mean-spirited) gossip
    De werksfeer was verziekt door al de achterklap onder het personeel.
    The working environment had been fouled by all the gossipping among the employees.
