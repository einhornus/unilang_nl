Etymology[edit]

From Dutch chef, from French chef, from Middle French chief, from Old French chief, from Vulgar Latin *capum, from Latin caput (“head”), from Proto-Italic *kaput, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kauput-, *kaput-.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Hyphenation: sèp
