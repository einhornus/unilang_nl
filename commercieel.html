commercieel (17684)


Etymology[edit]

commercie 'commerce, trade' + -ieel '-ial'

Pronunciation[edit]

Adjective[edit]

commercieel (comparative commerciëler, superlative commercieelst)

  1. commercial (of or pertaining to commerce)
