veldtocht (32197)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

17th century. From veld (“field”) +‎ tocht (“expedition”), calque of German Feldzug.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɛl.tɔxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: veld‧tocht

Noun[edit]

veldtocht m (plural veldtochten, diminutive veldtochtje n)

  1. military campaign
    Synonyms: campagne, offensief

Zelfstandig naamwoord

veldtocht
