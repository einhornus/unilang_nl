Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch vrêse, from Old Dutch frēsa, from Proto-Germanic *fraisō (“danger”).

Noun

vrees f (plural vrezen)

  1. (chiefly uncountable) fear, apprehension, angst
Related terms
  • vrezen

vrees
