goggles (69821)



Pronunciation

  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈɡɑ.ɡəlz/
  • Audio (Us)
    		(file)

Etymology 1

Probably from goggle, from the appearance it gives the wearer.

Noun

goggles pl (plural only)

  1. Protective eyewear set in a flexible frame to fit snugly against the face.
  2. Blinds for shying horses.

Synonyms

  • pair of goggles
Related terms
  • safety glasses
  • swimming goggles

Etymology 2

Noun

goggles

  1. plural of goggle

goggles
