teringlijder (48345)



Etymology

Compound of tering (“tuberculosis”) +‎ lijder (“someone afflicted by a disease”).

Pronunciation

Noun

teringlijder m (plural teringlijders, diminutive teringlijdertje n)

  1. (Netherlands, derogatory) asshole (generic term of abuse)
  2. (historical) consumptive, tuberculotic
