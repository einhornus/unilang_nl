zoonlief (55331)



Etymology

Compound of zoon (“son”) + lief (“dear, beloved”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈzoːn.lif/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zoon‧lief

Noun

zoonlief m (uncountable)

  1. A parent's beloved son; sonny boy (frequently used ironically).
