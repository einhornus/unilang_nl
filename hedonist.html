hedonist (105643)



Etymology

From Ancient Greek ἡδονή (hēdonḗ, “pleasure”) + -ist.

Pronunciation

  • EnPR: hēdənĭst, IPA(key): /ˈhiːdənɪst/
  • Hyphenation: he‧don‧ist

Noun

hedonist (plural hedonists)

  1. Someone devoted to hedonism.

Synonyms

  • See also Thesaurus:sensualist

Related terms

  • hedonic
  • hedonism
