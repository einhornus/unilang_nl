Etymology[edit]

Participle[edit]

benauwd

  1. past participle of benauwen

Adjective[edit]

benauwd (comparative benauwder, superlative benauwdst)

  1. stuffy, poorly ventilated
  2. having difficulties with breathing
  3. cramped, too confined
