pleegkind (41709)



Etymology

From plegen (“to care for”) +‎ kind (“child”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: pleeg‧kind

Noun

pleegkind n (plural pleegkinderen, diminutive pleegkindje n)

  1. A foster child, which is neither biological nor adoptive offspring but raised rather as if, temporarily or indefinitely

Related terms

  • pleegouder m
  • pleegzoon m
  • stiefkind n
  • weeskind n

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pleegkind o
