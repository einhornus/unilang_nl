vuurtoren (10425)



Etymology

From vuur (“fire”) +‎ toren (“tower”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvyːrˌtoː.rə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vuur‧to‧ren

Noun

vuurtoren m (plural vuurtorens, diminutive vuurtorentje n)

  1. a lighthouse
  2. (Netherlands) a 250 guilder banknote (before the introduction of the euro)
  3. (derogatory) a redhead, ginger
    Synonyms: roodhaar, roodharige, rosse, rossekop

Related terms

  • vuurbaken
  • vuursein
  • vuursignaal
