Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch suvel, from Old Dutch *sūval (“food eaten with bread”), from Proto-Germanic *suflą, *sūflą (“entremets, viands”).

Cognate with Middle Low German sûvel (“dairy products”), Old High German suvil, sufili, sufila, Old English sufl, sufel, sufol (“sowl”).
