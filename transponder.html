transponder (26432)



Etymology

Blend of transmitter +‎ responder.

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɒndə(ɹ)

Noun

transponder (plural transponders)

  1. A radio or radar transceiver that transmits some signal in response to receiving a predetermined signal

Related terms

  • transpond
