koffietje (60388)



Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

koffietje n (plural koffietjes)

  1. Diminutive of koffie
  2. a cup of coffee

Usage notes

Like some other diminutives (for example: ijsje), this diminutive ("a small coffee") has taken on an additional and related but different meaning than its original noun ("a cup of coffee").
