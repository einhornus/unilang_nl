pizzeria (17593)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian pizzeria, from pizza + -eria (“-ery”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌpiːt.səˈɹiː.ə/
  • Rhymes: -iːə

Noun

pizzeria (plural pizzerias or pizzerie)

  1. A restaurant that bakes and sells pizzas.

Synonyms

  • pizza parlor (Us)
  • pizza parlour (Canada)
  • pizza joint
  • pizza place

Related terms

  • pizza
  • pizza boy
  • pizza pie
  • pizza slice
  • pizza topping

Zelfstandig naamwoord

pizzeria v

  1. een restaurant dat zich toelegt op pizza's
