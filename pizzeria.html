pizzeria (17593)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Italian pizzeria, from pizza + -eria (“-ery”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌpiːt.səˈɹiː.ə/
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

pizzeria (plural pizzerias or pizzerie)

  1. A restaurant that bakes and sells pizzas.

Synonyms[edit]

  • pizza parlor (Us)
  • pizza parlour (Canada)
  • pizza joint
  • pizza place

Related terms[edit]

  • pizza slice
