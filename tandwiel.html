tandwiel (87553)



Etymology

tand (“tooth”) +‎ wiel (“wheel”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: tand‧wiel

Noun

tandwiel n (plural tandwielen, diminutive tandwieltje n)

  1. gearwheel, cogwheel

Synonyms
