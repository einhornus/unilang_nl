binnenbrengen (25887)



Etymology

Compound of binnen + brengen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɪ.nə(n)ˌbrɛ.ŋə(n)/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: bin‧nen‧breng‧en

Verb

binnenbrengen

  1. to come in, to move in; to get closer
  2. to bring in (to earn money for a family or company)
