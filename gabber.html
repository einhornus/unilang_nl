gabber (12465)



Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish חבֿר (khaver, “friend”), from Hebrew חָבֵר (khavér, “friend”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɑ.bər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: gab‧ber
  • Rhymes: -ɑbər

Noun

gabber m (plural gabbers, diminutive gabbertje n)

  1. a guy
  2. a friend; a pal
  3. (music) gabber (hardcore subgenre)
  4. A fan of gabber music, a gabber
