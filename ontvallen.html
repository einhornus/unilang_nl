ontvallen (37924)



Etymology

From ont- (“away”) +‎ vallen (“to fall”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔntˈvɑlə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ont‧val‧len
  • Rhymes: -ɑlən

Verb

ontvallen

  1. (intransitive) to slip out, to say something unintended
  2. (intransitive) to become lost
  3. (intransitive) to die
