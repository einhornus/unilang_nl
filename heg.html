Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch hegge, from Old Dutch *hegga, from Proto-West Germanic *haggju (compare English hedge German Hecke), from Proto-Indo-European *kagʰyo-, *kagʰyóm (“enclosure”), related to Latin cohum, Welsh cae.
