handig (3720)


Etymology

From Old Dutch *handag, from Proto-Germanic *handugaz. Corresponds to hand +‎ -ig.

Pronunciation

  • (Netherlands) IPA(key): /ɦɑndɪx/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

handig (comparative handiger, superlative handigst)

  1. handy, convenient, practical
  2. handy, capable, dexterous
  3. clever, crafty, cunning
