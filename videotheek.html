videotheek (16749)



Etymology

From video + -theek. Compare German Videothek, which could well be the origin of the Dutch term.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

  • Rhymes: -eːk

Noun

videotheek f (plural videotheken, diminutive videotheekje n)

  1. video rental store [from 1969]
