zeilschip (48015)

Etymology[edit]

Compound of zeilen (“to sail”) +‎ schip (“ship”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzɛi̯l.sxɪp/
  • Audio

Noun[edit]

zeilschip n (plural zeilschepen, diminutive zeilscheepje n)

  1. A sailer, ship powered by wind blowing in its sail(s)
