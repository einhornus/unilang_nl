frater (54502)



Etymology

From Dutch frater, from Latin frater, from Proto-Italic *frātēr, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₂tēr.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fratər/
  • Hyphenation: fra‧têr

Noun

fratêr (plural, first-person possessive fraterku, second-person possessive fratermu, third-person possessive fraternya)

  1. (Catholicism) a candidate for priesthood

Related terms

  • padri
  • pastor

Zelfstandig naamwoord

frater m
