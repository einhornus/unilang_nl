barbier (36324)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch barbier, from Old French barbier.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bɑrˈbiːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bar‧bier
  • Rhymes: -iːr

Noun

barbier m (plural barbiers, diminutive barbiertje n, feminine barbierster)

  1. (chiefly historical) barber (hairdresser)
  2. (historical) surgeon
