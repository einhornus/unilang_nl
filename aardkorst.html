aardkorst (41161)



Etymology

Compound of Aarde (“Earth”) + korst (“crust”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaːrt.kɔrst/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: aard‧korst

Noun

aardkorst f or m (uncountable)

  1. (geology) Earth's crust
