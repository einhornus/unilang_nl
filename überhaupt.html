Adverb[edit]

überhaupt

  1. at all; anyway; in the first place
    Hoe is het heelal überhaupt ontstaan?
    How did the universe originate in the first place?
    Weet je überhaupt wel wat dat betekent, of praat je gewoon uit je nek?
    Do you know what that means at all, or are you just making things up as you go along?
