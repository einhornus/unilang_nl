koud (816)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch cout, from Old Dutch *kalt, from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gel-.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /kɑu̯t/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: koud
  • Rhymes:
  • Homophone: kauwt

Adjective[edit]

koud (comparative kouder, superlative koudst)

  1. cold (temperature)
    Antonyms: warm, heet
  2. cold (unfriendly)
    Antonym: warm

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

koud
