zeeman (7895)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch seeman, equivalent to zee (“sea”) +‎ man.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeː.mɑn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zee‧man

Noun[edit]

zeeman m (plural zeemannen or zeelui or zeelieden, diminutive zeemannetje n)

  1. A seaman, a male sailor.

Synonyms[edit]

  • matroos m
  • zeevaarder m

Related terms[edit]

  • zeemaat m
  • zeemacht m or f
  • zeeschuimer m
