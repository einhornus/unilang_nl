handicap (10411)


Etymology

Borrowed from English handicap.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛn.diˌkɛp/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

handicap m (plural handicaps, diminutive handicapje n)

  1. disability
  2. handicap, disadvantage
  3. (sports, golf) handicap, measure of ability

Zelfstandig naamwoord
