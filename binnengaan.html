binnengaan (9652)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From binnen (“inside”) +‎ gaan (“walk, go”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɪnə(n)ɣaːn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: bin‧nen‧gaan

Verb[edit]

binnengaan

  1. (transitive) to enter, go into (a room, etc.)
  2. (intransitive) to go inside
