reebok (31625)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch reeboc. Equivalent to ree (“roe”) +‎ bok (“buck”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈreː.bɔk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ree‧bok

Noun

reebok m (plural reebokken, diminutive reebokje n)

  1. roebuck
  2. (obsolete, South Africa) An antelope native to southern Africa.
