alleszins (15207)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch allesins. Morphologically, it consists of alle (“all”) +‎ -s- (“genitival interfix”) +‎ zin (“sense”) +‎ -s (“adverb-forming suffix”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑ.ləˈsɪns/, /ˈɑ.ləˌsɪns/
  • Audio

  • Hyphenation: al‧les‧zins

Adverb

alleszins
