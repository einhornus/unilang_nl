heinen (92594)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch heinen. Related to heg and haag.

Pronunciation

  • Hyphenation: hei‧nen
  • Rhymes: -ɛi̯nən

Verb

heinen

  1. (transitive, dated) to fence (off), to enclose
