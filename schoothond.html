schoothond (59285)



Etymology

From schoot (“lap”) + hond (“hond”), perhaps a calque of German Schoßhund.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsxoːt.ɦɔnt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: schoot‧hond

Noun

schoothond m (plural schoothonden, diminutive schoothondje n)

  1. (literally) A lapdog, a canine pet which is small and/or frequently cuddled.
  2. (figurative) A human, (nearly) willless 'pet' or flunky.

Related terms

  • schootkind n
  • schootzoon
  • voorschoot
