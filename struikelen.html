struikelen (14495)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch strukelen. A frequentative of older struiken.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈstrœy̯kələ(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

struikelen

  1. To trip, fall over or stumble over an object

Related terms[edit]

  • struik
