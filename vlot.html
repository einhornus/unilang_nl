Etymology 1

From earlier vlot (“flowing; fluent; fluid”), from the Middle Dutch phrases int vlot, ant vlot (“on water; floating”). Compare German flott (from Middle Dutch).

Adjective

vlot (comparative vlotter, superlative vlotst)

  1. smooth, easy
  2. quick, fast
