ammunitie (22331)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French amunition, from Latin munire (“to equip”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌɑ.myˈni.(t)si/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: am‧mu‧ni‧tie
  • Rhymes: -itsi

Noun

ammunitie f (uncountable)

  1. ammunition, ammo

Synonyms

  • munitie
  • schietvoorraad

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ammunitie v
