verwoording (97641)



Etymology

From verwoorden (“to word”) +‎ -ing.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

verwoording f (plural verwoordingen, diminutive verwoordingetje n)

  1. the choice of words; wording
  2. to articulate something with words; articulation

Synonyms

  • (wording): woordkeuze, formulering
  • (articulation): uitspreking, articulatie
