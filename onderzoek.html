Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔn.dərˌzuk/
  • audio

  • Hyphenation: on‧der‧zoek

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch ondersoec.

Noun

onderzoek n (plural onderzoeken, diminutive onderzoekje n)

  1. research, experiment, study, trial, test, survey
  2. investigation, inquiry, examination, survey

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

onderzoek

  1. first-person singular present indicative of onderzoeken
  2. imperative of onderzoeken
