ruitenwisser (38968)

Pronunciation

  • Audio

  • Hyphenation: rui‧ten‧wis‧ser

Noun

ruitenwisser m (plural ruitenwissers, diminutive ruitenwissertje n)

Related terms

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ruitenwisser m
