decoratie (22587)



Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

decoratie f (plural decoraties, diminutive decoratietje n)

  1. ornamental decoration, such as for various festivities
  2. honorary decoration, such as a medal

Synonyms

  • (ornamental) ornamentatie, versiering
  • (honorary) ereteken, onderscheiding; versiersel

Related terms

  • decoreren
  • gedecoreerde
