gissen (18800)



Pronunciation

  • gissen
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪsən

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch gissen, further etymology unclear.

Cognate with English guess, West Frisian gissen, Swedish gissa, Danish gisse.

Verb

gissen

  1. To make a guess, estimate, have a go
  2. To presume, reckon
