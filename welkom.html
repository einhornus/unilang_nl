welkom (559)


Etymology[edit]

Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *wiljakumô. Cognate to English welcome, West Frisian wolkom, German willkommen, Danish velkommen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɛlkɔm/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

welkom (not comparable)

  1. welcome

Interjection[edit]

welkom

  1. welcome!
    Welkom in Brabant
    Welcome to Brabant
