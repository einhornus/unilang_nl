warenhuis (10293)


Etymology

From waren (“goods”) +‎ huis (“house”), calque of German Warenhaus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋaː.rə(n)ˌɦœy̯s/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wa‧ren‧huis

Noun

warenhuis n (plural warenhuizen, diminutive warenhuisje n)

  1. A department store.
    Synonym: warenmagazijn
  2. A warehouse.
    Synonym: pakhuis
  3. A greenhouse in which crop rotation is practised.
