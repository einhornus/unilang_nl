vorstin (31473)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vorstinne. Equivalent to vorst +‎ -in.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vɔrˈstɪn/, /vɔrsˈtɪn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vor‧stin
  • Rhymes: -ɪn

Noun

vorstin f (plural vorstinnen, diminutive vorstinnetje n, masculine vorst)

  1. a princess, the daughter of a king, elector (in the Holy Roman Empire), emperor, or the wife or daughter of certain princes
  2. a princess, the female reigning monarch of a principality
  3. (generic) a female potentate, such as certain especially mediaeval duchesses

Zelfstandig naamwoord

vorstin v

    • Juliana en Beatrix waren Nederlandse vorstinnen.
