ploffen (43207)


Etymology

From plof +‎ -en.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɔfən

Verb

ploffen

  1. to thud; to make a dull sound (such as the sound made of an object falling on concrete)
  2. to burst, to explode
