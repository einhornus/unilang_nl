kachel (11676)


Etymology[edit]

Shortening of kacheloven, partially a borrowing, partially a calque of German Kachelofen, from Kachel (“tile”) +‎ Ofen (“stove”). The original sense of “tiled stove” has been generalized to any type of stove.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • kachel

Noun[edit]

kachel f (plural kachels, diminutive kacheltje n)

Adjective[edit]

kachel (used only predicatively, comparative meer kachel, superlative meest kachel)
