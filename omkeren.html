omkeren (8480)



Etymology

From om + keren. Compare German umkehren.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

omkeren

  1. (transitive or intransitive) to turn around
  2. (transitive) to reverse
  3. (transitive) to turn upside down
