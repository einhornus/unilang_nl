tegen (130)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch tegen, tjegen, from te jegen, the latter from Old Dutch gegin, from Proto-Germanic *gagin. See also jegens.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈteːɣə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: ,

Preposition[edit]

tegen

  1. against
  2. to, towards
    lief tegen iemand doen ― to act friendly towards someone
