vaderland (6373)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch vāderlant. Equivalent to vader (“father”) +‎ land (“land”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvaː.dərˌlɑnt/
  • vaderland
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: va‧der‧land

Noun[edit]

vaderland n (plural vaderlanden, diminutive vaderlandje n)

  1. fatherland, one's own native land/nation and/or the country of one's forefathers, the possible object of patriotism
  2. (religion) Heaven
    Synonym: hemel
