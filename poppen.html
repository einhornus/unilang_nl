Etymology 1[edit]

From pop +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

poppen

  1. (obsolete) To play with puppets
  2. To play (games etc.) in general
  3. To court and/or have sex
  4. To show off
  5. To make or provide (a) puppet(s) etc.

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch [Term?], of unclear origin.

poppen
