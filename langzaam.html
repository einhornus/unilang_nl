langzaam (1342)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch lancsam, lancsom, lancsaem, from Old Dutch *langsam, from Proto-West Germanic *langasam (“lengthy, long-lasting”). Equivalent to lang +‎ -zaam.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɑŋ.zaːm/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

langzaam (comparative langzamer, superlative langzaamst)
