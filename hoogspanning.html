hoogspanning (57220)



Etymology

From hoog (“high”) +‎ spanning (“voltage”), calque of German Hochspannung.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hoog‧span‧ning

Noun

hoogspanning f (plural hoogspanningen, diminutive hoogspanninkje n)

  1. (electricity) high voltage

hoogspanning v
