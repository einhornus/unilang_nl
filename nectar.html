Noun[edit]

nectar m (plural nectars)

  1. (Greek mythology, Roman mythology) nectar, beverage drunk by the Olympians
    Synonym: godendrank
    Coordinate term: ambrozijn
  2. (botany, insects) nectar, liquid produced by flowers
