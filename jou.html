jou (88)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch jou, from Old Dutch">Old Dutch *jū, a northern (Frisian?) variant of *iu, from Proto-Germanic *iwwiz, a West Germanic variant of *izwiz. Doublet of u.

Pronoun

jou

  1. objective form of jij (“you (singular)”): you
