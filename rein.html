rein (14908)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch reine, from Old Dutch reini, from Proto-West Germanic *hrainī, from Proto-Germanic *hrainiz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /rɛi̯n/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

rein (comparative reiner, superlative reinst)

  1. (formal) clean, spotless
  2. pure, sheer

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

rein

  1. zonder vuil
Synoniemen
  • net, proper, schoon, zindelijk
Afgeleide begrippen
  • reincultuur, reindoorn, reingenoot, reinheid, reinig, reinsteen, reinvaar, reinwater
