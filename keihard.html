keihard (5272)


Etymology[edit]

kei- (“boulder”) +‎ hard (“hard”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kei‧hard

Adjective[edit]

keihard (not comparable)

  1. hard as a rock, extremely powerful
  2. very loud (sound)

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

keihard
