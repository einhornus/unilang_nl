ranja (67862)



Etymology

Genericised trademark, from oranje or a related word. The trademarked name was coined specifically for an orange-based cordial.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɑn.jaː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ran‧ja

Noun

ranja f (uncountable)

  1. (Netherlands) sweetened drink (sweet drink based on processed fruit juice, may be based on cordial, may or may not be carbonated)
  2. (Netherlands, dated) orange-based sweetened drink
