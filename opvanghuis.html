opvanghuis (22142)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

Compound of opvangen (“to shelter, to temporarily house”) +‎ huis (“house”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔp.fɑŋˌɦœy̯s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: op‧vang‧huis

Noun[edit]

opvanghuis n (plural opvanghuizen, diminutive opvanghuisje n)

  1. A shelter, a building where people or animals who are threatened, victimised or homeless are (temporarily) housed.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

opvanghuis
