bandenlichter (49963)



Etymology

Compound of band + -en- + lichten + -er.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ban‧den‧lich‧ter

Noun

bandenlichter m (plural bandenlichters, diminutive bandenlichtertje n)

  1. tyre lever, tire iron (for bike wheels)
    Synonym: bandafnemer
