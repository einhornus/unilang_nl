scheet (6377)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sxeːt/, [sxeːt], [sxei̯t]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: scheet
  • Rhymes: -eːt

Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schete. Ablaut formation from schijten.

Noun

scheet m (plural scheten, diminutive scheetje n)

  1. (vulgar) fart
    Synonyms: bout, buikwind, ruft, wind
  2. (humorous) a windy quantity, a bit
    Synonym: zucht
Related terms
  • scheetje
  • scheten
  • schijt
  • schijten
