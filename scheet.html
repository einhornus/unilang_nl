Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch schete. Ablaut formation from schijten.

Noun[edit]

scheet m (plural scheten, diminutive scheetje n)

  1. (vulgar) fart
    Synonyms: bout, buikwind, ruft, wind
  2. (humorous) a windy quantity, a bit
    Synonym: zucht
