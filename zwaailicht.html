zwaailicht (37520)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From zwaaien +‎ licht.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzʋaːi̯.lɪxt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zwaai‧licht

Noun[edit]

zwaailicht n (plural zwaailichten, diminutive zwaailichtje n)

  1. A revolving light used to signal danger, commonly used on emergency vehicles; a rotating emergency light.
