Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch reise, from Old Dutch *reisa, from Proto-Germanic *raisō.

Noun[edit]

reis f or m (plural reizen, diminutive reisje n)

  1. travel
  2. trip, tour

See the etymology of the main entry.

reis

  2. imperative of reizen
