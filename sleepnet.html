sleepnet (88127)



Etymology

Compound of slepen (“to drag”) +‎ net (“net”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsleːp.nɛt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sleep‧net

Noun

sleepnet n (plural sleepnetten, diminutive sleepnetje n)

  1. trawl, dragnet

Zelfstandig naamwoord

sleepnet o
