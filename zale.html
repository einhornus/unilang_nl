zale (110723)



Etymology

From Middle High German zaln, zalen, from Old High German zalōn, from Proto-Germanic *talōną. Cognate with German zahlen, Dutch talen, English tale, Icelandic tala.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtsɑlə/

Verb

zale (third-person singular simple present zalt, past participle zalt, past subjunctive zalti, auxiliary haa)

  1. to pay
