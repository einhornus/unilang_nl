schutter (3660)

<br>

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch schutter, schuttre, schotter, scuttere, alteration of earlier schutte, scutte (“shooter”), from Old Dutch *scutto, from Proto-Germanic *skutjô.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsxʏtər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes:

Noun[edit]

schutter m (plural schutters, diminutive schuttertje n)

  1. marksman, shooter

schutter
