Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch salve, from Old Dutch salva, from Proto-Germanic *salbō.

Noun[edit]

zalf f (plural zalven, diminutive zalfje n)

  1. salve, cream, ointment
  2. (The Hague, colloquial) mayonnaise
    Synonyms: mayo, mayonaise

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

zalf

  1. first-person singular present indicative of zalven
  2. imperative of zalven
