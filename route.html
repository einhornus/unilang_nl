route (2765)


Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Middle French route, from Old French route, from Latin rupta (via).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈru.tə/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: rou‧te
  • Rhymes: -utə

Noun[edit]

route f (plural routes or routen, diminutive routetje n)

  1. route, course, way (particular pathway or direction one travels)
  2. road, route
