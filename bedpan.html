bedpan (71060)



Etymology

From bed + pan ("chamber pot").

Noun

bedpan (plural bedpans)

  1. A pan used for urination and defecation while in bed, now usually for reasons of medical necessity or convenience.
    The nurse emptied the patient's bedpan each morning.

Synonyms

  • See Thesaurus:chamber pot
