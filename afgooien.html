Etymology

Verb

afgooien

  1. (transitive) to throw off (to throw something of oneself, to remove from one's body by throwing)
  2. (transitive, games) to throw out (to make someone go out of a game by throwing and hitting)
  3. (transitive) to throw off (to make something fall down by throwing)
