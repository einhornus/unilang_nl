speld (12268)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch spelde, from older spelle, from Latin spīnula, diminutive of spīna.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /spɛlt/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: speld
  • Rhymes:
  • Homophone: spelt

Noun[edit]

speld f (plural spelden, diminutive speldje n)

  1. A pin (fastening device).
  2. A brooch.
    Synonym: broche

Synonyms[edit]

  • (pin): pin
