enigszins (7606)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch enegssins. Morphologically, it consists of enig (“some”) +‎ -s- (“genitival interfix”) +‎ zin (“sense”) +‎ -s (“adverb-forming suffix”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

enigszins

  1. somewhat; to some degree; rather; slightly
  2. at all; in any way
