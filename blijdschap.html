blijdschap (10935)



Etymology

From blij + -schap. The -d- is a relic of blijde, the archaic form of blij.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈblɛi̯t.sxɑp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: blijd‧schap

Noun

blijdschap f (uncountable)

  1. joy, gladness

Synonyms

  • vreugde
