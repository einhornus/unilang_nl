edelman (13032)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch ēdelman. Equivalent to edel +‎ man. Cognate with German Edelmann.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈeː.dəlˌmɑn/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: edel‧man

Noun[edit]

edelman m (plural edellieden or edelmannen, diminutive edelmannetje n, feminine edelvrouw)

  1. nobleman, aristocrat

Synonyms[edit]

  • adellijke m
  • aristocraat m
  • edele m
