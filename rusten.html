rusten (1579)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈrʏstə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch rusten. Equivalent to rust +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

rusten

  1. (intransitive) to rest
  2. (intransitive) to lie, be supported (by something)

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch rusten, from Old Dutch *rusten, from Proto-Germanic *hrustijaną.

Verb[edit]

rusten

  1. (transitive, obsolete) to equip, to provide with what is needed

Werkwoord

rusten

  1. werk of andere activiteit staken om het lichaam in staat te stellen weer op krachten te komen
