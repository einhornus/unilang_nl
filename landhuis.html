landhuis (12916)


Etymology

16th century. Compound of land (“land, country”) +‎ huis (“house”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɑnt.ɦœy̯s/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: land‧huis

Noun

landhuis n (plural landhuizen, diminutive landhuisje n)

  1. country house (UK), villa (large, luxury residence in a campestral or semirural environment)
  2. (obsolete) farmhouse (residence as part of a farm)
