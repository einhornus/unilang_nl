slavenschip (40171)



Etymology

Compound of slaaf +‎ -en +‎ schip.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈslaː.və(n)ˌsxɪp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: sla‧ven‧schip

Noun

slavenschip n (plural slavenschepen, diminutive slavenscheepje n)

slavenschip o
