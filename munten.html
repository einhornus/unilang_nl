munten (6565)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈmʏntə(n)/
  • Rhymes:

Etymology 1[edit]

From munt +‎ -en.

Verb[edit]

munten

  1. (transitive) to mint, to coin

Etymology 2[edit]

From Middle Dutch munten, from Germanic. Cognate with Low German münten, West Frisian muntsje, mintsje, German münzen, English mint (“to intend, purpose”). More at mint.

Verb[edit]

munten

  1. (transitive) to aim at, target
Related terms[edit]
  • monter

munten
