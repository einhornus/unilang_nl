ontdoen (11558)


Etymology[edit]

From ont- +‎ doen

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɔntˈdun/
  		• (file)
  • Hyphenation: ont‧doen
  • Rhymes:

Verb[edit]

ontdoen

  1. to doff (to remove or take off, especially of clothing)
  2. to disabuse (free of a misconception)
  3. to undo (to reverse)

Werkwoord

ontdoen
