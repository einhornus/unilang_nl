surfer (19531)


Etymology

surf +‎ -er.

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɜː(r)fə(r)

Noun

surfer (plural surfers)

  1. A person who rides a surfboard.
  2. A person who surfs the Internet.
  3. A duck, the surf scoter (Melanitta perspicillata).

Synonyms

  • surf rider (obsolete)
