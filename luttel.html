luttel (127568)



Etymology

From Old Dutch *luttil, from Proto-Germanic *lūtilaz.

Cognate with English little, regional German lütt and German (archaic) lützel.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈlʏ.təl/
  • Rhymes: -ʏtəl

Determiner

luttel (comparative lutteler, superlative luttelst)

  1. little, few, mere
    In luttele seconden had hij met het apparaatje de zware groene deur geopend en gleed naar binnen.
    In just a few seconds, he had opened the heavy green door with his gadget and slid inside.
