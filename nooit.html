nooit (98)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch nooit. Equivalent to n- + ooit.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /noːit/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nooit
  • Rhymes: -oːi̯t

Adverb

nooit

  1. never
