armpit (55124)



Etymology

From Middle English armpitt, arme-pitt, armput, equivalent to arm + pit.

Pronunciation

  • (Us) IPA(key): /ˈɑɹmˌpɪt/
  • Audio (Us)
    		(file)

Noun

armpit (plural armpits)

  1. The cavity beneath the junction of the arm and shoulder.
  2. Somewhere or something considered unpleasant or undesirable.
    the armpit of America

Synonyms

  • (cavity beneath the junction of the arm and shoulder): axilla, oxter, underarm; armhole (now Uk dialect)
