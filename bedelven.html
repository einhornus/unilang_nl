bedelven (58093)



Etymology

From Middle Dutch bedelven. Equivalent to be- +‎ delven.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bəˈdɛlvə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧del‧ven
  • Rhymes: -ɛlvən

Verb

bedelven

  1. (transitive) to bury, to cover with earth
  2. (transitive, figurative) to overwhelm
