Noun[edit]

vrijer m (plural vrijers, diminutive vrijertje n, feminine vrijster)

  1. (obsolete) suitor
  2. lover, friend
  3. (derogatory, of women) old spinster (“oude vrijster”)

  2. (informeel) vriend, geliefde
