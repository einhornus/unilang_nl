nieuwsgierig (2735)


Etymology[edit]

From Middle Dutch [Term?], mentioned in the Teuthonista. Equivalent to nieuws (“news”) +‎ gierig (“greedy”), literally 'greedy for news'. Compare German neugierig.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌniu̯ˈsxiː.rəx/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: nieuws‧gie‧rig

Adjective[edit]

nieuwsgierig (comparative nieuwsgieriger, superlative nieuwsgierigst)

  1. curious, inquisitive
