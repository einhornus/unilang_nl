zeebodem (18305)

Etymology[edit]

Compound of zee +‎ bodem.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzeːˌboː.dəm/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: zee‧bo‧dem

Noun[edit]

zeebodem m (plural zeebodems)

  1. The bottom of the sea, seafloor, seabed.
    Het schip zonk naar de zeebodem.
    The ship sank to the bottom of the sea.

zeebodem
