wedloop (55450)



Etymology

Compound of wedde +‎ loop.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋɛt.loːp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wed‧loop

Noun

wedloop m (plural wedlopen, diminutive wedloopje n)

  1. A race in running (speed contest in running).
    Synonym: wedren
  2. Any competition, instance of competing.
