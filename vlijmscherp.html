vlijmscherp (27340)



Etymology

From vlijm (“lancet-type knife”) + scherp (“sharp”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vlɛi̯mˈsxɛrp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: vlijm‧scherp

Adjective

vlijmscherp (comparative vlijmscherper, superlative vlijmscherpst)

  1. (literally) razor-sharp, said of blades and other sharpened objects
  2. (by extension) painfully sharp or acute, notably said of sensorial functions
  3. (figuratively) precisely, either usefully, dangerously or harmfully, cutting, biting, burning, penetrating etc., said of speech, reasoning etc.
