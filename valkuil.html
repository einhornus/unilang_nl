valkuil (33718)

<br>

Etymology 1[edit]

From vallen (“to fall”) +‎ kuil or from val (“trap”) +‎ kuil.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɑlˌkœy̯l/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: val‧kuil

Noun[edit]

valkuil m (plural valkuilen, diminutive valkuiltje n)

  1. pitfall (type of trap)
  2. pitfall (hazard in general)

Etymology 2[edit]

From valk (“falcon”) +‎ uil (“owl”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvɑlkˌœy̯l/, also /ˈvɑləkœy̯l/ in the south
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: valk‧uil

Noun[edit]

valkuil m or f (plural valkuilen, diminutive valkuiltje n)

  1. hawk owl (owl in the genus Ninox)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

valkuil

  1. gecamoufleerde kuil om dieren (of mensen) in te vangen
  2. wat iemand tot fouten kan verleiden

valkuil
