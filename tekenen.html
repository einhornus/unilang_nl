Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch têkenen, from Old Dutch *tēkanen, from Proto-Germanic *taiknijaną.

Verb[edit]

tekenen

  1. (transitive) to draw (by pencil)
  2. (transitive) to sign (write a signature)
