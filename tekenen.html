tekenen (1423)


Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈteːkənə(n)/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: te‧ke‧nen

Etymology 1[edit]

From Middle Dutch têkenen, from Old Dutch *tēkanen, from Proto-Germanic *taiknijaną.

Verb[edit]

tekenen

  1. (transitive) to draw (by pencil)
  2. (transitive) to sign (write a signature)

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun[edit]

tekenen
