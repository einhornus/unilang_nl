Etymology[edit]

From goud (“gold”) +‎ mijn (“mine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɣɑu̯t.mɛi̯n/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: goud‧mijn
  • Rhymes: -ɑu̯tmɛi̯n

Noun[edit]

goudmijn f (plural goudmijnen, diminutive goudmijntje n)

  1. (literally) goldmine (mine where gold ore is extracted)
  2. (figuratively) goldmine (source of rich, easy profit)
