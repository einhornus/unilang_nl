spijkerbroek (15115)


Etymology

Compound of spijker (“nail (metal fastener)”) +‎ broek (“trousers, pants”). Named after the use of rivets in jeans, which were thought to resemble the heads of nails.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈspɛi̯kərˌbruk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: spij‧ker‧broek

Noun

spijkerbroek f (plural spijkerbroeken, diminutive spijkerbroekje n)

  1. (Netherlands) A pair of jeans.

Usage notes

Not used in Flanders, where the loanword jeans is used instead.

Synonyms

  • jeans
  • texasbroek

Zelfstandig naamwoord

spijkerbroek v/m

  1. (kleding) een broek van spijkerstof
