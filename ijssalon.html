ijssalon (35068)


Etymology

Compound of ijs (“ice cream, ice”) +‎ salon (“salon, café”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: ijs‧sa‧lon

Noun

ijssalon m or n (plural ijssalons)

  1. ice-cream bar
