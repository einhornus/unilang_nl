gangsta (47408)


Etymology

Eye dialect spelling of gangster.

Pronunciation

  • Audio (Au)
    		(file)

Noun

gangsta (plural gangstas)

  1. (slang) A member of an urban criminal or street gang.

Adjective

gangsta

  1. (slang) Exhibiting the traits or demeanor associated with the traits or lifestyle of urban thugs or hustlers.
