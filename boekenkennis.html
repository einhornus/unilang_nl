boekenkennis (108712)



Etymology

Compound of boek + -en- + kennis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbu.kə(n)ˌkɛ.nɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: boe‧ken‧ken‧nis

Noun

boekenkennis f (uncountable)

  1. (chiefly derogatory) knowledge primarily based on reading about a subject rather than personal experience, book knowledge
    Synonym: boekenwijsheid
  2. knowledge about books, such as bibliography or bibliology
