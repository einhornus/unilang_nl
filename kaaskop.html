kaaskop (54174)



Etymology

Compound of kaas (“cheese”) + kop (“cup”, “head”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkaːs.kɔp/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: kaas‧kop

Noun

kaaskop m (plural kaaskoppen, diminutive kaaskopje n)

  1. a mold in which Edam cheese is pressed
  2. (derogatory) idiot, stupid person
  3. (derogatory) Dutch person (in general)
  4. (derogatory, Southern Netherlands) Hollander

Usage notes

As a term for a Dutch person kaaskop is also often used in a self-referential or self-deprecating manner. It is not considered particularly insulting.

Related terms

  • kopkaas
