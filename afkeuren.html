afkeuren (25052)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch afcoren. Equivalent to af +‎ keuren.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑfkøːrə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: af‧keu‧ren

Verb

afkeuren

  1. (transitive) to disapprove of
  2. (transitive) to condemn
  3. (transitive) to fail (at an inspection)
  4. (transitive) to refuse, reject, declare unfit (for military service)
