Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /mən/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Pronoun[edit]

m'n (dependent possessive)

  1. contraction of mijn
    Ik word altijd wakker met een wijsje in m'n hoofd
    I always wake up with a song in my head

Usage notes[edit]

This contracted form is seen chiefly in informal writing. Otherwise the full form mijn is written out even though the pronunciation is that of m’n.
