overwinning (2515)


Etymology

From overwinnen +‎ -ing

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌoːvərˈʋɪnɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: over‧win‧ning

Noun

overwinning f (plural overwinningen, diminutive overwinningetje n or overwinninkje n)

  1. victory, triumph
  2. individual win, as in a contest

Synonyms

  • triomf
  • victorie
  • zege

Related terms
