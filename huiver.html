huiver (25774)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦœy̯.vər/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: hui‧ver
  • Rhymes: -œy̯vər

Etymology 1

From huiveren.

Noun

huiver m (plural huivers, diminutive huivertje n)

  1. shiver (out of fear or from the cold), tremble
    Synonyms: angst, huivering, vrees
  2. fear, trepidation
    Synonyms: huivering, rilling, sidder

Etymology 2

Verb

huiver

  1. first-person singular present indicative of huiveren
  2. imperative of huiveren

Zelfstandig naamwoord

huiver m [1]
