doodlopen (131852)



Etymology

From dood +‎ lopen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈdoːtloːpə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

doodlopen

  1. (intransitive) to end in a dead end
  2. (reflexive) to exhaust oneself by running
  3. (transitive, nautical) to outsail another ship
